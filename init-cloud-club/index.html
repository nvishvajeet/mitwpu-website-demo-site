INIT -Cloud Club is dedicated to fostering a dynamic learning environment where members can actively participate in various projects, community-driven educational initiatives, meetups and coding competitions. Our primary goal is to cultivate a community of passionate cloud computing enthusiasts who are well-equipped to meet the increasing demand for cloud computing in the years ahead.

Our mission is centred around promoting coding practices on campus and establishing interest groups focused on cloud computing and technological advancements. We are committed to organising events and workshops that are in alignment with our mission, offering a platform for members to network with like-minded individuals, not only within academia but also at an industry level.
