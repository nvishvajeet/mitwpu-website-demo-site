The MIT-WPU Cultural Club, also known as the Theatre Club, has been an integral part of MIT World Peace University since 1983. Our goal is to nurture creativity, promote artistic expression, and foster a deep appreciation for the performing arts among our students. We offer a supportive and inclusive environment for budding actors, directors, playwrights, and all theatre enthusiasts. Through workshops, competitions, and events, we aim to help students understand the power of storytelling and theatre. We actively participate in state-level and national-level competitions and festivals, celebrating India's rich cultural heritage through theatre.
