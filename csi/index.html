The Computer Society of India (CSI) is a prestigious organization representing computer professionals in India. Established in 1965, CSI boasts a network of 72 chapters, 511 student branches, and over 100,000 members. CSI is dedicated to upholding professional integrity, fostering collaboration, and providing knowledge exchange opportunities.

CSI's mission is to advance computer and IT knowledge in India through conferences, workshops, training programmes, and publications. It is committed to holistic student development, bridging the gap between industry and academia, and promoting social responsibility. Join us to explore the world of technology.
