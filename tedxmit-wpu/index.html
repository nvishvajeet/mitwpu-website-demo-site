TEDxMIT-WPU is a local gathering where we curate and share live TED-like talks and performances with our community. As a TEDx University event, we follow the well-established TED talk conference format, offering diverse topics and content free from bias. The 'x' in TEDx signifies our independent status and as a university event, we are entirely student-driven, operating within the framework set by the TED Foundation.

Team TEDxMIT-WPU comprises a group of inquisitive individuals from various disciplines across our campus, all eager to absorb knowledge and insights from our esteemed speakers. Committed to our promise of providing a "brain spa," we have hosted truly unique and inspiring speakers, including thought leaders such as Gaur Gopal Das (spiritual guidance), Ashish Chanchlani (youth entertainment), Mohammed Salim Khan (social media influencer), Vedant Lamba (Entrepreneurship and Sneaker Culture), Deepanshu Singh (Governance) and many more.
