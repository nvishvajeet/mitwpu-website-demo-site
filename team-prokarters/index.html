Team Prokarters is the official Go-Kart racing team of MIT-WPU, known for its groundbreaking achievements and accolades. We take pride in our recognition as the developers of the first electric vehicle at MIT-WPU. Our team boasts a stellar track record of competing in and triumphing at national-level Go-Kart racing competitions.

In the most recent competition hosted by ISIE at the Buddh International Circuit, we clinched the coveted 1st place All India Rank. Additionally, at the GKDC event held at Kari Motor Speedway in Coimbatore, we achieved an impressive 7th place All India Rank. Our dedication and hard work have led us to become a prominent force in the world of Go-Kart racing.
