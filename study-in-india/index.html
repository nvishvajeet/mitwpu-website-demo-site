Study in India

MIT-WPU is the proud partner of The Study In India (SII) Programme - a flagship project by the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India.

Choosing to study abroad can be the most decisive moment in a student’s life, and MIT-WPU promises to make your academic journey in India the most rewarding one ever! Get ready for one of the most enriching campus experiences with world-class facilities in one of the most culturally diverse university campuses in India!

  • Learner Centric

MIT-WPU is a learner focussed campus with more than 18000+ students learning through multiple academic disciplines.

  • Industry-Ready Academic Model

The university ensures excellence through a flexible and interdisciplinary academic model matching the international standards keeping our courses contemporary, relevant, and cutting edge.

  • Global Learning

MIT-WPU is partnered with over 30 leading institutions worldwide to enable a holistic approach to global learning for its students. The university offers every opportunity to enrich and diversify academic degrees with an overseas study experience through exchange and higher studies programs.

  • Culturally Diverse Community

Home to students from 30 countries across the globe, MIT-WPU has a multi‐cultural approach in academic programs making the experience of campus life as diverse as the people who live here.

Director’s Message

Director's Message

Director - Admissions MIT-WPU

Greetings from MIT-WPU’s International Education Centre!

It is a pleasure for me to welcome you and inform you that your choice of seeking Higher Education in India and at MIT-WPU will prove to bring a positive transformation in your life.

A large number of students come from all parts of the world to pursue their higher studies here. They look at the Indian education system with trust and belief, as it is a diversified and innovative education destination for international students.

Our unique MIT-WPU methodology ensures that students derive academic knowledge through research, technology, interactive discussions with experts, participation in social & national Initiatives, rural immersions, and national study tours & international credits. Our university is well associated with leading organisations and research institutions offering scope for learning through a blend of theory and practice. At MIT-WPU, our curriculum emphasizes on sensitizing the youth about the human dimensions of not just professional pursuits but life itself through incorporation of Peace Studies in regular curriculums. We welcome you and assure you of a wonderful and life transforming experience at the campus of MIT-World Peace University, Pune-India.
