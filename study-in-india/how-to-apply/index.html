How to Apply

MIT-WPU follows UGC-specified eligibility norms while reviewing international students’ admission applications.

Study in India

International Admission Categories

Foreign Nationals Students

A student is eligible to apply as a Foreign National (FN) if he/she is a citizen of any country other than India and holds a foreign passport (FN - SAARC countries / FN – Non-SAARC Countries)

CIWGC/CIWSEA Students

Children of Indian Working in Gulf Countries & Southeast Asian countries: The candidates whose parents are working at Gulf countries or Southeast Asian countries only are eligible under this category

OCI Students

A student is eligible to apply as OCI if he/she is granted with Overseas Citizenship of India and has an OCI card

NRI Students

A student is eligible for admission under the NRI category if his/her parents (Father/ Mother or legal guardian) are holding an NRI status and working outside India in countries other than Gulf or Southeast Asia.

Application Process

Step

Apply online on admissions.mitwpu.edu.in and submit registration form

Receive Login ID and Password (Auto redirect to Student Dashboard)

Fill Application Form (Pay application form Fees - Rs.1800)

Receive provisional offer letter

Complete Fee Payment (Pay Processing Fee & 1st Instalment of Programme Fee)

Verification (Payment/Personal/Education/Documents)

Receive Final Offer Letter and Offer Guide with Student PRN Number

Original Document Submission

Please Note:

  • On submission of application IEC department will share email with programme details, fees, scholarship, list of documents and provisional admission process
  • To confirm the admission in all undergraduate programmes is submission of a certificate equivalent to Class 12th examination of the country and meeting the equivalence criteria of Association of Indian University (AIU).
  • To confirm the admission in all postgraduate programmes is the submission of degree awarded by a university in a particular country and meeting the equivalence criteria of Association of Indian Universities (AIU).
