Study in India

India is said to be particularly well placed among emerging nations to play an important role in the internationalization of higher education and is poised to emerge as a major hub for international students. While living and learning in India, international students get to experience one of the oldest and richest cultures in the world.

Students educated at Indian universities are leaving their mark on a global scale with their astute leadership skills. High quality instruction with increased affordability offers international students a high return on their investment in education.

  • Quality & Affordable Education
  • Diversity in Courses Offered
  • Holistic Learning
  • English a Primary Language
  • Multicultural Society
  • An Active Global Alumni Network

MIT-WPU is located primely in the picturesque and lush green city of Pune, Maharashtra – also known as ‘Oxford of the East’, and is surrounded by key civic infrastructures like the Pune International Airport, Shopping Malls, Hospitals, Food Joints, Restaurants, Pune – Mumbai Expressway, Historical Places etc.

The city of Pune boasts of a wide array of social engagement attractions including cultural festivals, music shows, theatre groups among several others and has arguably the best climate in the world with mellow summers and moderate temperatures. With green hills, forts, and beaches all within 4 hours of driving distance from the city centre, Pune brings together the best of vibrant traditions and cultures with a fast-paced modern lifestyle and make it one of the most preferred destinations for higher education.
