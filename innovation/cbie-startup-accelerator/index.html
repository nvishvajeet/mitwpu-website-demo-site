Overview

The Centre for Business Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CBIE) is a dynamic and progressive organisation dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in the business world. Primary purpose of CBIE is to provide a collaborative platform that empowers aspiring entrepreneurs and established businesses to explore, develop, and implement innovative ideas and solutions. By offering a range of comprehensive resources, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities, CBIE aims to inspire and support individuals in turning their innovative concepts into successful ventures by also helping them with Seed Grants and Connecting them to the right investors. Moreover, the centre serves as a knowledge hub, conducting research and disseminating valuable insights on emerging market trends, disruptive technologies, and best practices in entrepreneurship. Ultimately, the CBIE endeavours to create a vibrant ecosystem that encourages creativity, nurtures talent, and propels economic growth by fostering a thriving community of forward-thinking entrepreneurs and visionary business leaders.

MISSION

Transforming Businesses through the Power of People, Technology and Innovation

VISION

Enabling startups to improve their business strategy, organisation and to be successful and raise capital for expansion growth

PRIMARY GOAL

CBIE's primary goal is to blend theory and practice to create a hyper-active learning community of innovation practitioners, entrepreneurs, funders, policy-makers, and technologists. We intend to create a sustainable ecosystem of true value creation in innovation through research, outreach, and education activities.

Our Investment Approach

CBIE takes a proactive and impactful investment approach towards startups, with a strong focus on nurturing early-stage entrepreneurs and helping them transform their innovative ideas into tangible realities. One of the key pillars of CBIE investment strategy is the provision of seed grants, enabling aspiring entrepreneurs to take the crucial first steps towards building their ventures. These seed grants offer essential financial support, facilitating product development, market research, and initial operations. In addition to the financial assistance, CBIE acts as a facilitator, connecting these startups with a diverse network of investors and venture capitalists. By leveraging its extensive relationships within the investment community, CBIE creates valuable opportunities for startups to pitch their concepts and secure additional funding to scale their businesses. Through this comprehensive support system, CBIE strives to empower early-stage entrepreneurs with the resources, guidance, and capital they need to turn their dreams into successful and sustainable businesses, driving innovation and economic growth in the process.

Services Offered

  • Fund Raising - 3 to 4 rounds or Series A whichever is agreed upon. Putting them to the right investors to pitch.
  • Guidance on Pitch Deck, Financials, Go To Market Strategy, and any other concerns which need to be corrected.
  • Operational Review calls - Fortnightly/ Monthly Calls to solve operational challenges.
  • Be part of the Advisory Board/ Board of Directors.
  • Helping in identifying the right market, potential hires, and specific challenges which are critical for the success.
  • Reviewing documents or taking opinions on contracts or fundraising documents wherein no external payments or personnel are involved.
  • Referrals & Market connects on % of revenue/margin share (Mutually agreed as per case to case with corresponding equity stake as well)
  • An Initial fund of 20 lacs as a grant from MIT WPU Startup Incubation - section 8 company.
  • Other services - Debt Financing, Real estate assistance, Mystery audits, internal audits, Stat audits, Setting up of SOP/Controls, Facilitating Due Diligence/Valuations, Mentoring, Help in Search of Cofounders, Company incorporation.
  • 9% sweat equity from the startup upfront which will cover (Seedfund + Fund Raise + Go-to-Market Strategy)
  • Investment Banking fee of 5% for the fundraise
  • Retainership for any service like accounting, HR, marketing, Debt Financing, Real estate assistance, Mystery audits, internal audits, Stat audits, Setting up of SOP/Controls, Facilitating Due Diligence/Valuations, Mentoring, Help in Search of Cofounders, Company incorporation. Early Seed Ventures

At Earlyseed Ventures, we collaborate with business founders and private equity investors to build strategic partnerships. With founders, we build expertise through research and in-depth analysis of their business to become their well-wishing consultants. We go to the core of every business before presenting it to our Private Equity investor network, to find investors who truly connect with the story/purpose of the company. To understand our investors' mindsets, we spend one-on-one time with each of them to identify their goal, appetite and individual investment philosophy. We then bring to the table investment opportunities that we know will fully benefit everyone in the process.
