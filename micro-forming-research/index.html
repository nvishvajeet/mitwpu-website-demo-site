Micro forming Research

Under the guidance of Dr. Kakandikar Ganesh, the Micro forming Research COE was established. The Center of Excellence is generously gifted with equipment from Aeronautics Research and Development Board, DRDO, New Delhi. The centre houses formality testing tools and equipment to facilitate micro structural study and mechanical testing analysis for the researchers. It also provides adequate heat treatment facilities for enhancement in micro structures.

Under the guidance of Dr. Kakandikar Ganesh, the Micro forming Research COE was established. The Center of Excellence is generously gifted with equipment from Aeronautics Research and Development Board, DRDO, New Delhi. The centre houses formality testing tools and equipment to facilitate micro structural study and mechanical testing analysis for the researchers. It also provides adequate heat treatment facilities for enhancement in micro structures.

Established in the year 2021, this centre of excellence has swiftly garnered prominence as an intellectual crucible for erudite researchers, offering an environment of heightened discernment to propel pioneering inquiries across a spectrum of academic frontiers.
