MIT-WPU / International Immersion

International Immersion

Expand your horizons with MIT-WPU's International Immersion Programme. Gain global exposure, experience international best practices.

  • International Immersion

The International Immersion Programme is a unique initiative by MIT-WPU conducted with the aim to expose students to the best of international institutions, organizations and practices in their field of study.

Students get to visit the research organizations, universities, industries and governmental agencies of repute and gain a global perspective as well as advance their knowledge.

They are made aware of the latest international developments in their field and the possible opportunities in research and industry. They get to interact with foreign faculty and experts and work on group projects, case studies and projects to implement the knowledge acquired.

They are exposed to the benchmarked academic and industry practices and get global networking opportunities which gives them an edge in the highly competitive job market, and helps them climb the corporate ladder.

The International Immersion programmes have been conducted at Nanyang Technical University & National University - Singapore, University of Hull & University of Leicester, UK , NTU , NUS, Singapore, University of South Florida, Tampa, Florida, USA, University of Tennessee, USA, Maharishi University of Management, USA, La Trobe University, Victoria, Melbourne, Australia, University of Cardiff, Wales, UK, University of Dalarna ,Sweden, Telecom Ecole de Management, Paris, France, VU University, Amsterdam, Netherlands among other institutes till date.
