SC / ST Cell and Equal Opportunity Cell

As per the guidelines received from University Grants Commission (Prevention of caste-based discrimination in higher education institutions). MIT-WPU has constituted a dedicated SC/ST Committee to address complaints related to caste-based discrimination. The committee is responsible for investigating incidents, taking appropriate actions and ensuring that justice is served. We guarantee confidentiality and impartiality throughout the complaint resolution process.

At MIT World Peace University, we are committed to fostering an inclusive and respectful environment for all students and staff. We strictly prohibit any form of caste-based discrimination and strive to ensure that all students and staff, regardless of their caste background, feel safe and supported on our campus.
