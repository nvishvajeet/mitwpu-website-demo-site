IQAC

MIT-WPU IQAC: Fostering academic excellence through quality initiatives, ensuring student success, and promoting a culture of continuous improvement.

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) has been established in alignment with the directives of the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Bangalore. As a vital component of our university, IQAC serves as a dedicated body committed to the continuous enhancement and sustenance of academic quality. Having evolved from its proposal stage, IQAC has become an integral part of our institution's fabric, working diligently to realize our goals of academic excellence and improvement. Its primary role is to develop a comprehensive framework for deliberate and consistent enhancements across all university aspects, drawing inspiration from peer committee recommendations. IQAC operates as a dynamic, participative system, striving to internalize and institutionalize quality enhancement initiatives, much like the "Quality Circles" in industries.

  • Vision

Ensuring excellence in all aspects of the university’s functioning through Continuous Quality Enhancement

  • Quality Policy

We are committed to fostering a culture of continuous improvement, innovation and excellence, aiming to uphold the highest standards of education, research, governance and societal engagement across all areas of the University & functioning.

  • Objectives
  • To establish Quality Standards by defining clear quality benchmarks for academic programs and support services
  • To implement robust mechanisms for monitoring and evaluating quality indicators
  • To develop transparent data collection and reporting systems
  • To foster a culture of continuous improvement based on data-driven insights and best practices
  • To implement Quality Initiatives for faculty & staff development, enhancing teaching learning process and improving quality indicators
  • To implement academic and administrative audits regularly and monitor its compliance
  • To develop effective feedback channels for all stakeholders to provide input and suggestions
  • To facilitate and ensure regular statutory compliances for accreditation and university rankings
  • Mission
  • To foster a dynamic environment that promotes innovation, collaboration and continuous improvement in all academic and administrative aspects of education in alignment with alignment with accreditation standards and ranking benchmarks.
  • To deliver quality education that empowers individuals and positions the institution on national and global platforms
  • To promote a culture of excellence, integrity and inclusivity, that drives societal impact and institutional recognition.
  • IQAC Roles & Responsibilities
  • Developing Quality Policies: Formulating and revising quality policies in alignment with national and international accreditation standards and the university's vision.
  • Quality Assurance Processes: Designing and implementing quality assurance processes for various aspects of academic and administrative functions.
  • Monitoring and Evaluation: Establishing mechanisms for monitoring and evaluating the quality of teaching, research, infrastructure and support services.
  • Data Collection and Analysis: Collecting data through surveys, feedback mechanisms and performance indicators and analyzing this data to identify areas for improvement.
  • Internal Audits: Conducting internal audits to assess compliance with quality standards and identifying corrective actions.
  • Documentation: Ensuring proper documentation of quality-related activities, including policies, procedures, reports and outcomes.
  • Training and Capacity Building: Organizing workshops, training programs and seminars to enhance the capacity of faculty, staff, and students in quality assurance practices.
  • External Liaison: Collaborating with external agencies, accrediting bodies and stakeholders to benchmark and enhance the quality of education and research.
  • Quality Enhancement: Developing strategies for quality enhancement based on feedback, data analysis and best practices in higher education.
  • Reporting: Preparing and presenting regular reports on quality assurance activities, outcomes and recommendations to university authorities and stakeholders.
  • Continuous Improvement: Facilitating a culture of continuous improvement by fostering innovation, best practices and benchmarking against peer institutions.
  • Compliance: Ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and accreditation standards related to quality assurance in education.

By effectively managing these responsibilities, IQAC will contribute significantly to enhancing the overall quality and reputation of the university's academic programs and services.

  • Short-Term Goals
  • Adoption of ISO 21001:2018 standards for Educational Organizations Management System (EOMS) : To establish global quality standards in academic and administrative processes, improving stakeholder confidence and operational efficiency.
  • Achieve NIRF Top 100 Ranking : Targeting a position within the top 100 of the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) to enhance national visibility and competitiveness, attracting quality faculty, students, and funding opportunities.
  • NAAC Accreditation / Reaccreditation : To apply for accreditation with a focus on improving institutional credibility and academic benchmarking.
  • QS I-GAUGE Rating : Obtaining QS I-GAUGE certification, which evaluates Indian institutions on global parameters, promoting international benchmarking, branding and quality assurance recognition.
  • Green Campus Certification : Certification demonstrating commitment to environmental sustainability through eco-friendly policies, infrastructure and practices.
  • UI Green Metric World University Rankings : It is about evaluating and promoting sustainability practices in higher education institutions. It is a global ranking system initiated by the University of Indonesia to measure efforts toward environmental sustainability and green campus initiatives.
  • Feedback Mechanism Enhancement : Digitization, analysis and closing of the feedback loop across all stakeholders driving continuous improvement, enhancing teaching-learning quality and meeting accreditation criteria.
  • Long-Term Goals
  • QS International Rankings : Aspiring to be ranked in the QS World University Rankings or QS Asia Rankings. to positions the university globally, strengthen international collaborations and attract global talent.
  • Collaborate with NGOs/Government for Socially Impactful Projects : Partnering with public bodies and NGOs on education, health, sustainability and inclusion projects to build community engagement, fulfil institutional social responsibility (ISR) and improve societal impact metrics.
  • Quality Circle : Formation of intra-institutional quality circles involving faculty, students and staff for problem-solving and innovation, to foster participatory governance, quality improvements at the grassroot level and institutional ownership of change initiatives.
  • To be recognised as a Center for Quality Excellence at the National level : To establish the institution as a benchmark for quality in higher education by embedding best practices, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and achieving recognition through accreditations, rankings and national quality frameworks.
