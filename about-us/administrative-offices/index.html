Registrar's Office

The office of the Registrar plays a pivotal role in maintaining the academic integrity and administrative efficiency of the university, furthering the educational mission of MIT-WPU and contributing to its overall success. The Registrar office oversees academic and administrative appointments, drives policy implementation and serves as a vital link between faculty, students, and administrative units of the university.

Human Resources Office

Welcome to the university's Office of Human Resource and Establishment, your trusted partner in all matters related to our dedicated faculty and staff. Our department plays a pivotal role in recruitment, benefits administration, professional development, and employee relations. From job opportunities to payroll inquiries, we're here to support and empower our talented workforce. With a commitment to fostering a positive workplace culture, we ensure compliance with policies and promote diversity and inclusion.

Dr. Prakash Mainkar Director - Admissions director.admissions@mitwpu.edu.in

Admissions

MIT-WPU Admissions Department coordinates and manages the entire admission process for the various academic programmes offered by the university. They manage entrance examinations, stakeholder outreach, admission counselling, and document verifications. The Counseling Division aids students in addressing challenges related to admissions.

Dr. Veera Talukdar Controller of Examinations veera.talukdar@mitwpu.edu.in

Examination Department

The Examination Department plays a crucial role in upholding the sanctity, fairness, and integrity of academic evaluation. With a dedicated and coordinated team, we ensure the smooth conduct of examinations, timely declaration of results, and strict adherence to confidentiality and accuracy—reinforcing trust in every process.

Mr. Haranath Ghosh Director - Branding & Communications director.marketing@mitwpu.edu.in

Branding & Communications

At MIT-WPU, the Branding & Communications Department drives the university’s identity and vision through strategic branding, creative content, and effective communication across all platforms. We work closely with academic and administrative units to produce high-quality marketing materials, manage public relations, and ensure consistent messaging. Focused on innovation, collaboration, and inclusivity, our team promotes MIT-WPU’s commitment to academic excellence, peace, and holistic education, helping engage diverse stakeholders and enhance the university’s reputation locally and globally.

Mr. Pradnesh Desai Director - International pradnesh.desai@mitwpu.edu.in

International Collaboration Department (ICD)

The International Collaboration Department (ICD), dedicated to fostering global institutional partnerships at MIT-WPU, facilitates collaborations by building a strong network of leading educational institutions worldwide. The primary objective of ICD is to create diverse opportunities for students and faculty to gain global exposure and cross-cultural understanding through student and faculty exchange programmes, international internships, and twinning and exchange programmes. ICD also strives to facilitate initiatives that enrich the academic and overall experience of international students at MIT World Peace University.

Mr. Manas Mohanty Director - CIAP (Head – Placements) manas.mohanty@mitwpu.edu.in

CIAP - Centre for Industry and Academia Partnerships

MIT-WPU Centre for Industry-Academia Partnerships (CIAP) fosters strong ties between the university and industry, enriching learning through a blend of academic, research, and industrial perspectives. CIAP leverages its industry network and on-campus centres of excellence to provide students with real-world industry exposure through projects and lectures. Its core functions include enhancing industry partnerships, career services, internships, and alumni engagement, empowering students with skills, training, and opportunities for top-tier job placements.

Mr. Nitinkumar Chandak Deputy CAFO - Finance & Accounts nitinkumar.chandak@mitwpu.edu.in

Finance & Accounting Office

Welcome to our university's Office of Finance and Accounts, where academic excellence meets meticulous financial management. As the heartbeat of fiscal operations, we are dedicated to ensuring the institution's financial health and integrity. Our expert team adeptly handles budgeting, fund allocation, and transparent reporting, fostering a secure environment for financial transactions. Collaborating across departments, we streamline resources to support educational innovation and growth.

Yogesh Duse Civil Engineer yogesh.duse@mitwpu.edu.in

Office of Infra & Facilities

The Infrastructure Office at MIT-WPU plays a pivotal role in shaping the university's physical environment. Tasked with designing, constructing, and maintaining state-of-the-art facilities, it ensures a conducive learning and research environment. From cutting-edge classrooms to advanced laboratories, it spearheads the development of academic spaces, and handles infrastructure planning, expansion, and sustainability initiatives. Their commitment to quality ensures a comfortable, secure, and inspiring atmosphere for students and staff.

Dr. Hitesh Joshi Dean - Student Affairs hitesh.joshi@mitwpu.edu.in

Office of Student Affairs

The Office of Student Affairs is the core of MIT-WPU’s vibrant and diverse campus life. The team of student affairs directly manages the student council, its election, all academic, cultural, and professional student clubs. The team ensures that all students on campus can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Dr. Padmanabh Deshmukh Joint Director-Communication (PR & Media) padmanabh.deshmukh@mitwpu.edu.in

Public Relations Office

The Public Relations Office at MIT-WPU is committed towards effective communication and engagement with the external stakeholders. Our team diligently analyses public opinion to measure the public image and acceptability of the University and it’s activities while identifying the public concerns and expectations.

The Public Relations Office adeptly handles media relations, crisis management, event promotion and building a strong brand image. The Office serves as a platform to amplify the accomplishments of the university while maintaining a consistent and impactful image.
