The International Collaboration Department (ICD), dedicated to fostering global institutional partnerships at MIT-WPU, facilitates collaborations by building a strong network of leading educational institutions worldwide. MIT-WPU Centre for Industry-Academia Partnerships (CIAP) fosters strong ties between the university and industry, enriching learning through a blend of academic, research, and industrial perspectives.
