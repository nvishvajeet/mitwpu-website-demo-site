Leadership and Governance

The Academic Council at MIT World Peace University holds the paramount authority in all academic matters and is entrusted with the vital responsibility of overseeing, regulating, and upholding the highest standards in teaching, research, and evaluation throughout the university.

The Academic Council at MIT World Peace University holds the paramount authority in all academic matters and is entrusted with the vital responsibility of overseeing, regulating, and upholding the highest standards in teaching, research, and evaluation throughout the university. The council is also vested with the pivotal duty of formulating and guiding academic policies aimed at enhancing and sustaining the quality of teaching, research, extension activities, collaborative programmes, and the equitable assessment of faculty workloads.
