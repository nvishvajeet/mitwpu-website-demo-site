Leadership and Governance

The Board of Studies (BoS) at MIT-WPU is a committee dedicated to the overall development of academic courses and programmes. This board consists of eminent scholars and industry experts who are dedicated to incorporating and maintaining high educational standards for the overall development of our students.

The Board of Studies (BoS) at MIT-WPU is a committee dedicated to the overall development of academic courses and programmes. This board consists of eminent scholars and industry experts who are dedicated to incorporating and maintaining high educational standards for the overall development of our students. With a focus on interdisciplinary collaboration, the Board plays a pivotal role in shaping the academic landscape, fostering research, and driving student success. Following is the list of these board members who are the pillars of strength and innovation behind the successful governance and effective implementation of quality education at MIT-WPU, that meets the evolving needs of society and industry.
