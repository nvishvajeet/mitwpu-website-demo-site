World Peace Dome

The peace monument at Vishwarajbaug, Loni Kalbhor, Pune.

The World Peace Dome stands at Vishwarajbaug in Loni Kalbhor, Pune. Conceived by Prof. Dr. Vishwanath D. Karad as a space dedicated to the union of science and spirituality, it forms part of MAEER's institutional legacy and its public work for peace.

Beneath the dome lies a large prayer and assembly hall used for convocations, conferences, and public gatherings. The monument also houses a library and gilded statues of philosophers, scientists, and saints drawn from many traditions — the “Idols of Peace” — which together express the university's founding idea of peace through knowledge.
