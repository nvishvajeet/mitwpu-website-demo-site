Visionary Leader

Dr. Aditi Karad serves as the Executive Director of MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), where she plays a pivotal role in shaping the university’s academic and institutional vision. Under her leadership, MIT-WPU has strengthened its reputation as a leading institution that blends academic excellence with human values, peace education, and nation-building. Dr. Karad has been instrumental in initiating outcome-driven reforms that focus on industry-aligned curricula, experiential learning, and leadership development—preparing students to become global professionals and responsible changemakers.

Her educational vision extends beyond higher education through her stewardship of World Peace Schools in Alandi, Latur, and Indore, where she is transforming school education by integrating value-based learning, modern pedagogy, and character building, thereby nurturing future-ready leaders from an early age.

In addition to her role in education, Dr. Aditi Karad, an MBBS graduate with a Master’s in Hospital Management from the University of Leeds, England, UK, is a respected leader in the healthcare and service sectors. She serves as the Executive Director of VishwaRaj Hospital, known for its quality and affordable care, and founded the nationally acclaimed Vishwaraj Institute of Nursing.

Champion for Social Change & Women Empowerment

A staunch advocate for women’s empowerment, Dr. Karad has been at the forefront of initiatives that position women from diverse backgrounds into leadership roles. Her efforts go beyond professional development—she actively supports community-driven programmes that promote education, economic independence, and social well-being.

Her leadership is deeply rooted in peacebuilding and social impact, ensuring that every initiative she spearheads aligns with the values of integrity, service, and empowerment. By fostering a culture of inclusion and progress, she continues to inspire individuals and communities to work towards meaningful change for the nation.

Dr. Aditi Karad is more than a leader—she is a force of transformation, shaping the future of healthcare, education, and social reform with passion, purpose, and an unyielding commitment to excellence.
