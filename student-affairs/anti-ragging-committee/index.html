Anti-Ragging Committee at MIT-WPU

MIT-WPU Anti-Ragging Committee is dedicated to preserving a safe and respectful environment on the campus, the Anti-Ragging Committee at MIT-WPU is committed to preventing any form of bullying, intimidation, coercion, or harassment. The committee enforces a zero-tolerance policy against these malpractices, and conducts various awareness programmes and seminars. The committee takes quick and strict actions towards the reported incidents, ensuring the well-being and safety of all the students.
