Internal Complaints Committee

Dedicated to ensuring adherence to various policies, guidelines, and legal requirements, the Internal Complaints committee oversees the university's internal processes and procedures. This committee continuously reviews and assesses the compliance framework, providing guidance and recommendations to align practices with regulatory standards and best practices, thereby maintaining integrity and accountability across the organisation.
