Overview

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), with a legacy of over 40 years in higher education, is dedicated to driving innovation and excellence through strategic partnerships. Our collaborations with leading corporate partners have fostered impactful research and development (R&D) initiatives, aligned with global sustainable development goals (SDGs).

With over four decades of experience, MIT-WPU has established numerous Centers of Excellence (COEs) and innovation hubs in diverse fields. These partnerships, supported through Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funding and corporate philanthropy, enable the university to contribute to critical R&D areas essential for India's strategic growth.

Join us in advancing cutting-edge research, shaping the future of technology, and addressing global challenges through our rich tradition of academic and research excellence.

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) invites corporate partners to join its mission of developing cutting-edge technologies and innovations that contribute to national self-reliance and societal well-being.

MIT-WPU is seeking collaboration in key research areas, including:

  • Strategic national priorities such as defense technologies, semiconductors, quantum computing, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity.
  • Fostering economic growth by supporting critical industries like green energy, electric vehicles, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and more.
  • Making a lasting impact with technologies that address essential sectors such as healthcare, sanitation, clean water, and education.

Sponsored Research & Development Projects

At MIT WPU, we believe that every student who is talented and deserving, irrespective of their background, must be given an opportunity to pursue higher education and to utilise their full potential. Therefore through CSR partnerships, we collaborate with companies to provide scholarships and fellowships to such potential students. These financial aids help them pursue academic excellence without any financial barriers. By supporting and empowering young minds through rewarding scholarships, to pursue degrees in engineering, management, sciences, and other fields of study, companies create a ripple effect that will benefit the entire nation for years to come. Your contribution to such fellowships not only supports outstanding students to engage in advanced research and innovation, but motivates them to grow as the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and innovators.

Faculty Recognition & Chair Professorships

The faculties at MIT-WPU serve as the backbone of this institution and are committed to providing quality education and groundbreaking research. By supporting Faculty Recognition and Chair Professorships, your company can help them perform at their maximum potential and catering to academic excellence including research, teaching, and mentorship. This support also helps attract, support and retain world-class faculty that engages in knowledge sharing and brings experience and leadership to the university. Contributions made under the chair professorships ensure long-term benefits to these faculties and help MIT-WPU continue to provide a world-class learning experience for students along with an environment that promotes pioneering research and academic innovation. With this initiative, not only your business will support the growth of faculty members but also upgrade the entire educational ecosystem, leaving a positive impact on future generations.

Establishing Centres of Excellence

Your company can contribute to the development of a Centre of Excellence (CoE) at MIT WPU and contribute to the advancement of specialised knowledge and industry-driven research. These centres will serve as a dedicated hub for excellence, focussed on emerging areas like Sustainability, Renewable Energy, Robotics, Drug Discovery, Petroleum Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and more. These centres will also help bring together world-class faculty, top-tier students, and industry leaders to work on innovative projects, addressing global challenges and advancing technological frontiers. Your support in establishing a CoE at MIT-WPU will directly contribute to pursuing high-quality research, cultivating industry-ready talent, and creating long-lasting solutions to critical problems faced in India and across the globe.

Startup Seed Funding

The Technology Business Incubator (TBI) at MIT-WPU serves as a launchpad for entrepreneurial ventures, driving innovation and supporting early-stage startups. This TBI unit was set up in 2016 and endorsed by the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India. MIT-WPU TBI creates an ecosystem where startups, especially those founded by students, can work and thrive. seed funding, your company can directly influence the growth of these upcoming businesses, helping them to develop innovative ideas into scalable solutions.

This Technology Business Incubation Centre not only supports these startups financially but also offers mentorship, networking, and access to a state-of-the-art 11,000-square-foot facility on the MIT-WPU campus in Pune. The unit includes co-working spaces and a rapid prototyping lab, designed to promote creativity and product development. With a focus on technology-led startups, the TBI supports collaborative student and alumni ventures through early-stage assistance and a one-year rent waiver for MIT WPU startups.

Your contribution to seed funding will help provide essential resources to these young and budding entrepreneurs, accelerating their journey from a simple concept to effective market-ready solutions. This partnership also encourages companies to engage with cutting-edge technologies and collaborate with the next generation of business leaders, creating a mutually beneficial ecosystem that drives innovation.

Case Studies

SUBSEA Lab

The Department of Petroleum Engineering has been instrumental in developing a state-of-the-art subsea engineering laboratory at MITWPU Pune. Under the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiative by Aker Solutions, the real-life laboratory aims at training students, industry professionals and carrying out research and consultancy projects

Advanced Manufacturing & Engineering Centre

Advanced Manufacturing & Engineering Centre, is a collaborative effort with Tata Technologies, highlights the institution's commitment to development of cutting-edge technology, holistic, hands-on education and industry integration. The Memorandum of Understanding solidifies this collaboration, showcasing the dedication to fostering innovation.

Rivvity Bioimaging Laboratory

Revvity – the Centre of Excellence for Bioimaging Technologies and Innovation at MIT-WPU is an outcome of an innovative blend between the industry and academia to facilitate research, development, and innovation in the field of bioimaging and bio-photonics. The CoE involves state-of-the-art facilities including equipment such as confocal microscopes
