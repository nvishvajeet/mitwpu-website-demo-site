Bio

Dr. Shridevi Karande has been working as an assistant professor at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University since 2005. With over 21 years of experience, she has managed various academic responsibilities and placement-related tasks. She holds a PhD in the AI and ML healthcare domain, and her research project, titled "Detection of Pre-Dementia," received funding from IBM. She has authored more than 20 research papers and articles in international journals and conferences. Her research interests include Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, and Healthcare Analytics.

Specialisation: Cloud Computing

Area of Expertise: AI/ML, Cloud , Healthcare AI, Big data analytics
