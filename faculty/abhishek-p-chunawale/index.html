Bio

Dr. Abhishek Prakash Chunawale is an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University in Pune, India. With 9.5 years of teaching experience, he received his B.E. in IT from Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University in 2011, where he was a Gold Medalist, and his M.E. in IT from Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2014. He has completed his PhD in Computer Engineering, and his research area is Affective Computing. He has research paper publications in prestigious international journals and conferences such as IEEE and Springer. His areas of interest include IoT, Information and Cyber Security, Social Media Privacy and Security, and AR/VR.
