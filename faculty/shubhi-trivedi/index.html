Bio

Ms. Shubhi Trivedi is currently pursuing her Ph.D. from Gujarat National Law University, Gandhinagar. She completed her LL.M. in IPR laws from National Law University Jodhpur.She holds the Proud Achiever Award for the Batch of B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) at Law College Dehradun, Uttaranchal University.

Her area of interests includes intellectual property rights law, general principles of contract, constitutional law, law of torts, family law to name a few. Prof. Trivedi has published papers in various areas of law pertaining to protection of sports moves through intellectual property rights, patents and ethics, artificial intelligence copyright and patents and the arbitration and consolidation and others.

Apart from academics she also has interest in debating, writing, mooting and dramatics. She has presented several papers in conferences of repute.
