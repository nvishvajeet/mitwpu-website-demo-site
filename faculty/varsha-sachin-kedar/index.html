Bio

Prof. Varsha Sachin Kedar is an academician and researcher with over 12 years of experience in management education. She specializes in Financial Markets, Investments, Behavioural Finance, and Managerial Economics, with a keen interest in understanding investor behaviour and decision-making. Her doctoral research focuses on the role of behavioural biases in equity market investment decisions. She has published research papers in reputed journals and presented award-winning papers at national and international conferences. In addition to teaching and research, she has actively contributed to academic administration, curriculum development, student mentoring, and financial literacy initiatives, bridging contemporary industry practices with classroom learning.

Educational Qualifications : MBA (Finance), M Com, SET (Management), PhD (Submitted)

Areas of Expertise : Financial Markets and Investments, Portfolio Management, Behavioral Finance, and Applied Economics.

Teaching Experience : 11 Years

Publications and Research Work

Published research papers in reputed journals on Behavioral Finance, Stock Market Investments, Digital Payments, Financial Literacy, FinTech, and Bounded Rationality (2019–2024).

Authored notable papers including “Beyond Rationality: Assessing Behavioral Biases’ Influence on Stock Market Investors” and “Futuristic FinTech: Sustainable Banking Model.”

Presented research papers at national and international conferences organized by institutions such as PIBM Pune, NMIMS Indore, Sri Balaji University Pune, SSMRV College Bangalore, and Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University.

Developed and obtained copyright for the Bounded Rationality Calculator (2025).

Awards and Recognitions

Best Paper Award at the International Conference organized by SSMRV College, Bangalore (2026) for research on cognitive and behavioral limitations in retail equity investment.

Best Paper Award at MIMANSA 2024, Sri Balaji University, Pune, for the paper “Beyond Rationality: Assessing Behavioral Biases’ Influence on Stock Market Investors.”

Best Paper Award at NMIMS, Indore (2023) for research on bounded rationality among retail equity investors.

Second Prize (University Level – PPG) at Aavishkar 2023, Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, for the research project on bounded rationality in retail investor equity portfolios.
