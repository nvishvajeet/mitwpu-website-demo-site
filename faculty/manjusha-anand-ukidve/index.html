Bio

An architect by profession and a teacher by passion, I believe that training young minds to stay rooted in the context, while imparting technical skills to keep up with the world, is the need of the hour. As rightly said by Sir Winston Churchill “We shape our buildings; thereafter they shape us.” I believe shaping young minds who will shape the world of the future is at the same time a challenging yet rewarding task - a responsibility that I accept with commitment and gratitude. I believe that education should facilitate not only transfer of knowledge but also pass on the right values to budding professionals.
