Bio

Ar. Shefali Arora is a landscape architect, academician and researcher with over 19 years of experience in teaching, professional practice and academic leadership. She holds a Masters degree in Landscape Architecture and is pursuing a PhD. focusing on urban parks, children’s well-being and sustainable urban design. At MIT-WPU, she aims to foster research-led learning, interdisciplinary collaborations and industry engagement while mentoring students to develop innovative, ethical and sustainable design solutions to create resilient, people-centric environments.

Educational Qualifications : B.Arch (2007)- Sinhagad College of Architecture, Pune M.LA- Master’s in Landscape Architecture- (2009)- School of Planning and Architecture, New Delhi Pursuing PhD- UID, Gandhinagar

Areas of Expertise : Urban Ecology, Evidence- Based Design solutions, Sustainable Landscape planning.

Teaching Experience : 19 Years of teaching experience in architecture and design education. Taught undergraduate and post-graduate students. Mentored thesis and dissertation projects at various institutions across Pune, Mumbai. Associated with B.N.College of architecture, D.Y.Patil College of Architecture Ambi, Talegaon, Minerva College of Architecture, Symbiosis institute of Design, Symbiosis Skills and Professional university, Pune Design School, Marathwada Mitramadal College of Architecture, MIT WPU School of Design.

Industry / Research Experience : 19 years industry experience in Landscape architecture along with teaching. Currently co- founder of Architecture firm Design Attitude.
