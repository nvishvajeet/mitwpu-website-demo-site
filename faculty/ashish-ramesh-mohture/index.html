Bio

Dr. Ashish Ramesh Mohture's teaching philosophy is rooted in learner-centric, outcome-based education that integrates theory with real-world application. He is motivated by a strong commitment to academic excellence, research-led teaching, and holistic student development. With over 16 years of academic experience and active engagement in research, FDPs, and industry consultancy, Dr. Mohture strives to nurture ethical, competent, and future-ready professionals. His contribution to MIT-WPU's vision lies in strengthening research culture, fostering innovation, integrating technology in pedagogy, and aligning teaching, research, and leadership with the University's ethos of peace, values, and societal impact.
