Bio

Dr. Ritu Goel is an accomplished economist who recently completed her PhD in Industrial Economics from Panjab University, Chandigarh. With a solid grasp of research software such as SPSS, EVIEWS and STATA, Dr. Goel is an expert in regression analysis and hypothesis testing. She has attended numerous workshops and training programs, and has presented several research papers at national conferences. Her work has been published in highly respected journals, attesting to her strong research skills and analytical abilities.
