Bio

Dr. Vikrant Yadav earned his Ph.D. in 'International Commercial Arbitration' from the University of Pune. He has more than ten years of teaching experience in legal domain. He has worked with prestigious educational institutions such as Modern Law College- Pune, Ajeenkya DY Patil University- Pune, Government Law College- Mumbai and NICMAR- Hyderabad. In pursuit of academic excellence, he has written and published several research papers, three books, and one book chapter. He is currently a member of YIAG, the London Court of International Arbitration, Young ICCA (International Council for Commercial Arbitration), and Young MCIA (Mumbai Center for International Arbitration). He has imparted several training sessions to various government and industry officers in area of law.
