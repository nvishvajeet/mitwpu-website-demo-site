Bio

I have extensive academic experience with around 20-23 years of teaching experience in computer engineering fields like data mining, data warehousing, machine learning, and data analytics. I have published more than 20 research publications in international journals and conference proceedings related to data analytics and mining .

I am also involved in department activities such as academic coordination, student technical events, and ISO quality processes. My vision is to contribute effectively to the academic environment through continuous professional growth, responsible leadership, and collaborative teamwork that brings out the best in everyone.
