Bio

Dr. Kishen R. Patil is an Assistant Professor with over eight years of experience in teaching, academic administration, and industry engagement. He specializes in Marketing and General Management subjects and has served in key academic leadership roles such as Batch In-Charge, Academic Coordinator, and Department Coordinator. His research interests include Green Marketing, Sustainable Business Practices, Consumer Behaviour, and Tourism Development. With a strong blend of academic expertise and industry exposure, he is committed to student mentoring, institutional growth, and research-driven teaching excellence.
