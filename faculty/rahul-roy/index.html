Bio

Dr. Rahul Roy is an Assistant Professor of Finance, Area Chair – Finance, and Ph.D. Coordinator (Business Administration) at the Ramcharan School of Leadership, MIT World Peace University, Pune. He holds a Ph.D. in Finance from Pondicherry University and is currently pursuing a second Ph.D. in Economics.

A recipient of prestigious fellowships and international research grants, his research and teaching expertise span Asset Pricing, Capital Markets, FinTech, and Machine Learning applications in finance. Before academia, he worked as a Senior Manager at Solytics Partners, leading advanced analytics and model risk management initiatives. Dr. Roy has published in reputed international journals and serves as a reviewer for several top tier, internationally recognized academic publications.
