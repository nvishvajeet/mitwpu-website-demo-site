Bio

Dr. Ashwani Pant joined MIT World Peace University, Pune as Assistant Professor of Law Senior Grade has demonstrated excellence in the efficiency, productivity, and quality of his work in the capacity of Teacher, Programme Coordinator and other administrative responsibilities with his 13 years of experience at Amity Law School, Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Noida . He has a keen knowledge of Criminal and Procedural Laws, Environmental Law and Constitutional Law. He hails from a hill station named Almora, Uttarakhand and has done his schooling form there. He did his LL.B.in 2004 from Kumaun University, Nainital and LL.M. in 2010 form Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi. He has completed his Ph.D. in Law on multidisciplinary topic of Criminal & Environmental Law from Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Noida. His core research areas of interest are Criminal Law, Environment and Constitutional Law. He has presented papers in various national and international seminar and conferences. He has published papers in reputed journals and contributed chapters to edited books including SCOPUS Indexed journal as well. He has also authored on book on Clinical Legal Education. He has been associated with various Environmental awareness, Legal-Aid programmes and key role in the establishment of Environmental Law Clinics at institutional level under the aegis of Legal Aid and Awareness Society. He has conferred with “ABHINANDAN- Distinguished Teacher’s Award 2018” by ROTARY CLUB, New Delhi and Best Teachers Award-2019 by Lions Club- Noida on the occasion of Teacher Day Celebration.
