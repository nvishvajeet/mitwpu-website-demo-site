Dean & Professor

Bio

Prof. Dr. Urvashi Makkar has over 28 years of experience and held prominent academic and administrative positions, including Director & Professor - Marketing at IMS Ghaziabad and Director General at GL Bajaj Institute of Management & Research.

Her contributions span across academic quality enhancement, institutional accreditation (NBA, NAAC), global collaborations, research mentoring, and fostering industry-academia partnerships.

Dr. Makkar holds a Ph.D. in Management, an Executive Programme in HRM from IIM Calcutta, an MBA in Marketing, PGDCA, and a B.Sc. in Physics. She has driven institutional growth through strategic academic initiatives, impactful placement efforts, and international academic alliances, including delivering a Global Executive MBA module at UCS, Brazil. Her commitment to academic leadership is further evidenced by her participation in international faculty development programs, including the UK-India Education and Research Initiative (UKIERI) in collaboration with AICTE, the AICTE-UKIERI Master Trainer Development Program, and the Higher Education Leadership Program at Harvard Graduate School of Education, USA.

Her scholarly contributions are equally noteworthy:

  • Authored/edited 13 books, including titles published by TATA McGraw Hill and Bharti Publications
  • Published over 85 research papers in national and international journals and conference proceedings
  • Developed 12 published case studies and contributed to 50+ book chapters
  • Supervised 11 Ph.D. theses and evaluated 14 doctoral dissertations
  • Mentored 1000+ MBA/master’s dissertations
  • Presented at leading global institutions including Harvard University, Zhejiang University (China), and Universiti Malaysia Sarawak

Recognitions & Contributions:

  • Recipient of prestigious awards including “North India’s Woman Leader”, “Best Director Award”, “Leadership in Institution Building”, and “Lifetime Achievement Award”
  • Honoured by renowned organizations such as AIMA, Competition Success Review, ASSOCHAM, and Dialogue India
  • Completed the Higher Education Leadership Program at Harvard Graduate School of Education
  • Serves on the editorial boards of multiple international journals
  • Former Council Member of AIMA; associated with DMA, CEGR, and other leading forums
  • Conducted over 65 MDPs and workshops for senior executives from BSNL, India Post, and other public sector organizations
