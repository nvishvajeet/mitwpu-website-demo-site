Bio

Dr. Gaurav Saxena is an Associate Professor with over 17 years of teaching experience across PGDM/MBA programs. He holds a Ph.D. in Management, an FDPM from IIM Ahmedabad, and a PG Diploma in Entrepreneur and Business Management. His core teaching and research interests include Marketing Management, Strategic Management, Entrepreneurship, and Supply Chain Management. He has led student-development initiatives and industry- interface roles, and has published work on supply chain effectiveness, value-chain competitiveness, and e-shopping behaviour. At MIT-WPU, he focuses on rigorous, practice- oriented learning.

Educational Qualifications:

1. Ph.D. (Management) - Mangalayatan University, Aligarh (2016)

2. FDPM (Management) - Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (2012)

3. PG Diploma in Entrepreneur and Business Management (Marketing major; Finance minor) EMPI Business School, New Delhi (2008)

4. BCA - Rajiv Academy, Mathura (Dr. B.R. Ambedkar University) (2003)

Areas of Expertise:

Marketing Management, Strategic Management, Entrepreneurship, Supply Chain and Logistics Management, and Industry Interface and Incubation / Entrepreneurship Development.

Teaching Experience:

Over 17 years of teaching experience (Dec 2008 - Present) across PGDM/MBA programs at IMS Ghaziabad, GLA University (Mathura), and Mangalayatan University (Aligarh).

Industry / Research Experience:
