Bio

Mandar Bapat completed B.A. in English and also holds a degree of Master in Personnel Management and Diploma in Labour Law. He is currently working as a full time faculty at MIT World Peace University, Kothrud to teach PEACE Subjects for World Peace University. He has corporate working experience of around 12 years which includes manufacturing as well as Multinational Software Company; He has worked in Admin and HR Department.

He is Certified Master NLP – Coach Practitioner from (ICTA), He also Practices Emotional Intelligence and HUNA. (A Hawaiian Method of Healing. He conducts Counselling, Meditation and various Therapy sessions for healing, helps to overcome mild depression and personality development. He has done marital counselling courses and does premarital & marital counselling at his office. He has conducted various soft skills training at manufacturing companies as well as software companies and at PMI as well. He has conducted Personality Development Training at Symbiosis University Lavale Pune Campus for the last four years.
