Professor and Associate Director

Bio

Anand J Kulkarni holds a PhD in Artificial Intelligence (AI) based Distributed Optimization from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, MS in AI from University of Regina, Canada. He worked as Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Odette School of Business, University of Windsor, Canada. Anand is a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from the Shivaji University, India and holds a Diploma from Board of Technical Education, Mumbai, India. Since 2021, he is working as Research Professor and Associate Director of the Institute of Artificial Intelligence at the MIT-WPU, Pune, India. His research interests include AI based Nature Inspired optimization algorithms, and self-organizing systems. Anand pioneered optimization methodologies such as Cohort Intelligence, Ideology Algorithm, Expectation Algorithm, Socio Evolution & Learning Optimization Algorithm, Leader-Advocate-Believer Algorithm and Snail Homing and Mating Search Algorithm. Anand has published over 90 research papers in peer-reviewed reputed journals, chapters and conferences along with 7 authored and 15 edited books. He has so far guided 6 doctoral, 10 masters and over 100 UG students. Anand is the lead series editor for Springer and Taylor & Francis as well as associate editor of Elsevier journals such as ‘Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence’ and ‘Systems and Soft Computing’ as well as IOS Press KES journal. He is the recipient of the best paper award in IEEE ICNSC, Chicago, USA and 'Swatantryveer Savarakar Award' 2023 by ‘Pune Marathi Granthalay’, Pune for his Marathi book entitled 'Artificial Intelligencechya Watewar'.

Research Areas

Nature Inspired AI based Methods, Self Organizing Systems, Optimization and Machine Learning
