Bio

Dr. Rushabh Trivedi’s teaching approach emphasizes conceptual clarity, analytical rigor, and the application of management theories to contemporary organizational contexts. His pedagogy fosters a structured and inclusive learning environment that encourages critical inquiry and reflective thinking. At MIT World Peace University, he contributes through research-informed teaching, curriculum development, and student mentoring, with a focus on organizational behaviour, leadership, and emerging technologies. His academic engagement aligns with the University’s vision of academic excellence, responsible leadership development, and the integration of research, innovation and social relevance.
