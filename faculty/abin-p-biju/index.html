Assistant Professor

Bio

With a passion for literature and a commitment to fostering a love for the English language, Mr. Abin P. Biju serves as an Assistant Professor of English. He has completed his Masters in English literature and he is a proud alumnus of the Department of English, Savitribai Phule Pune University. He brings to the classroom a wealth of knowledge and experience accumulated over 4 years in the field of academia.

He is dedicated to creating an engaging and inclusive learning environment where critical thinking, creativity, and effective communication skills are nurtured. His academic journey has not only been marked by teaching but also by ongoing research and publications in the field of English literature. His work delves mainly into the fields of feminism and cultural studies, providing him with valuable insights that he shares with his students, enriching their academic experience.

As an Assistant Professor of English, Mr. Abin is committed to promoting intellectual growth, encouraging students to think critically, and helping them excel in their academic pursuits.
