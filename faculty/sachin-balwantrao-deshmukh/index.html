Bio

Dr. Sachin Deshmukh is an accomplished academician and industry professional with over 20 years of combined experience in teaching, research, and corporate roles. Currently serving as an Associate Professor at MIT World Peace University, Pune, he specializes in Marketing Management, Digital Marketing, Services Marketing, and Insurance Laws. His industry tenure includes leadership roles at HDFC SLIC, Cipla Pharmaceuticals, and Macleods Pharmaceuticals.

Research Contributions : Presented Research papers in InternationalConferences in Dubai , Indonesia etc and Various National level conferences including IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Indore, IIM Kozhikode and IIM Bodhgaya.

Scopus-indexed publications, including Q1/Q2 journals

7 Authored Books on Digital Marketing, HR Analytics, Companies Act, and Labour Laws (Published by Vikas Publishing House).

2 Patents Filed : AI-Based Robotic Display for Interactive Marketing (2024) AI-Based Smart Finance & Accounting Model (2022)
