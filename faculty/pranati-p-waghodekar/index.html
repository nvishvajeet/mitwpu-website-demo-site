Bio

A Red Hat–certified and Cloud-certified Architect (AWS & Azure), I bring over 7.5 years of academic experience and 2 years of industry exposure to deliver outcome-driven, industry-aligned education. My teaching philosophy emphasizes experiential learning, critical thinking, and hands-on skill development to prepare students for real-world challenges. I actively integrate emerging technologies such as Cloud, DevOps, and Blockchain into the curriculum, fostering innovation and research orientation among students. Currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Blockchain, I remain committed to advancing MIT-WPU’s vision of value-based, technology-driven education and holistic student development.
