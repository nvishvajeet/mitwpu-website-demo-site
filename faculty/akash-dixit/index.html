Bio

Mr. Akash Dixit is an Assistant Professor at the School of Law, MIT-World Peace University. He completed his LLM in Business Laws from National Law University, Delhi, and holds a B.A. LL.B with a specialization in Energy Laws from the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, Dehradun. He qualified for the UGC NET exam in 2022.

Mr. Dixit has served as an Assistant Professor of Law at Jaipur National University, Jaipur, where he taught Constitutional Law, Banking Laws, Law of the Seas. He also worked closely with Prof. (Dr.) M.S. Sahoo, former Chairman of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India (IBBI), at National Law University, Delhi.

His interests include Constitutional Law, Banking Laws, and Insolvency Law. In addition to his academic pursuits, Mr. Dixit has experience in organizing and judging moot court competitions and has guided many students in preparing for LLM entrance exams.
