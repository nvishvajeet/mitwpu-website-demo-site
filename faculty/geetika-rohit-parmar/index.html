Bio

Geetika Parmar is a Computer Applications and Computer Management faculty with extensive academic experience of almost 15 years. She is currently associated with MIT World Peace University for the past decade. Geetika has taught various courses, including MCA, M.Sc (Computer Science), B.Sc (Computer Science), BCS, BCA, BBA(CA), and BBA. Her expertise lies in Operating Systems, Data Mining, Data Warehousing, Business Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning, Business Analytics, and Big Data Analytics. With a never-ending quest for learning, Geetika is passionate about working with students and helping them realize their potential. She firmly believes that learning keeps the mind young!

Research Area : Business Analytics and Data Analytics
