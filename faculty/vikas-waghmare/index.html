Bio

Vikas Waghmare is an Assistant Professor of Sociology in the School of Law, MITWPU. He has a bachelor's degree in Political Science from Fergusson college, with University rank. And has masters degrees in Sociology, Political Science and English. With over 6.5 years experience in academics and research, he has contributed to various publications and conferences in the field of Sociology and Political Science. His research interests include Sociology of Development and Political Theory. He is responsible for CAP and Cultural events at the school.
