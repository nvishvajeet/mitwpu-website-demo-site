Bio

Dr. Trupti Bhosale is an academician with over 20 years of combined experience in academia holding a Ph.D. in Management from Symbiosis International (Deemed University), in management education for the area of Analytics and Quantitative techniques centres on learner-centric, application-oriented education that integrates quantitative rigor with real-world problem solving. She also brings 3 years of industry experience.

Her research interest area lies in education, financial inclusion, financial literacy, artificial intelligence with research and publication in SCOPUS and Web of Science indexed journals, books, complemented by active contributions to international and national conferences and workshops and gaining research grant.

She is recognized for her strong commitment to academic excellence and leadership with over 6 years as the Deputy Director at Symbiosis School of Banking and Finance in academic administration to enhance institutional reputation and academic integrity managing academic operations, adept at designing and leading significant educational innovations, including international collaborations for the dual-degree and student exchange programmes.

Educational Qualifications : PhD (Management), Symbiosis International (Deemed University), MBA, B.Sc. (Statistics), Savitribai Phule Pune University, UGC-NET

Areas of Expertise : Business Analytics

Teaching Experience : 18 years

Industry / Research Experience : Authored research papers in reputable SCOPUS indexed journals and conferences on topics including Education, Artificial Intelligence, Financial Distress, Digital Payments, and Economic Impacts of Pandemic on Financial Markets Participated in international research project with substantial funding, at the SWIFT Institute, Belgium, research on EMI Development Principles. Industry experience Management Representative at a manufacturing firm (3 years)

Publications and Research Work : 13 Scopus indexed research paper publications in journals and conferences

Awards and Recognitions : Research Project with funding of Eur 20,000

Professional Affiliations : Member of BoS at Symbiosis College of Arts and Commerce

Subjects Taught : Business Mathematics, Business Statistics
