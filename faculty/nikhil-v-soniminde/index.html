Bio

Prof. Nikhil Soniminde is an Assistant Professor committed to outcome-based education, experiential learning, and research-driven teaching. His teaching approach emphasizes conceptual clarity, hands-on learning, and the integration of emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and image processing to address real-world challenges.

Actively engaged in research, publications, and innovation initiatives, he strives to create an inclusive, engaging, and student-centric learning environment. Aligned with the vision of MIT World Peace University, he focuses on fostering critical thinking, ethical values, innovation, and lifelong learning among students.
