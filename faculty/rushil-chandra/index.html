Bio

Mr. Rushil Chandra has a work experience of 4 years. He has worked in the legal industry as an Associate in a corporate-cum-dispute resolution firm in New Delhi for 1 year and there after as a faculty of law for 3 years. He has successfully completed his Under Graduation from Dr. Ram Manohar Lohiya National Law University (RML NLU, Lucknow) and his Post Graduation from Hidayatullah National Law University (HNLU, Raipur). He is currently enrolled as a Ph.D. Scholar at Gujarat National Law University (GNLU, Gandhinagar). His areas of interest include Company Law, Artificial Intelligence and Law, Information Technology Law, International Laws, Banking Laws and Jurisprudence. He is oriented towards research and innovation in law. Amongst his publications, some of the notable works include:

  • Research paper titled, “Algorithmic Fairness and Bias in Machine Learning Systems”, published as a part of the proceedings in the International Conference on Newer Engineering Concepts and Technology (ICONNECT-2023) by E3S; DOI: https://doi.org/10.1051/e3sconf/202339904036 [SCOPUS indexed]
  • Research paper titled, “Punishing the Unpunishable: A Liability Framework for Artificial Intelligence Systems”, published as a book chapter as a part of the proceedings in the International Conference on Digital Technology Applications by Springer Link; DOI: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-031-29860-8_6 [SCOPUS indexed]
  • Article titled, “Adapting to the AI Revolution” published in Economic and Political Weekly, JOUR, Volume 58, Issue No. 19, May 2023. [SCOPUS indexed]
  • Research Paper titled, “The Digital Gaming Revolution: An analysis of current trends, issues and future prospects” published in the Russian Law Journal, Volume 11, Issue No. 1 (2023); DOI: https://doi.org/10.52783/rlj.v11i1.288 [Web Of Science indexed]
  • Research Paper titled, “A Critical Study Of The Demand And Supply In Relation To Food Delivery Apps Like Zomato And Swiggy In India” published in the Russian Law Journal, Volume 11, Issue No. 1 (2023). [Web Of Science indexed]
