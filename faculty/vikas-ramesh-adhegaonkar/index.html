Bio

Dr. Vikas R. Adhegaonkar is an academician, researcher and trainer with over twelve years of experience in management education. His expertise spans Strategic Management, Business Analytics, Financial Analytics, Machine Learning and Financial Management. He has published more than twenty research papers in Scopus, IEEE and peer-reviewed journals, authored international book chapters and published patents. He has conducted numerous Faculty Development Programmes and Management Development Programmes in analytics, Excel, Power BI and data visualization. His teaching philosophy emphasizes experiential learning, industry relevance and analytical decision-making, supporting MIT- WPU's commitment to innovation, leadership and outcome-based education.

Educational Qualifications : Ph.D. (Finance), MMS (Finance), PGDBM, M.Com., DTL, Executive Diploma in Financial Analytics (XLRI)

Areas of Expertise : Business Strategy, Business Analytics, Financial Analytics, Financial Management, Machine Learning, Data Visualization, Financial Modelling, Excel Analytics

Teaching Experience : 12 years of teaching experience, associated with Christ University, Sri Balaji University

Industry / Research Experience : Conducted two Management Development Programmes on Excel, Power BI for industry executives

Publications and Research Work : Scopus Q2 publication: Measuring Employee Attrition Intention in an Auto-component Manufacturing Organization. IEEE publications on Customer Lifetime Value Prediction, Explainable Machine Learning for Credit Card Fraud Detection and Predictive Analytics. Published more than 15 papers in international conferences Three published patents in workspace design, HR data recording devices and e- commerce fraud detection

Subjects Taught Strategic Management, Business Analytics, Financial Analytics, Financial Management, Financial Modelling, Data Visualization, Machine Learning for Business, Data Visualization with Power BI and Tableau
