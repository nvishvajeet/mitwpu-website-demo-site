Bio

Dr. Ramkrishna Manatkar is a highly experienced academician and industry professional with over 35 years of expertise in Industrial Engineering, Marketing, Sales, Logistics, Supply Chain, Projects, TQM, and Continuous Improvement. He completed his Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Govt. College of Engineering, Amravati, India, and M. Tech. in Industrial Engineering and Management from IIT, Dhanbad, India. He also holds an MBA in Marketing and Finance from XLRI, Jamshedpur, and a Ph.D. in Logistics and Supply Chain from IIT Kharagpur, India. Dr. Manatkar has authored numerous technical papers published in reputed journals and has worked with renowned organizations like Tata Steel Ltd. and Arcelor Mittal Group Company. He is currently serving as a Professor of Operations and Supply Chain Management and Associate Dean of Ramcharan School of Leadership at MIT World Peace University, Pune.
