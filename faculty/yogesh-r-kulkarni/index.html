Bio

Dr. Yogesh R. Kulkarni serves as an Assistant Professor at SCET, MIT World Peace University College of Engineering and brings over 18 years of rich teaching experience in the field of engineering education. His primary research interests include Data Mining and Data Warehousing, areas in which he has made notable academic contributions.

Dr. Kulkarni has published research papers indexed in Elsevier Scopus and several additional papers in reputed national and international journals and conferences. He is an active member of professional bodies and holds lifetime memberships in the Computer Society of India (CSI) and the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE).

Known for his dedication and strong work ethic, Dr. Kulkarni has successfully guided more than ten undergraduate project groups, MTech students, PhD Scholar in process, supporting students in developing practical and research-oriented skills. In addition to his academic contributions, his effective administrative and organizational abilities enable him to efficiently manage teaching responsibilities and institutional academic activities.
