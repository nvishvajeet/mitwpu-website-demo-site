Bio

Mr Akshay Ugale has a total experience of 7 years with 5+ years of experience in teaching and 2 years of experience in the public policy and analysis field. He has pursued his Bachelors and Master’s degree in Economics from Savitribai Phule Pune University and cleared his NET qualification in the subject of economics in the year 2018. His area of interest are Banking, Finance, Macro Economics and Law & Economics. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D from the esteemed Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics in the field of banking and law.

His commitment to fostering a dynamic learning environment is evident in his engaging teaching style, where he combines theoretical concepts with real-world applications, ensuring her students gain a comprehensive understanding of economic principles. Along with engaging students in the classroom he has been mentoring students for various different competitions and social engagement activities creating curious minds which will become good citizens in the future. He has a number of publications in academic journals and books and working towards more publications.
