Bio

I, Dhanashri Wategaonkar working as an Assistant professor in School of Computer Engineering at MIT-World Peace University, Pune, India. Dr. Wategaonkar has completed her doctoral degree (Ph.D.) from the premier and India’s top-ranked institutes in engineering, VIT University Chennai Campus, India. Her area of research in Wireless Sensor Network. In the same domain, I have published 1 patent and 15 papers.

All Papers are published in various reputed journals with standard indexing like IEEE, Scopus, and Elsevier Scopus. Recently she has completed two consultancy projects. One is funded by BCUD, Pune with the amount of 2 Lakhs. Another project was under the guidance of TechMahindra where they have implemented a retail system and also we have signed MoU with them.

Currently, in the school, she is programme coordinator for the Cybersecurity and Forensics programme. She has successfully completed Remote Pilot Certification, on 05th March 2023 for 10 years from Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DCGA), Certificate No. PC032300002NW.
