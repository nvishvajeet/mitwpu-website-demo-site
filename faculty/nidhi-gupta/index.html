Bio

Dr. Nidhi Gupta is an Assistant Professor of Commerce specializing in Finance and Data Analytics. She holds a Ph.D. from Banaras Hindu University and has qualified UGC-NET JRF . Her research interests include financial management, corporate governance, data- driven decision-making, and applied econometrics. Proficient in tools such as Gretl, STATA, EViews, SPSS, and R Studio, she tries to integrate technology with pedagogy to enhance learning outcomes. She has published research papers, participated in national and international conferences, and actively contributes to academic administration as Faculty Exam Officer at the Ramcharan School of Leadership, MIT-WPU.
