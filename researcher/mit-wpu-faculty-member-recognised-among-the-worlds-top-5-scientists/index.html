MIT-WPU Faculty Member Recognised Among the World’s Top 5% Scientists

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune is proud to announce that Dr. Shvetank Bhatt, Associate Professor, Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences, has been recognised among the World’s Top 5% Scientists and featured in the 2025 SciRank Global Registry.

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune is proud to announce that Dr. Shvetank Bhatt , Associate Professor, Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences , has been recognised among the World’s Top 5% Scientists and featured in the 2025 SciRank Global Registry .

This prestigious recognition highlights Dr. Bhatt’s exceptional global research impact, significant scholarly contributions, and unwavering commitment to advancing the field of pharmacology. His work has played a vital role in driving scientific innovation and fostering academic excellence.

Over the years, Dr. Bhatt has made noteworthy contributions to pharmaceutical sciences through impactful research and a sustained pursuit of knowledge that addresses contemporary healthcare challenges. His inclusion in the 2025 SciRank Global Registry is a testament to his dedication to research excellence and his influence within the global scientific community.

MIT-WPU congratulates Dr. Shvetank Bhatt on this remarkable achievement and celebrates his continued efforts in contributing to scientific advancement and meaningful societal progress.
