Dr. Anand Kulkarni, Professor & Associate Director, Research Institute of AI, MIT-WPU, Pune, has been conferred the Editorial Contribution Award 2026 by Springer Nature under its Editor of Distinction recognition for Scientific Reports.

The award recognises his outstanding contribution to maintaining the quality and integrity of scientific publishing through the peer review process. This prestigious recognition reflects his commitment to advancing research excellence and strengthening the global scientific community.

MIT-WPU, Pune congratulates Dr. Kulkarni on this well-deserved achievement and wishes him continued success in his academic and research endeavours.
