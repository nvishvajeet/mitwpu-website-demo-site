MIT-WPU proudly announces a significant accomplishment by Dr. Anand Kulkarni, who has served as the Editor of a comprehensive handbook published by Springer Nature, one of the world’s leading international publishers. Dr. Kulkarni completed this monumental editorial task in less than three years, setting a new milestone in MIT-WPU’s history.

This handbook, an extensive reference on AI and Mathematics-based optimization methods, spans 2 volumes, 15 sections, and a total of 1,426 pages. With contributions from over 125 leading experts and researchers from across the globe, it provides an invaluable resource to the academic and research community.

Key Features

  • Contributions from global experts in the field of AI and optimization
  • Comprehensive coverage of state-of-the-art formal optimization methods and real-world applications
  • Includes critical literature reviews, detailed method descriptions, mathematical formulations, flowcharts, and more

Dr. Kulkarni’s remarkable dedication and expertise have not only resulted in this landmark publication but have also led to his selection by Springer Nature to edit an upcoming Encyclopedia in a specialized domain.

As a dedicated researcher in AI, Dr. Anand Kulkarni continues to bring distinction to MIT-WPU.

Congratulations to Dr. Anand Kulkarni on this exceptional achievement!
