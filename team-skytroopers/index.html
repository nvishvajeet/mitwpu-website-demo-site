Team Skytroopers is the official Aerodesign team of MIT-WPU, Pune, dedicated to inspiring the next generation of engineers and fostering technological advancement in the field of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). We provide a platform for passionate engineering students committed to designing and crafting innovative fixed-wing UAVs.

At Team Skytroopers, our mission is to instil a spirit of innovation and technological excellence, with a vision to contribute to the development of a technologically advanced nation. We are committed to nurturing talent and enthusiasm among engineering students and providing them with a space to design and create cutting-edge UAVs.
