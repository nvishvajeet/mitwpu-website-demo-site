We are a group of MIT Pune individuals representing their Institution at National and International BAJA events. Team Piranha Racing is the official BAJA SAE team of MIT-WPU. In seeking to achieve this, we hope to promote a culture of applied learning, encourage students to pursue exciting engineering careers, and imbibe in them a sense of teamwork and dedication. As a part of Piranha Racing, students get acquainted with the latest computer design and analysis software like SOLIDWORKS, Ansys, Hypermesh, etc. Students also learn innovative manufacturing and fabrication processes and various validation and testing methods.

We are dedicated to designing and manufacturing an All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV) for competing in BAJA SAE competitions worldwide. The team consists of about 30 aspiring engineers from the first year to the final year aiming to build the best BAJA SAE car in the world.
