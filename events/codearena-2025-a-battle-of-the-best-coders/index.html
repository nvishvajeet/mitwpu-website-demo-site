The Department of Computer Science and Applications at MIT-WPU successfully organized CodeArena 2025 – A Battle of the Best Coders on 16 September 2025, bringing together undergraduate and postgraduate students for an exciting offline coding competition.

Designed to test participants' programming expertise and problem-solving abilities, the competition featured real-time coding challenges in C, C++, and Java. Students solved problems of varying complexity and were evaluated on parameters such as code correctness, logical implementation, computational efficiency, and coding best practices.

CodeArena 2025 provided a dynamic platform for students to strengthen their analytical thinking, algorithmic skills, and coding proficiency while fostering a spirit of healthy competition. Outstanding performers from both undergraduate and postgraduate categories were felicitated for their exceptional performance, making the event a resounding success in promoting technical excellence and competitive programming within the department.
