Deepotsav 2025 at MIT-WPU, Pune, was a heartwarming, immersive celebration that brought the entire campus together in joy, music, and togetherness. The evening honored our teaching and non-teaching staff—the true pillars of MIT-WPU—whose unwavering dedication lights the path of learning, innovation, and service for every student.

The evening was a beautiful blend of culture, creativity, and camaraderie, where our talented staff members showcased their vibrant spirits through soulful musical performances, graceful dances, and entertaining skits. Laughter and applause filled the air as the MIT-WPU family came together to celebrate the festival of lights in its truest essence.

More than just a Diwali celebration, Deepotsav 2025 symbolized the light within each member of our community — the light that guides, inspires, and nurtures young minds every day. It was a reminder of the strength of our shared values, unity, and the collective pursuit of peace and knowledge.

As we continue to illuminate the path of learning and growth, MIT-WPU, Pune wishes everyone a Happy and Prosperous Diwali! May this festival of lights bring happiness, harmony, and new beginnings for all.
