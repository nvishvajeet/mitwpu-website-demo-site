The 5th International Symposium on Law & Peace 2025: Advancing Global Dialogue on Law & Peace

Explore key insights from the 5th International Symposium on Law & Peace at MIT-WPU. Read about discussions on cross-border trade, refugee law, and the role of justice in global harmony.

The 5th International Symposium on Law & Peace, hosted by the School of Law at MIT-WPU Pune, has successfully concluded, marking a significant milestone in advancing global dialogue on law and peace.

This landmark two-day event brought together legal professionals, policymakers, jurists, academicians, and youth to engage in non-political, non-aligned discussions focused on heralding peace through the power of law.

The symposium underscored the critical role of law in nurturing peace and harmony within a complex global society. Key sessions explored a wide range of pressing topics:

  • Legal System: The Foundation for World Peace
  • Cross-Border Trade and Investment
  • Innovate to Resolve: Advancing Dispute Resolution in a Changing World
  • Humanitarian, Refugee, and International Law
  • Law, Technology, and Healthcare: Safeguarding Mental and Emotional Well-being in the Digital Era

These sessions not only fostered insightful dialogue but also emphasized law’s foundational role in addressing contemporary challenges and promoting peaceful societies.

In addition to discussions, the symposium facilitated valuable networking opportunities among global legal leaders and emerging professionals. The valedictory session honored distinguished contributors for their exemplary dedication to justice and peace.

Aligned with MIT-WPU’s vision to promote justice, equity, and good conscience, the School of Law empowered emerging legal professionals as Conflict Resolution Ambassadors, equipping them to address socio-legal challenges and contribute effectively to peacebuilding.

The 5th International Symposium on Law & Peace at MIT-WPU Pune stands as a beacon of hope and progress, showcasing the university’s commitment to harnessing the power of law to create a harmonious and just global society.
