MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, celebrated the 12th International Day of Yoga with a special yoga session, reaffirming its commitment to holistic education, wellness, and mindful living.

Aligned with this year’s theme, "Yoga for Healthy Ageing," the event witnessed enthusiastic participation from the university's faculty and staff members, who came together to embrace the physical, mental, and emotional benefits of yoga.

Rooted in the vision of Revered Prof. (Dr.) Vishwanath D. Karad, Founder & Chief Patron, MAEER's MIT Group of Institutions; Founder President, MIT-WPU; and Chairman (Governing Body), MIT-WPU, and further championed by Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Managing Trustee & Executive President, MAEER's MIT Group of Institutions, and Executive President, MIT-WPU, yoga has remained an integral part of the university's educational philosophy since 1996.

The celebration reflected MIT-WPU's continued efforts to foster a culture of well-being and value-based learning while reinforcing the timeless relevance of yoga in promoting a healthy and balanced lifestyle.
