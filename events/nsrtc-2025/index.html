NSRTC 2025

Science, innovation, and national research priorities

Date
Three-day conference · 2025
Location
MIT World Peace University, Pune
Format
Conference
Status
Past event
Official MIT-WPU event report ↗

Overview

A national meeting of scientists, technologists, researchers, policy leaders, and students organised around research priorities for India.

The programme opened with plenary sessions on Nobel Prize-recognised work in medicine, chemistry, physics, and engineering, followed by specialist talks and a formal inaugural session.

Technical sessions and round tables connected fundamental research, applied science, technology development, and questions of national capacity.

Themes

  • Artificial intelligence
  • Advanced materials and processing
  • Digital transformation, energy, and sustainability
  • Healthcare, pharmaceutical science, and biotechnology

Programme

Special talks

Ashutosh Sharma spoke on science and technology for national development; Arindam Ghosh on materials for quantum technologies; and Ajit Kembhavi on the life and scientific journey of Jayant Vishnu Narlikar.

Scientific programme

Parallel sessions covered artificial intelligence, materials and devices, energy and sustainability, healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, with posters and round-table discussions.
