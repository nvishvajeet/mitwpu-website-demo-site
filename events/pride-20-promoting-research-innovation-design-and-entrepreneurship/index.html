The Department of Petroleum Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, successfully organized PRIDE 2.0 – Promoting Research, Innovation, Design & Entrepreneurship, a project presentation competition that encouraged students to showcase their research-driven ideas, innovative designs, and entrepreneurial solutions to real-world challenges.

The event provided a platform for students to demonstrate their technical expertise, enhance their presentation skills, and engage in knowledge exchange. PRIDE 2.0 promoted innovation, creative problem-solving, and forward-thinking approaches in engineering and technology, reflecting MIT-WPU's commitment to fostering a culture of research and entrepreneurship.
