MIT World Peace University’s Summer School 2025, held from May 8th to 10th, brought together young innovators from 9th to 12th grade for an exhilarating journey through the world of innovation.

Students immersed themselves in hands-on workshops - building drones and robots, exploring the wonders of AR/VR, mastering 3D printing, and visualising complex data. Guided by industry experts, every session transformed theory into real-world problem-solving, igniting creativity and confidence.

We were honoured to welcome special guest Mr. Atul Patil, DIG, Motor Transport, Pune, whose inspiring words encouraged participants to dream big and embrace the limitless opportunities in engineering and technology. The dynamic three-day summer school featured expert-led sessions, interactive quizzes, collaborative challenges, and a vibrant Students Club Road Show, fostering teamwork and lasting friendships.

The grand finale celebrated each student’s achievements with a heartfelt Valedictory Ceremony and Certificate Distribution, recognising their growth from curious learners to confident innovators.

At MIT-WPU Summer School, students left with more than just technical skills-they carried home a vision of limitless possibilities and the confidence to shape their futures.
