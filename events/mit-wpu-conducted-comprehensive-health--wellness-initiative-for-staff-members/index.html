MIT-WPU Conducted Comprehensive Health & Wellness Initiative for Staff Members

MIT-WPU Pune conducts a comprehensive health and wellness initiative for staff, prioritising preventive care, fitness, and overall employee wellbeing.

Events

  • Happenings
  • Events

Reaffirming its commitment to employee well-being and preventive healthcare, MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, conducted a comprehensive health and wellness check-up initiative for staff members aged 40 years and above.

The initiative, undertaken under the guidance of Dr. Aditi Karad, Hon'ble Executive Director, MIT-WPU , and Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Hon'ble Executive President, MIT-WPU , reflects the University's continued efforts to foster a healthy, supportive, and people-centric work environment.

Conducted in association with VishwaRaj Hospital, Loni Kalbhor , the programme aimed to encourage regular health assessments, facilitate early detection of potential health concerns, and promote a culture of preventive healthcare among employees.

As part of the initiative, staff members underwent a comprehensive set of diagnostic evaluations and medical assessments designed to support overall health and well-being. The programme was fully supported by the University as part of its employee welfare initiatives.

This thoughtful initiative highlights MIT-WPU's belief that the health and well-being of its employees are integral to building a vibrant and thriving academic community. It also reflects the compassionate leadership and unwavering commitment of the University's leadership towards creating a workplace that prioritises the welfare of its people.

MIT-WPU extends its sincere appreciation to VishwaRaj Hospital, Loni Kalbhor , and its dedicated medical professionals and staff for their support and collaboration in facilitating this initiative.

Through such meaningful endeavours, MIT-WPU continues to strengthen its commitment to the holistic well-being and overall development of its employees.
