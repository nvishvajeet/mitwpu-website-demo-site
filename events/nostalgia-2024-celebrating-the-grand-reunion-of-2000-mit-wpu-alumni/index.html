NOSTALGIA 2024: Celebrating the Grand Reunion of 2000+ MIT-WPU Alumni

MIT‑WPU’s Nostalgia 2024 reunites 2000+ alumni, celebrating memories, networking, and lifelong connections on campus.

Events

  • Happenings
  • Events

On the crisp morning of December 22, 2024, the corridors of MIT World Peace University hummed with extraordinary energy as over 2,000 alumni made their way back to the place where their dreams first took flight. NOSTALGIA'24 transcended the typical reunion as an extraordinary convergence of brilliance where over 200 CXOs, 50 entrepreneurs, 30 directors, 30 industry experts, and 1,000 top-notch professionals returned to their alma mater, transforming MIT-WPU's campus into a living masterclass of success and inspiration.

The day unfolded with the groundbreaking CXO Alumni Meet, a visionary initiative by Shri Rahul V. Karad, Executive President of MIT-WPU. The summit opened with an enlightening fireside chat featuring Dr. Saurabh Gadgil, MD and CEO of PNG Jewellers. As Dr. Gadgil shared his entrepreneurial journey, the audience sat captivated, drawing inspiration from his experiences and insights. His words served as a bridge between academic foundations and real-world success, demonstrating the transformative power of education.

Later, the intellectual core of the event emerged through a series of dynamic panel discussions, where over 200 industry leaders converged to tackle pressing contemporary challenges. The discussions delved deep into smart city governance, exploring innovative approaches to urban solutions through cross-sector collaboration. Equally compelling were the conversations around new-age community outreach and women's empowerment, reflecting MIT-WPU's commitment to holistic societal development.

As day transformed into evening, the Grand Reunion took on a different hue with two remarkable keynote sessions. Spiritual scientist Swami Sukhabodhananda offered a unique perspective on excellence, weaving together spiritual awareness with professional success. Marketing virtuoso Siddhant Narayan, recognised among India's top 10 marketers, followed with cutting-edge insights into brand building in our digital era, exemplifying the blend of tradition and innovation that MIT-WPU champions.

The true magic of NOSTALGIA'24 manifested in the countless informal moments between scheduled events. Current students found themselves in impromptu mentoring sessions with accomplished alumni, their eyes bright with possibility as they absorbed wisdom from those who had walked their path before. Cultural performances brought past and present together in a seamless celebration of talent, while corridor conversations sparked potential collaborations and lifelong connections.

The event reached its emotional zenith with Dr. Vishwanath Karad's address. The Founder President's vision of education – one that harmoniously blends values, innovation, and global peace – resonated deeply with alumni who had transformed these principles into successful careers worldwide. His words served as a powerful reminder of the institution's foundational values and its continuing relevance in shaping global leaders.

Eventually, NOSTALGIA'24 transcended the conventional boundaries of an alumni meet by becoming a living testament to MIT-WPU's legacy of excellence. The event created a platform where each success story shared became a beacon of inspiration for future generations. More than a celebration of past achievements, this event evolved into a platform for future collaborations, mentorship opportunities, and innovative partnerships.
