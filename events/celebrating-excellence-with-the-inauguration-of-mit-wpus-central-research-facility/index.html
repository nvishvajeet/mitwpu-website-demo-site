Celebrating Excellence With the Inauguration of MIT-WPU's Central Research Facility

MIT‑WPU opens a state‑of‑the‑art Central Research Facility to accelerate interdisciplinary research and innovation.

  • Happenings
  • Events

Welcome to the future of research and innovation at MIT World Peace University! We are proud to announce the successful launch of our cutting-edge Central Research Facility (CRF), inaugurated on January 7, 2025, at the prestigious Maitreyi Building. This state-of-the-art facility marks a transformative milestone in our commitment to advancing scientific research and technological innovation.

Under the visionary guidance of our Executive President, Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Chief Academic Officer Dr. Sanjay Kamtekar, and Dean Dr. Murthy Chavali, the CRF stands as a testament to MIT-WPU's dedication to pushing the boundaries of scientific exploration and academic excellence.

The Central Research Facility houses world-class research infrastructure including advanced characterisation and research equipment, featuring :

  • Field Emission Scanning Electron Microscopy (FESEM) for cutting-edge material analysis
  • Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS) for precise elemental analysis
  • State-of-the-art Li-ion/Na-ion cell fabrication facilities
  • Advanced analytical instruments and testing equipment

At MIT-WPU's Central Research Facility, we empower both students and research scholars to push the boundaries of innovation. Students gain invaluable hands-on experience with advanced equipment and real-world methodologies, while working on cutting-edge projects under expert mentorship. For our M.Tech, M.Sc., and Ph.D. scholars, the facility opens doors to advanced research capabilities and collaborative opportunities, supported by state-of-the-art equipment and guidance from renowned scientists. This dynamic environment creates a perfect ecosystem where theoretical knowledge meets practical application, nurturing the next generation of scientific pioneers.

The CRF facilitates groundbreaking research across multiple disciplines :

  • Materials Science and Engineering
  • Energy Storage Solutions
  • Environmental Analysis
  • Nanotechnology
  • Advanced Manufacturing
  • Sustainable Technology Development

Our Central Research Facility bridges the gap between academic excellence and industrial innovation, fostering powerful collaborations that drive real-world impact. Through strategic research partnerships with leading industries, comprehensive consultancy services, and robust technical support for industrial projects, we create an ecosystem where academic insights meet practical applications. This synergy opens doors to collaborative research opportunities, ensuring that groundbreaking discoveries in our labs translate into meaningful solutions for industry challenges.

The establishment of the Central Research Facility represents more than just an infrastructure upgrade – it's a commitment to :

  • Advancing scientific knowledge
  • Fostering innovation and research excellence
  • Supporting sustainable technological development
  • Creating real-world solutions for global challenges

The Central Research Facility (CRF) at MIT-WPU stands as a beacon of innovation and excellence, providing a unique platform for students, researchers, and industry partners to engage in transformative research. This state-of-the-art facility fosters a collaborative environment where groundbreaking ideas can flourish, ultimately contributing to advancements in technology and science. By embracing a multidisciplinary approach, MIT-WPU not only enhances academic pursuits but also addresses critical global challenges through insightful investigations and inventions. The commitment to research at MIT-WPU reflects a dedication to shaping a brighter future for all, underscoring the institution's role as a leader in higher education and innovation.
