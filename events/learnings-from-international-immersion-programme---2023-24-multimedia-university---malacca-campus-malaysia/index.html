Learnings from International Immersion Programme - 2023-24, Multimedia University - Malacca Campus, Malaysia

Discover the key learnings and student experiences from our 2023-24 International Immersion Programme at Multimedia University in Malaysia.

The International Immersion Programme at Multimedia University's Malacca campus, Malaysia, took place from February 26 to March 3, 2024 (6 nights, 7 days). Organised for students in the Department of Computer Science and Applications, the programme attracted 17 participants (16 students and 1 faculty member) demonstrating a keen interest in the experience. This opportunity enriched their educational experience and contributed to their personal and professional development.

Students learned about digital image processing, ethical hacking and penetration testing, and inventive problem-solving through TRIZ at Multimedia University. They also explored the historical city of Melaka, Malaysia.

Students spent 3 days in Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia, exploring the beautiful city, including industry visits, Batu Caves religious site, and various towers. They thoroughly enjoyed the academic trip.
