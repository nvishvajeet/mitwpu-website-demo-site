NSRTC 2026

A three-day conclave advancing science, technology, and innovation

Date
17–19 July 2026
Location
MIT World Peace University, Pune
Format
Conference
Status
Past event
Official MIT-WPU event report ↗

Overview

Scientists, researchers, industry leaders, policymakers, innovators, and students met for interdisciplinary discussions on emerging research and technology.

The programme combined invited talks and parallel technical sessions with round-table discussions, poster presentations, project exhibitions, and a Scholars-to-Scientists Connect initiative.

The conference brought together contributors from universities, national laboratories, research institutes, industry, and public organisations.

Themes

  • Reimagining the future with artificial intelligence
  • Materials, energy, and sustainability
  • Emergent device technologies
  • New biology and health sciences

Programme

Day 1

Opening sessions considered major scientific and engineering prizes, followed by the inaugural programme, research and entrepreneurship awards, keynote addresses, and a lecture on national wealth through science and technology.

Days 2–3

Four parallel research tracks covered artificial intelligence, sustainable energy, advanced materials, semiconductors, health technologies, biotechnology, quantum technologies, and indigenous innovation.
