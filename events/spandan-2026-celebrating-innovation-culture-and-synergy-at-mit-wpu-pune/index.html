SPANDAN 2026: Celebrating Innovation, Culture, and Synergy at MIT-WPU Pune

Experience Spandan 2026 at MIT-WPU Pune! A grand celebration of innovation, culture, and synergy. Explore workshops, performances, and student competitions.

The flagship Annual Social Gathering of MIT World Peace University, Pune, recently concluded as a magnificent four-day odyssey of talent, tradition, and togetherness. Held from 16–18 and on 25 February 2026, the event brought together diverse disciplines under the ‘One University, One Family’ ethos, serving as a vibrant canvas where academic rigor met cultural brilliance.

The festivities commenced with an electrifying showcase where the spirit of innovation harmonised with high-energy performances. This momentum surged into the second day, marked by a seamless blend of professional synergy and creative flair across diverse disciplines. The celebration continued with vibrant presentations that highlighted students’ multifaceted brilliance in bridging the gap between scientific inquiry and community well-being.

The grand finale brought the entire university community together in a spectacular celebration of talent, unity, and shared purpose. This ultimate expression of the SPANDAN spirit showcased a unique fusion of artistic expression and intellectual excellence, leaving an indelible mark on all attendees. The festivities reached a pinnacle with a University-Level Traditional Day, where vibrant performances and heritage attire reinforced the MIT-WPU Pune community’s bond in a beautiful display of unity and diversity.

Throughout the four days, MIT-WPU was privileged to host a galaxy of distinguished industry leaders, visionary technocrats, and eminent legal and cultural experts. Their presence and insights on leadership, innovation, and purpose-driven growth deeply inspired the next generation of global leaders. The occasion was graced by guests like:

  • Shri. Kirtiraj Jilkar, Executive Vice President, Thermax Limited, Pune
  • Shri. Rajeev Malik, Advisor & Business Head at Mahindra
  • Dr. A. M. Deshmukh, President, Microbiologist Society of India (MBSI)
  • Mrs. Kavita Dubepatil, Director, ORAH Nutrichem Private Limited
  • Mr. Ramprasad Santhanagopalan, President, UST Product Engineering
  • Adv. Jagdish Shirke, Sr. GM & Lead Counsel-Engine Business Unit, Cummins India
  • Pt. Yadavraj Phad, Renowned Indian Classical Vocalist

These distinguished guests shared invaluable insights that inspired students toward new heights of excellence and innovation.

Beyond the captivating stage performances and vibrant cultural showcases, SPANDAN 2026 reinforced MIT-WPU’s commitment to fostering a unified community. It was more than just a gathering; it was a celebration of collective identity, proving that at MIT-WPU Pune, tradition and modern innovation thrive in perfect harmony.
