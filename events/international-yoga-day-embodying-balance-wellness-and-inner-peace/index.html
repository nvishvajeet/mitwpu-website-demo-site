MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) commemorated International Yoga Day 2025 with an inspiring event that brought the university community together in the spirit of unity and holistic well-being. The event was graced by Acharya Shri Shivam, spiritual guide, motivational speaker, and Dean at VVSS Viswasanthi Foundation, who led a transformative guided meditation that resonated deeply with all attendees.

The event was graced by Rev. Prof. Dr. Vishwanath D. Karad, Founder-President of MIT-WPU, whose visionary leadership continues to guide MIT-WPU’s commitment to holistic education and the advancement of peace through learning.

Faculty, and staff participated enthusiastically in yoga asanas, pranayama, and mindful reflection, embracing yoga as a way of life that nurtures body, mind, and soul. The event underscored the importance of integrating yoga into daily routines to promote physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual harmony.

MIT-WPU remains committed to carrying forward the light of yoga, encouraging its community to embody its principles every day. This event reaffirmed the university’s mission to promote wellness, unity, and peace through education, inspiring all to live with greater mindfulness and harmony.
