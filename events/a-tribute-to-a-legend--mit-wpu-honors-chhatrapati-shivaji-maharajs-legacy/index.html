A Tribute to a Legend : MIT-WPU Honors Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's Legacy

MIT‑WPU pays tribute to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, celebrating leadership, valor, and nation‑building ideals.

  • Happenings
  • Events

MIT-WPU celebrated the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti on 19 February 2025, through a commemorative event honouring his remarkable vision and leadership. The programme provided a platform to reflect on the historical significance of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's contributions and their continued relevance today.

The event witnessed the presence of Founder President Prof. Dr. Vishwanath Karad, Vice Chancellor Dr. R.M. Chitnis, and Registrar Mr. Ganesh Pokale, who shared insightful perspectives on Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's lasting impact.

A significant emphasis was placed on how Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's enduring values of courage, resilience, and ethical governance can inspire and guide students in their academic pursuits and personal journeys. Interactive sessions encouraged students to reflect on his legacy and consider how they can contribute to a future built on wisdom and strength.

MIT-WPU's tribute was a powerful reminder of a leader whose legacy continues to influence and motivate both students and faculty alike.
