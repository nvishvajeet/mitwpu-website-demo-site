The Viksit Bharat Youth Parliament 2025, hosted by MIT World Peace University, successfully brought together dynamic youth and esteemed dignitaries to shape a self-reliant India. This event aimed to amplify the voice of youth, encouraging them to engage with public issues at district, state, and national levels.

Objectives of the Event included :

  • Amplifying youth voices through structured deliberations.
  • Encouraging civic engagement and informed decision-making.
  • Promoting respect for diverse perspectives and systematic discussions.
  • Documenting youth opinions on the vision of a developed Bharat to guide policymakers.

Discussion Topics spanned critical themes :

  • District Level : One Nation, One Election: Paving the Way for Viksit Bharat.
  • State Level : 75 Years of the Indian Constitution: A Journey of Rights, Duties, and Progress; The 11 Sankalp of Samvidhan Divas: Strengthening Constitutional Values.
  • National Level : One Nation, One Election: Simplifying Democracy, Amplifying Progress, Ensuring Stability.

Graced by distinguished guests such as Dr. Uday Tekale, Prof. Swanand Galgale, Prof. Shriranjan Awate, and Mr. Ashish Shete, the event featured key highlights including a symbolic lamp-lighting ceremony, inspiring addresses by dignitaries, and engaging multimedia presentations showcasing initiatives like Bharatiya Chhatra Sansad and the National Legislators Conference.
