Igniting Young Innovators: MIT-WPU Pune's Winter School 2025 Sparks Future Tech Leaders

Discover how MIT-WPU's Winter School 2025 sparked innovation! Read highlights from the 3-day tech workshop for students (Grades 9-12) in Robotics, AI & Design

MIT-WPU Pune’s Winter School Programme recently brought together students from Grades 9 to 12 for an intensive three-day immersion into future-ready learning. Designed to spark curiosity, unlock creativity, and inspire innovation, the programme seamlessly connected classroom concepts with real-world application.

Students engaged in hands-on modules spanning robotics, drone technology, AI coding, user experience design, and filmmaking. Leveraging industry-relevant tools and expert mentorship, participants transformed ideas into tangible outcomes—developing intelligent systems, designing user-focused solutions, and producing compelling visual narratives.

Collaborative hackathons, quizzes, and challenge-based activities strengthened teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership skills, shaping confident and capable creators. All participants received Certificates of Participation, with exceptional performers earning special recognition.

Through such experiential initiatives, MIT-WPU Pune continues to empower young minds for a technology-driven future—cultivating explorers who translate curiosity into capability, and learning into impact.
