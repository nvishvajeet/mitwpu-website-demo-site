Cybersecurity Placement Accelerator Bootcamp

MIT-WPU's Cybersecurity Bootcamp trained students in SOC, VAPT, Cloud Security & GRC - bridging the gap between academics and industry hiring. Read highlights.

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune conducted the Cybersecurity Placement Accelerator Bootcamp to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations by providing students with practical exposure, domain-specific skills, and placement-oriented preparation in the field of cybersecurity.

The bootcamp prepared students for entry-level cybersecurity roles across key domains, including:

  • Security Operations Centre (SOC)
  • Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT)
  • Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)
  • Cloud Security
  • Data Privacy & Information Security

The programme also focused on building strong technical fundamentals, enhancing problem-solving capabilities, and improving students' confidence for campus recruitment and industry interviews.
