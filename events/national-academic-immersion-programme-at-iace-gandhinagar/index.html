National Academic Immersion Programme at iACE, Gandhinagar

Third-year students from the B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering AI/ML and B.Tech ECE programmes under the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Events

  • Happenings
  • Events

Third-year students from the B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) and B.Tech Electrical & Computer Engineering programmes under the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, participated in the National Academic Immersion Program (NAIP) held at the International Automobile Centre of Excellence (iACE), Gandhinagar, Gujarat, from 18th to 28th June 2026.

Conducted as part of the academic curriculum, the programme brought together students from four divisions of ECE-AIML and one division of E&CE, accompanied by faculty members, for an immersive learning experience focused on emerging technologies in the automotive and electronics sectors.

The programme provided hands-on training in advanced automotive systems, electronics, robotics, and Industry 4.0, enabling students to gain practical insights into evolving industry practices. In addition to the technical sessions, participants undertook an industrial visit and a cultural tour, offering them an opportunity to understand Gujarat's industrial ecosystem and cultural heritage.

The National Academic Immersion Program reflects MIT-WPU's commitment to experiential learning by complementing classroom education with industry exposure, practical training, and holistic development, preparing students to excel in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.
