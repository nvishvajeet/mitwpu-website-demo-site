Industry Academia Conclave 2026: Shaping Future Talent in the Age of AI

MIT-WPU and HR Association India hosted the Industry Academia Conclave 2026 in New Delhi - exploring AI, global talent trends & the future of industry-academia collaboration

Events

  • Happenings
  • Events

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, in collaboration with the HR Association India, successfully hosted the Industry Academia Conclave 2026 in New Delhi, bringing together industry leaders, HR professionals, and academic experts to discuss the future of talent in an era of AI, global realignment, and rapid technological change.

The conclave featured insightful keynote addresses and a thought-provoking panel discussion on "Shaping Future Talent: AI, Global Realignment & the Next Frontier of Industry-Academia Collaboration." Discussions explored the evolving world of work, leadership, innovation, and the growing importance of industry-academia partnerships in preparing future-ready professionals.

The event also served as a valuable platform for networking, knowledge exchange, and strengthening collaborations between academia and industry, reaffirming MIT-WPU's commitment to bridging education with industry needs and fostering future-ready talent.
