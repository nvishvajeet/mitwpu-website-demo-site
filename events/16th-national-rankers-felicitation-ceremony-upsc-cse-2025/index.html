The 16th National Rankers Felicitation Ceremony – UPSC CSE 2025 and Service Orientation Program was successfully held on 10th and 11th July 2026 at MIT-WPU, Pune.

The two-day program brought together UPSC CSE 2025 National Rankers for a series of orientation sessions, interactive discussions, and mentoring opportunities designed to prepare them for their journey in public service. The sessions focused on leadership, integrity, ethical governance, and nation-building, while encouraging meaningful dialogue between the rankers and accomplished public administrators.

The program concluded with the National Rankers Felicitation Ceremony, celebrating the achievements of the UPSC CSE 2025 qualifiers. The event also provided participants with an opportunity to interact, network, and gain valuable insights into the responsibilities and expectations of a career in the civil services.
