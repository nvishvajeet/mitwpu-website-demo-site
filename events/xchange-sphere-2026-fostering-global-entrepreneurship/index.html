Xchange Sphere 2026: Fostering Global Entrepreneurship

MIT World Peace University successfully hosted Xchange Sphere 2026 on 2 February 2026, a unique India–Spain entrepreneurship exchange that brought together students from MIT-WPU, University of Mondragon, and Travelling University, Spain.

The event featured 20 startup teams—10 from MIT-WPU and 10 from Spain—who presented innovative business ideas through concise three-minute pitches, followed by interactive Q&A sessions. The platform encouraged participants to exchange ideas, receive constructive feedback, and refine their entrepreneurial thinking in a collaborative international environment.

A key highlight of the event was the opportunity for students from both countries to engage in meaningful cross-cultural interactions, sharing diverse perspectives on innovation, market potential, business scalability, and entrepreneurship. These discussions not only broadened their global outlook but also encouraged collaborative problem-solving and knowledge exchange.

The presence of the Spanish delegation further strengthened academic and entrepreneurial ties between the participating institutions, reinforcing the importance of international partnerships in nurturing innovation and fostering global competencies among students.

Xchange Sphere 2026 reflects MIT-WPU's continued commitment to promoting entrepreneurship, experiential learning, and global collaboration, empowering students to become future-ready innovators, business leaders, and changemakers capable of creating impact beyond borders.
