Technophilia 2026

Dive into the future at Technophilia 2026, MIT-WPU's premier tech fest. Explore cutting-edge innovations, tech workshops, and live demonstrations. Don’t miss out—register today!

Technophilia 2026 was a two-day state-level event conducted Department of Polytechnic and Skill Development, on 2 nd April and 3 rd April 2026. The event featured a mix of technical and non-technical competitions designed to test and showcase the knowledge, creativity, problem-solving ability, and practical skills of diploma-level students from various institutions. The primary objective was to foster innovation, team spirit, and inter-collegiate collaboration through well-structured, theme-based events. This year, the event witnessed overwhelming participation from students across the state, with approximately 298 students actively competing in various challenges. The competitions were held on-campus, utilizing the institution's classrooms and laboratories

Technophilia was featured with following technical events hosted by every program at Department of Polytechnic 2 nd April 2026 :

  • Modelverse
  • Prompt War
  • Codelock
  • Solidsprint
  • Sparktech

Technophilia also featured Non-Technical Events on 3 rd April 2026 :

  • Slow Cycling
  • Tug Of War
  • Octopus Race
