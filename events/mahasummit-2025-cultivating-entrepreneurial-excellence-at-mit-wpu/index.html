"महाराष्ट्राची माती उद्योजकतेच्या यशासाठी"

On 17-18 February 2025, the E-Cell at MIT-WPU hosted Maharashtra's premier entrepreneurial event, ‘MahaSummit 2025’. This event embodied MIT-WPU’s vision that entrepreneurship transcends mere ideation—it's about execution, collaboration, and creating meaningful impact, by bringing together Maharashtra's brightest minds across a dynamic two-day celebration of innovation and enterprise.

The MahaSummit launched with an inspiring inaugural ceremony featuring distinguished leaders from academia and business. MIT-WPU’s Vice Chancellor Prof. R.M. Chitnis set an inspiring tone, followed by Navnath Yewale (Founder, Yewale Amrutulya) who shared his remarkable journey from local tea seller to business magnate. Also, over 200 startups connected with prominent investors and mentors including:

Neeraj Tyagi (Cofounder & CEO, WeFounder Circle) Kshama Jain (Founder & CEO, Rise Global) Pradeep Chatterjee (VP - AI & ML, Motherson Group) Bharat Oswal (Vice Chairman, JIIF, India)

The day fostered meaningful connections and practical knowledge exchange through targeted mentorship sessions, interactive panels, and networking opportunities.

Day 2 expanded the entrepreneurial ecosystem with representatives from 10+ colleges and 20+ incubators and accelerators. Dr. Rahul Karad (Executive President, MIT-WPU) delivered a powerful address on entrepreneurial thinking as a catalyst for societal transformation. The highlights for day 2 included:

A captivating fireside chat with Ayushi Ashar on revolutionising family businesses An electrifying Shark Tank-style panel with seasoned investors Shelly Bulchandani, Saurabh Mangrulkar, and Vaishali Mehta The inspiring Women in Entrepreneurship panel featuring Prachi Shevgoankar, Ritika Saraswat, and Nidhi Agarwal, showcasing female leadership in building sustainable ventures

In a nutshell, MahaSummit 2025 attracted over 5,000 participants, facilitated 100+ mentor-startup connections, and showcased innovations across fintech, agritech, cleantech, and social enterprise sectors. The event created an unparalleled platform where Maharashtra's entrepreneurial heritage embraced global innovation practices. The connections made and knowledge shared during these two days will continue to fuel Maharashtra's entrepreneurial ecosystem for years to come.
