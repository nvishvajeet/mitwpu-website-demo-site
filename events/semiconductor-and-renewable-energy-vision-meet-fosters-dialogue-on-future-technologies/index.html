Semiconductor and Renewable Energy Vision Meet Fosters Dialogue on Future Technologies

MIT-WPU hosted a Vision Meet on semiconductor and renewable energy technologies, encouraging dialogue on innovation and future industries.

Events

  • Happenings
  • Events

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, successfully organised the Semiconductor & Renewable Energy Vision Meet on 24 January 2026, bringing together eminent academicians, industry leaders, policymakers, and researchers to discuss emerging trends and opportunities in future technologies.

The event served as a platform for meaningful deliberations on semiconductor technologies, renewable energy, smart infrastructure, and workforce development, highlighting their role in shaping a sustainable and technologically advanced future.

The programme featured insightful keynote addresses by distinguished experts, high-impact panel discussions, and student poster presentations, fostering knowledge exchange and collaboration between academia, industry, and policymakers.

By encouraging interdisciplinary dialogue and innovation, the Vision Meet reinforced MIT-WPU's commitment to advancing research, promoting industry-academia collaboration, and preparing future-ready professionals capable of addressing global technological challenges.
