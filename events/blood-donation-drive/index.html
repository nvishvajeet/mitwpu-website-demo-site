Blood Donation Drive

Read about the successful Blood Donation Drive at MIT-WPU. See how our students, faculty, and staff came together to contribute to this vital community service.

In honor of the 40th Foundation Day of MAEER, we are elated to share the success of the Blood Donation drive organised at MIT-WPU. As a continued commitment to our society, this noble endeavor was another humble contribution towards community welfare. The drive saw an enthusiastic turnout from the university community, an effort that not only enhanced the well-being of our community but has also extended a lifeline to patients in need of critical medical care and hope. A heartfelt thank you to our experienced medical professionals who ensured a smooth and secure donation process, and to those who became a part of this drive.
