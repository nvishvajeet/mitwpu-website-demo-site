Shaping the Future of HR: MIT-WPU Annual HR Conclave 2025

The convergence of industry expertise and academic excellence marked MIT World Peace University's Annual HR Conclave 2025, where the Department of Liberal Arts partnered with HR Shapers Group. This dynamic gathering, held on 22 February 2025, emerged as a hub of innovation, bringing together over 100 HR professionals to explore ground-breaking strategies reshaping the future of human resources.

The convergence of industry expertise and academic excellence marked MIT World Peace University's Annual HR Conclave 2025, where the Department of Liberal Arts partnered with HR Shapers Group. This dynamic gathering, held on 22 February 2025, emerged as a hub of innovation, bringing together over 100 HR professionals to explore ground-breaking strategies reshaping the future of human resources.

Visionary Leadership Insights

The conclave opened with powerful keynote addresses:

  • Prof. Dr. R.M. Chitnis, Vice Chancellor of MIT-WPU, shared valuable perspectives on educational excellence and industry integration
  • Mr. Ashish Gakrey [Founder HR Shapers, HR Leaders- Capgemini, Teleperformance, Loop Mobile, ZycusInfotech], offered insights into emerging HR trends

Revolutionary Panel Discussions

The event featured two thought-provoking panel discussions that delved into crucial aspects of HR industry :

Future-Ready Workforce : The first panel explored "2025 Workforce Trends & Scope of Psychologists," examining the evolving role of psychological expertise in tomorrow's workplace.

AI-Human Synergy : The second discussion focused on "Harnessing Synergy in Human-centric Workforce with Gen AI," addressing the delicate balance between technological advancement and human- centred approaches.

The conclave served as a powerful platform for networking, knowledge exchange, and professional growth, equipping attendees with innovative tools to excel in today's dynamic business environment.
