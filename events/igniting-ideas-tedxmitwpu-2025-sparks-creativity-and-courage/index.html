Igniting Ideas: TEDxMITWPU 2025 Sparks Creativity and Courage

TEDxMITWPU 2025 was a remarkable event where inspiration, creativity, and courage took center stage. A diverse group of changemakers and visionaries shared their stories and ideas, including:

  • Samaakshi Jha, Content Creator
  • Capt. Dharmveer Singh, Army Veteran & Motivational Speaker
  • Nitish Bharti, Visual Storyteller & Sand Artist
  • Angkit Joshi, Content Creator
  • Parnavi Bangar, Architectural Designer
  • Sahil Juneja, Founder of The Chai Kaapi
  • Manthan Shah, Business Strategist & Speaker
  • Chinmay & Aaryan, Indie Unplugged

Each speaker captivated the audience with stories that challenged perspectives and encouraged innovation. More than just a series of talks, TEDxMITWPU 2025 highlighted the power of ideas to transform lives and build communities. The dedication of everyone involved-speakers, volunteers, sponsors, and attendees-made this event a memorable platform for inspiration and positive change, reinforcing the impact of collective creativity and shared vision.
