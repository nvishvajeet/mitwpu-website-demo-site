Design Discovery: Exploring Dubai Design Week '23 with the WPU School of Design

MIT‑WPU design students explore Dubai Design Week 2023—global trends, sustainability, innovation, and immersive learning experiences.

Seventy-two students from Semesters V and VII of the School of Design had the incredible opportunity to immerse themselves in the vibrant world of design during their visit to Dubai Design Week '23 at Design District. They embarked on a captivating journey, participating in enriching workshops, exploring awe-inspiring exhibitions featuring renowned international brands, marvelling at exquisite Italian furniture displays, and delving into the innovative realm of futuristic sustainable materials revolutionizing various design fields, such as Fashion, Products, Visual Communication, Furniture, and Interior Space Design.
