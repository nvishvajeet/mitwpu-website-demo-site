Strengthening India's Infrastructure: MIT-WPU's Training Programme for Border Roads Organisation Officers

MIT‑WPU conducts a specialised training programme for Border Roads Organisation officers to strengthen national infrastructure.

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) recently conducted a two-week intensive training programme for young officers of the Border Roads Organisation (BRO). Led by Prof. Dr. Sandeep Potnis, the programme focused on "Planning, Design, Construction and Equipment Management for Tunnel Construction."

This initiative facilitated a valuable exchange of knowledge, equipping BRO officers with the expertise needed to develop critical road infrastructure in border areas, supporting our Armed Forces.

Through this collaboration, MIT-WPU reaffirms its commitment to national development by enhancing the capabilities of those who serve the nation. This programme highlights the university's role in shaping the future of India's infrastructure, fostering a stronger and more secure nation for generations to come.
