IEEE HSWTech-2025 International Conference Advances Sustainable Technologies for a Smarter Future

Explore IEEE HSWTech 2025 at MIT-WPU, bringing together researchers, academicians, and industry experts to discuss sustainable technologies, innovation, and smart solutions for the future.

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, successfully organised the IEEE HSWTech-2025 International Conference on Sustainable Technologies for Humanity and Smart World (Conference Code: 64936) from 2 to 4 September 2025 under the aegis of the IEEE Communications Society Pune Chapter.

The conference brought together researchers, academicians, scholars, and industry professionals from diverse disciplines to exchange ideas, present research findings, and discuss emerging technological advancements aimed at creating a sustainable and intelligent future.

Serving as a vibrant platform for collaboration and knowledge sharing, the conference featured deliberations across a wide range of domains, including green communication and networks, semiconductors and chip design, artificial intelligence and computing, automation and UxVs, green energy and sustainability, and engineering solutions for humanity and healthcare.

Through technical sessions and research presentations, participants explored innovative approaches to addressing contemporary challenges and highlighted the role of technology in fostering sustainable development and improving quality of life.

The successful organisation of IEEE HSWTech-2025 reaffirmed MIT-WPU's commitment to promoting cutting-edge research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and technological innovations that contribute to the advancement of society and the creation of a smarter, more sustainable world.
