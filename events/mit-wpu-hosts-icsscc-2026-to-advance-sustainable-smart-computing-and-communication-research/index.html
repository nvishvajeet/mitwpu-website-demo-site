MIT-WPU Hosts ICSSCC-2026 to Advance Sustainable Smart Computing and Communication Research

The School of Computer Science and Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, successfully organized the 2nd International Conference on Sustainable Smart Computing and Communications (ICSSCC- 2026) on 13–14 March 2026.

The School of Computer Science and Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, successfully organized the 2nd International Conference on Sustainable Smart Computing and Communications (ICSSCC- 2026) on 13–14 March 2026.

The conference brought together researchers, academicians, industry experts, and students from across the globe to discuss advancements in sustainable computing, artificial intelligence, next-generation communication technologies, and emerging digital innovations. The conference proceedings are being published by Springer Nature in the Advances in Science, Technology & Innovation (ASTI) series, indexed in Scopus.

Conference Highlights :

  • Event: 2nd International Conference on Sustainable Smart Computing and Communications (ICSSCC-2026)
  • Dates: 13–14 March 2026
  • Organized by: School of Computer Science and Engineering, MIT-WPU
  • 1,300+ research paper submissions from India and several other countries
  • Springer Nature publication in the ASTI series, indexed in Scopus
  • Distinguished keynote addresses, technical sessions, and paper presentations
  • Interactive discussions on AI, sustainable computing, research methodologies, AI-assisted scientific writing, ethical AI, and emerging technologies
  • Best Paper Awards presented to recognize outstanding research contributions

ICSSCC-2026 strengthened global academic collaboration and reaffirmed MIT-WPU's commitment to promoting interdisciplinary research, innovation, and sustainable technological development.
