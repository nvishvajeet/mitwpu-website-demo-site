The Department of Petroleum Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, successfully organized Magma 2026, a technical event focused on the study and advancement of geothermal energy.

The event covered key technical domains, including heat flow studies, geothermal exploration, drilling technologies, reservoir modelling and simulation, and geothermal production systems. It provided students, researchers, and industry professionals with a platform to enhance their technical understanding, exchange innovative ideas, and discuss sustainable energy solutions.

Magma 2026 reaffirmed MIT-WPU's commitment to promoting technical excellence, research, and innovation in the field of energy and petroleum engineering.
