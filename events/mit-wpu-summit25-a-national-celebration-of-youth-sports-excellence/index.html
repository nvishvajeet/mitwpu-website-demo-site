On February 25, 2025, the 18th edition of National Level Sports Summit '25 transformed the Atri Lawns at MIT-WPU Kothrud Campus into a vibrant arena of athletic prowess. This prestigious national sports event, dedicated to youth development and welfare, was inaugurated at 5:00 PM by Paralympic Gold Medalist and Padma Shri recipient Hon. Shri Murlikant Petkar.

Distinguished guests included renowned Judo Coach Hon. Shri Yogesh Dhadave (Krida Acharya Awardee) and Olympian Hockey Player Hon. Smt. Rekha G. Bhide (Krida Maharshi Awardee), with Revered Prof. Vishwanath D. Karad and Hon. Shri Rahul V. Karad presiding.

Running for five action-packed days from February 25th to March 1st, 2025, the event features 130 teams competing across 9 sports including cricket, volleyball, football, table tennis, kabaddi and more. This national level tournament celebrates skill, determination, and teamwork among all participants.
