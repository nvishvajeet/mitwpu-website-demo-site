The Boston Alumni Meet, hosted on August 7, 2025, wasn’t just a reunion—it was a lively celebration of personal and collective journeys, triumphs, and a shared commitment to the MIT-WPU legacy. The evening sparkled with renewed friendships, inspiring stories, and the promise of fresh collaborations, all infused with an atmosphere of positivity and forward-thinking energy.

The gathering was elevated by the presence of Honourable Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Executive President, MIT-WPU. He highlighted the vital role alumni play in nurturing ongoing ties with their alma mater, supporting its progress, and empowering the next generation. He reaffirmed that alumni are an essential thread in MIT-WPU’s enduring legacy and its vision for the future.

Highlights of the Event

  • Reconnect : Alumni across diverse batches and professional spheres reunited with lifelong friends and beloved professors.
  • Network : Career insights and stories were exchanged, sparking valuable professional connections across numerous industries.
  • Collaborate : Attendees discussed exciting opportunities for mentorship, collaboration, and the growth of both our institution and alumni network.
  • Celebrate Impact : The open-floor session saw alumni sharing profound reflections on their journeys since graduation, reaffirming our widespread global impact.

Special appreciation was extended to our advisors, Dr. Dinkar Kharat and Dr. Ashok Joshi, whose support helped make the event possible. Immense gratitude to Dr. Sriman Banerjee [Executive Director at Takeda Pharmaceutical Company Limited] and our dedicated alumni relations team for the gracious hospitality. The Boston Alumni Meet stands as a testament to the enduring spirit of our MIT-WPU family. Here’s to old memories, new connections, and a visionary future together!
