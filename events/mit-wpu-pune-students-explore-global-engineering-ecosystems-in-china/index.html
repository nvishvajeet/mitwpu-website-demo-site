MIT-WPU, Pune Students Explore Global Engineering Ecosystems in China

MIT-WPU Mechanical & ECE students visited Shanghai's S-Factory, Volkswagen plant & Shanghai Jiao Tong University, gaining hands-on insights into Industry 4.0 and smart manufacturing.

  • Happenings
  • Events

Mechanical Engineering and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) students from MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, participated in an international immersion programme in China, gaining first-hand exposure to global engineering practices, advanced manufacturing technologies, and academic excellence.

As part of the programme, students visited the S-Factory Exhibition in Shanghai, the Volkswagen Manufacturing Facility, and Shanghai Jiao Tong University. These immersive experiences provided valuable insights into smart manufacturing, automation, Industry 4.0 practices, and cutting-edge engineering innovation while enabling students to observe global industry standards and technological advancements.

The programme also fostered cross-cultural learning, encouraging students to broaden their perspectives, engage with diverse environments, and develop a deeper appreciation of engineering in an international context.

At MIT-WPU, international immersion programmes form an integral part of the university's experiential learning approach, empowering students with global exposure, industry relevance, and practical insights that prepare them to thrive in an increasingly interconnected world.
