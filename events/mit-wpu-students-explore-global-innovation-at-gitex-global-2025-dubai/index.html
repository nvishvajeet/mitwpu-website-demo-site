MIT-WPU Students Explore Global Innovation at GITEX GLOBAL 2025, Dubai

MIT-WPU students gained global tech exposure at GITEX GLOBAL 2025, Dubai - exploring AI, blockchain & smart cities alongside 2,000+ startups and 1,200+ investors.

MIT World Peace University, Pune organised an educational visit for 91 students from the School of Computer Engineering and Technology to GITEX GLOBAL 2025 in Dubai, one of the world's largest and most influential technology exhibitions.

Bringing together over 2,000 startups, 1,200+ investors, and participants from more than 100 countries, GITEX GLOBAL 2025 offered students a first-hand opportunity to explore the latest advancements shaping the future of technology. The exhibition showcased innovations across key domains, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, the Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain, Robotics, and Smart Cities.

The visit enabled students to interact with cutting-edge technologies, gain valuable industry insights, and understand emerging global technology trends. It also provided a platform to connect with innovators, entrepreneurs, and technology leaders from around the world, broadening their perspectives on the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

This international exposure reflects MIT-WPU's commitment to experiential learning and global engagement, equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and industry awareness required to become future-ready technology professionals.
