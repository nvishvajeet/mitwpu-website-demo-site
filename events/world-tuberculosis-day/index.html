The Department of Public Health recently organizes two impactful events, showcasing their commitment to advancing healthcare and research. The World Tuberculosis Day event, held at MIT-World Peace University in Pune on March 24, 2023, epitomized a concerted effort in combating tuberculosis on a global scale. The event themed "Yes! We can end TB!" provided the participants with profound insights, cutting-edge research updates, and engaging discussions. Notably, the afternoon session infused fun-filled activities promoting TB awareness, fostering a sense of community involvement. This event underscored the collective determination to combat TB and marked a significant step towards a TB-free world.
