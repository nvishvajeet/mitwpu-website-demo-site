Research Methodologies

Research That Listens, Learns, and Transforms

  • We conducting systematic studies of ocean species, ecosystems, and evolving ocean conditions.
  • Developing and deploying advanced instruments, sensors, and AI-enabled systems to support ocean research.
  • Assessing how coastal communities derive, share, and benefit from ocean-based livelihoods and resources.
  • Evaluating legal frameworks and institutional structures to strengthen ocean governance and management.
  • Collaborating with local and Indigenous communities to integrate their knowledge, priorities, and practices.
  • Drawing expertise from science, engineering, economics, law, and cultural studies to address complex ocean challenges..
  • We Implementing and assessing innovative approaches through real-world ocean trials and demonstration sites.

Prof. Sunil Kumar Singh

Advisor

Director, CSIR - National Institute of Oceanography, Goa Prof. Sunil Kumar Singh is the Director of CSIR – National Institute of Oceanography (CSIR-NIO), Goa, and a Professor at the Physical Research Laboratory, Ahmedabad. Born in 1971, he completed his B.Sc. and M.Sc. (Tech.) from Banaras Hindu University and his PhD in Geology from the Physical Research Laboratory and M.S. University, Vadodara.
