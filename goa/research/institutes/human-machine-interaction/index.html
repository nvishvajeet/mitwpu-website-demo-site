Prof. Siba Kumar Udgata

Professor, School of Computer & Information Sciences, University of Hyderabad Prof. Siba Kumar Udgata is a Professor at the School of Computer and Information Sciences, University of Hyderabad, and heads the Centre for Modelling, Simulation and Design (CMSD), a high-performance computing facility at the university. He holds a Master’s and PhD in Computer Science with specialisation in mobile computing and wireless communication, and his primary research interests span wireless communication, wireless sensor networks, IoT, intelligent algorithms, and soft computing. He was a United Nations Fellow and worked as a research fellow at the United Nations University / International Institute for Software Technology (UNU/IIST), Macau in 2001. He serves as Principal Investigator on multiple Government of India funded research projects focused on sensor network applications and swarm intelligence techniques, and has over 2,700 citations with more than 161 publications to his credit.
