Centre of Excellence

Centre for Music, Film & Creative Futures

Explore the Center for Music, Film and Creative Futures at WPU Goa, fostering innovation in media, storytelling, arts, and emerging creative industries.

Role context
People listed under Advisory network are external advisors to the institute or centre. They are not represented as resident WPU Goa researchers or faculty.

Rationale

India is one of the world’s most culturally vibrant nations, with creative traditions spanning classical arts, folk practices, contemporary cinema, digital media, and emerging interactive forms. As the global creative economy rapidly expands and converges with technology, there is a need for an institutional ecosystem that can bridge India’s artistic depth with future-ready creative innovation.

This Centre addresses that need by fostering interdisciplinary learning, production, and experimentation across music, film, media, storytelling, and creative technologies preparing students for impactful careers in India’s cultural economy and globally connected creative industries.

Objectives

  • Develop future-ready creators through interdisciplinary artistic training rooted in India’s cultural strengths.
  • Integrate music, film, technology, and storytelling for creative experimentation and innovation.
  • Offer live production, performance labs, and collaborative studio environments.- Build strong national and international industry partnerships for learning and employability.
  • Strengthen India’s creative footprint by promoting culturally grounded contemporary expression.

Mandate

To nurture next-generation creative excellence by integrating arts, culture, and technology; to advance India’s creative legacy through contemporary innovation; and to build globally relevant creators, storytellers, and industry contributors.

Activities

Interdisciplinary creative labs in music, film, and digital media. Built for experimentation and cross-disciplinary collaboration.

Workshops, masterclasses, and artist residencies. Offering mentorship and industry-led learning.

Production studios for film, sound, and immersive media. Supporting professional production and hands-on training.

Student showcases, festivals, and collaborative performances. Enabling visibility and creative exchange.

National and global partnerships with creative, academic, and industry bodies. Expanding access to global networks and opportunities.

Operational Plan

Annual creative festivals, film labs, and music showcases

Visiting artist and creator-in-residence programmes

Interdisciplinary project studios blending arts, science, and technology

Short-term certificate programmes and professional workshops

Student fellowships, internships, and collaborative industry projects

Partnerships and Alliances

Creative industry associations (film, music, design, media)

Production houses, studios, OTT platforms, and cultural organizations

National and global universities with strong creative-arts programs

Technology partners in XR, AI, sound engineering, animation, and interactive media

Outcomes and Impact

  • Creators who combine India’s cultural richness with global creative competencies. Positioned to produce work that is rooted in heritage while meeting international creative standards.
  • A dynamic ecosystem for interdisciplinary arts innovation. Where diverse disciplines intersect to enable experimentation, collaboration, and new forms of expression.
  • Strengthened industry linkages and employability pathways. Through sustained engagement with creative industries, professional networks, and real-world projects.
  • Increased representation of Indian creative talent on global platforms Enabling wider visibility, recognition, and participation in international creative forums.
  • Enhanced cultural entrepreneurship contributing to India’s creative economy. Supporting sustainable ventures, innovation, and long-term economic and cultural impact.
