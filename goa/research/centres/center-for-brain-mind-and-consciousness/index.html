Centre for Brain, Mind and Consciousness

Explore the Center for Brain, Mind and Consciousness at WPU Goa, advancing research in cognition, human awareness, and interdisciplinary neuroscience.

Role context
People listed under Advisory network are external advisors to the institute or centre. They are not represented as resident WPU Goa researchers or faculty.

Prof. Dr. Vishwanath Karad

Rationale

  • Pursuit of academic research and study of Brain, Mind and Consciousness at a graduate and post graduate level
  • Run a “Self-Actualisation Programme” for all students to support holistic emotional, spiritual, and psychological development alongside academic excellence.

Objectives

Aims and Objectives

  • To conduct graduate degree programmes for undergraduate and postgraduate students in Brain, Mind and Consciousness studies
  • To provide instructional programmes for 2 credits per academic year, mandatory for all students under the 'Self Actualization Programme'

Activities

  • Research
  • Training of Staff and Students
  • Mentoring students
  • Counselling

Mandate

  • Deliver education rooted in the rich traditions and legacy of Indian culture, with equal emphasis on values, discipline, compassion, tolerance, and holistic personality development alongside academic excellence.
  • Provide a platform for research in the domains of mind, consciousness, and neuroscience, informed by insights from Indian Knowledge Systems.

Partnerships and Alliances

  • Collaborate with industry and corporate partners to deliver workshops and seminars on team building and soft skills as part of organisational HR initiatives.
  • Maintain liaison with government programmes and policy bodies to promote holistic student development across all levels.

Operational Plan

  • Organise guest lectures by eminent researchers and scholars in the domains of consciousness studies and cognitive science.
  • Conduct annual conferences on themes related to cognitive science, mind, and consciousness.
  • Offer fellowships and internships for international university students.
  • Provide scholarships to deserving students to pursue research at leading higher education institutions.

Outcomes and Impact

  • The centre aims to develop well-rounded individuals and responsible citizens who will serve as ambassadors of the institution, while also becoming specialists in their chosen fields of study.
  • The centre also seeks to provide the perfect platform that fosters the integration of science and spirituality, in its endeavour to pursue research in the largely unexplored domains of consciousness and the chemistry of the mind.
