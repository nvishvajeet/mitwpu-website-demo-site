Centre of Excellence

Centre of excellence for Government Leadership And Public Policy

Discover the Center for Government Leadership and Public Policy at WPU Goa, shaping future leaders through policy research, governance, and public impact initiatives.

Role context
People listed under Advisory network are external advisors to the institute or centre. They are not represented as resident WPU Goa researchers or faculty.

The T. N. Seshan Centre of Excellence at WPU GŌA is envisioned as a national platform to nurture ethical, capable, and future-ready public leaders. Rooted in Shri T. N. Seshan’s legacy of administrative courage and constitutional integrity, it builds on nearly two decades of MIT-SOG’s leadership and governance work. Responding to India’s rapidly evolving governance landscape, the Centre will offer world-class leadership development, evidence-based policymaking, and public-sector innovation. Located at WPU GŌA, it integrates governance studies with civilizational wisdom, modern pedagogy, and experiential learning.

Shri T. N. Seshan

Key Pillars and Programme Areas

Leadership for a Developed Bharat@2047

Training programmes in public leadership, administrative excellence, statesmanship, and strategic governance to prepare leaders for India’s transition into a developed nation by the year 2047.

Civilizational Wisdom and Spiritual Core in Public Leadership

Curriculum integrating inner leadership ethics, self-awareness, emotional balance, and value-based decision making rooted in Bharat’s civilizational thought.

Youth, Citizenship and Democratic Participation

Initiatives to nurture informed, responsible, and participatory young citizens equipped to engage with democratic processes.

Global Partnerships and India’s Soft Power

Collaborations that strengthen India’s global governance discourse, enabling cross-cultural learning and international policy exposure.

Bharat’s Development Pathways

Research and academic pathways focusing on India’s socio-economic transitions, institutional reforms, federal dynamics, geopolitics, and sustainability challenges.

Activities

Leadership Development and Training Programmes

  • Certificate and diploma courses for emerging leaders, civil society actors, and young political workers.
  • Executive programmes for government officials and Panchayati Raj representatives
  • Workshops on crisis management, ethical leadership, policy planning, negotiation, and communication.

Innovation, Technology and Public Policy Lab

  • Governance and Data Lab for policy simulation, digital governance, behavioural insights
  • AI-enabled tools for electoral analysis, service delivery, and administrative decision-making

Ethics and Public Leadership Retreats

  • Residential retreats with meditation, discourses on dharma and leadership, and value-based governance dialogues.

National Development Case Competitions

  • Student solutions to real challenges: climate resilience, urban mobility, health governance, education reform.

Partnership and Collaboration Unit

  • Dedicated cell for forging national and international institutional collaborations.

Democratic Education and Public Outreach

  • Annual T. N. Seshan Lecture Series on Democracy and Governance
  • Programmes with Election Commission, NGOs, and global democracy bodies

Academic Integration

The Centre will integrate its learning philosophy across university programmes through Below Programmes where Students from political science, public policy, management, law, media, AI, data science, economics, social sciences, and peace studies will collaborate on governance challenges guided by an interdisciplinary framework.

Outcomes and Impact

  • A nationally recognized hub for leadership education and governance reform
  • Strengthened linkages between academia, government, and civil society
  • High-impact governance innovation pilots in Goa and beyond
