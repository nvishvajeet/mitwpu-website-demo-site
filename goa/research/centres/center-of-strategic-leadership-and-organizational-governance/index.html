Centre of Strategic Leadership and Organizational Governance

Explore the Center of Transdisciplinary Research and Education at WPU Goa, integrating diverse disciplines to solve complex global challenges through innovation.

Role context
Names and roles remain in their institute, centre or advisory context. Membership is not treated as a faculty appointment.

Organisations across the world face challenges arising from rapid market shifts, technology transitions, and rising expectations of governance. Many strategic plans do not translate into outcomes because they lack execution discipline, leadership depth, and systems that ensure accountability. Prof. Charan’s work shows that high performing institutions integrate strategy with people and operations. This centre responds to the need for leadership maturity and governance capability by embedding these principles and preparing individuals to address real organisational demands with clarity and responsibility.

Prof. Ram Charan

Key Pillars and Programme Areas

Training Programmes

Training programmes in public leadership, administrative excellence, statesmanship, and strategic governance to prepare leaders for India’s transition into a developed nation by the year 2047.

Leadership and Inner leadership

A curriculum integrating inner leadership, including ethics, self-awareness, emotional balance, and value-based decision-making, rooted in Bharat’s civilisational thought.

Youth Engagement

Initiatives to nurture informed, responsible, and participatory young citizens equipped to engage with democratic processes.

Collaborations

Collaborations that strengthen India’s global governance discourse, enabling cross-cultural learning and international policy exposure.

Research Pathways

Research and academic pathways focusing on India’s socio-economic transitions, institutional reforms, federal dynamics, geopolitics, and sustainability challenges.

Activities

  • Leadership academies, strategic boot camps, and governance workshops.
  • Boardroom simulation labs and real-time organisational problem-solving modules.
  • Research publications, case studies, policy briefs, and industry white papers.
  • Strategic consulting projects, internships, and mentorship programmes.
  • National and international leadership dialogues, master classes, and annual conferences.
  • Collaborations with corporations, consulting firms, public institutions, and global universities

Operational Plan

Distinguished lecture series featuring global CEOs and governance experts.

Annual Strategic Leadership Conclave and policy roundtables.

Summer and winter schools on strategy, execution, and governance.

White papers, theme papers, and leadership research reports.

Executive fellowships, visiting scholars, and practitioner-in-residence programmes.

Study circles and leadership labs embedded in academic curricula.​

Partnerships and Alliances

Industry

Leading corporates, family businesses, governance bodies, and consulting organisations.

Academia

Global business schools, leadership institutes, strategy centres, and think tanks.

Government

Public administration bodies, regulatory agencies, and policy organisations.

Civil Society

Social enterprises, non-profits, and community leadership ecosystems.

Outcomes and Impact

  • A new generation of leaders capable of strategic clarity, ethical judgement, and execution excellence.
  • Strengthened governance maturity across public, private, and social institutions.
  • High-impact collaborations with industry and global leadership bodies.
  • Publication of influential research, case studies, and policy documents.
  • Enhanced visibility of WPU GŌA as a national centre for leadership excellence.
  • A robust talent pipeline for organisations looking for future-ready leader.

Members

Prof. Shaphali Gupta

Professor, IIM Kozhikode Prof. Shaphali Gupta is a Professor of Marketing Management at the Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode (IIMK). She holds a PhD from the Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management, IIT Bombay, a Master's in Business Management with Marketing specialisation, and a Bachelor's in Mathematics, and is a gold medallist certified trainer by the Indian Society for Training and Development, Ministry of HRD. She serves as Senior Associate Editor of the Marketing Strategy Journal by Elsevier, and as Associate Editor of the European Journal of Marketing, Journal of Business Research, and Journal of Strategic Marketing. She has been a Research Fellow at Georgia State University's J. Mack Robinson College of Business for three years, and has been invited as a visiting professor at IPAG, Paris. Her research interests span sustainability, new-age technologies, innovation for greater good, and stakeholder engagement.

Kavita Dasan

Senior Vice-President, Talent Management & Mfg. HR Head, Suzlon Group Kavita Dasan is Senior Vice President – Talent Management & Manufacturing HR Head at Suzlon Group, India's leading renewable energy solutions company. A seasoned HR professional with over 31 years of experience, she specialises in result-oriented business leadership across all facets of talent management, including large-scale ramp-ups, talent retention during mergers and acquisitions, change management, performance-driven culture building, and career progression of the millennial workforce. Prior to Suzlon, she served as Chief People Officer at ABP Network for over four years, and earlier held Vice President – HR roles at DHFL and Zee Entertainment Enterprises. An alumna of Delhi University, she specialised in Human Resources Management from IIM Ahmedabad and is also a certified Grow More Coach.

Prof. Ronald Berenbeim

Adjunct Professor, The Stern School of Business Administration, NYU Senior Fellow, The Conference Board Prof. Ronald Berenbeim is an Adjunct Professor at the NYU Stern School of Business Administration, where he has taught Professional Responsibility: Markets, Ethics and Law since 1995, and is also a Senior Fellow at The Conference Board. A graduate of Cornell University, Balliol College Oxford (Keasbey Scholarship), and Harvard Law School, he co-authored Fighting Corruption in East Asia: Solutions from the Private Sector (The World Bank, 2003) following a World Bank study he directed on private sector anticorruption practices. He is a member of the United Nations Global Compact Anticorruption Working Group and Transparency International's Steering Committee on Business Principles for Resisting Corrupt Practices, and received a Fulbright grant in 2010 to teach business ethics at the University of Cergy-Pontoise, France. He is also a recognised Top 100 Thought Leader in Trustworthy Business Behavior.
