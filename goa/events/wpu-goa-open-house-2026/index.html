  • 20 June 2026

The WPU GŌA Open House , held on 20 June 2026, marked an important milestone in the university’s outreach efforts, engaging prospective students, parents, educators, and academic counsellors across more than 20 cities. The event served as a platform to introduce the vision, academic philosophy, and distinctive learning environment of WPU GŌA while addressing queries related to higher education and career opportunities.

The sessions highlighted the university’s student-centric approach, immersive learning ecosystem, industry engagement, global exposure opportunities, and emphasis on holistic development. The university’s senior leadership interacted with attendees, providing insights into WPU GŌA’s academic framework and the wide range of opportunities available to students.

Participants also received detailed information about the university’s undergraduate programmes, including B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering, Bachelor of Management Studies (Hons.), B.Des. in Integrated Product Design, B.Des. in Communication Design, and B.Sc. (Hons.) in Psychology. The interactive format encouraged meaningful discussions between the senior leadership, students, and parents, enabling them to better understand the university’s offerings and campus experience.

The successful completion of the multi-city Open House reflects WPU GŌA’s commitment to engaging with aspiring learners and their families while providing a comprehensive overview of its academic ecosystem and educational opportunities.
