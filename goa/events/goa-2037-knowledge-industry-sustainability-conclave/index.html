Overview:

The Goa 2037: Knowledge, Industry & Sustainability Conclave is a landmark initiative that brings together leaders from industry, academia, government, and society to shape a progressive and sustainable future for Goa.

Organised by the Goa Chamber of Commerce & Industry (GCCI) in collaboration with MIT World Peace University, Goa , the conclave aims to foster meaningful dialogue, encourage collaboration, and create actionable pathways for growth that balance economic development with ecological responsibility.

Key Highlight

  • Gomant Gaurav Sanman

A special highlight of the conclave is the prestigious Gomant Gaurav Sanman , honouring Prof. Dr. Raghunath Mashelkar for his exceptional contributions to science, innovation, and nation-building.

The award will be presented in the august presence of Shri. Pramod Sawant , Hon’ble Chief Minister of Goa.

Conclave Themes

Reimagining Goa’s Future: Industrial Growth with Ecological Balance

Exploring how Goa can achieve sustainable industrial growth while preserving its ecological heritage through responsible policies, innovation, and collaboration.

Goa as a Global Knowledge Hub: Transdisciplinary Education & Industry-Academia Synergy

Focusing on the role of education, research, and industry partnerships in positioning Goa as a global centre for knowledge and innovation.
