Goa 2037 Knowledge Industry Sustainability Conclave Event

Explore key takeaways and discussions from a landmark gathering focused on sustainable growth and knowledge industries.

Role context
This record documents a public event, conference or speaker appearance. Participation does not establish employment at WPU Goa.

The Goa 2037: Knowledge, Industry & Sustainability Conclave was conceptualised as a strategic platform to align Goa’s long-term development vision with the national goal of *Viksit Bharat@2047.

The conclave brought together policymakers, industry leaders, academicians, and thought leaders to deliberate on building a future-ready Goa focusing on sustainable industrial growth, innovation-driven development, and strengthening industry-academia collaboration.

The session focused on balancing industrial growth with environmental sustainability in Goa’s limited land context. Panellists emphasized the need for adopting the “more from less for more” philosophy maximising output while minimising resource consumption.

Key discussions revolved around promoting green industries, encouraging R&D-led manufacturing, and leveraging vertical expansion over horizontal sprawl. The panel also highlighted the importance of measurable sustainability goals and policy frameworks that enable responsible industrialisation while preserving Goa’s ecological identity.

This session explored how Goa can emerge as a global knowledge hub through stronger collaboration between academia and industry. Discussions highlighted the urgent need for curriculum reform, skill-based education, and experiential learning models that align with industry requirements.

Speakers stressed leveraging Goa’s unique strengths in tourism, research, and integrative health, while building a future-ready workforce. The importance of transdisciplinary education and innovation ecosystems was underscored as key drivers for global competitiveness.

Overview:

The highlight of the conclave was the conferment of the prestigious ‘Gomant Gaurav Sanman’ upon Dr. Raghunath Mashelkar at a grand ceremony held at Kala Academy, Goa. The award was presented by Hon’ble Chief Minister Dr. Pramod Sawant, who also announced the establishment of WPU Goa University, marking a significant milestone in the state’s educational landscape.

In his address, the Chief Minister highlighted Goa’s commitment to innovation through a strong government-industry-academia partnership. Mr. Mauvin Godinho acknowledged Dr. Mashelkar’s enduring contributions to science and society, calling it a proud moment for Goa.

In his acceptance speech, Dr. Mashelkar delivered an emotional tribute to his mother, reflecting on his humble beginnings and reaffirming his lifelong commitment to using science and innovation for the benefit of the poorest sections of society. The segment served as a powerful reminder of the impact of vision, perseverance, and purpose-driven leadership.
