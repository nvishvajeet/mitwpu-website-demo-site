The WPU GŌA symbol is rooted in a simple belief: education must serve humanity. The circle represents unity, balance, and continuity of learning. Inspired by the Ensō, it reflects mindfulness, wholeness, and the idea of becoming—never finished, always evolving. Blue stands for trust and global perspective.

Maroon reflects courage, leadership, and purposeful action. Together, they form the Circle of Peace: A symbol that reflects what we believe, how we learn, and who we aim to become.
