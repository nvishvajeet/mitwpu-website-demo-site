At WPU GŌA, MoUs and strategic partnerships are built to take transdisciplinary learning beyond the classroom, beyond a single discipline and beyond traditional modes of education. These collaborations bring together academic, industry, research and institutional partners to generate new opportunities for hands-on learning, innovation, mentoring, internships and real-world problem-solving. The purpose is to help students see the links between ideas from different subjects, learn from a wide range of experts and use knowledge in ways that make a difference. With each partnership, WPU GŌA aims to develop confident learners who are ready for the future, able to think for themselves, work together and create solutions that have a real impact on the world.

WPU GŌA Signs MoU with Makers Asylum Foundation to Advance Innovation and Maker-Led Learning

WPU GŌA has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Makers Asylum Foundation that opens new avenues of learning in the fields of innovation, entrepreneurship, design and maker-led education.
