Check mandatory documents for WPU Goa admissions including academic records, ID proof, photos, and certificates for smooth application processing.

  • Original Documents required for Physical Verification
  • Copy of the Class 10 marksheet issued by the respective board
  • Copy of Class 12/10+2 marksheet, issued by the respective board
  • Entrance Test Score card (as applicable). B.Des candidates are also required to submit their portfolios
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Recent passport-size photograph (as per specifications on portal)
  • Candidate signature scan

Supporting Documents will add value:

  • Updated resume/curriculum vitae
  • Certificates of extracurricular activities, achievements, awards, or recognitions earned during the last four academic years
  • Certificates of work experience, internships, or industry engagements
  • Letters of recommendation from academic faculty or professional mentors

Important Instructions:

  • All documents must be uploaded in the prescribed format on the portal
  • Scanned copies must be clear and legible
  • Original documents will be required for verification at the time of admission
  • Forms with missing mandatory documents will not be processed
  • Candidates are advised to retain copies of all submitted documents for future reference
  • In case the Class 12 /10+2 marksheet or the Entrance Test Score is not available during the time of registration, the candidate has the facility to upload the relevant document at a later date.
  • Our Admission Officers are available to assist you while filling the Registration Form
