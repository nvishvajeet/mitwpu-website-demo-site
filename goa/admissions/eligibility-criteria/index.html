At WPU GŌA, academic eligibility varies by programme, based on your academic scores and entrance exam performance.

Programme-wise Eligibility Criteria and Entrance Exam Requirements

B.Tech.

  • Class 12 / 10+2:
  • 1. Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics (PCM), OR
  • 2. Physics & Mathematics along with a Technical or Vocational subject

OR

  • Diploma:

Minimum 60% aggregate in a Diploma in Engineering & Technology (relevant branch) from a government-approved board.

B.Des.*

Minimum 50% aggregate in Class 12/10+2 in any stream with English as a compulsory subject

B.Sc. (Hons.)

Entrance Examination: WPU GŌA Entrance Examination

Bachelor of Management Studies (Hons.)

Academic Requirement: Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 12 / 10+2 (or equivalent) from any recognised board (CBSE, ISC, State Boards, IB, Cambridge, etc).

Academic Compulsory Subject: English must be one of the subjects.

Stream: Open to students from any stream (Science, Commerce, Arts, or Vocational).

Minimum Aggregate for Reserved Categories: 45%
