Scholarships for Indian Students

Award Theme : Premier institutional scholarship

Eligibility / Selection Basis :

Exceptional all-round performance in academics, co/extra-curricular activities, leadership, interview, and other significant accolades awarded as the university’s highest scholarship honour.

Tuition Fee Waiver (Up To) : 100%

Award Theme : Outstanding performance in an India-wide standardized entrance exam

Top performers in JEE / CUET / SAT / UCEED / NID DAT / equivalent standardized entrance exam, benchmarked by programme.

Tuition Fee Waiver (Up To) : 50%

Award Theme : Outstanding and consistent performance in academics

Eligibility / Selection Basis : Candidates with a strong and consistent prior academic record, including subject strength aligned to the chosen programme.

Tuition Fee Waiver (Up To) : 75%

Award Theme : Diversity, access, and inclusion to high-quality academia

Eligibility / Selection Basis : Candidates adding meaningful socio-economic, geographic, cultural, or first-generation representation to the cohort.

Tuition Fee Waiver (Up To) : 35%

5. Rising Star Scholarship

Award Theme : Outstanding performance by a female applicant

Eligibility / Selection Basis : Highest-ranking female applicant(s) by holistic admission score, leadership record, and interview strength.

Award Theme : Outstanding performance in one extra-curricular activity

Eligibility / Selection Basis : State, national, or international level extra-curricular achievement with verified certificates and potential to represent the university in the future.

Award Theme : Goa Domicile

Eligibility / Selection Basis : Students domiciled in Goa will be offered scholarship in the form of a 15% tuition fee waiver

Tuition Fee Waiver (Up To) : 15%
