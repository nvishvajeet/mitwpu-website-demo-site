Admission Process Flow

  • Step 1: Create an Online Account on the website
  • Step 2: Fill the Registration Form
  • Step 3: Appear for the Entrance Exam (As applicable)
  • Step 4: Appear for the Personal Interaction
  • Step 5: Check the Merit List (Dates/Results will be notified on the student dashboard)
  • Step 6: Receive Provisional Offer Letter upon Selection (Will be received on the registered email ID)
  • Step 7: Complete the Fee Payment (As per fee payment schedule)
  • Step 8: Verification of the Payment and Documents by the Admissions Officer
  • Step 9: Receive Confirmation of verification with the student's Unique Identification Number (Will be received on the registered email ID)
  • Step 10: Submit Original Documents (Original documents required for physical verification)
  • Step 11: Welcome to WPU GŌA
