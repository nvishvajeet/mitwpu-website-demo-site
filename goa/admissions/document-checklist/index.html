Documents Required for the Admission Process & Verification

Mandatory Documents for All Candidates

  • Class 10 Marksheet (issued by the respective board)
  • Class 12 / 10+2 Marksheet (issued by the respective board)
  • Entrance Test Scorecard (as applicable)
  • B.Des candidates must also submit a portfolio
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph (as per portal specifications)
  • Scanned Candidate Signature
  • Leaving / Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)

Supporting Documents (If Any)

  • Updated Resume / Curriculum Vitae
  • Certificates of Extracurricular Activities (achievements, awards, or recognitions from the last four academic years)
  • Certificates of Work Experience, Internships, or Industry Engagements
  • Letters of Recommendation(from academic faculty or professional mentors)

Additional Documents (If Applicable)

  • Indian Nationality Certificate/ Birth Certificate/ Photocopy of Passport.
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (issued by Competent Authority).
  • Any other document as prescribed by the admission team.
