What Do We Seek in Our Students?

The WPU GŌA admissions committee looks beyond academic performance in standardised tests and the board exams and seeks candidates who demonstrate strong character, meaningful experiences, and clear future motivation. Our admission process is designed to identify well-rounded, high-potential students who can actively contribute to and enrich the learning environment at WPU GŌA.
