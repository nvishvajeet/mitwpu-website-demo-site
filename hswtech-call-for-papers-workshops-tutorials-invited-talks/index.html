Technically Sponsored by IEEE Communications Society Pune Chapter

Over the last three decades, ICT technologies have revolutionized the lives, society and businesses through its innovative applications. Some of the biggest drivers for this transformation are advances in internet, communication technologies, mobile applications, artificial intelligence, data science, computing paradigms such as cloud, fog and edge, innovative electronics and chip designs, automation, advances in renewable and new energy sources, electric vehicles and several others. These innovative progressions have paved the way for a knowledge-driven economy and extended opportunities to bottom of the pyramid.

Along with the opportunities and avenues, these paradigm shifts have posed many challenges to humanity such as energy, environment, water, food, population, conflicts, security, societal changes, information explosion, morals, ethics and others. The 2025 IEEE International Conference on Sustainable Technologies for Humanity and Smart World ( HSW Tech-2025 ) aims to explore and advance the transformative potential of technology for creating a sustainable and intelligent world to benefit humanity. The conference is technically sponsored by IEEE Communications Society Pune Chapter (Conference Code: 64936).

This transdisciplinary conference will provide a platform for researchers, scientists, professionals from academia and Industry to share and discuss the advancements and address challenges in communication and networks, privacy and security, semiconductors, artificial intelligence and computing, automation and UxVs, green energy and sustainability, humanity, healthcare, hardware/software platforms and related areas. The conference will feature plenary and keynote speeches, interactive sessions, workshops, tutorials, exhibition, oral and poster paper presentations. We invite you to join us at HSW Tech-2025 and contribute in tackling the challenges of the current and next generations and shape the future where humanity, peace and technology coexist harmoniously for a sustainable and smart world.

1. Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical research papers for presentation at the conference and publication in conference proceedings.

2. Papers submitted to HSW Tech-2025 must not have been published, submitted, or accepted for publication elsewhere. Papers should be submitted electronically via Conference Management System. (https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/HSWTech2025)

3. Manuscripts should be prepared in the A4 paper size and two-column conference format.( https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html ).

4. All the settings such as margins, font size/styles should be strictly followed as per IEEE International Conference paper template guidelines.

5. Papers are to be submitted in both, MS Word and PDF formats. In MS word format, names, affiliations and contact details of the authors should not be included whereas in PDF format, these details are to be printed as per the template.

6. The submissions should not exceed six pages (10-point font), including all figures, tables and references.

7. The manuscripts not following the template guidelines may be rejected without review. The submissions received after the due date, with low resolution figures, equations not properly typed (Use of MS equation editor is recommended), exceeding the length limit, or not appropriately structured may also not be considered.

8. To ensure high quality, all submissions are double blind peer-reviewed by at least two reviewers.

9. All submitted papers will be checked for plagiarism through the IEEE CrossCheck system as per IEEE policy ( Plagiarism Information Center ). If a paper results with a high similarity (including self-plagiarism), it may be rejected at any stage.

10. The accepted papers must be presented by one of the concerned authors who must register for the conference and pay the fees.

11. Virtual presentations of the accepted papers may be allowed at the discretion of Conference organisers, if in-person attendance is not possible for authors.

12. Accepted and presented papers will be published in the HSW Tech-2025 Conference Proceedings and will also be submitted to IEEE Xplore for inclusion.

Proposals are invited from groups or individuals interested to host workshops and tutorials on conference themes and relevant fields during the pre/conference period. The purpose of workshops and tutorials is to provide an opportunity for participants from academia, industry, government and other related parties to present and discuss novel ideas on current and emerging topics relevant to the conference theme. We encourage workshops that bring together people working on new and developing research topics that favour highly interactive discussions. Tutorial proposals are encouraged that go in-depth into topics of interest to the fields of conference.

Please submit a proposal in MS Word format (2 to 4 pages) in the following format at hswtech@mitwpu.edu.in.

  • Title and Acronym of Activity
  • Requested Duration (in Hrs)
  • Abstract (Up to 200 words, to be used for the website)
  • Description of the Workshop/Tutorial Content
  • Name, Affiliation, Homepage, Short Biography and Contact Information of Experts
  • Proposed Plan of the Workshop/Tutorial (Approx. timeline and type of activities)
  • Intended Audience and Expected Number of Participants
  • How the funding would be acquired from external sources for conducting workshop/tutorial
  • Any Other Information
