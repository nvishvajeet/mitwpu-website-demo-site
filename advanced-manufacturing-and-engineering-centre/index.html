MIT-WPU Advanced Manufacturing and Engineering Centre, is a collaborative effort with Tata Technologies, highlights the institution's commitment to development of cutting-edge technology, holistic, hands-on education and industry integration. The Memorandum of Understanding solidifies this collaboration, showcasing the dedication to fostering innovation. The centre boasts advanced computer-controlled industrial machines, highly precise tools, and process simulation capabilities, Aiming to stay technologically ahead and at the forefront of creativity and innovation. With an emphasis on digital manufacturing and a stride toward Industry 4.0, the centre prepares students for the evolving landscape of manufacturing and engineering. The facility's commitment to well-trained professional technicians and faculty underlines the dedication to providing a comprehensive education that blends theoretical knowledge with practical expertise.
