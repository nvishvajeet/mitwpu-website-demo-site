Study Abroad

MIT-WPU offers study abroad experiences with its international programmes to all the students willing to study abroad. MIT-WPU’s International Collaboration Department facilitates multi-dimensional partnerships with leading universities across the world for greater receptivity to global education.

MIT-WPU’s International Collaboration Department facilitates multi-dimensional partnerships with leading universities across the world for greater receptivity to global education.

International education and global accessibility to learning offers a greater career mobility and a wider horizon of professional opportunities. The world-class pedagogy behind our international programmes focuses on learning beyond borders through the cross-pollination of international disciplinary approaches. MIT-WPU continues to cultivate, enhance, and sustain global relationships and expand inter-cultural networks for its students.

ICD – International Collaboration Department

MIT World Peace University believes in “Quality education and a successful career is the fundamental right of every student”.

International Collaboration Department (ICD), a dedicated centre at MIT-WPU makes collaboration possible by creating a global network of educational institutions. The key objective of ICD is to create a wide range of opportunities for the students and faculties to gain knowledge and awareness of various cultures across the globe, get global opportunities in the form of student & faculty exchange programmes, internships for students, twinning & exchange programmes. ICD also strives to facilitate the initiatives that offer greater academic and life experiences for the international students at MIT World Peace University.

Happenings

International Immersions

International Affairs organizes regular international immersion programmes for students fostering opportunities to broaden learning and deepen knowledge within the global learning ecosystems. These immersions involve visits to world-class Universities & Institutions to provide engaging, innovative, and enlightening experiences to the students.

Events

ICD (International Collaboration Department) participates in and organizes multiple events throughout the year to both acknowledge the achievements of our students and stay abreast of new trends and opportunities of global learning

MOU's

International Affairs builds partnerships with leading universities and institutions across the globe to open pathways of global learning for our students through Summer Exchange, Immersions, among many other study-abroad opportunities.

Embassy Visits

As part of building and strengthening the aspects of global knowledge exchange and cross-border learning opportunities for young students, International Affairs works together with Ambassadors and Emissaries of the world nations in India by way of Embassy visits.
