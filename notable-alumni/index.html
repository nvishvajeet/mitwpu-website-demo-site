Notable Alumni

Meet the notable alumni of MIT-WPU who are making an impact globally. Discover their inspiring journeys and achievements across diverse fields

  • MIT-WPU Alumni Network: A Lifelong Community

Notable alumni of MIT-WPU are distinguished individuals who have excelled in their fields after completing their education, reflecting the institution's commitment toward high-quality education and opportunities. With over 100,000 alumni spread across the world, many hold prominent positions in iconic organisations—from international Fortune 500 global giants to Bharat’s top public sector, bureaucratic, defence, and intelligence units. These alumni, including influential figures in business, politics, science, arts, and entertainment, enhance the university's reputation and inspire current and future students through groundbreaking research, entrepreneurial innovation, creative accomplishments, and significant societal contributions.
