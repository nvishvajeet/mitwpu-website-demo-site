The Young Democrats Club is an inspiring initiative led by students of BA (Hons) Political Science. It embodies the essence of democracy and serves as a platform for students to engage in a wide range of activities related to political awareness and leadership. From guest lectures by influential policymakers and political icons to stimulating debates, elocution and insightful polls, we aim to enhance students' understanding of the political landscape. This club nurtures the potential for future political leaders.

The Young Democrats Club is committed to fostering an environment that encourages democratic participation and civic engagement. Our activities extend beyond the classroom, providing opportunities for students to interact with social workers and eminent personalities. We organise thought-provoking events and campaigns that delve deep into political scenarios and stimulate meaningful discussions among our members.
