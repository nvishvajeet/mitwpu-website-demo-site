World Peace Dome

At Vishwarajbaug in Loni Kalbhor, the World Peace Dome houses the Philosopher Saint Shri Dnyaneshwara World Peace Prayer Hall and World Peace Library. It was created under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Vishwanath D. Karad.

World Peace Dome directory

Dome, prayer hall, and library

The 160-foot dome covers a prayer hall for 3,000 people. Its interior brings together 54 bronze statues of saints, scholars, scientists, philosophers, and spiritual teachers.

Inaugurated in 2018

The prayer hall, World Peace Library, and World Parliament of Science, Religion and Philosophy were inaugurated on 2 October 2018 by Vice President M. Venkaiah Naidu.
