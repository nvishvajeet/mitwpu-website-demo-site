Cosmos is an exciting and vibrant student club dedicated to nurturing the curiosity and passion of amateur astronomers. The club opens the door to a fascinating journey into the cosmos, providing unique experiences and opportunities for its members. Core Pillars: Astrophotography, Observational Astronomy, Radio Astronomy

Cosmos is more than just a club; it's a community of like-minded individuals who share an unwavering passion for the cosmos and a commitment to scientific exploration. We are a beacon for those who seek to unravel the mysteries of the night sky and kindle a lifelong love for the boundless wonders of the universe. Join us at Cosmos and embark on a cosmic journey. Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or a beginner with a curious mind, there's a place for you in our club. Together, we explore, discover, and marvel at the awe-inspiring beauty of the universe.
