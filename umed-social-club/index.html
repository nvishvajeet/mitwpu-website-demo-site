UMED Social Club, established in 2022, is a dynamic addition to the MIT-WPU School of Commerce. Our club is dedicated to raising awareness about the social issues that impact our city and society at large. We have already initiated impactful activities, such as creating a documentary on the NIRMALAY collection drive during Ganpati Puja and organising activities at SINHAGAD. Moreover, we have reached out to orphanages and spent quality time with special children, with many more such activities in the pipeline.
