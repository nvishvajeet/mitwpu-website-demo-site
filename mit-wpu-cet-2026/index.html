MIT-WPU CET 2026

MIT-WPU CET 2026 is the entrance exam for UG and PG admissions at MIT World Peace University, Pune. Check eligibility, exam dates, syllabus, and apply online.

Reconstructed from the retained MIT-WPU public capture. View official source

Dr Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, Bharat offers multiple education programs across various streams. MIT-WPU CET 2026 is great opportunity for aspirant learners who wish to take admission at MIT-WPU.

The MIT-WPU CET consist of 150 marks which includes Online Computer Based Test (CBT) of 100 marks and/or Online Personal Interaction (PI) of 50 marks.

  • All the entrance examination test will be online mode, and applicant can appear the same from their home or convenient place comfortably.
  • The test can be attempted through a laptop or mobile.
  • The candidate should ensure that a good internet connectivity (minimum 1 MBPS) and an uninterrupted power supply is available during online examinations
