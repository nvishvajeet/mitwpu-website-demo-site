  • Blockchain COE

The Blockchain Centre of Excellence was established in MIT-WPU with the aim to enhance technological skills among students and faculty, and make them industry ready. The COE was established by pairing the industry experience and skills of Snapper Future Tech with CIAP MIT-WPU and the Department of Computer Engineering and technology.

The workshops, initiatives and events organized under the purview of the COE aim to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to navigate Blockchain technology. The COE operates as a collaborative space, and a platform for students, researchers, and industry professionals to conduct interdisciplinary research. It aims to explore the vast potential of blockchain across numerous sectors, including finance, healthcare, supply chain, governance, and more. The COE is focused on the broader agenda of improving the skills and competency of the students and preparing them to be the industry leaders of tomorrow.
