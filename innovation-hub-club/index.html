Established in 2018, Innovators Hub is dedicated to harnessing technology, research, and innovation to address everyday challenges. Our mission is to empower the students of MIT-WPU to cultivate their projects and ideas. Whether you're exploring software, or hardware-based projects, or engaged in research, our club is here to support you from the ground up. Innovation is at the heart of our endeavours. We aim to inspire, guide, and equip students with the skills necessary to unlock their potential and drive innovation in various spheres.

Innovators Hub is on a mission to foster a culture of innovation and research. We believe that by harnessing the power of technology and innovative thinking, we can address real-world challenges and make a meaningful impact.
