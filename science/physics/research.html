Research

The Department of Physics brings together teaching, laboratory learning, and research across fundamental, computational, optical, materials, and device physics.

01

Quantum, optics & photonics

Nonlinear and quantum optics, structured light, nanophotonics, photonic systems, plasmonics, and spectroscopy.

Photonics Research GroupQuantum Science & Technology Group
02

Materials, devices & nanoscience

Semiconductor and dielectric thin films, nanomaterials and nanocomposites, sensors, photodetectors, and functional devices.

03

Astrophysics & fundamental physics

Cosmic magnetic fields, galactic dynamics, high-energy theory, gravitation, and the mathematical structure of fundamental interactions.

04

Applied & computational physics

Soft matter, quantum chemistry and computational physics; radiation processing of polymers; liquid acoustics; dielectric spectroscopy; and corrosion science.
