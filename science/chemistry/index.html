Department

Teaching grounded in active scientific work.

The Department of Chemistry combines foundational chemical science and laboratory learning with faculty research in polymer science, synthesis and catalysis, functional and molecular materials, nanomaterials, environmental chemistry, and photonics.

Research

Areas of enquiry

Research overview →
01

Nanomaterials, molecular materials & photonics

Nanomaterial synthesis, plasmonics and nanophotonics, metal-organic frameworks, molecular nanomagnets, switches, and fluorescent sensors.

02

Organic synthesis, catalysis & green chemistry

Synthetic organic chemistry, ionic liquids, organocatalysis, heterogeneous catalysis, asymmetric synthesis, biocatalysis, and green synthesis.

03

Polymers, coatings & sustainable materials

Liquid-crystalline and conducting polymers, biopolymers, nanocomposites, protective coatings, packaging, microplastics, and water purification.

04

Inorganic & environmental chemistry

Metal complexes, wastewater treatment, pollutant degradation, and materials chemistry for environmental applications.
