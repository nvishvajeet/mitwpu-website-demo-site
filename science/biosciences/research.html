Research

The Department of Biosciences and Technology integrates molecular and cellular science, biotechnology, microbiology, bioinformatics, and translational research.

01

Cancer, cell & regenerative biology

Cancer cell biology, genome stability, stem cells, tissue engineering, bone biology, and protein aggregation.

02

Genomics, bioinformatics & microbiomes

Genomics, transcriptomics, metagenomics, microbial and human microbiomes, protein structure, and computational biology.

03

Microbiology, immunology & bioprocessing

Applied and environmental microbiology, immune profiling, microbial metabolites, enzyme technology, and bioprocess development.

04

Plant molecular biology & sustainable biotechnology

Plant development, defence and metabolism; plant–microbe interactions; genome editing; and crop improvement.

05

Biosensors, diagnostics & bioimaging

Electrochemical and optical biosensors, pathogen and molecular diagnostics, bioimaging, and preclinical workflows.
