Team DART SoMe is the official motorsports team of Vishwanath Karad's MIT-WPU, one of India's premier private engineering colleges. We are dedicated to participating in international and national championships, currently competing in Asia's largest Solar Car Rally event, ESVC3000. Comprised of passionate and ambitious engineers, we are committed to motorsports excellence and have started from the ground up to empower students, helping them discover and fuel their passion for engineering and motorsports.

Our journey began with a series of technical workshops, which aimed to empower students and ignite their interest in motorsports. One pivotal moment in our journey was the RCDC (Rally Car Design Challenge), where we managed to secure the 8th All India Rank and achieved 1st place in cost report presentation in 2019. This achievement only fueled our determination to aim higher.
