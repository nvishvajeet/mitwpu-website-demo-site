Aatman, as the official psychology club of MIT-WPU, is dedicated to promoting awareness about psychology and eliminating the stigma associated with mental health. With a thriving community of over 500 active members, AATMAN consistently advocates for the significance of mental well-being and psycho-education. This mission is actively pursued through various initiatives, including suicide prevention campaigns, informative seminars, and our impactful social media presence. Aatman is resolute in its commitment to fostering an open, inclusive space where individuals can learn, grow, and embrace the importance of psychological well-being.
