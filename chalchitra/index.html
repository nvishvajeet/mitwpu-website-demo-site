Chalchitra is a dynamic group of student clubs that passionately encourages the use of filmmaking as a powerful medium of expression. At Chalchitra, we firmly believe that film and media serve as potent tools for both communication and artistic creativity. Our platform provides students with a unique opportunity to delve into the intricacies of filmmaking. With the guidance of experienced faculty mentors, we empower students to embark on exciting journeys in creating amateur film projects.

Chalchitra is not just a club; it's a vibrant showcase of emerging talent. Throughout the year, we organise numerous events that feature the expressive filmmaking prowess of our young members. It's a union of Uday Film Club, Filmmakers Club, and Iptada Film Club, and together, we are dedicated to nurturing and celebrating the art of cinema.
