Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU, Pune, proudly celebrates the remarkable achievements of Mr. Jinesh Nanal, a fourth-year BTech Computer Science student, in inline freestyle skating. His outstanding accomplishments include:

  • 63rd National Championship 2025, Visakhapatnam Category – Classic Slalom (Senior Men Inline Freestyle Skating category) - Gold medal Category – Speed Slalom (Senior Men Inline Freestyle Skating category) - Silver medal
  • Asian Roller Skating Championship 2025 in Jecheon, Korea Category – Senior Men - 4th position
  • World Games 2025 in Chengdu, China Category – Senior Men’s Inline Freestyle Skating – 5th position
  • Shiv Chhatrapati Award 2025 Maharashtra’s highest honour for sports excellence, recognizing his dedication, discipline, and passion for skating.
  • 2025 Chuncheon World Inline Freestyle Cup, Korea Category – Senior Men’s Speed Slalom – Bronze Medal

Jinesh’s achievements not only highlight his sporting excellence but also demonstrate his ability to balance academics and athletics with determination and grit. His journey stands as a true inspiration for the MIT-WPU community, embodying the spirit of perseverance, passion, and all-round excellence.
