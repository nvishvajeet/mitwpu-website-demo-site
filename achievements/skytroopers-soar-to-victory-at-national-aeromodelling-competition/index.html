Skytroopers Soar to Victory at National Aeromodelling Competition

We are proud to announce that Team Skytroopers has soared to new heights by securing All India Rank (AIR) 1 at the National Aeromodelling Competition held at the IIT Bombay!

Achievement

We are beyond proud to announce that Team Skytroopers has soared to new heights by securing All India Rank (AIR) 1 at the National Aeromodelling Competition held at the prestigious IIT Bombay!

With their exceptional skills and innovative approach, the team : Secured First Place. Achieved a record-breaking feat by successfully carrying the maximum amount of payload!

This incredible accomplishment is a testament to their hard work, creativity, and technical expertise in aeromodelling. The team has not only showcased excellence in engineering but has also inspired others with their determination to push boundaries.

Their success reflects the spirit of innovation and teamwork that defines champions. We are immensely proud of their achievement and the recognition they've earned for themselves and our institution.
