Ms. Shreyasi Joshi

MIT-WPU celebrates the exceptional achievements of Ms. Shreyasi Joshi, a B.Tech CSE student, in slalom skating.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU, Pune, celebrates the exceptional achievements of Ms. Shreyasi Joshi, a B.Tech CSE student, in slalom skating. Her remarkable achievements include:

Inline Freestyle World Championships 2025 Category - Senior Women - 5th Place

World Games 2025 in Chengdu, China Category - Senior Women’s Inline Freestyle Skating - 6th Place

Asian Roller Skating Championship 2025 in Jecheon, Korea. Category - Classic Slalom - Gold Medal With This achievement Shreyasi has made history as the first Indian to win gold at South Korea’s Asian Roller Skating Championships in 2025

2025 Chuncheon World Inline Freestyle Cup, Korea Category - Senior Women, Classic Slalom - 1st Position

Lucas Slalom World Cup in A Coruña, Spain Category- Pair Slalom -2nd place Category-Speed Slalom-2nd place

The Czech Freestyle World Cup in Olomouc, Czech Republic Category-Speed Slalom - 1st Position Category-Battle Slalom - 2nd Position Category-Classic Slalom - 3rd Position

Shreyasi's dedication to both sports and academics serves as an inspiration to her peers, exemplifying how passion and perseverance can lead to outstanding success on all fronts.
