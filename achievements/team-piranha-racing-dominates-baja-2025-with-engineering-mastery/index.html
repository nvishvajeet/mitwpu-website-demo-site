In an impressive showcase of engineering prowess, Team Piranha Racing from the Department of Mechanical Engineering has surged to remarkable achievements at the esteemed BAJA SAEINDIA 2025 Physical Round in Pithampur, Indore. Achieving an impressive All India Rank 3, our skilled team has not merely taken part – they have excelled in the competition!

A Tradition of Distinction Emerges

The figures tell a clear tale, yet the narrative they reveal is remarkable. Team Piranha Racing secured the top position across several categories,

  • Virtual Dynamics AIR 1
  • Acceleration AIR 1
  • Suspension and Traction AIR 1
  • Design Finals AIR 1
  • CAE Finals AIR 2

Their accuracy and skill were even more evident with AIR 2 in Overall Physical Dynamics and Maneuverability while achieving AIR 6 in other difficult competitions.

More Than the Medals

However, this goes beyond mere rankings. It's about endless hours in the studio, restless nights of computations, and the unwavering quest for excellence. Team Piranha Racing has shown that when enthusiasm combines with accuracy, remarkable outcomes ensue.
