On behalf of MIT-WPU, we extend our heartiest congratulations to Malhar Sameer Atre, a TY BBA student and our national champion swimmer, for his exceptional performance and remarkable achievements!

Malhar's dominance at the VSM State-Level Tournament, where he secured 4 Gold and 3 Silver medals in swimming, along with a Gold in Water Polo, and the Overall Individual Championship & Best Player Award, is truly inspiring. His winning streak continued at Flame University, where he added 2 Gold and 1 Bronze medal in swimming to his impressive record.

These outstanding achievements are a testament to Malhar's dedication, hard work, and talent. We are incredibly proud of his accomplishments and the pride he brings to MIT-WPU.

Malhar has expressed his sincere gratitude to our esteemed founder, Respected Dr. Vishwanath Karad Sir, Executive President Rahul Karad Sir, Pro VC Dr. Milind Pande Sir, and the entire MIT-WPU faculty for their unwavering support. We are delighted to have played a part in his journey and wish him continued success in all his future endeavours.
