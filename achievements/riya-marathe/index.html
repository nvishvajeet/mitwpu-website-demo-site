MIT-WPU proudly congratulates Riya Marathe, a BBA LLB - II student from the School of Law, on her outstanding achievements in chess. Riya secured a gold medal in the individual category and another gold as part of the winning team at the state-level VSM tournament. Her exceptional performance reflects her dedication, strategic thinking, and competitive spirit. MIT-WPU celebrates Riya's success and applauds her commitment to excellence in both academics and sports. She is an inspiration to her peers and a source of pride for the university.
