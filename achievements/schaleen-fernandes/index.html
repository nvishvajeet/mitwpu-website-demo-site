Schaleen Fernandes

MIT-WPU extends its warmest congratulations to Ms. Schaleen Fernandes, FY BBA LLB student, for her outstanding performance at the All India Inter-University Roller Skate Championship 2025, held at SAM Global University.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU extends its warmest congratulations to Ms. Schaleen Fernandes, a FY BBA LLB student, for her outstanding performance in skating. Schaleen's accomplishments include:

20th National Ice Skating Championship 2025 in Dehradun

  • Gold medal in the Senior Women category
  • 2 Silver medals in the Senior Women category

All India Inter-University Roller Skate Championship 2025 at SAM Global University

  • 1st place in the 500m event
  • 1st runner-up in the 1000m event
  • 2nd runner-up in the 5000m event

Her impressive accomplishments reflect her unwavering commitment to excellence, successfully balancing academic pursuits with exceptional athletic achievement.
