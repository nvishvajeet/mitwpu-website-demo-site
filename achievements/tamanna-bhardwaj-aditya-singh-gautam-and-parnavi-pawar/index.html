Celebrating Legal Excellence: Kudos to Our Outstanding Moot Court Team

We are delighted to announce that our talented Moot Court team—Tamanna Bhardwaj, Aditya Singh Gautam, and Parnavi Pawar, SY BA LLB students—secured the Runner-Up position at the 5th Bal Gangadhar Tilak National Level Moot Court Competition 2025.

Achievement

  • Achievements

We are delighted to announce that our talented Moot Court team—Tamanna Bhardwaj, Aditya Singh Gautam, and Parnavi Pawar, SY BA LLB students—secured the Runner-Up position at the 5th Bal Gangadhar Tilak National Level Moot Court Competition 2025. Their exceptional advocacy and teamwork shone throughout this highly competitive event.

Adding to the accolades, Aditya Singh Gautam was recognised with the Best Mooter award, celebrating his exemplary performance. This remarkable accomplishment brings immense pride to the School of Law and serves as an inspiration for the entire MIT-WPU community to pursue excellence in legal education and beyond.
