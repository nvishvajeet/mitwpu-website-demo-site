MIT-WPU, Pune, proudly congratulates Abeer Atmesh Dhond, a BA Psychology student, on his commendable performance at the All India Inter-University Aquatics (M & W) Championship 2025–26 held in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Abeer secured the 12th position in the Men’s Swimming category, placing him among the top 12 athletes selected for the prestigious Khelo India University Games.

This achievement reflects his dedication, discipline, and perseverance in competitive sports. Abeer's success is a proud moment for the MIT-WPU community, and we wish him continued excellence and the very best at the upcoming Khelo India University Games.
