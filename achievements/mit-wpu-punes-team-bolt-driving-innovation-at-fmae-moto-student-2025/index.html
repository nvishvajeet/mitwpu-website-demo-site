MIT-WPU Pune’s Team BOLT: Driving Innovation at FMAE Moto Student 2025

Team BOLT from MIT-WPU, Pune, showcased exceptional innovation and engineering mastery at the FMAE Moto Student 2025 held in Coimbatore.

Achievement

  • Achievements

Team BOLT from MIT-WPU, Pune, showcased exceptional innovation and engineering mastery at the FMAE Moto Student 2025 held in Coimbatore. The team secured five top awards, including All India Rank 1 and Best Design, along with wins in the Autocross event and strong performances in Cost and Acceleration categories.

Their achievements reflect outstanding skill, creativity, and a strong commitment to sustainable vehicle design. Team BOLT’s success exemplifies MIT-WPU Pune’s dedication to developing engineers equipped to address real-world challenges with precision, ingenuity, and a passion for advancing technology.
