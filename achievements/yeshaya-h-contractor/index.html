MIT World Peace University, Pune, proudly celebrates the outstanding achievements of Yeshaya H. Contractor, a FY BA Liberal Arts student, whose excellence in shooting continues to set new benchmarks.

68th National Shooting Championship, New Delhi

  • Gold – Junior Women Category
  • Gold – Youth Women Category
  • Bronze – Women (Senior) Category

44th Gujarat State Shotgun Shooting Championship, Mahemdavad

  • Three Gold medals in Double Trap and Trap events
  • Silver medal in Women’s Open Trap
  • Clinched her 5th consecutive State Championship title

Her consistent dominance reflects exceptional dedication, precision, and competitive spirit, making her achievement a proud moment for the MIT-WPU community.
