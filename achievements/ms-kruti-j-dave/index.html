Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU proudly congratulates Ms Kruti J. Dave, MBA-HR student, for her stellar performance in the Under-21 category at the 1st Asian Open International Taekwondo Championship 2025. Showcasing extraordinary skill, discipline, and determination, she secured:

  • Gold Medal - Fight
  • Gold Medal - Poomsae
  • Silver Medal - Speed Punching
  • Bronze Medal - Speed Kicking

Her impressive accomplishments reflect her unwavering commitment to excellence, successfully balancing academic pursuits with exceptional athletic achievement.
