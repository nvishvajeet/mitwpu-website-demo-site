Varun Kapur

Varun Kapur, a BBA student at MIT-WPU has been playing Badminton for over a decade and has earned many laurels at state, national, and international levels.

His achievements

  • Won two state championships in Maharashtra in the men’s singles category
  • Natekar Memorial Maharashtra Senior State Championship (Nagpur)
  • Yonex Sunrise Senior state Championship (Buldhana)
  • Gold medalists: 2021 Uganda International Series
  • Bronze medalists: Mixed Team Badminton Championship, 36th National Games

His support system

Varun has thrived at this sport thanks to his family's unwavering support, with his father also being a keen badminton enthusiast.

Varun has thanked MIT-WPU staff for their encouragement and for applauding him on all his accomplishments.
