Vaidehi Abhale & Rishav Bhalotia Clinch 1st at Economics Quiz

MIT-WPU, Pune, celebrates the outstanding achievement of Vaidehi Abhale and Rishav Bhalotia, S.Y. B.Sc. Economics students from the School of Economics and Commerce.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU, Pune, celebrates the outstanding achievement of Vaidehi Abhale and Rishav Bhalotia, S.Y. B.Sc. Economics students from the School of Economics and Commerce. They secured 1st place at the prestigious Late Mrs. Kaushalya Sangtani Memorial Intercollegiate Quiz 2025 on Economics, Banking & Business.

This remarkable success reflects their dedication, hard work, and the academic excellence nurtured at MIT-WPU Pune. Congratulations to Vaidehi and Rishav for bringing immense pride and honor to the university community!
