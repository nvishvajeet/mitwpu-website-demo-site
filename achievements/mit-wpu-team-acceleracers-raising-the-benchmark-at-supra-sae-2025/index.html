MIT-WPU Team Acceleracers: Raising the Benchmark at SUPRA SAE 2025

MIT-WPU’s Team Acceleracers showcased extraordinary skill and teamwork at the SUPRA SAE 2025, Buddh International Circuit, Noida.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU’s Team Acceleracers showcased extraordinary skill and teamwork at the SUPRA SAE 2025, Buddh International Circuit, Noida. The team demonstrated unwavering commitment, collaboration, and engineering excellence in every event.

Key Highlights

  • Overall AIR 2
  • AIR 1 Skidpad Event
  • AIR 1 Engineering Excellence Award
  • Overall AIR 1 in Static Events
  • AIR 2 Acceleration Event
  • First team to clear all three technical inspections

Guided by Prof. Heramb Phadake and Prof. Arun Mali, Team Acceleracers have set a new benchmark in automotive engineering, continually redefining standards and showcasing MIT-WPU’s commitment to quality and innovation.

Team Acceleracers’ achievements stand as a testament to MIT-WPU’s relentless pursuit of excellence and leadership in engineering education.
