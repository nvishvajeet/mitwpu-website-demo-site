Shravani Katke

MIT-WPU proudly celebrates the outstanding achievement of our MBA SY - RSOL student, Shravani Katke! Her bronze medal win in the Taijiquan Women's Event at the 38th National Games 2025 in Dehradun is a true testament to her hard work, dedication, and passion for Wushu. Shravani's remarkable performance has brought immense pride to MIT-WPU. Congratulations, Shravani! We are inspired by your success and wish you all the best in your future endeavours.
