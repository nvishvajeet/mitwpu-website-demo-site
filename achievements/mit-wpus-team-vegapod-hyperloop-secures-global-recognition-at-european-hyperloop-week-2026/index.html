MIT-WPU's Team Vegapod Hyperloop Secures Global Recognition at European Hyperloop Week 2026

Team Vegapod Hyperloop, the official Hyperloop Club of MIT World Peace University (MIT- WPU), Pune, has earned international acclaim by delivering an outstanding performance at the European Hyperloop Week (EHW) 2026, held at the European Hyperloop Center, Groningen, The Netherlands.

Achievement

  • Achievements

Team Vegapod Hyperloop, the official Hyperloop Club of MIT World Peace University (MIT- WPU), Pune, has earned international acclaim by delivering an outstanding performance at the European Hyperloop Week (EHW) 2026, held at the European Hyperloop Center, Groningen, The Netherlands.

Competing against some of the world's leading university teams, Team Vegapod showcased exceptional engineering expertise, innovation, and teamwork, securing the following prestigious achievements:

  • Emerging Engineering Talent Award
  • 7th Position Overall Worldwide
  • 6th Position Worldwide in Engineering Design

These remarkable accomplishments reflect the team's commitment to advancing next- generation transportation technologies through innovation, research, and engineering excellence.

MIT-WPU extends its heartfelt congratulations to the student team, faculty advisors, mentors, alumni, and supporters whose dedication and collaborative efforts made this global achievement possible. The University also expresses its sincere gratitude to the MIT-WPU Management for their constant encouragement and unwavering support in nurturing a culture of innovation and excellence.

The team was guided by Prof. Dr. D. P. Hujare, Program Director, Mechanical Engineering, and Dr. Omkar Kulkarni, Assistant Professor, whose mentorship played a significant role in the team's success.

This achievement further reinforces MIT-WPU's commitment to providing students with opportunities to excel on global platforms, fostering innovation, experiential learning, and technological leadership that contributes to shaping the future of mobility.
