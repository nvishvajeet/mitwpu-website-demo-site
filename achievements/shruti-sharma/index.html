Shruti Sharma

MIT-WPU proudly honours Shruti Sharma, a final-year B.Tech ECE (AI & ML) student, for her outstanding performance at the Southwest Zone Inter-University Karate (Women) Tournament.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU, Pune, proudly congratulates Shruti Sharma, First-Year MBA–Business Analytics student, for her outstanding performance at the Southwest Zone Inter-University Karate (Women) Tournament, earning a spot in the All India Inter-University Karate (Women) Tournament at Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak.

Representing MIT-WPU Pune in the kumite (fight) event in the 68+ kg category, Shruti also secured a Gold Medal in Senior Female Kumite at the 4th All India Inter-Zonal Karate Championship 2025. Her achievements reflect dedication, resilience, and holistic growth, balancing excellence in academics and sports.

Shruti's success inspires peers and embodies MIT-WPU Pune's commitment to discipline and all-round development.
