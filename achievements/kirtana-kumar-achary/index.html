Achievements

Ms. Kirtana Kumar Achary

MIT-WPU extends heartfelt congratulations to Ms. Kirtana Kumar Achary, a SY Bachelor of Arts (Hons.) Psychology student, on her outstanding achievements at the 6th West Zone Pencak Silat Championship 2025.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU proudly congratulates Ms. Kirtana Kumar Achary, BA (Hons.) Psychology student, for her exceptional achievements in Pencak Silat. At the 6th West Zone Pencak Silat Championship 2025, Kirtana earned a silver and a bronze medal in the Tanding (fight) category, demonstrating remarkable dedication and competitive spirit.

Additionally, she secured All India Rank 1 in Tunggal (Performance) at the Khelo India Beach Games 2025 in Diu. Kirtana’s outstanding accomplishments highlight her commitment to excellence in both academics and sports, serving as an inspiration to her peers and a source of pride for MIT-WPU.
