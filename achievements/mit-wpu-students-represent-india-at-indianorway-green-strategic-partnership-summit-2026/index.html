MIT-WPU Students Represent India at India–Norway Green Strategic Partnership Summit 2026

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, is proud to celebrate the achievements of Mrs. Arati Bhosale Ahiwale and Mr. Sagar Ahiwale , Founder and Managing Directors of Greenex Environmental, who were selected among the Top 20 Industry Delegates representing India at the prestigious India–Norway Green Strategic Partnership Summit held in Oslo.

The summit, which focused on sustainability, ESG leadership, and climate action, was held in the esteemed presence of Hon’ble Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi and Prime Minister of Norway H.E. Jonas Gahr Støre , bringing together global leaders and industry representatives committed to advancing sustainable development.

Their selection reflects a continued commitment to environmental innovation and green transformation, and underscores their contributions towards building a more sustainable future.

Adding to this remarkable achievement, both leaders are currently pursuing the M.Tech. in Environmental Engineering programme at MIT-WPU , further strengthening their expertise and reinforcing their commitment to sustainable development and impactful environmental leadership.

MIT-WPU, Pune congratulates Mrs. Arati Bhosale Ahiwale and Mr. Sagar Ahiwale on this significant recognition and wishes them continued success in their efforts to drive environmental innovation and create lasting societal impact.
