MIT-WPU's Team Piranha Racing Secures National Recognition at eBAJA SAE India 2025

MIT-WPU's Team Piranha Racing secures top national recognition at the eBAJA SAE India 2025 competition. Read about their engineering victory.

Achievement

  • Achievements

Team Piranha Racing, the eBAJA team of Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, has delivered a spectacular performance at the eBAJA SAE India Competition 2025, held at BVRIT, Hyderabad.

The team's engineering prowess and innovative approach to electric vehicle technology earned them an All India Rank 2 overall, complemented by multiple category awards.

Outstanding Achievements:

  • AIR 1 - Virtual Dynamics
  • AIR 2 - Durability/Endurance Race
  • AIR 2 - Validation Event
  • AIR 3 - Sustainability

Additional Achievements:

  • AIR 4 - Maneuverability
  • AIR 4 - CAE
  • AIR 6 - Phase 1 + Phase 2

This remarkable success showcases the team's technical expertise and commitment to innovation in electric vehicle technology, inspiring a new generation of engineers in sustainable mobility. Congratulations to Team Piranha Racing for fuelling the spirit of engineering excellence and sustainable mobility.
