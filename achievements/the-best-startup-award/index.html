The Best Startup Award

The students of the Department of Polytechnic were proudly awarded 'The Best Startup Award' at the Pune Startup Expo 2025.

Achievement

  • Achievements

The students of the Department of Polytechnic were proudly awarded 'The Best Startup Award' at the Pune Startup Expo 2025. The prestigious award was presented by Hon. Shri Ashish Shelar, Minister of Information & Technology, and Dr. Rahul V. Karad, further adding to the honor of the achievement.

Team Leader Aditya Shevate, along with team members Aarushi Javale, Aditya Patil , Janavi Honrao ,Om Desai, under the mentorship of Prof. Prerna Patil represented at Pune Startup Expo 2025.
