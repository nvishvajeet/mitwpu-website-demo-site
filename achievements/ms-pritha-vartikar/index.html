Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU extends its warmest congratulations to Ms. Pritha Vartikar, a FY BBA (Banking, Finance, and Insurance) student, on her remarkable achievements at the WTT Youth Contender Vadodara, 2025. Pritha secured bronze medals in both the Under 19 Girls Singles and Under 19 Mixed Doubles categories. Her outstanding performance showcases her unwavering dedication, strategic prowess, and unrelenting competitive spirit. MIT-WPU proudly celebrates Pritha's success, acknowledging her commitment to excellence in both academic and sporting endeavours. She serves as a beacon of inspiration for her peers and a shining example of the university's pride.
