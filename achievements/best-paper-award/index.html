Department of Polytechnic Wins 'Best Paper' Award at Prestigious IEEE Conference

We are proud to share that a team from the Department of Polytechnic has been honored with the 'Best Paper' Award at the 7th IEEE International Conference on Emerging Smart Computing and Informatics (ESCI-2025), held at AISSMS Institute of Information Technology, Pune.

The team members Tanushree Balote, Mansi Joshi, Harshada Suryawanshi, and Neel Suryawanshi under the guidance of Prof. Hemlata Ohal, presented their research paper titled "Leveraging Convolutional Neural Networks for AI Artwork Classification," which highlights their significant contribution to the field of AI-driven art classification.
