We are thrilled to congratulate Vijit Ayush Pandey, Sanskruti Rajesh Ninawe, Shubham Sarkar, and Akash Mohapatra, Third Year B.Tech CSE students for their incredible achievement in the prestigious e-Yantra Robotics Competition 2024-25, organised by IIT Bombay! Under the expert mentorship of Dr. Shilpa Paygude, this talented team secured 3rd place out of 1,008 teams in the Builder Bot theme during the grand finale held on March 28-29, 2025.

This remarkable accomplishment is the result of seven months of relentless dedication, innovative problem-solving, and teamwork. Their outstanding performance not only brings pride to the Computer Science department but also shines as a beacon of inspiration for our entire university.
