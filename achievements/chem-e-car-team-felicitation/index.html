We are the Team Skytroopers, the official Aero-Designing team of our college MIT World Peace University, Pune, India. We design UAVs and compete in national and international competitions conducted by SAE and Boeing – IIT. The team is multidisciplinary.

The team had participated at SAE ISS ADC 2022 competition which was hold at SRM University, Chennai during 1st to 3rd September 2022. The model presented in picture was prepared by our team.

The team is headed by Prof. Spruha Aniket Dhavale as faculty advisor. Team Captain is Mr. Kaustubh Kulkarni (B. Tech Mech Final Year Student) and Vice Captain is Mr. Parth Jain (B. Tech Mech Final Year Student).

In the event held at SRM, team grabbed ARI 2nd Position with prize money 45000/-. While in technical presentation team achieved 1st rank with 10000/- cash prize.

The team was appreciated for strict discipline and team work by the organizing committee of the SAEISS ADC 2022.

This was possible with the blessings of Prof P.B. Joshi Sir, HoS, Dr. Ganesh Kakandikar and guidance from our AHoS Dr. Deepak Hujare and Dr. Kundlik Mali.

The School of Mechanical Engineering is proud to announce that MITWPU's official team of Aero-design: The Skytroopers bagged 1st prize in Technical presentation and achieved 2nd AIR in AERO DESIGN CHALLENGE 2022 which was held in Chennai and was organised by the SAEINDIA. Heartily

The team is headed by Prof. Mrs. Spruha Dhavale as a faculty advisor. Team captain Mr. Kaustubh Kulkarni and Vice-captain Mr. Parth Jain lead the team to the victory.
