Team RoboVin continues to raise the bar in Military Robotics and Automation with their creative vision and technical mastery. Their pioneering research won them the 2nd Prize at the prestigious ARAI–AMTIF Ideathon, earning national acclaim and expert mentorship from ARAI.

Building on this success, Team RoboVin secured the Second Prize at Yuvahan 2025, showcased during the PIECC Nexgen Mobility Show in Pune. Their innovative solutions highlight a forward-thinking approach to next-generation mobility and advanced national security technologies.

These achievements reflect Team RoboVin’s dedication to innovation, collaboration, and real-world impact—championing MIT-WPU Pune’s leadership in defense technology and robotics for a safer, smarter future.
