Smashing Success: Girls Badminton Team Qualifies for Khelo India Games

Smashing success! The MIT-WPU Girls Badminton Team has qualified for the prestigious Khelo India Games. Read about their road to victory here.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT World Peace University, Pune, proudly commends the Girls Badminton Team for their spirited performance at the All India Inter Zone Inter University Badminton (Women) Tournament 2026, hosted by PES University, Bengaluru. Facing intense competition, Shreeya Utpat, Kunjal Mandlik, Sanika Sutar, and Bhavna Agrawal demonstrated exceptional grit, discipline, and teamwork.

Their commendable effort has earned them qualification for the prestigious Khelo India Games. The team’s achievement reflects MIT-WPU Pune’s strong sporting culture and commitment to holistic development. We congratulate the players on representing the university with distinction and wish them continued success at the Khelo India Games.
