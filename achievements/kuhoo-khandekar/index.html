MIT-WPU, Pune, celebrates the outstanding achievements of Ms. Kuhoo Khandekar, a second-year Integrated M.Sc. Biotechnology student, in inline freestyle roller skating. Her notable accomplishments include:

63rd National Roller Skating Championship 2025, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh Category – Classic Slalom – 3rd Position (Bronze Medal)

Inline Freestyle World Cup – Hero Battle Cup 2025, Milano Category – Battle Slalom – 5th Position

Kuhoo’s consistent performances at both international and national platforms reflect her exceptional skill, discipline, and passion for the sport. Balancing academic excellence with competitive sports, she continues to inspire the MIT-WPU community and exemplifies the university’s commitment to nurturing well-rounded talent.
