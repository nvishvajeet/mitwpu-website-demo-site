Triumph at the 62nd National Skating Championship 2024

We are thrilled to celebrate the outstanding achievements of our students at the 62nd National Championship 2024, held in Bangalore from December 5th to December 15th. Their dedication, perseverance, and exceptional skills have brought home well-deserved accolades!

We are thrilled to celebrate the outstanding achievements of our students at the 62nd National Championship 2024, held in Bangalore from December 5th to December 15th. Their dedication, perseverance, and exceptional skills have brought home well-deserved accolades!

Jinesh Nanal (TY B.Tech CSE) : Two Gold Medals in Speed Slalom & Classic Slalom

Shreyasi Joshi (SY B.Tech CSE) : Two Gold Medals in Speed Slalom & Classic Slalom

Kuhoo Khandekar (FY M.Sc Integrated Biotechnology) : Bronze Medal in Speed Slalom

A special recognition goes to Jinesh Nanal and Shreyasi Joshi, who have been crowned National Champions at the 62nd National Championships 2024. They are the only skaters across India to win two gold medals in both events respectively, marking an extraordinary achievement in the world of skating.

Their success is a true inspiration and a testament to their relentless pursuit of excellence. We are immensely proud of their remarkable performance on the national stage !
