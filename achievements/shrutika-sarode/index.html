Achievements

  • Shrutika Jaykant Sarode is an alumna of MIT-WPU. Currently, she is pursuing her MBA degree and has also completed a degree in Computer Engineering. Additionally, she holds a Diploma in Computer Engineering from MIT-WPU.
  • She has been an ace skater for 17 years, winning numerous medals in competitions at various levels.
  • Recently, she was honored with the most prestigious and highest sports award by the State Government of Maharashtra, the Shiv Chhatrapati Award, for her exceptional achievements in the field of Roller Skating.
  • Throughout her career, she has represented India in World and Asian Championships. She has consistently won medals at the national level for the past 15 years and has secured 7 consecutive national gold medals. In total, she has earned over 100 medals and trophies.
