1st Place at ProTech 2025

Team of Polytechnic CTRL+ALT+ELITE secured 1st Place at ProTech 2025 in the AI-ML category Project Competition, held at Symbiosis Institute Of Technology Pune.

Achievement

  • Achievements

Team of Polytechnic CTRL+ALT+ELITE secured 1st Place at ProTech 2025 in the AI-ML category Project Competition, held at Symbiosis Institute Of Technology Pune, A National Level Project & Poster Competition. The team of Tanushree Balote, Mansi Joshi,Harshada Suryawanshi, and Neel Suryawanshi under the guidance of Prof. Hemlata Ohal worked on the Project titled “AI Vs AI: Detection of AI Art using ML Models”
