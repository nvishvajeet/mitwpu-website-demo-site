First Prize in the Green Campus Challenge

Discover how MIT-WPU achieved first prize in the Green Campus Challenge through sustainability initiatives, environmental awareness, and eco-friendly campus practices.

Achievement

  • Achievements

M.Tech. students from the Department of Civil Engineering at MIT-WPU, Pune—Chandrika Gandgale, Prajwal Rajput, and Yuvraj Dhargalkar—have secured First Prize in the Green Campus Challenge organized by the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, competing against numerous teams from across the country.

This achievement reflects the team’s dedication, innovation, and consistent effort. A key contributor to their success was the mentorship and guidance provided by Dr. Pravin Minde from the Department of Civil Engineering.

MIT World Peace University · Pune, India
