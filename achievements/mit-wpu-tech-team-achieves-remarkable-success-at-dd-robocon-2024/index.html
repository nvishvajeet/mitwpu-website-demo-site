MIT-WPU Tech Team Achieves Remarkable Success at DD Robocon 2024

The MIT-WPU Tech Team has secured an impressive second runner-up position (AIR 3) at the prestigious DD Robocon 2024, held at Thyagaraj Stadium, New Delhi.

The MIT-WPU Tech Team has secured an impressive second runner-up position (AIR 3) at the prestigious DD Robocon 2024, held at Thyagaraj Stadium, New Delhi. This annual national-level robotics contest, organised by Doordarshan and hosted by IIT Delhi, brings together top engineering minds across India to showcase innovative skills and strategic thinking.

This achievement is a testament to the team's unwavering dedication, hard work, and exceptional talent. By competing against over 750 students from more than 45 institutions, the MIT-WPU Tech Team has proven its ability to thrive in a highly competitive environment. This success highlights the team's commitment to innovation and excellence in robotics, positioning them as a force to be reckoned with in the field.

MIT-WPU Tech Team is now more determined than ever to dominate Robocon, aiming for international standards. 2024 was a journey of learning and teamwork, setting the stage for a stronger comeback in 2025.
