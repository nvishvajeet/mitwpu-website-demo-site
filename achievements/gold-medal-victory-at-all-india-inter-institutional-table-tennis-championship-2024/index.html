Gold Medal Victory at All India Inter-Institutional Table Tennis Championship 2024

MIT-WPU celebrates a gold medal victory at the All India Inter-Institutional Table Tennis Championship 2024. A proud moment for our athletes!

Achievement

  • Achievements

We are proud to celebrate the outstanding achievements of our students at the All India Inter-Institutional Table Tennis Championship 2024, held in Chandigarh, Punjab. Their dedication, skill, and competitive spirit have led them to a remarkable victory!

Achievements at a Glance

Ms. Pritha Vartikar (First-Year BBA BFI) : Gold Medal

Ms. Sayali Wani (First-Year BBA Business Analytics) : Gold Medal

Their remarkable performance at this prestigious national-level competition is a testament to their hard work and passion for the sport. We extend our heartfelt congratulations to both champions for bringing pride and glory to our institution!
