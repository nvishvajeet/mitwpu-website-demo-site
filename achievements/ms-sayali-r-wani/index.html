MIT-WPU, Pune, proudly celebrates the exceptional achievements of Sayali R. Wani, a 2nd-year BBA student, whose consistent excellence in table tennis at national and international platforms reflects remarkable talent, discipline, and versatility.

Key Achievements :

  • Silver Medal at the 5th National Ranking Table Tennis Tournament
  • Gold Medal in Women’s (Open) category at the 52nd All India Inter-Institutional Table Tennis Championship 2025, Airport Authority of India, Indore
  • Gold, Silver, and Bronze Medals at the 43rd InterOil Unit Tournament (ONGC Uttarakhand) in the category : Women’s Singles Women’s Doubles Women’s Team events
  • Bronze Medals (U19 Girls Individuals) at WTT Youth Contender Tournaments in Havířov, Czech Republic, and Bulgaria (May 2025)

Sayali’s achievements exemplify her unwavering commitment to sporting excellence alongside academic pursuits, making her a true inspiration for the MIT-WPU community.
