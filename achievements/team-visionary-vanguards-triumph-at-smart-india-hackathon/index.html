Team Visionary Vanguards Triumph at Smart India Hackathon

MIT-WPU's Team Visionary Vanguards triumphs at the Smart India Hackathon! Read about their innovative solution and national-level victory here.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU, Pune, proudly congratulates Team Visionary Vanguards for securing the First Prize in the Hardware category at the Smart India Hackathon (SIH), organized by the Ministry of Education and AICTE.

This triumph reflects strong technical execution, strategic thinking, and effective teamwork, proving that innovation thrives when preparation, purpose, and collaboration align.

Special thanks to mentors Dr. Deepali Javale and Dr. Kishanprasad Gunale for their guidance and sustained commitment, inspiring the next generation of innovators at MIT-WPU Pune.
