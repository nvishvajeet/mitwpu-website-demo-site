Team AVION Secures National Recognition at NIDAR 2025

MIT-WPU congratulates Team AVION (Aerial Vehicle Innovation in Operation and Navigation) for its commendable performance at NIDAR 2025, a national-level drone innovation competition held at Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU congratulates Team AVION (Aerial Vehicle Innovation in Operation and Navigation) for its commendable performance at NIDAR 2025, a national-level drone innovation competition held at Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida.

The team presented an autonomous drone solution for disaster management, designed to support rapid disaster assessment through large-area aerial surveys, victim and hotspot identification, real-time situational awareness, and coordinated assistance for emergency response teams. The project highlighted scalable deployment, operational reliability, and a service-based model for disaster response authorities.

Achievement Highlights:

  • Event: NIDAR 2025 – National-Level Drone Innovation Competition
  • Venue: Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida
  • Project: Autonomous Drone Solution for Disaster Management
  • Business Presentation: AIR 8 (Presented by Rabi Joshi)
  • Overall Rank: AIR 27
  • Ignite Prize: 26th Rank

Team AVION's achievement reflects MIT-WPU's commitment to fostering innovation, problem-solving, and technology-driven solutions for real-world challenges.
