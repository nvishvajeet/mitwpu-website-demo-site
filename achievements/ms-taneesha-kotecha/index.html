  • Achievements

MIT-WPU proudly congratulates Ms. Taneesha Kotecha, FY BBA (Business Analytics) student, for her remarkable table tennis achievements:

  • Silver Medal in U-19 Girls at WTT Youth Contender, Havirov, Czech Republic (March 2025).
  • Silver Medal in U-19 Mixed Doubles at WTT Youth Contender, Vadodara, India.
  • Historic Bronze Medal in Mixed Doubles at the 29th Asian Youth Table Tennis Championships, Tashkent, Uzbekistan.

This isn’t just any medal—it is the first-ever Mixed Doubles medal from Maharashtra and only the second in Indian Table Tennis history at the Asian Junior level . Taneesha and Ankur Bhattacharjee made history by defeating Japan’s top pair, YOSHIYAMA Kazuki & MENDE Rin, in a spectacular semifinal win.
