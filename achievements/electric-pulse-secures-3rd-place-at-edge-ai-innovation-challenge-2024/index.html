Electric Pulse Secures 3rd Place at Edge AI Innovation Challenge 2024

MIT-WPU's Team Electric Pulse secures 3rd place in the prestigious Edge AI Innovation Challenge 2024. Read about their innovative project.

Reconstructed from the retained MIT-WPU public capture. View official source

Achievement

  • Achievements

We are thrilled to announce that Team Electric Pulse has achieved a remarkable 3rd place in the final stage (Stage 3) of the Edge AI Innovation Challenge 2024! Hosted by Digi Toad Technologies in partnership with STMicroelectronics, the competition featured India’s top 10 teams, selected from an initial 180 entries.

On October 4th, the team presented their prototype and pitch on the "Integrated Active and Passive Cell Equalization System," implementing AI in SOC calculation, at the grand finale in Bangalore.

Vipin Jain : Third-Year Electrical and Computer Engineering

Omkar Kolhe : Third-Year Electrical and Computer Engineering

Samyak Bakliwal : Third-Year Electrical and Computer Engineering

A special appreciation goes to their mentor, Dr. Netra Lokhande, for her invaluable guidance, and to the management, Dean, Programme Director, and Programme Coordinator for their unwavering support.
