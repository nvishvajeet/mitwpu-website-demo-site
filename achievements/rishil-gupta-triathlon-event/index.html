Rishil Gupta, Triathlon event

Rishil Gupta from Integrated BTech Mechanical (Robotics and automation) branch competed Triathlon event (Ironman) successfully at Baharin. For a total distance of 113 km ( Running+swimming+cycling) He took 5 hrs 55 minutes. He stood 11 th amongst 1000 participants from all over the world. Stood 4 th from 200 Indian participants.
