Achievement of Team Pro-Karters

The team has won 5 awards in different categories and cash prize worth 75000 rupees. The team is headed by Prof. Surendra Barhatte and prof. Shailendra Dayane as faculty advisors.

Achievement

  • Achievements

The team had participated in Indian Karting Race IKR 7.0 organized by Imperial Society of innovative engineers (ISIE).

The team has won 5 awards in different categories and cash prize worth 75000 rupees. The team is headed by Prof. Surendra Barhatte and prof. Shailendra Dayane as faculty advisors. Team captain Niranjan Jadhav and vice-captain Mr. Apratim Ukalkar and kart drivers Atman Derashree and Mithil Nambiar lead the team to victory.

This was possible with the blessings of HoS, Dr. Ganesh Kakandikar and guidance from our AHoS Dr. Deepak Hujare and Dr. Kundlik Mali A total of 350 teams had registered for the competition. Some of the teams could not qualify for the race due to failure in Technical Inspection and safety.

Accolades

  • AIR 1 in EV category
  • AIR 2 Overall IC n EV categories
  • AIR 1 in Endurance Race
  • AIR 1 in Acceleration
  • AIR 2 in Crosspad
  • Winner of the ISIE future award
  • Cash prize worth 75000 rupees
