MIT-WPU Team Chemy Chem: Champions of Innovation at YUVA AI-THON 2025

MIT-WPU Pune's Team Chemy Chem, proudly secured the 1st position in the Innovation Domain at the prestigious YUVA AI-THON 2025, hosted by VIT Chennai.

Achievement

  • Achievements

MIT-WPU Pune's Team Chemy Chem, proudly secured the 1st position in the Innovation Domain at the prestigious YUVA AI-THON 2025, hosted by VIT Chennai. Their groundbreaking project, “Biofert Matrix: Empowering Sustainable Agriculture with Smart Tech,” won acclaim for its creativity and impactful real-world application.

This remarkable achievement highlights the vibrant research culture flourishing within the Department of Chemistry and underscores MIT-WPU Pune’s dedication to fostering scientific innovation and future-ready talent.
