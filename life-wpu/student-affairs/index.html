Overview

Welcome to the vibrant center of student life at MIT-WPU – the Student Affairs Department. As the guardians of all matters related to students, we are wholeheartedly committed to enhancing your campus experience. Whether it's supervising student council elections, nurturing a diverse range of student clubs, orchestrating exciting events, or providing essential support services, our purpose is to ensure that your time at MIT-WPU is both unforgettable and rewarding. Our mission revolves around empowering and involving students, fostering personal development, leadership skills, and a deep sense of belonging within our dynamic community. Join us in shaping your educational journey, creating lasting memories, and fully embracing the diverse spectrum of campus life.
