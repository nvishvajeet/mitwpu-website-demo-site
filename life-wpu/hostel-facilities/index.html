Hostel Facilities

Discover the Best Hostel Facilities at MIT-WPU - Experience comfortable and modern accommodations for students. Explore our well-equipped hostels, conducive study spaces, and a vibrant campus community. Amenities are Wifi, Gym, Canteen, Lifts, Air Conditioned rooms, sharing options etc.

Welcome to the MIT World Peace University Hostel!

Located in the vibrant city of Pune, Maharashtra, our hostel offers exceptional accommodation facilities designed to meet the needs of our students. We prioritize creating a comfortable and safe environment, allowing students to focus on their academic goals without the worry of finding suitable accommodation. Our hostel boasts a capacity of 3000 beds, featuring well-planned and high-quality fittings for a comfortable stay. We understand the importance of a balanced lifestyle, which is why we provide various recreational facilities including a gym, game rooms with carrom, pool, chess, and other board games, study rooms, and a basketball court, ensuring students can relax and unwind after a long day of classes.

At MIT-WPU, we are dedicated to providing an exceptional living experience, ensuring the safety and security of our residents while creating a welcoming and student-friendly environment. Our commitment is to support students in achieving their academic aspirations by offering top-notch accommodation services tailored to their needs.

Join us at MIT-WPU Hostel and embark on a journey toward academic success in a home away from home.

Our Amenities

  • Single, double, and triple occupancy with state-of-the-art furniture.
  • RO treated drinking water at convenient locations on every alternate floor of the hostel building.
  • Washing machines, Induction,and microwaves are also available in common area.
  • Daily provision of breakfast and dinner, lunch on weekends with versatile menus
  • Student shuttle facilities from hostel to university campus
  • Vigilant 24x7 security guards
  • Student recreation zones like basic gym, game room, study room, and dance/yoga rooms (Subject to hostel)
  • Hot Water is available in all rooms.
  • Laundry services
  • Premises under CCTV surveillance 24x7
  • The hostel premises are fire compliant and are equipped with fire extinguishers
  • Treatment plants like STP/WTP are installed at all premises.

In-room Description

Girls Hostels

The Girls' Hostel at MIT-WPU is a secure and nurturing space designed for the comfort and convenience of female students. Located within the vibrant areas of Pune, it offers modern amenities, well-appointed rooms, and 24/7 security. More than just a place to stay, it fosters a sense of community, encouraging lifelong friendships and supporting holistic development.

Boys Hostels

The Boys' Hostel at MIT-WPU is a dynamic and secure residence tailored to male students' needs. Situated within the lively campus, it provides modern amenities, comfortable rooms, and round-the-clock security. More than a living space, the hostel promotes a sense of brotherhood, fostering strong connections and supporting comprehensive personal development.
