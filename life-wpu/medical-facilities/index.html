Medical Facilities

MIT-WPU offers top-notch medical facilities on campus to address health emergencies and issues. Medical facilities at MIT-WPU offer laboratory services, OPD consultation, dressing facilities (major & minor), 24/7 ambulance service, pharmacy, ECG, day care, and Covid-19 vaccination.

MIT-WPU has a full-time doctor, 24/7 pharmacy, a facility with three beds, ambulances, and a group affiliated world-class hospital. Medical facilities at MIT-WPU offer:

  • Laboratory Services
  • OPD Consultation
  • Dressing facilities (Major & Minor)
  • 24X 7 Ambulance
  • Pharmacy
  • ECG
  • Day Care
  • Covid-19 Vaccination
