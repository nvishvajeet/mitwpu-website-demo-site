Student Achievements

We are immensely proud of our students for their remarkable achievements across diverse fields. Whether excelling in academics, winning national and international competitions, leading groundbreaking research, or innovating in entrepreneurship, they continue to inspire with their dedication and talent. Their success in sports, cultural events, and community-driven initiatives showcases their versatility and commitment to making a difference. Each milestone they achieve reflects their hard work, passion, and determination, and we celebrate their journey toward excellence.

MIT-WPU Tech Team Robocon Secures AIR 2 at DD Robocon India 2026

The MIT-WPU Tech Team Robocon has once again demonstrated its excellence in robotics and engineering innovation by securing All India Rank (AIR) 2 and emerging as the National Runner-up at DD Robocon India 2026, held at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi on 4 and 5 July 2026.
