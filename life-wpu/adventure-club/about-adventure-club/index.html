Find Adventure Club at MIT-WPU! Discover our goal to excite and challenge students by means of fascinating outdoor activities, treks, and experiences thereby fostering a spirit of inquiry.

MIT-WPU Adventure Club - “NISARGVEDH”

NISARGVEDH is a unique and exciting platform that pushes your physical and mental boundaries, enables you to master outdoor skills, ignites the leadership potential in you, allows you to forge unbreakable bonds with fellow adventurers, and share knowledge that transcends the ordinary.

MIT-WPU Adventure Club (now Nisargvedh) has joined hands with the renowned Giripremi Adventure Foundation (GAF) to create a dynamic synergy of innovation and exploration.

MIT-WPU Adventure Club (now Nisargvedh) was inaugurated on the auspicious occasion of 'National Youth Day, 12th January 12, 2023, at the hands of distinguished dignitaries, Hon’ble Prof. (Dr.) Vishwanath Karad and Shri. Umesh Zirpe, and ideated by Hon’ble Shri. Rahul Karad.
