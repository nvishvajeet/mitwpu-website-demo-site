Yoga lawns

At MIT-WPU, we emphasise this by offering sprawling yoga lawns on our campus. These lush, green spaces serve as the perfect backdrop for the numerous yoga sessions conducted throughout the academic year, promoting holistic well-being among our students and faculty.

Banks and ATM

That's why MIT-WPU provides on-campus banking services, eliminating the need to leave the campus or endure long queues. These services cater to various financial transactions, including student loans, and are complemented by a 24-hour ATM facility for round-the-clock cash access.

Open Air Amphitheatre

This space comes alive with co-curricular and extracurricular activities, serving as the perfect venue for student presentations, cultural programmes, youth festivals, and annual functions.

WiFi facilities

In today's education landscape, uninterrupted internet access is essential. At MIT-WPU, we offer 24/7 WiFi facilities, enabling students to access up-to-date information and knowledge resources from around the world, enhancing their learning experience.

Eco Park

Amid the bustling campus, MIT-WPU offers an Eco Park that serves as a tranquil retreat for meditation and recreation. This park provides students with a serene escape from the city's hustle and bustle, allowing them to find moments of quiet reflection.

24/7 security

Ensuring the safety of our students is paramount. To this end, MIT-WPU has implemented high-quality CCTV camera surveillance across the campus, providing round-the-clock security and monitoring for everyone's peace of mind.
