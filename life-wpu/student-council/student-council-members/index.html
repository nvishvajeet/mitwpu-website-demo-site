Student Council

The MIT-WPU Student Council was established to foster a vibrant and inclusive university culture, promoting student engagement and participation. As part of the council's commitment to nurturing the youth, the council aims to cultivate a sense of unity and leadership values among students, positively impacting their lives. The Student Council acts as a bridge between MIT-WPU and the student body, addressing matters related to students' social, cultural, and academic well-being.
