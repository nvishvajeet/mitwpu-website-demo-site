  • Institute of AI

IoAI aims to be the leading Artificial Intelligence (AI) institute empowering the next generation of students to become industry leaders, AI and Machine Learning (ML) researchers and practitioners to lead innovation by solving socially important issues.

We at IoAI utilise cutting-edge research, education, and collaboration with universities, industry, and society to advance the field of AI & ML. Our goals address diverse fields for sustainable, ethical and responsible use of AI. Our initiatives are geared towards training and educating a diverse and inclusive community by providing world-class training programs, workshops, conferences, and elite publication platforms that promote collaboration and knowledge-sharing.
