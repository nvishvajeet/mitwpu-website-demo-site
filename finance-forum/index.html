The Finance Forum is a dynamic and student-driven initiative aimed at fostering a deep understanding of finance and its broader implications in the field of management. With a dedicated faculty head, Dr. Rupali Gupta, and an Associate Head, Dr. Varsha Nerlekar, this club is entirely student-run and managed by individuals passionate about finance as a specialisation. The central objective of the Finance Forum is to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical application in the realm of finance. We strive to make students more competitive by enhancing their understanding of both financial concepts and their practical implications.

At the Finance Forum, we are committed to proliferating financial literacy. We believe that a sound understanding of finance is not only essential for personal growth but is also a crucial skill in the business world. Our mission is to upgrade and enhance financial knowledge across various specialisations. We aim to provide students with a platform where they can hone their financial acumen and excel in their chosen fields.
