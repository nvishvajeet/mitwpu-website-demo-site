Academic Research

Academic research at MIT-WPU is deeply rooted in a systematic and methodical approach to problem-solving. Our institution places a strong emphasis on collaborative research endeavours, often conducted in partnership with faculty and students, and backed by the generous support of the MIT-WPU Seed Money Scheme. This initiative grants funding to faculty members embarking on research projects with a dual purpose: to foster a research-oriented mindset among our students and to tackle real-world challenges.

The Research and Development Cell (RDC) plays a pivotal role in orchestrating this research ecosystem. We provide expert guidance, meticulous planning, and valuable resources to ensure the execution of high-quality research that aligns with the overarching vision of our university.
