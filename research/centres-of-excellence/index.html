Centres of Excellence

At MIT-WPU, the Centers of Excellence(COEs) are the intellectual core dedicated to pioneering research and development in various disciplines. With an unparalleled commitment towards research and advancement, the COEs are the forerunners of groundbreaking research and driving technological progress. They also serve as the platform that facilitates the exchange of ideas and knowledge among some of the best minds in the country. They are the embodiment of the University’s dedication towards the pursuit of knowledge and excellence to shape a brighter future.
