Dr. Omkar Kulkarni Honored with the Prestigious Teaching Excellence Award

MIT World Peace University proudly congratulates Dr. Omkar Kulkarni, Assistant Professor [Department of Mechanical Engineering], for being honored with the IEI NMLC–FCRIT Excellence Award 2025 .

MIT World Peace University proudly congratulates Dr. Omkar Kulkarni, Assistant Professor [Department of Mechanical Engineering], for being honored with the IEI NMLC–FCRIT Excellence Award 2025 (Teaching Excellence Award 2025), conferred by The Institution of Engineers (India), Navi Mumbai Local Centre and Fr. C. Rodrigues Institute of Technology.

What makes this accolade truly special is that Dr.Omkar Kulkarni received the award from the distinguished hands of Shri. Kishan Narayanrao Jawale (IAS), District Collector of Raigad.

This achievement is not just a recognition of his unwavering commitment to teaching and research excellence, but also a reminder of the vital role educators play in shaping future leaders, fostering innovation, and driving societal progress.

His milestone inspires the entire MIT-WPU fraternity to continue striving for academic brilliance and advancing engineering education with purpose and passion.
