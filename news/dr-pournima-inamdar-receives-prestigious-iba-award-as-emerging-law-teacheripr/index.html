Dr. Pournima Inamdar Receives Prestigious IBA Award as emerging Law Teacher(IPR)

MIT‑WPU’s Dr. Pournima Inamdar receives the prestigious IBA Emerging Law Teacher (IPR) Award for academic excellence.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

Happy to inform everyone that Dr. Pournima Inamdar, Associate Dean (External Relations ) of the School of Law was awarded the 9th Indian Bar Association Award as emerging Law Teacher ( IPR ) of the Year 2023 by Justice Dharmesh Sharma, Sitting Judge Delhi High Court yesterday at New Delhi on the occassion of Constitution Day.
