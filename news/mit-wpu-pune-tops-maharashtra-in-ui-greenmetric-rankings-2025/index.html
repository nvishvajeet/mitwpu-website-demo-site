MIT-WPU Pune Tops Maharashtra in UI GreenMetric Rankings 2025

A milestone for sustainability! MIT-WPU secures the top spot in Maharashtra in the UI GreenMetric World University Rankings 2025. Read the full report.

Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune has earned a landmark position in the prestigious UI GreenMetric World University Rankings 2025, advancing our sustainability mission.

Ranking Highlights:

  • 1st Rank in Maharashtra
  • 25th Rank in India
  • 620th Rank Globally

This achievement outpaces many premier institutions worldwide, showcasing MIT-WPU Pune's dedication to green infrastructure, sustainable practices, and environmental stewardship at the core of our vision.

The success reflects every member's commitment and drives us toward an even greener future.
