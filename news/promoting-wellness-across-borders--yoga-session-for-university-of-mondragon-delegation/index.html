Promoting Wellness Across Borders : Yoga Session for University of Mondragon Delegation

MIT-WPU hosts a yoga session for a delegation from the University of Mondragon, Spain, promoting wellness and cultural exchange.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

On 07 February 2025, MIT-World Peace University hosted a rejuvenating yoga session for 38 students and 5 faculty members from the University of Mondragon, Spain. The session took place on the ground floor of the Chanakya building, offering a serene and peaceful environment.

The session was conducted by Prof. Akash Homkar, Assistant Professor, Department of Wellness and Yogic Science, with the able assistance of his MSc students. The participants, both students and faculty, thoroughly enjoyed the session, which ran from 8 AM to 9 AM, leaving everyone refreshed and energized.

Such initiatives continue to reflect MIT-WPU’s commitment to fostering global harmony and well-being through the practice of yoga and other collaborative programs.
