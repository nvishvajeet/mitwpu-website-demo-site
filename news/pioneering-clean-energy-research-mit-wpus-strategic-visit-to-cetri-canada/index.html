Prof. Murthy Chavali, Dean R&D, and Prof. Niraj S. Topare from Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, recently visited the prestigious Clean Energy Technologies Research Institute (CETRI) at the University of Regina, Canada. This strategic visit was facilitated by Prof. Hussameldin Ibrahim, Director of CETRI and Professor in Industrial and Process Systems Engineering.

The discussions centered around CETRI's groundbreaking work in carbon capture and utilization as well as blue hydrogen production technologies. The meeting fostered insightful exchanges on potential future collaborations between MIT-WPU and CETRI in the clean energy sector.

This visit marks a significant step towards fostering global partnerships aimed at advancing sustainable energy solutions. It aligns with MIT-WPU's commitment to innovation and environmental stewardship, reflecting its founder Dr. Vishwanath Karad's vision for transformative education that contributes to global peace and harmony.
