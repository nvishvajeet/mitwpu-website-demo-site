MIT-WPU at NAFSA 2025 | Strengthening Global Educational Alliances

MIT-WPU participates in NAFSA 2025, the world's largest international education event, to strengthen global educational alliances.

MIT-WPU is proud to announce the signing of two significant MoUs with Korea University (South Korea) and Auburn University at Montgomery (USA). The MoUs were formalised at the NAFSA 2025 Annual Conference & Expo in San Diego, USA—a leading event for international education professionals.

Dr. Prasad Khandekar (Chief Academic Officer, MIT-WPU), Dr. Prakash Mainkar (Director, Admissions, MIT-WPU), and Dr. Nirupama Kocherlakota (Director, International Affairs, MIT-WPU) represented MIT-WPU at the conference and signed the agreements on behalf of the university.

These strategic partnerships will enable student and faculty exchanges, joint research, immersive academic programmes, and cross-border learning opportunities, aligned with our vision of preparing globally competent, peace-driven leaders. We extend our gratitude to the NAFSA community and our new global partners for embracing our shared commitment to internationalisation in higher education.
