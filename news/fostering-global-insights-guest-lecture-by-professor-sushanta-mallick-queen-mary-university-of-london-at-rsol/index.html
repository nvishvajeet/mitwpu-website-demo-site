Fostering Global Insights: Guest Lecture by Professor Sushanta Mallick, Queen Mary University of London at RSOL

The Ramcharan School of Leadership hosted a guest lecture by Professor Sushanta Mallick from Queen Mary University of London for global insights.

MIT World Peace University's Ramcharan School of Leadership (RSOL) recently hosted a guest lecture by Professor Sushanta Mallick, International Finance, Queen Mary University, London, accompanied by Abhishek Boral, Senior Recruitment Adviser, South Asia.

The lecture focused on the intriguing topic, "What happens to listed firms after they obtain costly shadow loans?" This event not only provided valuable insights into international finance but also explored opportunities to strengthen bilateral strategic relationships between MIT-WPU and Queen Mary University of London.

  • Engaging Discussion : The lecture offered a deep dive into the financial implications of shadow loans on listed firms.
  • Global Collaboration : The event highlighted potential partnerships between MIT-WPU and QMUL, fostering a stronger academic and research network.
  • Student Engagement : Second-year BBA students pursuing International Business at RSOL actively participated in the session, gaining insights into global financial dynamics.

This collaboration underscores the commitment of both institutions to enhance global understanding and academic excellence.
