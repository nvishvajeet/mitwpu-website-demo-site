MIT-WPU is immensely proud to announce that our Hon'ble Executive President, Dr. Rahul V. Karad, has been felicitated at the 11th Commonwealth Parliamentary Association (CPA) India Region Conference in Bengaluru.

Hon'ble Shri Om Birla, the Speaker of the Lok Sabha, and other top constitutional leaders, presented the honour to Dr. Rahul V. Karad. This recognition is a testament to his visionary leadership and unwavering commitment to fortifying the pillars of Indian democracy.

Under Dr. Rahul V. Karad’s guidance, NLC Bharat has become a landmark initiative, bringing together legislators from across the nation to foster dialogue, collaboration, and democratic development. His passion for capacity building and legislative cooperation was recently highlighted during a significant program in Boston, where 130 legislators from 21 different political parties united on an international stage.

This felicitation is a powerful validation of his dedication to democratic values and his tireless efforts to build a stronger, more collaborative legislative framework nationwide.
