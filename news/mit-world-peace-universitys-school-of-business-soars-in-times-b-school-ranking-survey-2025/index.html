MIT World Peace University's School of Business Soars in Times B-School Ranking Survey 2025

The School of Business at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune has achieved remarkable success in the Times B-School Ranking Survey 2025. The institution has been ranked 11th among the Top 100 B-Schools in India, solidifying its position as a leading management institute. MIT-WPU also secured 3rd place among the Top 40 Private Universities and 5th place among the Top 20 B-Schools in the West Zone, further highlighting its regional and national prominence.

These impressive rankings reflect the School of Business's commitment toward academic excellence, innovative teaching methodologies, and industry-relevant curriculum. The Times B-School Ranking Survey is a highly respected and comprehensive assessment that evaluates business schools across various parameters, including faculty expertise, research output, infrastructure, and placement records. MIT-WPU's strong performance highlights its dedication toward providing students with a world-class management education.

This achievement is a testament to the hard work, dedication, and passion of our exceptional faculty, staff, and students. MIT-WPU is grateful for their unwavering commitment toward excellence, which has enabled the university to achieve this milestone. These rankings inspire MIT-WPU to continue striving for even greater heights and to provide our students with the best possible learning experience.

The School of Business at MIT-WPU offers a diverse range of management programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in today's dynamic business world. The university's focus on holistic development, along with its emphasis on peace and sustainability, sets it apart and prepares students to become responsible and ethical leaders.

The MIT-WPU celebrates this achievement and looks forward to continuing its journey of academic excellence and contributing to the field of management education.
