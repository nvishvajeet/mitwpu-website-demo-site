Department of Mechanical Engineering MOU with Regal Rexnord Company

MIT‑WPU Mechanical Engineering Department partners with Regal Rexnord to enhance research, internships, and industry projects.

We are thrilled to share that the Department of Mechanical Engineering recently signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with Regal Rexnord, a renowned company located in Hinjewadi, Pune. This collaboration signifies a strong bond between academia and industry, offering our students invaluable opportunities for real-world learning and professional development.

Key to this partnership was Prof. (Dr.) Pankaj Dhatrak, our Single Point of Contact (SPOC) coordinated and implemented the MOU. His dedication and expertise ensured the successful alignment of our academic goals with industry requirements. The signing ceremony took place with great enthusiasm and pride. Prof. (Dr.) Deepak Hujare, Programme Coordinator from Robotics and Automation, proudly represented our institution during the signing of the MOU between MITWPU and Regal Rexnord, Pune.

This collaboration paves the way for a mutually beneficial relationship that fosters advancements in both education and industry practices. We extend our gratitude to everyone involved in making this collaboration a reality.

The Department of Mechanical Engineering remains committed to providing our students with cutting-edge opportunities. This partnership with Regal Rexnord exemplifies our dedication to excellence.
