MIT-WPU’s School of Law Honoured with Distinguished Award of Excellence

MIT-WPU's School of Law received the Award of Excellence at the International Legal Conference 2025 - presented by a Supreme Court Judge and backed by India's Ministry of Law.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

MIT World Peace University’s School of Law has been awarded the esteemed ‘Award of Excellence’ at the International Legal Conference 2025, organised by the Services Export Promotion Council (SEPC) in collaboration with the International Association of Legal Professionals. The event was supported by the Ministry of Law and Justice, as well as the Department of Commerce, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India.

The award was received on behalf of the School of Law by Dr. Santosh Aghav, Professor & Dean, and Dr. Pournima Inamdar, Associate Dean of External Relations. The honour was presented by Hon’ble Mr. Justice Manmohan, Judge of the Supreme Court of India, together with Adv. (Dr.) Lalit Bhasin, President of SILF and Founder Chairman-SEPC, and Adv. (Dr.) Anju Rathi Rana, Secretary, Department of Legal Affairs, Ministry of Law & Justice.

This accolade highlights MIT-WPU School of Law’s dedication to legal excellence, innovation, and its significant contribution to the advancement of legal education in India.
