Dr. Gopinath T. Elected as a Young Associate Member of the MASc for his contributions under Engineering and Technology

MIT World Peace University proudly announces that Dr. Gopinath T., Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, was elected as a Young Associate of Maharashtra Academy of Sciences (MASc) for his significant contributions in the field of Engineering & Technology in 2024.

MIT World Peace University proudly announces that Dr. Gopinath T., Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, was elected as a Young Associate of Maharashtra Academy of Sciences (MASc) for his significant contributions in the field of Engineering & Technology in 2024.

This Certificate of Honour was conferred by Padma Shri Prof. Dr. G. D. Yadav, The President, Maharashtra Academy of Sciences. The Maharashtra Academy of Sciences is committed to advancing the fields of science and technology and inspiring scientists and researchers.

MIT-WPU congratulates Dr. Gopinath T. on this well-deserved honour and looks forward to his continued contributions to the Engineering & Technology field.
