MIT-WPU, Pune, Receives the Prestigious National Heartful Campus Award

MIT-WPU Pune has been honored with the National Heartful Campus Award 2025 for excellence in holistic education. Read about this national recognition here.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, is honoured to receive the National Heartful Campus Award 2025, a prestigious recognition of the University’s sustained efforts in promoting heart-centered education and holistic development from 2020 to 2025.

The award was presented by Dr. Anil Sahasrabudhe, Chairman of NAAC, NBA, and NETF, in the esteemed presence of Shri Kunaljeet Singh, Director, AICTE, and Shri Ramesh Krishnan, Director, Heartful Campus, at a grand ceremony held at the Global Headquarters of Heartfulness, Kanha Shantivanam, Hyderabad. This national recognition underscores MIT-WPU’s commitment to integrating mindfulness, value-based education, and inner well-being into its academic and campus life.

This achievement reflects the University’s enduring vision of education as a transformative journey—one that builds character, leadership, and a strong sense of purpose alongside academic and professional excellence. MIT-WPU, Pune, gratefully acknowledges the continued guidance and inspiration of its leadership, whose unwavering support has been instrumental in achieving this milestone.
