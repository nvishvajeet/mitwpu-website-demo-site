MIT World Peace University proudly announces that Dr. Bharat Kale, Emeritus Professor & Director of Material Science [COE], has been honoured with the prestigious Prof. S. K. Bhattacharya Eminent Scientist Award (2020) from the Catalysis Society of India (CSI) for his outstanding research in Photocatalysis.

This distinguished accolade was presented at the CSI Conference, recognising Dr. Kale's exceptional contributions to a field that holds transformative potential for sustainable solutions and technological innovation.

Dr. Bharat Kale's scientific excellence extends beyond national boundaries—he has been ranked among the top 2% of scientists worldwide by Stanford University, a testament to the global impact and significance of his research.

His work in Photocatalysis represents cutting-edge science with far-reaching applications for environmental sustainability and clean energy. His research continues to push boundaries in this critical field, providing pathways to address some of today's most pressing global challenges.

This recognition honours Dr. Bharat Kale's achievement and strengthens MIT-WPU's position as a centre of academic and research excellence on the global stage.
