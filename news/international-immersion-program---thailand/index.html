MIT-WPU paved the way for 15 students from the School of Business and School of Engineering & Technology to participate in the International Immersion Program in Thailand. In this 2-week program, students immersed themselves in intellectually stimulating & fun-filled learning experiences. They explored trending technologies like Drone technology, GIS Mapping, and business topics like Fintech and Digital Marketing to name a few. A visit to Bangkok, Safari Tour, and a visit to Science Museum enabled them to understand the rich culture of Thailand as well.
