A Recognition of Vision and Commitment: Dr. Rahul V. Karad Honoured by Legal Fraternity

MIT-WPU President Dr. Rahul V. Karad receives recognition from the legal fraternity for his vision, leadership, and contribution to education, peace studies, and social impact initiatives.

MIT World Peace University proudly announces that Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Executive President, MIT-WPU, has been honoured with the Special Recognition Award by the Bar Council of Maharashtra & Goa and the Pune Bar Association.

The honour, presented in the esteemed presence of Hon’ble Judges and senior members of the legal fraternity, acknowledges his impactful contributions to Democracy, Governance, and Peace.

This recognition reflects not only his role as an educationist but also as a visionary advocate of democratic values, inclusive governance, and ethical leadership. Dr. Rahul V. Karad’s belief that the true strength of a nation lies in its people—especially its youth—continues to inspire movements for dialogue, inclusivity, and nation-building.

More than an individual milestone, this accolade embodies the larger mission of MIT-WPU: to create a transformative learning ecosystem where science and spirituality converge, nurturing future leaders who advance humanity through wisdom, peace, and progress.
