Arnd Franz, CEO of MAHLE Group Germany, Inspires Innovation at MIT World Peace University

MIT World Peace University was honoured to host Mr. Arnd Franz, the Chairman of Group Management Board and CEO of MAHLE Group Germany, for an insightful and inspiring interaction with our students. In his keynote address, he shared his journey and vision for innovation, inspiring students to embrace bold ideas.

The session transitioned into an engaging interactive Q&A, where students had the opportunity to pose their queries directly to Mr. Franz. His thoughtful responses and valuable guidance resonated deeply with the audience, leaving a profound impact. A highlight of his visit was the inauguration of the official Vegapod Hyperloop Club website, marking a significant milestone for MIT-WPU.

Mr. Franz also reviewed the MAHLE supported student club projects, including Vegapod Hyperloop’s innovative Hyperloop prototype and Piranha Racing’s All Terrain E-Vehicle, providing expert feedback to refine their innovations. His visit was a true source of inspiration, driving students to push boundaries and achieve excellence. Mr. Arnd Franz was accompanied by Dr. Roger Busch, Vice President, Sales and Engineering, Mr. Milind Godbole, President of MAHLE India, Mr. Ashutosh Deshpande, Head of Engineering Services, and Mr. Joji Philips, Head of Human Resources, MAHLE India. The delegation had a detailed discussion on collaboration with Hon. Vice Chancellor Dr. R. M. Chitnis, Mr. Ganesh Pokale, Dr. Siddharth Chakrabarti, Dr. Mangesh Bedekar, Dr. Ganesh Kakandikar, Dr. Shivprakash Barve, and Dr. Deepak Hujare.
