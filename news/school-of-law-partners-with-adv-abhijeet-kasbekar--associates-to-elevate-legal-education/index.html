School of Law Partners with Adv. Abhijeet Kasbekar & Associates to Elevate Legal Education

MIT-WPU School of Law signs an MoU with Adv. Abhijeet Kasbekar & Associates, offering students internships, placements & practice-oriented legal training.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

MIT-WPU School of Law, in collaboration with CIAP, is proud to announce the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Adv. Abhijeet Kasbekar & Associates on 8th May 2025. This strategic partnership is a significant step towards bridging academic learning with real-world legal practice.

Unlocking Opportunities for Excellence

  • Internship Opportunities: Gain hands-on experience by working alongside seasoned legal professionals.
  • Placement Support: Unlock pathways to promising career opportunities in the legal field.
  • Research Collaboration: Engage in impactful legal research projects with expert guidance.

Curriculum Enhancement: Benefit from industry-driven insights to ensure our curriculum remains relevant and future-ready. Continuing Education: Access specialized programs designed to keep students updated with the latest legal trends and practices.

This collaboration underscores our commitment to providing holistic, practice-oriented legal education and empowering our students to become industry-ready professionals.
