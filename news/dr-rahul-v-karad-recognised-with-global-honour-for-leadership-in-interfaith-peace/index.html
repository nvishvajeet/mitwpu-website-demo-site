Dr. Rahul V. Karad Recognised with Global Honour for Leadership in Interfaith Peace

MIT World Peace University proudly shares that Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Executive President, MIT-WPU, has been honoured with the 2025 Global Business and Interfaith Peace Award at the Inaugural IRF Builders Forum & Roger Williams IRF Awards in Washington, D.C.

Organised by the International Religious Freedom (IRF) Secretariat, the event gathered global leaders dedicated to advancing religious freedom and interfaith harmony. The award celebrates individuals who have made significant contributions to building peace at the intersection of business, education, and faith.

This prestigious global accolade places Dr. Rahul V. Karad among an esteemed cohort of global changemakers, including previous awardees such as the CEO of Intel, Chair of Tyson Foods, Chief Communications Officer of American Airlines, and the Chairman of Godrej Industries.

This recognition is a testament to MIT World Peace University's unwavering mission — to disseminate peace through the unification of science and spirituality.
