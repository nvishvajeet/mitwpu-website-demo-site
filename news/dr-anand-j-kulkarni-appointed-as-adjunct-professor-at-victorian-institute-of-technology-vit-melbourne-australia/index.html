MIT-WPU proudly announces that Dr. Anand J. Kulkarni, Professor & Associate Director of the Research Institute of Artificial Intelligence (Center of Excellence of AI), has been appointed as an Adjunct Professor in the Faculty of Information Technology at the Victorian Institute of Technology (VIT), Melbourne, Australia.

This prestigious two-year appointment is a testament to Dr. Kulkarni’s outstanding contributions to research and academia, particularly in the domains of optimisation, artificial intelligence, and computational intelligence. His expertise and leadership in these fields will further strengthen international academic collaborations and cutting-edge research initiatives between institutions.

MIT-WPU extends heartfelt congratulations to Dr. Kulkarni on this remarkable achievement and wishes him continued success in his academic and research endeavors.
