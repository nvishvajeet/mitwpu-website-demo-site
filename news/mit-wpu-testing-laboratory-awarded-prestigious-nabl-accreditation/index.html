Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) has been awarded NABL accreditation (TC-16042) for its Testing Laboratory by the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL).

This significant achievement, in alignment with ISO/IEC 17025:2017 standards, covers 51 specialised tests utilising advanced equipment across five laboratories within the School of Engineering and Technology. This accreditation highlights MIT-WPU’s steadfast dedication to maintaining the highest standards of quality, precision, and professionalism.

Advancing Learning and Research : Students and researchers benefit from access to cutting-edge testing facilities, enhancing both academic and research outcomes.

Reinforcing University Credibility : The NABL accreditation further solidifies MIT-WPU’s reputation for excellence and strict adherence to regulatory standards.

Fostering Industry Collaboration : This recognition expands opportunities for consultancy, industry partnerships, and future growth.
