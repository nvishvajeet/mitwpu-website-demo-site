MIT-WPU Signs MoU with Elevanta Systems Pvt. Ltd. to Strengthen Industry-Academia Collaboration

MIT-WPU partners with Nirvaa Solutions to introduce India's first M.Tech in Data Centre Systems Engineering, preparing professionals for next-generation infrastructure.

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Elevanta Systems Pvt. Ltd. to foster stronger industry-academia collaboration and enhance experiential learning opportunities for students.

The partnership aims to create a collaborative ecosystem through student internships, live industry projects, technical training, skill development initiatives, research collaborations, and knowledge exchange. It will also facilitate greater industry interaction, enabling students to gain practical exposure and develop competencies aligned with evolving industry requirements.

The MoU reinforces MIT-WPU's commitment to bridging the gap between academia and industry by equipping students with industry-relevant skills and preparing them for successful professional careers.
