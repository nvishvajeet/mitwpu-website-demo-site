MIT-WPU Champions Sustainable Transport with Eco-Friendly E-Scooters

MIT‑WPU promotes sustainable transport with eco‑friendly e‑scooters, cutting carbon emissions and enhancing mobility.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) has taken a major step toward sustainability by becoming the first institution in Pune to introduce an eco-friendly transportation solution. In collaboration with Genesys Electric Motors Pvt. Ltd. and Maha Metro Rail Corporation Ltd., MIT-WPU has launched a fleet of electric scooters (E-Scoots), providing students with a greener, convenient option for last-mile commutes.

This forward-thinking initiative not only simplifies commuting for students but also reflects MIT-WPU’s long-standing commitment to sustainability. The university’s efforts extend beyond academics, aiming to create a campus environment where eco-consciousness thrives. The introduction of E-Scoots is just one of many steps MIT-WPU is taking to integrate green technologies and innovative solutions that reduce its carbon footprint.

With this initiative, MIT-WPU showcases its leadership in environmental stewardship. By offering electric transportation options, the university is empowering students to adopt sustainable practices in their daily lives. E-Scoots offer an emission-free, energy-efficient alternative to traditional transportation, reducing traffic congestion and air pollution around the campus.

This project is part of MIT-WPU’s broader strategy to promote a cleaner, healthier future. It complements the university’s initiatives in renewable energy, waste management, and green infrastructure, aligning with its vision of a world rooted in peace, progress, and environmental sustainability. Stay connected as MIT-WPU continues to champion eco-friendly innovations, paving the way for greener lifestyles and a more sustainable future for all!
