MIT-WPU takes immense pride in celebrating its faculty members who continue to make their mark in the international research community. Featured in the World’s Top 2% Scientists list by Stanford University and Elsevier, their achievements highlight the institution’s commitment to fostering academic excellence and impactful research.

  • Dr. Shvetank Bhatt, Associate Professor, Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences, SoHST, MIT-WPU, Pune – Recognised for the second consecutive year in Clinical Medicine (Pharmacology & Pharmacy). His impactful research, publications, and academic contributions stand as a testament to his dedication to advancing healthcare and pharmaceutical sciences.
  • Dr. Pralhad Pesode, Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, MIT-WPU – Honoured in Enabling & Strategic Technologies, highlighting his innovative research and contributions to the field of engineering.

These global accolades not only celebrate the excellence of our faculty but also reinforce the rising visibility of Indian academia on an international platform. Their achievements inspire young scholars to pursue meaningful, interdisciplinary research that drives progress and innovation across diverse fields.
