MIT-WPU School of Business Ranks 9th Nationally

We are proud to announce that the School of Business at MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) has been ranked 9th in India in the prestigious TIMES BBA Education Ranking Survey 2024. This recognition is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence in business education. The ranking is based on the rigorous i3RC-BRM model developed by i3RC insights, which evaluates institutions through a comprehensive 360-degree perspective. This proprietary methodology assesses BBA programs across three critical dimensions: resources, effort, and outcomes. The resources dimension evaluates the quality and availability of facilities, faculty, and learning materials.

Our inclusion in the top 10 of India reflects the outstanding quality of our academic programs, the dedication of our faculty, and the exceptional achievements of our students. We remain committed to nurturing future leaders and innovators who will make a significant impact in the global business landscape. Explore the world of opportunities at MIT-WPU School of Business and join us in our journey towards excellence.
