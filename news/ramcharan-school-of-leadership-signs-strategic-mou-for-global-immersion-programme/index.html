Ram Charan School of Leadership Signs Strategic MoU for Global Immersion Programme

MIT-WPU's Ramcharan School of Leadership signs a strategic MOU for its Global Immersion Programme — giving students access to world-class international universities and dual certifications.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

The Ram Charan School of Leadership (RSOL) at MIT World Peace University, Pune, has taken a significant step towards enhancing global exposure for its students through a strategic international collaboration.

In the presence of Hon’ble Vice Chancellor Dr. R. M. Chitnis, MIT World Peace University, Pune formalised a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the University of Twente (Netherlands) and Recess College (UK).

This collaboration introduces a 2-week Global Immersion Programme for RSOL MBA students, designed to provide a well-rounded international learning experience. Key features of the programme include :

  • A customised Leadership Live Lab Simulation
  • International academic and industry exposure
  • A 3-credit certification integrated into the MBA curriculum

Through this initiative, students will gain global perspectives, engage with real-world leadership challenges, and develop skills essential for today’s dynamic business environment.

This partnership reflects RSOL’s commitment to designing transformative learning experiences that extend beyond the classroom—integrating experiential learning, global exposure, and industry relevance into its academic framework.

MIT-WPU looks forward to the impactful opportunities and transformative journeys this initiative will create for its students.
