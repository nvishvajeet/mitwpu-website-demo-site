MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) has taken a significant step forward in fostering international academic cooperation by formalising a partnership with SRH University, Germany. This strategic alliance was solidified through a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed by Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Executive President of MIT-WPU, and Dr. Thorsten Bagschik, Managing Director of SRH University. The delegation also included Prof. Dr. Gerd Moeckel, Vice Academic Director of the School of Technology & Architecture at SRH University.

Unlocking Opportunities for Excellence

This partnership is designed to enhance academic excellence and global cooperation in higher education between India and Germany. Key initiatives include :

Faculty and Student Exchanges : Providing opportunities for educators and students to share knowledge and experiences across borders.

Joint Research Initiatives : Collaborative projects that leverage the expertise of both institutions to drive innovation.

Articulation Agreements : Streamlining academic pathways for students to pursue higher education in both countries.

Summer School Opportunities : Offering immersive academic experiences in Germany through academic immersion programmes.

This collaboration marks a significant milestone in fostering international academic cooperation between India and Germany. By bringing together the best minds from both nations, MIT-WPU and SRH University aim to create a global network of scholars and researchers who can address the challenges of the future with innovative solutions.
