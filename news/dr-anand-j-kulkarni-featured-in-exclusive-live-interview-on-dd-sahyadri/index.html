MIT-WPU is proud to share that Dr. Anand J Kulkarni, Professor & Associate Director, Research Institute of Artificial Intelligence (Center of Excellence of AI) was recently featured in an exclusive LIVE interview on DD Sahyadri as part of the special discussion series 'Aatmanirbhar Bharat' on 07 February 2025. The discussion focused on India’s progress in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the India AI Mission.

During the LIVE session, Dr. Kulkarni shared his expert insights on how AI is shaping India’s future, driving self-reliance, and positioning the country as a global leader in AI. He elaborated on the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence across industries, the role of AI in national development, and the emerging opportunities in this rapidly evolving field.

This prestigious feature highlights our university’s commitment toward fostering cutting-edge research, innovation, and thought leadership in Artificial Intelligence and technology. Dr. Kulkarni’s participation in this national-level dialogue highlights the growing significance of AI in India’s technological and economic progress.
