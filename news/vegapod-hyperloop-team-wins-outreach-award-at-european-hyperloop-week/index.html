Vegapod Hyperloop Team Wins Outreach Award at European Hyperloop Week

MIT‑WPU’s Vegapod Hyperloop team clinches the Outreach Award at European Hyperloop Week for impactful community engagement.

We are thrilled to announce that the Vegapod Hyperloop team from MIT World Peace University has made history as the first team from Asia to win an award in the Outreach Category at the prestigious European Hyperloop Week (EHW) 2024. This award is a testament to their exceptional efforts in promoting the values and potential of hyperloop technology to a global audience.

Originating from our esteemed School of Mechanical Engineering, Team Vegapod Hyperloop has been showcasing their innovative work on the international stage since 2019. Their participation in significant events such as the SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competition has placed them at the forefront of this revolutionary field.

Their dedication to advancing hyperloop technology is not only a matter of technical prowess but also of inspiring others to see the transformative possibilities of this cutting-edge transportation system. The team's continuous engagement and educational initiatives have greatly contributed to the broader understanding and acceptance of hyperloop technology.

We extend our heartiest congratulations to all the team members and their mentors for their unwavering dedication and hard work. Their achievements reflect the spirit of innovation and excellence that MIT World Peace University strives to foster in all its students and faculty. We are immensely proud of their accomplishments and look forward to their future contributions to the field of hyperloop technology.
