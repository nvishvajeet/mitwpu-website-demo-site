Dr. Rahul V. Karad Conferred with “I AM PEACEMAKER CHAMPION AWARD”

Dr. Rahul V. Karad has been honored with the I Am Peacemaker Champion Award, recognizing his leadership, contributions to education, and commitment to promoting peace and global harmony.

MIT-WPU, Pune proudly celebrates a moment of immense honour and global recognition as Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Executive President, MIT-WPU, was conferred with the prestigious “I AM PEACEMAKER CHAMPION AWARD” at the Billionaires for Peace Conclave held in Mumbai.

The award was presented in the esteemed presence of four Nobel Peace Laureates — Hon’ble H.E. Dr. José Manuel Ramos-Horta, Hon’ble Houcine Abassi, Hon’ble Prof. Mohan Munasinghe, and Hon’ble Kailash Satyarthi.

The conclave was chaired by Dr. Huzaifa Khorakiwala, Chairman of the Conclave, and witnessed the presence of distinguished dignitaries including Shri Bhaiyuji Joshi of RSS and Dr. Lokesh Muni, Prominent Jain Guru.

The prestigious conclave was inaugurated by Former President of India, Hon’ble Shri Ram Nath Kovind Ji, and Governor of Maharashtra, Hon’ble Jishnu Dev Varma Ji.

This recognition reflects Dr. Rahul V. Karad’s continued commitment towards peace, humanity, value-based leadership, and societal transformation, making the entire MIT-WPU family proud.
