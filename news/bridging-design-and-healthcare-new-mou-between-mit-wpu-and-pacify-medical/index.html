Bridging Design and Healthcare: New MoU Between MIT-WPU and Pacify Medical

Discover the new MIT-WPU School of Design & Pacify Medical MoU, transforming healthcare through design innovation, live projects, and cutting-edge wound care technology.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

We are excited to share that the MIT-WPU School of Design, in collaboration with CIAP, has entered into a Memorandum of Understanding with Pacify Medical Technologies—a trailblazing healthcare startup transforming wound care through their patented Skin Spray Technology.

This partnership represents a major stride in strengthening the connection between industry and academia, with a mutual focus on:

  • Live Project Engagement
  • Internship Opportunities
  • Hands-on Learning Opportunities
  • Startup Ecosystem Exposure
  • Innovation & Research

We extend our gratitude to Mr. Saiprasad Poyarekar, CEO of Pacify Medical Technologies Pvt. Ltd., and his dedicated team for their commitment to this impactful partnership. We also sincerely thank our leadership for their vision and steadfast support, which make such collaborations possible.

Together, we are shaping the future of healthcare innovation through the power of design.
