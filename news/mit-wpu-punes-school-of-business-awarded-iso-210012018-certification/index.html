MIT-WPU, Pune’s School of Business Awarded ISO 21001:2018 Certification

The School of Business at MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, has been awarded the prestigious ISO 21001:2018 Certification, reaffirming its commitment to excellence in educational quality and institutional effectiveness.

The certification recognises the School's successful implementation of the Educational Organization Management System (EOMS), reflecting its focus on standardising academic and administrative processes while fostering a culture of continuous improvement.

This achievement underscores the School of Business's dedication to delivering quality education through robust academic practices, efficient governance, and learner-centric processes. It also reinforces MIT-WPU's commitment to maintaining globally recognised standards in higher education and enhancing the overall learning experience for its students.
