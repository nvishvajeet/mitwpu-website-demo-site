MIT-WPU’s School of Engineering & Technology Makes Its Mark in 2025 Times Rankings

School of Engineering & Technology at MIT-WPU, Pune, has been recognised among India’s top engineering institutions in the Times Engineering Ranking Survey 2025.

School of Engineering & Technology at MIT-WPU, Pune, has been recognised among India’s top engineering institutions in the Times Engineering Ranking Survey 2025. The school has been ranked 17th among the Top 175 Engineering Institutes in India, reinforcing its standing as one of the premier engineering institutions in the country.

In addition, MIT-WPU has also secured 15th position among the Top 125 Private Engineering Institutes and achieved 4th place in the West Zone’s Top 25 Engineering Institutes. These rankings reflect the university’s strong focus on quality education, research innovation, and industry collaboration.

The Times Engineering Ranking Survey evaluates institutes based on faculty qualifications, research contributions, infrastructure, and placement outcomes. MIT-WPU’s performance is a testament to its commitment to fostering academic excellence and preparing students for successful engineering careers.

The university’s engineering programmes emphasise holistic development, integrating peace and sustainability principles to nurture responsible and ethical professionals. MIT-WPU looks forward to building on this achievement and continuing to contribute to India’s engineering education landscape.
