We are thrilled to announce a significant milestone in our journey towards academic excellence and global collaboration! On Thursday, February 20, 2025, a delegation from Nottingham Trent University (NTU), UK, visited Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University to explore new avenues of partnership and mutual growth.

Delegation Included

  • Dr. Clare Newstead, Associate Director of NTU Global, Nottingham Trent University
  • Dr. Gareth Cave, Academic in the Department of Chemistry and Forensics, Nottingham Trent University

A New Era of Collaboration

This visit marks the beginning of a promising partnership aimed at enhancing faculty and student activities through :

  • Visiting Research Scholar Programme : Fostering research collaborations and knowledge exchange.
  • Collaborative Academic Projects : Joint projects that drive innovation and solve real-world challenges.
  • Pathway and Summer Programmes : Offering students unique learning experiences abroad.
  • Knowledge-Sharing Sessions, Workshops, and Masterclasses : Enriching academic experiences through shared expertise.

Engaging with Our Team

Driven by a shared vision of cross-cultural learning, Dr. Clare Newstead and Dr. Gareth Cave met with Dr. Nirupama Kocherlakota's (International Relations) and Dr. Anup Kale's (School of Sciences and Environmental Studies) teams to explore opportunities for strengthening academic ties and creating enriching experiences for students and faculty.

A Bright Future Ahead:

With this exciting collaboration underway, we anticipate this partnership will broaden horizons for our students and faculty, enriching their academic journeys and providing access to global opportunities.
