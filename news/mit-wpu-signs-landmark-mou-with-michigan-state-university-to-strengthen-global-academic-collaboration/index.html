MIT-WPU Signs Landmark MoU with Michigan State University to Strengthen Global Academic Collaboration

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, has further strengthened its global academic footprint by signing a landmark Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Michigan State University (MSU), USA, marking the beginning of a strategic partnership focused on advancing excellence in education, research, innovation, and international engagement.

The MoU was formalised during the visit of Dr. Parul Jadhav, Programme Director, Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (DoEEE), to Michigan State University, where extensive discussions were held with the university's leadership to explore opportunities for long-term institutional collaboration. The engagement reflects the shared commitment of both universities to fostering meaningful academic exchange, interdisciplinary research, innovation, and global learning.

The collaboration with Michigan State University marks another significant milestone in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering's (DoEEE) ongoing efforts to build strategic global partnerships that enrich teaching, research, and student experiences while contributing to the advancement of higher education on an international scale.
