Empowering students with future-ready skills, innovation spaces, and global opportunities.

MIT-WPU is proud to announce the inauguration of its iOS Development Centre, powered by Apple and Infosys. Launched on the birthday of our hon. Executive President, Dr. Rahul V. Karad, this milestone reflects our commitment to pioneering education, innovation, and global opportunities for students.

What Makes the iOS Development Centre Exceptional?

  • State-of-the-Art Infrastructure : Equipped with the latest iMacs, MacBooks. iPhones, iPads and Apple Watch.
  • Collaborative Learning Spaces : Huddle rooms, brainstorming zones, and modern conference facilities designed to nurture creativity and teamwork.
  • Industry-Aligned Curriculum : Developed with leading industry experts to ensure students are prepared for real-world challenges.
  • Internship Opportunities : Exclusive pathways with top firms, bridging the gap between academics and professional application.

At MIT-WPU, this initiative is more than just a centre—it’s a launchpad for innovation. By integrating academic excellence with cutting-edge technology and strong industry mentorship, we are preparing students to become innovators, entrepreneurs, and leaders ready to thrive in the ever-evolving global technology landscape.

The inauguration also marks the launch of the iOS Student Developer Program, powered by Apple and Infosys, a pioneering effort to equip students with advanced skills, a global mindset, and the confidence to excel in the digital age.
