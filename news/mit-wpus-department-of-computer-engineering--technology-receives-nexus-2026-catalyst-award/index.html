MIT-WPU's Department of Computer Engineering & Technology Receives NEXUS 2026 Catalyst Award

The Department of Computer Engineering & Technology (DCET), MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, has been conferred with the NEXUS 2026 Catalyst Award – Innovation Hub Pioneer Award by L&T EduTech, Mumbai.

The Department of Computer Engineering & Technology (DCET), MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune, has been conferred with the NEXUS 2026 Catalyst Award – Innovation Hub Pioneer Award by L&T EduTech, Mumbai.

This prestigious recognition underscores the department's commitment to fostering innovation, academic excellence, and industry collaboration, empowering students with future-ready skills and transformative learning experiences.

The achievement reflects the collective efforts of faculty, students, and industry partners in driving impactful education and technological innovation, further strengthening MIT-WPU's position as a hub for excellence in engineering and technology.
