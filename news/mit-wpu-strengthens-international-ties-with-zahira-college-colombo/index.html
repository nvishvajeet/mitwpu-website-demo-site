MIT-WPU Strengthens International Ties with Zahira College, Colombo

MIT-WPU is proud to announce a new partnership with Zahira College in Colombo, Sri Lanka, to strengthen international academic ties.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) has taken a significant step forward in fostering international academic cooperation by formalising a partnership with Zahira College, Colombo, Sri Lanka. This landmark alliance was sealed through a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed by Ninad Sapre, Assistant Director-Admission, MIT-WPU, and Mr. Trizvy Marikkar, Principal of Zahira College.

The delegation from Zahira College, Colombo, Sri Lanka also included Mr. Fouzul Hameed, Chairman, Board of Governors, Mr. Dilshard Jiffry, Treasurer & Board Member, Mr. Ameer Hamza, Career Guidance Officer.

We express our sincere appreciation to Mr. Ismail Sadurdeen, Founder of the UNIVISER platform, whose unwavering support and friendship have been pivotal in making this collaboration a reality.

Through this partnership, we aim to enrich global perspectives, build a stronger foundation for international academic collaborations, and empower future leaders to make a positive impact worldwide.
