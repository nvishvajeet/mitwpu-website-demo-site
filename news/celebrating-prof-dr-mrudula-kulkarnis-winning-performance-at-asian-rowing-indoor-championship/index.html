Celebrating Prof. Dr. Mrudula Kulkarni’s Winning Performance at Asian Rowing Indoor Championship

MIT‑WPU’s Prof. Dr. Mrudula Kulkarni secures a winning performance at the Asian Rowing Indoor Championship—pride for the university.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

MIT World Peace University proudly congratulates Prof. Dr. Mrudula Sanjay Kulkarni (Department of Civil Engineering), who has brought great honor to both the university and the nation through her outstanding performance at the 2025 Asian Rowing Indoor Championship in Pattaya, Thailand.

Her stellar achievements includes:

  • Silver Medal in 2000m Women’s Singles with an impressive timing of 9 minutes 57.003 seconds
  • Silver Medal in 2000m Mixed Pair, finishing in 9 minutes 04.009 seconds
  • Silver Medal in the 4x500m Mixed Relay, with a time of 7 minutes 37.004 seconds
  • Bronze Medal in Masters Women’s Single 500m Sprint, completing the race in 2 minutes 9.002 seconds

Dr. Kulkarni’s extraordinary accomplishments on the international stage are a testament to her dedication, perseverance, and pursuit of excellence. Her achievements not only bring immense pride to MIT-WPU but also serve as a powerful inspiration to students, faculty, and the entire university.
