The national study tour for students of the School of Economics & Commerce (SoEC) was an enriching journey through the dynamic state of Gujarat, encompassing visits to Ahmedabad, GIFT City, Mundhra Port, and Anand. Under the guidance of seasoned trainers, students delved into the realms of effective living and leadership.

During their expedition, our students gleaned insights from eminent scholars at Pandit Deendayal University, the Institute of Rural Management in Anand, and GIFT City. Complementing these academic engagements were immersive visits to Mundhra Port and the Amul Dairy and Chocolate Factory. Exploring Gujarat's cultural and spiritual tapestry, students also embarked on enlightening visits to Sabarmati Ashram and the Akshardham temple.

This journey epitomised a harmonious fusion of academic rigour, industry exposure, and cultural immersion, furnishing our students with a superlative experiential learning odyssey.
