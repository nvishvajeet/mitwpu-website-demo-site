Department of Biosciences and Technology Signs MoU with Haystack Analytics

MIT-WPU's Department of Biosciences & Technology signs an MOU with Haystack Analytics - IIT Bombay's clinical genomics leader - unlocking research and internship access for biotech students.

Happenings

  • Happenings
  • News

The Department of Biosciences and Technology at MIT World Peace University, Pune, has recently signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Haystack Analytics, a genomics-driven diagnostics company focused on delivering clinically actionable insights through advanced sequencing technologies and automated analysis.

Haystack Analytics is dedicated to developing integrated solutions for real-world healthcare settings, enabling faster, standardized, and scalable genomic decision-making.

This collaboration aims to create a strong academic-industry interface, opening avenues for joint workshops, collaborative research projects, and knowledge exchange initiatives. These efforts will provide students and faculty with meaningful opportunities to engage with emerging advancements in genomics and healthcare technologies.

The partnership reflects MIT-WPU’s continued commitment to fostering industry-aligned learning, promoting research excellence, and enhancing the overall academic experience.

The University looks forward to a productive and impactful association that contributes to strengthening learning, research, and industry readiness among its students.
