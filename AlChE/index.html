Welcome to the AIChE (American Institute of Chemical Engineers) as a part of a global organisation dedicated to chemical engineering, the club offers an extensive range of resources and expertise that cater to professionals across various industries, including emerging fields like nanobiotechnology.

Established in December 2017, under the visionary guidance of Professor Datta Dandge, the AIChE MIT-WPU Student Club is a dynamic platform for students to experience growth and professional development. Our chapter facilitates involvement in professional programmes, fosters connections with other student chapters, and encourages engagement with established industry professionals.
