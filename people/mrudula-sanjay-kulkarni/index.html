Dr. Mrudula Sanjay Kulkarni

Education

  1. BE Civil, ME Civil -Structures, Ph D Civil Engineering

About

Dr. Mrudula S. Kulkarni is presently the In-Charge Principal of MIT-Pune, former Dean of Students Affairs at MIT-WPU, and a Professor at the School of Engineering and Technology at MIT-WPU and has headed the Department of Civil Engineering since its inception. Before this, she was the Professor and Head of the Department of Structural Engineering at MIT-WPU, Pune, till June 2017. Sancheti (2003-2007). She has a Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering, Pune University in 1990, and a Master's in Structural Engineering from Government College of Engineering Karad in 1994. She joined MIT as a teaching faculty in 1990 and has since taught various structural mechanics and design subjects at undergraduate and postgraduate levels. She has been instrumental in setting up the MIT CAD CAM CAE Design and Training centre (2000-2008). She was the PI of many funded research projects at international levels and had many academic collaborations with International Universities and Industries for the university. Her specific research expertise is Biomechanics, Precast Structure, Innovative Designs, and Construction Technology. She has worked with various administrative bodies at MIT-WPU as a member of the academic council, member of the management council, and has served in the capacity of elected Senate Member, member Board of Studies, and member Faculty of Engineering at Pune University.
