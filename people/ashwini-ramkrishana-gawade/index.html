Dr. Ashwini Ramkrishana Gawade

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Ashwini Gawade is a highly qualified academic with a diverse range of experience in the pharmaceutical industry. She holds a B. Pharm. (2011), M. Pharm (2013) and Ph.D (2020) from Pune University. After completing her studies, she joined Sai Life Sciences in Pune as a Research Associate, where she gained valuable experience in formulation development of innovative drug delivery systems, scale-up of formulations, and product technology transfer. Dr. Gawade is currently an Assistant Professor at Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences, School of Health Sciences and Technology, Dr. Vishwanath Karad’s MIT World Peace University, Pune. She has an impressive track record in research and has presented her work at various International and National conferences. Dr. Gawade has also published several research and review articles in National and International journals. She is a lifetime member of APTI, which reflects her commitment to the field of pharmacy.. Dr. Gawade's expertise in the pharmaceutical industry is vast. She has worked on different projects, including oncology projects, during her industrial tenure. She has sound knowledge of GMP, cGMP, and has experience in handling various instruments such as Double Beam UV Spectrophotometer, Brookfield Viscometer, Rotary Tablet Compression Machine, Digital Tablet Dissolution Test Apparatus, R & D Coater, Lyophilizer, and Fluidized bed coater. Additionally, she has hands-on experience with software like PCP Disso and Design Expert Software.
