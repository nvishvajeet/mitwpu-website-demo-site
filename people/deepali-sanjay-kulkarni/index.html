Deepali Sanjay Kulkarni

Assistant Professor

Department of Materials Science & Engineering

About

Mrs. Deepali Kulkarni has a vast teaching experience of 28 years in the field of polymer materials, processing of composites, rubber technology, and design of equipment and machine elements. She has also conducted research on topics such as the effect of egg shell powder and fish bone powder content on the mechanical performance of PP, the effect of Nano Silica on phase transformation from Form-II to Form-I of Polybutene-1, and polyfurfuryl-based polymer concrete.
