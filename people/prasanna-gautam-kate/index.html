Prof. Prasanna Gautam Kate

Associate Professor of Practice

Education

  1. B.E. Mechanical Engineering from Shivaji University M. Design in Design from IIT Guwahati

About

UX Consultant with 18+ years’ experience leading large-scale design engagements in national design agencies and global software service organisations and majorly worked in Tata Consultancy Services. With expertise in end-to-end User-Centred Design, RFPs (Response for Proposals). And I conducted several stakeholder & UX workshops internationally, did secondary/primary research internationally across the UK, Sweden, Europe, and Taiwan etc and conducted remote user testing sessions. With this vast industrial experience, I am committed to bridge the gap between industry and academia by mentoring design students and contributing to the growth of MIT-WPU, Pune.

Publications
