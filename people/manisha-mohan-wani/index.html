Prof. Manisha Mohan Wani

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Manisha Mohan Wani is an accomplished electrical engineer and educator with over 11 years of teaching experience. She graduated with a degree in Electrical Engineering in 2004 from Pune Vidyrthi Gruha’s College of Engineering, Pune University, and completed her ME in Electrical Power Systems in 2012 from the same institution. Since August 1, 2007, Prof. Wani has been working at MIT-WPU, where she has taken on a range of academic and administrative responsibilities. She is a permanent member of the Institution of Engineers, India and has served as a class teacher, ISO & NBA coordinator of CEES department, felicitation committee head of CEES department, and ARC officer for FE, direct second year, and ME admissions, among other duties. Additionally, Prof. Wani has been a paper setting committee member at SPPU and MIT-WPU and a university flying squad member at MIT-WPU. Her expertise in electrical engineering and her dedication to teaching have made her an asset to the academic community.

Research interests

Electrical Power systems
