Dr. Ameya Anil Patil

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Ameya Patil has more than 12 years of Teaching experience and 3.5 years of Industrial experience. He teaches subjects related to Finance and International Business. He has 25 Scopus indexed research articles to his credit and has authored more than 25 books. He has been invited as a keynote speaker at several national conferences. He is a member of the Indian Institute of Banking and Finance (IIBF) and ASCB(UK). 18 of his research works have appeared in Scopus and ABDC journals, and with reputed publishers such as Wiley, Inderscience, Taylor & Francis, IGI Global, Emerald and Springer.

Research interests

Finance, Management, International Business
