Dr. Pallavi Prashant Tatpate

Assistant Professor

Department of Materials Science & Engineering

Education

  1. PhD in Chemical Engineering from Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University Pune

About

Dr. Pallavi Tatpate is an experienced academician and researcher with a background in Chemical and Polymer Engineering. She has more than 10 years of academic experience and 1 year of industrial experience. She teaches courses such as Momentum Transfer, Process Heat Transfer, Mass Transfer, Process Calculations, and Process Instrumentation. Dr. Tatpate’s research work is focused on the membrane formation, characterization and application for wastewater and effluent treatment. Also her research interests include adsorption, membrane separation, polymer composite, green composite, waste management, sustainable processes, and renewable energy.

Research interests

Adsorption, membrane separation, polymer composite, green composite, waste management, sustainable processes and renewable energy
