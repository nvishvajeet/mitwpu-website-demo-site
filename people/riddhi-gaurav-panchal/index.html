Dr. Riddhi Gaurav Panchal

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Riddhi Panchal holds an MPhil in Computer Science from Gujarat and a Ph.D. from Savitribai Phule Pune University. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor and Program Head for M.Sc. Data Science and Big Data Analytics in the School of Computer Science at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University in Pune, India. she brings over 16 years of extensive experience in both teaching and research. She has made significant contributions to the field, authoring more than 15 research articles published in renowned international journals and conferences. Additionally, she has authored a book chapter and a textbook focused on Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning. Her research expertise encompasses areas such as data science, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning.
