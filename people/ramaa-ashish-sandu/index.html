Prof. Ramaa Ashish Sandu

Associate Professor

About

Prof. Ramaa Sandu is working as an Associate Professor in the department of Mathematics and Statistics at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University in Pune, India. She has a vast experience of teaching Engineering Mathematics since 1992 & completed 27 years in MIT

Research interests

Differential equations and Laplace transforms.

Academic work

  • 1.Presented research paper in “International Conference on Applications of Fluid Dynamics (ICAFD)”, 13-15 December 2018 at VIT Vellore.
  • 2.Presented research paper on “Homotopy Perturbation Transform Method” in National Conference on Pure and Applied Mathematics(NCPAM),17-19 May 2017 at COEP ,Pune.
  • 3.Convener for “National Workshop of Computational Mathematics in Engineering and Engineering Sciences” with the sponsorship of Rs 2 lacs from MHRD-- 21-24 December 2009.
  • 4.Best Paper Award in National Level Student’s Research conference –13 March 2021 organized by School of Computer Science, MITWPU.
  • 5.Convenor for International Symposium on Vedik Mathematics at MITWPU in April 2022.
  • 6.Sangeet Visharad in Hindustani classical music from Gandharva Mahavidyalaya.
