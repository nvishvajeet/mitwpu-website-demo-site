Dr. Nathrao Bhimrao Jadhav

Assistant Professor and Assistant Director, Admissions

About

Nathrao B. Jadhav is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering with 17 years of teaching experience. He has an impressive academic background with a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, a Master's degree in Digital Electronics and Advanced Communication Systems, and a Ph.D. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University. His research interests lie in the field of Photonic Integrated Circuit, Unmanned Aerial Vehicle, Image Processing, and Optical Character Recognition. Nathrao has published seven research papers and received one patent grant for his innovative work in these areas. With his extensive knowledge and expertise, he is highly regarded by his colleagues and students alike. Nathrao is also actively involved in various academic and research activities and is always on the lookout for opportunities to contribute to the advancement of technology in his field.

Research interests

Photonic Integrated Circuit, Unmanned Aerial Vehicle, Image Processing, Optical Character Recognition.
