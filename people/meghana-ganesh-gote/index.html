Dr. Meghana Ganesh Gote

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Meghana Gote earned her PhD from the University of Mumbai, specializing in ionic liquids. With approximately 15 years of academic experience, she now serves as the Program Director for the Department of Chemistry. Dr. Gote possesses extensive practical knowledge in synthetic organic chemistry…

Research interests

Synthetic organic chemistry, catalysis, water remediation, and asymmetric synthesis.
