Dr. Anil Tukaram Pawar

Associate Professor

Education

  1. M. Pharm, PhD

About

Dr. Anil Pawar is an Associate Professor at the School of Health Sciences and Technology, Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, with over 17 years of experience in academic teaching, research, and industry. Pharm from Savitribai Phule Pune University, an M. Pharm from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Karnataka, and a Ph. D. in Pharmacology. He has guided M. Pharm students and is currently supervising Ph. D. scholars. Dr. Pawar has also received consultancy grants, reflecting his engagement in applied research and industry collaboration, and has established strong national and international research collaborations. He has received multiple awards for excellence in teaching and research, including the Ideal Teacher Award by Maharashtra Academy of Engineering and Educational Research (MAEER), Pune.
