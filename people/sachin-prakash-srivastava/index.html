Dr. Sachin Prakash Srivastava

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Sachin Prakash Srivastava is presently working as an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, India. Before that. He obtained a Ph.D. (Management) and, MBA (Finance), B.Tech (Mechanical Engineering). He is a life member of the Indian Political Economy Association. His research areas include Initial public offering (IPO), Blockchain, and sustainability.
