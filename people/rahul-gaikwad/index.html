Prof. Rahul Gaikwad

Assistant Professor

About

Rahul Gaikwad is an illustrator and design tutor with over 7 years of combined experience in the education and creative industries. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Fine Art from Sir J.J. School of Art and a Master's in Illustration and Animation from Coventry University, UK. His professional work spans across diverse domains such as illustration, asset building, animation, character design, branding, graphic design and storyboarding. Showcasing his versatility and command over visual storytelling. Rahul's teaching approach blends industry insight with academic rigor, helping students build strong creative foundations. In 2012, he was honored as a young cartoonist by the legendary R.K. Laxman through a recognition by the Times of India, marking a significant early milestone in his career. Passionate about both craft and mentorship, Rahul continues to inspire and guide aspiring designers through his practice and pedagogy.
