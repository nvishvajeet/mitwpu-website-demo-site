Dr. Vinayak Sudhakar Wadgaonkar

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Vinayak S. Wadgaonkar has completed his doctoral study from L.I.T. nagpur. His doctoral study was focused on Studies on Removal of Heavy Metals By Low Cost Adsorbents. During his doctoral study, he has also carried out collaborative research work in the area of waste water treatment and environmental engineering. He has also presented her research work in several national and international conferences. He pursued his post-graduation (M. E.) in Chemical Engineering from Shivaji University and completed his graduation (B.E) in Chemical Engineering from Amravati University, Amravati. His research area of interests includes natural gas engineering, enhanced oil recovery, flow assurance, waste water treatment, hydrogen production and environmental engineering. Presently he is teaching basic and applied courses at undergraduate, post graduate. He is also recognized Phd guide in Petroleum and Chemical Engineering. He also mentors students for their research project. Ph.D. from Laxminarayan Institute of Technology (L.I.T.), Nagpur.
