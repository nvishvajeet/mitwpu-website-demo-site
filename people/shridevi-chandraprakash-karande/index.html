Dr. Shridevi Chandraprakash Karande

Associate Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D: Ph.D. In Computer Engineering - DVK MITWPU, Pune
  2. M.Tech: M.E. In Computer Engineering - Savitribai Phule Pune University
  3. B.Tech: B.Tech. In Computer Engineering - Swami Ramanand Tirth Marathwada University, Nanded

About

Dr. Shridevi Karande has been working as an assistant professor at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University since 2005. With over 21 years of experience, she has managed various academic responsibilities and placement-related tasks. She has authored more than 20 research papers and articles in international journals and conferences. Her research interests include Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, and Healthcare Analytics.
