Dr. Milan Yadav

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. PhD in Commerce- Central University of South Bihar

About

Dr. Milan Yadav is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Commerce and Business studies MIT World Peace University, Pune. D. from the Department of Commerce and Business Studies, Central University of South Bihar with a specialization in Finance. She is UGC-NET and JRF qualified and has prior teaching experience at Galgotias University. Her research is published in reputed Scopus- and ABDC- indexed journals, and She also serves as a reviewer for leading international journals.
