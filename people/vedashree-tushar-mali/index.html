Dr. Vedashree Tushar Mali

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Vedashree Mali is an Assistant Professor at the MIT-WPU School of Business (WPU-SOB) in Pune, India. She completed her Ph.D. in Finance and an MBA in Finance from Savitribai Phule Pune University. At WPU-SOB, she teaches courses such as Corporate Finance, Financial Modeling, and Corporate Valuation. Her research interests include Microfinance, Financial Inclusion, and Corporate Finance. With her expertise in these areas, she has contributed to multiple research publications and conference presentations. Dr. Vedashree has also participated in various workshops, seminars, and training programs related to Finance and Financial Modeling.

Research interests

Microfinance, Financial Inclusion, Corporate Finance
