Dr. Siraj Ahmed Bhatkar

Program Director

About

Dr. Siraj Ahmed Bhatkar is a prominent figure in the field of Petroleum Engineering, currently serving as the Program Director at the Department of Petroleum Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University (MIT WPU), Pune. With over 15 years of experience in academia and research, Dr. Bhatkar has made significant contributions to the advancement of petroleum engineering education and practices. Ph.D. from Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU). Dr. Bhatkar holds a Ph.D. in Petroleum Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University, complemented by a Master of Engineering in Petroleum Engineering from Maharashtra Institute of Technology and a Bachelor of Engineering in Chemical Engineering from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology. His specialization encompasses Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR), drilling engineering, artificial lift systems, and produced water management. Since joining MIT WPU in 2010, Dr. Bhatkar has been instrumental in curriculum development and student placements, successfully coordinating opportunities for over 250 students. He has chaired multiple subjects within the Petroleum Engineering curriculum and has held various administrative roles, including admission coordinator for academic collaborations. Dr. Bhatkar is also an active researcher with a focus on clean energy solutions and optimizing oil production processes. His notable publications include studies on dynamic adsorption in alkaline surfactant polymer flooding and optimization techniques for produced water quality. His research contributions have garnered recognition in international journals, covering topics such as EOR, petroleum produced water treatment, and drilling well design. He is affiliated with several professional organizations, including the Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE) and the International Association of Drilling Contractors (IADC), where he serves as a faculty advisor. Through his commitment to education, research, and industry collaboration, Dr. Siraj Bhatkar continues to be a pivotal force in shaping the future of Petroleum Engineering at MIT WPU. His dedication not only enhances the academic landscape but also contributes to sustainable practices within the petroleum industry.
