Dr. Deobrat Singh

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Deobrat Singh is an Assistant Professor of Physics. He completed his Ph.D. from the University of Delhi in theoretical High Energy Physics. His research interests lie in the field of Theoretical High Energy Physics. Dr. Singh has published several papers in various international journals and conferences. Some of his notable research publications include: Quantum effects in topological and Schwarzschild de Sitter brane: Aspects of torsion on (DD)4-brane universe"" by Richa Kapoor, Supriya Kar, and Deobrat Singh, published in the International Journal of Modern Physics D24, No:2 (2015) 15 50015. CFT6 Bulk/Boundary AdS5 Q Correspondence and Emergent Gravity"" by R. Nitish, Deobrat Singh, and Supriya Kar, published in Physica Scripta. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1088/1402-4896/ab144e ArXiv:1612.01065 Torsion geometries in U (1) gauge theory on D5-brane"" by Deobrat Singh, Richa Kapoor, and Supriya Kar, published in Springer Proceedings in Physics Vol.174, Ch.77, pg.507-512, ISBN 978-3-319-25617-7. Dr. Singh's research contributions have been recognized in the academic community, and he has an impressive citation record on Google Scholar and Scopus. He also has an ORCID ID with publications listed in his profile.

Research interests

High-energy theory, gravitation, and fundamental interactions.
