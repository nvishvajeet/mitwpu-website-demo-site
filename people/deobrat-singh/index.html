Dr. Deobrat Singh

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Deobrat Singh is an Assistant Professor of Physics. He completed his Ph.D. from the University of Delhi in theoretical High Energy Physics. His research interests lie in the field of Theoretical High Energy Physics. Dr. Singh has published several papers in various international journals and conferences. Some of his notable research publications include:

Research interests

High-energy theory, gravitation, and fundamental interactions.
