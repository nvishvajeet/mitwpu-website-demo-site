Dr. Amit Mishra

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Amit, an accomplished academician with over 20 years of experience in India and overseas, has a proven track record in university settings. Proficient in Department Administration, Computer Science, Software Engineering, Databases, Management, Networking and Organizational Development he holds a PhD degree. Currently, he is associated with Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT-WPU in Pune, India, following over 17 years of academic service abroad.

His research focuses on Computer Science, Machine Learning, Software Engineering, and Web Development, reflecting his expertise in these areas. Dr. Amit is an active member of the Computer Science Teacher Association (CSTA) and the International Association of Engineers (IAENG). Moreover, he contributes significantly to academia as an Editor for esteemed journals and a reviewer for various conferences.

Beyond his professional achievements, Dr. Amit stands out as a dedicated and experienced educator, driven by a passion for teaching and a proven ability to motivate students to excel. He excels in crafting captivating lesson plans, integrating technology, and employing innovative teaching methodologies to instil a culture of learning among students.
