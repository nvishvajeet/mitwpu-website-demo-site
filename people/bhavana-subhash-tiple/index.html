Dr. Bhavana Subhash Tiple

Associate Professor

About

Bhavana Tiple is a highly accomplished academician and researcher with over 20 years of experience in the field of computer engineering. She graduated with first-class honors in BE (Comp) from SGGS, Nanded in 1996 and completed her ME from PICT Pune in 2003. She completed her Ph.D. from RTM University. Her areas of specialization include Data Mining and Software Engineering and Design, and she has published several national and international research papers. Bhavana has also guided numerous UG and PG level projects and served on various administrative committees at her department and institute.

Research interests

Deep Leaning, Machine Learning, Data analytics, software Engineering
