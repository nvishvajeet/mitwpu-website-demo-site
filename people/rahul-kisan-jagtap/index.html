Dr. Rahul Kisan Jagtap

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Rahul Jagtap is an Assistant Professor in the School of Engineering and Technology at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune. He completed his M. Tech. and Ph.D. from Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat. With over seven years of experience in teaching and research, Dr. Jagtap has also worked as a Junior Research Fellow on a SERB-DST funded project for more than 2.5 years. He has published and presented more than 24 research papers in reputed international journals and conferences.

Research interests

"Incremental Sheet Forming, Additive Manufacturing, CAD/CAM, Manufacturing Engineering"
