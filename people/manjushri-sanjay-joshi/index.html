Prof. Manjushri Sanjay Joshi

Lecturer

About

Mrs. Manjushri Joshi is an experienced educator and Assistant Professor at MIT-WPU School of Polytechnic. With 20 years of teaching experience, she has worked as a Sr. Selection Grade Lecturer at MAEER’S MIT Polytechnic, Pune since August 1997. She holds a M.Tech degree in Communication System and a B.E. in Instrumentation. Her areas of expertise include PEC, DCO, ACO, MEC, EEN, DT, and DCS. As an accomplished instructor, she is dedicated to imparting knowledge and skills to her students, and has helped numerous students succeed in their academic pursuits.

Research interests

IoT,Wireless Communication ,Mechatronics
