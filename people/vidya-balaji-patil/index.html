Dr. Vidya Balaji Patil

Assistant Professor

About

In a variety of areas, including Python programming, machine learning, microprocessors, digital electronics, computer architecture, and network security, my teaching strategy is focused on conceptual clarity, hands-on learning, and problem-based learning. Through projects, coding exercises, and using real world analogy, I encourage students to apply theoretical knowledge to practical issues. I want to develop technically proficient and socially conscious engineers by encouraging curiosity, creativity, and ethical responsibility. This strategy is consistent with MIT-WPU's objective to merge academics, technology, research, and social innovation for societal impact and global peace, as well as its vision of "Powering the Future" through education, innovation, and culture.
