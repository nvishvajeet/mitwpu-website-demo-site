About

Dr. Prashant S. Lahane is an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science Engineering at MIT-WPU, Pune, with over 17 years of teaching and academic administration experience.

His contributions include academic coordination, industry collaboration, NAIP training, and conducting major national examinations such as JEE, GATE, MHT-CET, and AIAPGET.

He has published 42 research papers and holds 5 patents in areas including machine learning, EEG-based stress detection, and intelligent systems, focusing on industry-oriented learning, innovation, and student skill development
