Dr. Parineeta Das

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Parineeta Das has completed her doctoral study at National Institute of Technology Nagaland (NIT Nagaland). Her doctoral study focused on dendrimers, the emerging polymeric architectures having several interesting features suitable for application in many diverse fields including biomedical…

Research interests

Polymer science, biopolymers, biocatalysis, dendrimers, green synthesis, anticorrosive coatings, biodiesel, and computational toxicology.
