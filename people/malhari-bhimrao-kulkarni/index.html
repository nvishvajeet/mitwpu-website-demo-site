Dr. Malhari Bhimrao Kulkarni

Program Coordinator (MS & E) & Associate Professor

Department of Materials Science & Engineering

Education

  1. PhD in Polymer Technology from ICT Mumbai

About

Dr. Malhari B Kulkarni is an Associate Professor with a highly accomplished background in Chemical and Polymer Engineering. E in Chemical Engineering and a Ph. D in Polymer Engineering & Technology. He is currently involved in teaching both Undergraduate and Postgraduate courses in Chemical and Polymer Engineering. Dr. Kulkarni's research is focused on the development of Sustainable Eco-friendly materials in the field of Chemical and Polymer Engineering. He is a Life Member of AICER (Associate, International Congress of Environmental Research), Indian Plastics Institute (PUN-231-LIF), and Asian Polymer Association (L-604).

Research interests

Green Technology, Polymer Materials and Structure Property Relationship, Rheology of polymeric systems, Microwave Assisted Reactions, Natural Waste based Product Development
