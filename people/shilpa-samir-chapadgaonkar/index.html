Prof. Shilpa Samir Chapadgaonkar

Associate Professor and Program Director

About

Dr. Shilpa S. Chapadgaonkar is an experienced academician and researcher with expertise in upstream processing, bioseparation, and enzyme technology. With over 15 years of teaching experience at UG, PG, and PhD levels, she has guided four PhD candidates to completion and is currently supervising three more, along with several M.Sc. and M.Tech. projects. She is leading the Department of Biosciences and Technology at MITWPU as Program Director. She has held key roles such as MSc (Biotech) Program Coordinator, Research Cluster Head, and PhD Program Coordinator, contributing significantly to NAAC and UGC audits. Her research focuses on drug discovery, bioprocess development for biopharmaceuticals, and investigating genetic disorders like infertility and autism. Passionate about sustainability, she has developed technologies for waste valorization, enzyme formulations, and textile wastewater treatment. Dr. Chapadgaonkar’s work has been supported by grants from the Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB), and she has led industrial collaborations with global companies like BASF and Gennova Biopharmaceuticals. She has published over 40 papers in high-impact journals and authored two textbooks adopted by universities worldwide. Qualification: B.Pharm, M.Sc. Tech (ICT Mumbai), Ph.D. IIT Delhi Experience: 16Y Research: Enzyme Technology, Bioprocess Technology,

Research interests

Bioprocess and enzyme technology, biopharmaceutical production and purification, tissue engineering, disease models, and sustainable biotechnology.
