Prof. Shreeya Chidambar Rajpurohit

Assistant Professor

About

Shreeya Rajpurohit is a faculty for Accounting, Costing and Finance subjects has been a wide teaching experience of 12+ years in the field of Commerce with and industry experience of two years. She has completed MCom with Costing specialisation, MBA in Financial Management, CMA Inter, UGC - NET in Commerce and currently pursuing PhD in Behavioral Finance from Symbiosis International (Deemed)University Pune. Working with Department of Commerce and Accounting as an Internship and Placement Coordinator and IQAC coordinator.

Research interests

Behavioral Finance, Supply Chain Management
