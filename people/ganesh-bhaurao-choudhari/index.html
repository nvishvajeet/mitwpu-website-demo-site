Dr. Ganesh Bhaurao Choudhari

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. M. Pharm, PhD

About

Dr. Ganesh Bhaurao Choudhari is an Assistant Professor with a M. Pharm. in Pharmaceutical Chemistry and a submitted Ph. D. With over 12. 6 years of experience in undergraduate academics, 7 years in postgraduate academics, and 3 years in industry, he is a seasoned professional in his field. He has 10 research publications, 4 review/special articles, and 3 patents to his credit. Dr. Choudhari has also presented 8 papers nationally, 9 papers internationally, and 4 papers regionally. He has attended 17 STTPs/Workshops/FDPs and has received research grants of 1. 60 lakhs from BCUD, Savitribai Phule Pune University and research seed fund of 3 lakhs from Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune for his research projects.
