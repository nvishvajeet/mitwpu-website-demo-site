Dr. Saurabh Ardra

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Saurabh Ardra has earned his Ph.D. in Operations and Supply Chain Management from the Department of Management Studies, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee. His doctoral research was titled "Analysis and Design of Framework for Circular Food Supply Chain in India". He holds a B. Tech in Mechanical Engineering and an M. Tech in Industrial Engineering from Lovely Professional University, Punjab, where he was honoured with the Academic Excellence Award. Dr. Ardra has contributed to high-impact research, with publications in reputed A-category and Q1 journals. He presented his research at the Society of Operations Management (SOM) Conference held at IIT Kanpur in December 2019. He also serves as a reviewer for esteemed international journals, including the Journal of Cleaner Production and Environment, Development and Sustainability. His teaching interests span across core and applied areas such as Operations Management, Supply Chain Management, Supply Chain Analytics, Inventory Management, Project Management, and Operations Research. Dr. Ardra brings academic rigour combined with practical insight to his teaching and research in the field of operations and supply chain management.
