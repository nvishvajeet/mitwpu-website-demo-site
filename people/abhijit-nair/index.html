Dr. Abhijit Nair

Associate Professor & Dean

About

Dr. Abhijit Nair has over 25 years of experience and held prominent academic and administrative positions, including Dean-Online Programs, Pan-Campus Area Chair for Liberal Arts and Business Communication, Head Placements and Head Admissions at Jaipuria Institute of Management Noida. He has dabbled in entrepreneurship running an ad-agency, a travel magazine and an agriculture-venture. His contributions involve being part of academic quality enhancement, institutional accreditation (NBA, NAAC), AACSB, curriculum building and designing courses and programs. He has been involved in building curricula based on Objective Based Education (OBE). He has also designed learner engagement differentiating based on pedagogy, andragogy and Heutagogy. He has also taken part in improving industry- academia engagements, student training and development, and student affairs to improve their life at campus. Dr. Nair holds a Ph.D. in English language and literature, an Executive Program in HR Leadership from SP Jain, and MA and BA in English language and literature with Sociology and Anthropology as minors. He has been involved in Management Development programs for SME's, Public Sector undertakings and consulting assignments as a communication consultant with marketing sales boutiques and advertising agencies
