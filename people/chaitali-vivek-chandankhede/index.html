About

Dr. Chaitali Vivek Chandankhede is an experienced academician and researcher in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. She serves as an Assistant Professor at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University with over 23 years of teaching experience. Her expertise includes Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Analytics, Computer Vision, and Information Security. D. in Computer Science along with an M. E. She has guided numerous undergraduate and postgraduate projects and published several research papers in international journals and conferences, actively contributing to research, innovation, and student mentoring in emerging AI technologies.
