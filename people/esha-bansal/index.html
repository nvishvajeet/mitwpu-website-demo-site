Dr. Esha Bansal

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Esha Bansal is an Assistant Professor at MIT World Peace University, Pune, with over Ten years of teaching experience and five years of dedicated research as a Junior Research Fellow at Himachal Pradesh University Business School. She is also an UGC-NET qualified in Labour Laws. Her academic focus lies in Organizational Behaviour, Human Resource Management, Labour Laws, and Industrial Relations.

Research interests

Personality Traits & Burnout, Social Media, Anxiety, Work Engagement & Employee Well-being, Emotional Intelligence & Leadership

Additional information

  • Teaching Philosophy: She believes that knowledge is co-created in the classroom through meaningful interaction and a stimulating educational environment. Her teaching philosophy emphasizes nurturing students holistically—intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically. I am committed to using innovative and flexible teaching strategies that help learners reach their highest potential.
  • Academic & Research Background: Her journey in academia has been enriched by her commitment to research and scholarly work. She has published extensively in UGC-approved and Scopus-indexed journals, with research interests including work engagement, personality traits, emotional intelligence, sustainable HR practices, and the psychological effects of social media.
