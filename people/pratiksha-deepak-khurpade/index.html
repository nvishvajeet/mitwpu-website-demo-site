Dr. Pratiksha Deepak Khurpade

Assistant Professor

Department of Materials Science & Engineering

About

Dr. Pratiksha Khurpade is an experienced academician and researcher with a background in Chemical and Petroleum Engineering. She holds an M. Tech in Chemical Engineering from LIT Nagpur and a Ph. D in Petroleum Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University. With over 14 years of academic experience and 2 years of industrial experience, she teaches courses such as Momentum Transfer, Process Heat Transfer, Process Instrumentation at the undergraduate level, and Catalysis Technology for Masters level students. Dr. Khurpade's previous research work focused on the synthesis of biodiesel from various feedstocks and its blending with petroleum diesel, along with the prediction of properties of diesel-biodiesel blends. Her present research interests include flow through porous media, nanomaterial synthesis and its applications, and green corrosion inhibitors.

Research interests

Nanomaterials, Catalysis, Biofuels, Flow through Porous Media, Energy
