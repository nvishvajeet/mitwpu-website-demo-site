Dr. Ranjeet Vinayak Gadhave

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Ranjit Vinayak Gadhave is an experienced academician and researcher in Pharmaceutical Sciences with over 20 years of experience in teaching and research. He completed his post-graduation and doctoral studies in Pharmaceutical Sciences from Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune. Currently, he is working as an Associate Professor at the School of Health Sciences and Technology, Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune. His primary research interests include the synthesis and biological evaluation of rationally designed heterocyclic molecules. Dr. Gadhave has made significant contributions to pharmaceutical research through publications in reputed national and international journals and by presenting his work at various academic and scientific forums. He has successfully guided more than 20 postgraduate students and continues to actively contribute to academics, research, and student mentorship.
