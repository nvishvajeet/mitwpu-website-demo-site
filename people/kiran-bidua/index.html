Dr. Kiran Bidua

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Kiran Bidua brings over a decade of experience in teaching B.Tech students, primarily in the areas of networking and cybersecurity. He combines strong academic expertise with industry-aligned knowledge to create a relevant and engaging learning environment. A certified professional in Palo Alto Networks technologies, OPSWAT Network Security, and OSINT, he continuously upskills to deliver industry-relevant insights that enhance student employability in a competitive landscape. He is passionate about translating theoretical concepts into real-world applications and actively contributes to technical initiatives and student clubs. Prof. Bidua remains committed to contributing meaningfully to the sustained excellence and innovation of MIT World Peace University.
