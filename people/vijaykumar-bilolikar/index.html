Dr. Vijaykumar Bilolikar

Professor

About

Prof Bilolikar has a rich academic and administrative experience of 28 years and is a professor of Operations Management at MIT-WPU. He has handled various administrative responsibilities such as Dean of Student Affairs, Controller of examination, NBA coordinator etc. He holds a Bachelors in Production Engineering from Government College of Engineering and a Masters in manufacturing engineering. He holds a doctoral degree from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in the area of operations management. His papers have been published in reputed international conferences and journals including International Journal of Production Research, Benchmarking an International Journal, International Journal of Logistics and Management, Annals of operations research etc. He is on the reviewer board of various journals. His research interests include supply chain management, application of AI techniques to operations and project management, simulation of manufacturing systems, sustainable manufacturing and Industry 4.0 architecture.
