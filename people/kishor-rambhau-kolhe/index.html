Prof. Kishor Rambhau Kolhe

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Kishor Kolhe has more than 28.6 years of teaching and industry experience. His areas of interest are Network and Security, Cyber Security, Blockchain Technology, and Internet of Things. He has published more than 79 research papers in reputed journals and conferences. He has also guided more than 76 undergraduate students, 18 post graduate students. 1 Research Scholar: completed her PhD under his supervision and is presently guiding. 2 Ph.D. research scholars. He has also worked as Head, I.T. Department, System In-charge of Center for Professional Courses and Member of various committees. He has reviewed research papers in national and international conferences and journals. He is a life member of CSI, ISTE and Fellow member of IETE etc. He has organized several conferences, workshops, seminars, Graduation Ceremony programs and short term training programmes etc. He had been invited as session chair of National and International conferences. He participated in various innovative workshops on Education and Training.
