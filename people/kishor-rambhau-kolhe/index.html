Prof. Kishor Rambhau Kolhe

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Kishor Kolhe has more than 28.6 years of teaching and industry experience. His areas of interest are Network and Security, Cyber Security, Blockchain Technology, and Internet of Things. He has published more than 79 research papers in reputed journals and conferences. He has also guided more than 76 undergraduate students, 18 post graduate students.

Education

  1. Ph.D: Ph.D. in Computer Engineering
  2. M.Tech: M. Tech. in Information Technology (IT)
  3. B.Tech: B. Tech in Electronics Engineering
