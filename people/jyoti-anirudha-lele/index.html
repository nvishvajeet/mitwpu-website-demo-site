Prof. Jyoti Anirudha Lele

Assistant Professor

About

Jyoti Lele is an Assistant Professor at the School of Electronics and Communication, with a B.E. degree in Electronics Engineering from Shivaji University in 2000, and an M.E. in Electronics (Digital Systems) from Pune University in 2009. Currently, she is pursuing her Ph.D. from the Department of Technology at SP Pune University, focusing on music signal analysis and synthesis. Her research interests span a variety of areas, including pattern recognition, machine learning, speech processing, computer vision, and artificial intelligence. With over 20 years of teaching experience, she has published more than 15 technical papers in peer-reviewed journals and conference proceedings.

Research interests

Music Signal Analysis and Synthesis, Speech Processing, Pattern Recognition, Computer Vision, Machine Learning
