Prof. Vaibhav Narendra Deshmukh

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Vaibhav Narendra Deshmukh is a senior academician with a total of 21 years of experience, including 1.5 years in industry and 12 years at MIT. He completed his Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2018. He has published ten papers and has one granted and one published Indian patent. Dr. Deshmukh has also guided ten M.Tech. dissertations and is currently guiding three PhD scholars. He is known for his expertise in the field of mechanical engineering and his passion for teaching and research.

Research interests

Nanofluid Heat Transfer, Adsorption Technology, Battery Thermal Management System, Biofuels, Electronic Devices Cooling, Renewable and Sustainable Energy
