Dr. Meenal Kaustubh Jabde

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D. in Computer Science & Applications- MIT-WPU M. Sc. in Computer Science- NMU Jalgaon B.Sc. in Computer Science- NMU Jalgaon

About

Dr. Meenal Kaustubh Jabde believes in creating an engaging, student-focused learning environment where conceptual understanding is complemented by practical knowledge. As an educator, her primary objective is to simplify complex ideas, foster curiosity, and develop critical thinking abilities among students. She is continuously motivated to evolve her teaching methodologies by integrating technology and real-life applications. At MIT-WPU, she contributes to fostering academic excellence, encouraging a research-oriented mindset, and nurturing well-rounded individuals in alignment with the university’s mission.

Research interests

: Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Block-chain, Image Processing, Pattern Recognition
