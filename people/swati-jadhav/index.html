Dr. Swati Jadhav

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D: Ph.D. In Computer Engineering -MIT-WPU
  2. M.Tech: M.Tech. In Computer Engineering - BVUCOE, Pune
  3. B.Tech: B.Tech. In IT - SIT, Pune

About

Dr. Swati Vilas Jadhav is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering with over 12 years of teaching and research experience. Her areas of expertise include Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Software Engineering. She is passionate about creating an engaging learning environment that encourages critical thinking, innovation, and ethical technology development. With more than 40 research publications, patents, she actively contributes to research, industry collaboration, and student mentorship.
