Dr. Seema Idhate

Assistant Professor

About

Mrs. Seema B. Idhate is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University. With 19 years of teaching experience, she is dedicated to nurturing analytical thinking, innovation, and research aptitude among students. Her expertise spans Digital Electronics, Algorithms, Computer Organization, Cloud Computing, Analog Communication, and MMA. She believes in outcome-based, student-centric learning aligned with industry and research needs. With notable publications and a patented innovation, she actively contributes to academic excellence, technological advancement, and the university’s vision of value-based, research-driven education.
