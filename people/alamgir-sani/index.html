Prof. Alamgir Sani

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Alamgir Sani is a distinguished academician and professional with over 24 years of combined experience across academia and industry, marked by a rich blend of teaching, research, and corporate exposure. He holds a Ph.D. in Management (Marketing) from Patna University and an MBA in Marketing and Finance from Aligarh Muslim University, and has qualified the UGC- NET. Dr. Sani’s corporate tenure includes impactful roles such as Cargo Manager with the Airports Authority of India and customer operations with EXL Services and DHL, which provided him deep expertise in logistics, air cargo operations, and global supply chains.

Research interests

Dr. Alamgir Sani is actively pursuing and publishing research in the domain of Consumer Buying Behaviour and Brand Preference drivers; Multi-Sided Technology Platform Business Models; Passenger Cars Buying Behaviour and Preference Drivers, Impact of emerging technologies, including Blockchain Technologies on business models and Supply Chains, Application of AI and Human Mimicking Technology (HMT) in Customer Engagement etc.
