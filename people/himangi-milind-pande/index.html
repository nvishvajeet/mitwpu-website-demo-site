Prof. Himangi Milind Pande

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Himangi Pande is Associate professor at MIT World Peace University. She has 26 years of experience in field of education also has been delivering experts talks and professing in vast area like Mathematical Foundation, Mobile Computing, Discrete Structure, Multimedia Technologies, Data Structures & Files, Fundamental of Data Structure, Natural Language Processing, Object Oriented Modeling and Design, Design and Analysis of Algorithm, Object Oriented Programming Language. Dr. Himangi Pande is a researcher. Her area of research is Wireless Body Area Network, Wireless Sensor Networks & IOT, Date Structure, and Data Analytics. Her core research area is in the field of Wireless Body Area Network in health care. She has developed a system for Adaptive Energy Efficient MAC for Wireless Body Area Network in health care. She has been a mentor and guide for more than 200 students. She has published more than 25 quality papers till now. A dedicated Professional with excellent Logic and Analytical Skills, Excellent Academic track record, Exceptional student relationship skills, relate and interface easily at student levels, in-depth understanding of various academic processes
