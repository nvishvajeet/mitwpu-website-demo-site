Prof. Kishanprasad Gangadhar Gunale

Associate Professor and Director, Career Development Centre (CDC)

About

"Dr. Kishanprasad Gunale is a highly motivated academic professional and a dedicated career counsellor. He received his Ph.D. from Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2019 and completed his B.Tech. and M.Tech. degrees with First Class with Distinction from Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere, in 2001 and 2006, respectively.

Research interests

Automotive Electronics, Computer Vision
