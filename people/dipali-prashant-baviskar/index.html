About

Dr. Dipali Baviskar adopts a clear, student-centric teaching approach that emphasizes strong fundamentals and practical understanding. As an educator, she is motivated to guide students in building confidence, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities. She actively engages in mentoring, academic coordination, and research to enhance the overall learning experience. Through her consistent efforts in teaching and professional development, she contributes to academic excellence. In alignment with the vision of MIT World Peace University, she strives to nurture responsible, skilled, and ethically aware graduates who can contribute positively to society.
