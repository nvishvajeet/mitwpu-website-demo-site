Ambrish Gaur

Assistant Professor

About

Ambrish Gaur is presently an assistant professor at MIT World Peace University, Pune, school of design. He accomplishes Govt. projects through DC Handicrafts, attending seminars as a guest speaker. As an academician, his area of interest is illustration, textile and fashion design, educational leadership, curriculum development, sustainable design, syllabus creation, conducting workshop, traditional textiles and crafts, student and artisan linkage, student and industry linkage, enabling educational workshop, entrepreneurial ability, design and product development, market research, print courses, elements and principal of design, fashion illustration, freehand design drawing, the process of design and documentation, surface design technique, figure drawing, Indian craft, and a several courses for B. Des, M. sc. and M. des. He actively participates in administrative work, new course launches, course design and review, constructing student assessments, and preparing examinations. He is versatile with capacity in the design process at all levels and skilled in digital techniques like Photoshop, Coral draw, InDesign, and NedGraphics. He advises and mentors students about their coursework and professional planning. Provide departmental leadership and coordination as needed. Additional responsibilities include academic and administrative work for the School and University, and graduate advising assignments. He has been an innovative teacher with expertise in curriculum design, and innovative teaching pedagogy with 16+ years of rich experience including 09 years of industrial experience and 6.5 years of teaching experience. As an interdisciplinary professional practice at Lovely Professional University, he successfully launched in house brand “UNIFASHION” including the brand’s logo design. The motto of the brand is to facilitate custom-made uniforms and accessories through design solutions for other departments. Unifashion was successfully launched in the year 2022. As a part of consultation projects, he has done 4 govt. research projects in the area of fashion, textiles, and crafts. Worked with Taiping carpets USA as sr. Designer based in Gurgaon and Brintons carpets Asia Pvt. Ltd. Pune, Maharashtra

Research interests

Illustration, textile and fashion design, art and craft
