Prof. Manisha Rajendra Kuveskar

Lecturer (Selection Grade)

About

Prof. Manisha Rajendra Kuveskar is an experienced Assistant Professor at MIT-WPU Department of Polytechnic with 25 years of teaching experience. She completed her post-graduation, Master of Engineering in Digital Systems from Pune University. She has presented four conference papers in National and International Conference.

Research interests

IOT, Automation, Robotics

Publications

  2. 2) Vehicle Security and Road Safety System Based on Internet of Things
  3. 3) ECG signal feature extraction using DB wavelets
  4. 4) ECG signal analysis using Matlab
