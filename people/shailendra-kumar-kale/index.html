Dr. Shailendra Kumar Kale

Professor

About

Dr. Shailendra Kumar Kale is a proficient teacher with over 23 years of experience in the faculty of management. He is passionate about work. Being an academic leader, he has proficiency in handling the institutional academic activities and administrative activities as well. As a Ph.D. research guide, he worked to develop a research attitude among his scholars. Four research scholars completed Ph.D. under his guidance. He has worked for developing the curriculum for MBA in academic autonomy structure. Dr. Kale has served as a member of the Board of Studies and Academic Council at a few autonomous colleges and universities. In his research domain, he has published 2 books, 2 book chapters and his 13 research articles are indexed in Scopus database. He has completed two minor research projects. Dr. Kale holds a BE, MBA, and a PhD in Management. Dr. Kale has undergone Faculty Development Program from IIM-A. He is currently working as a Professor in the School of Business (SoB), MIT WPU, Pune

Research interests

Logistics, Supply Chain Management, Operations, Entrepreneurship
