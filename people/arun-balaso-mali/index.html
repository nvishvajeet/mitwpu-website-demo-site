Prof. Arun Balaso Mali

Assistant Professor

About

Mr. Arun Mali is currently working as Assistant Professor in Mechanical Engineering Department of Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Kothrud Pune. He is having more than 12 years of teaching experiance. He holds Masters of Engineering in Design Engineering Domain and Currently pursuing Ph. D. in Mechanical Engineering. Master's work was associated with Design and Numerical Experiment on Compliant Mechanisms. Previously worked on Flexure mechanisms in Precision Applications. Research work was coined to achieve a specific objective of demonstration of dual use of Voice Coil Motor (i.e as actuator as well as sensor) which eliminates need of sensor in precision scanning applications . It was planned to design frictionless, backlash free XY flexural mechanism and experimentally demonstrate the dual use of VCM. His research areas are Compliant Mechanisms, Mechanical Design, Vibrations and acoustics, condition monitoring of Machines etc. A devoted educator, he focuses on inspiring students to engage critically with Engineering and Technology while contributing meaningfully to the study of diverse perspectives. His commitment to fostering intellectual growth reflects his belief in the transformative power of education.

Research interests

Mechanical Design, Compliant Mechanisms Condition Monitoring
