Prof. Akashdeep Sharma

Assistant Professor

About

Akashdeep Sharma is an Assistant Professor with over Eight years of academic and professional experience in the fields of architecture and interior design. He holds a Master of Architecture (M.Arch) in Construction Management and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Design and Architecture at Symbiosis International University (SIU). He is a Certified Architect registered with the Council of Architecture, New Delhi. With extensive exposure to residential, commercial, and institutional projects, he brings strong practical insight into design education. His core competencies include a solid understanding of design principles, proficiency in AutoCAD, SketchUp, and other design tools, and effective verbal and written communication skills. Actively engaged with the industry, he facilitates student internships, collaborative projects, and placement opportunities, while demonstrating leadership in academic programs and design initiatives.
