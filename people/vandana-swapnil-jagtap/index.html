Vandana Swapnil Jagtap

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Vandana Jagtap is an Assistant Professor at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, India. She has an M.Tech degree in Computer Science & Engineering from the University of Pune and is currently pursuing her Ph.D. in the same field. With 23 years of teaching experience, her areas of interest include Advanced Machine Learning, High-performance Computing, Distributed and Parallel computing, Cloud Computing, Algorithms, and Assistive Technology. Prof. Jagtap is the author of the book "Cloud Computing Policies, Outsourcing and Computing Keys: Cloud Computing policies" and has 50+ research publications in refereed international journals and conferences. She has also reviewed many refereed International journals and worked as an Advisory member and Editorial Board Member. Prof. Jagtap has delivered many expert lectures and is a member of various professional bodies such as ACM, CSI, ISTE, IEI. Under her guidance, 25 B.Tech (CSE) Project Groups and 13 M.Tech (CSE) Scholars have completed their projects.

Research interests

Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science
