Dr. Anagha Yogesh Deshpande

Assistant Professor

About

Anagha Deshpande is an experienced Assistant Professor in the School of Electronics and Communication Engineering at an esteemed university. She completed her Bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering from North Maharashtra University and went on to pursue a Master's degree in Digital Systems from Pune University. With 17 years of teaching experience and 1.5 years of industry experience, she has gained extensive knowledge and expertise in her field. Her contributions to research have also been recognized, as she has been awarded research funding by BCUD, Pune, and twice by IEEE SPS and AICTE. She has utilized these grants to organize summer schools, winter schools, and events, allowing her to share her expertise with students and peers in the field. Overall, Anagha Deshpande's impressive background and experience make her a valuable member of the School of Electronics and Communication Engineering, and her dedication to research and teaching have made her a respected figure in the academic community."
