Prof. Ratna Vadra

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Ratna Vadra is currently working as an Associate Professor in the Department of Business - School of Business under Division-2 (Faculty of Business and Leadership), Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune. Prior to MIT World Peace University she was an Assistant Professor in the area of Economics and Sustainability, Institute of Management Technology, Ghaziabad. With over 19 years of teaching and research experience, she's a true veteran in management education. She has a Ph.D. in Economics with specialization in Public Finance, and an M.A. (Economics) from Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh. She has also qualified UGC (NET) exam in Economics. She's a prolific researcher with 54 papers published in various international/national journals, contributed to book chapters in various management books, and also published 13 Case Studies in Business Management. She has presented her research work in 37 plus conferences globally. She has also attended Faculty Development Programme in the area of International Business at Georgia State University, Atlanta, Georgia, and at Darla Moore School of Business, University of South Carolina, USA. Her areas of teaching and research interest are Macroeconomics, Public Finance, International Trade, International Business, Multinational Strategy, Case study teaching, and writing.
