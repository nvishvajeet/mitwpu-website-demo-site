Prof. Vilas Sahebrao Kanthale

Associate Professor

About

Prof. Vilas Sahebrao Kanthale is an experienced educator and administrator with 27 years of professional experience, including 25 years of teaching and 2 years of industry experience. He holds a PhD from COEP, Pune and has a strong record in both teaching and administration. He is currently serving as the Chief Examination Officer for the Faculty of Engineering and Technology. Prof. Kanthale has guided numerous B.Tech and M.Tech project students, as well as PhD research scholars. His expertise includes the subjects of EGR, SOM, TOM, DME-I, II, MSD, and DOM.

Research interests

Design, Vibration and Manufacturing
