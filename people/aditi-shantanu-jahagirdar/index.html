Prof. Aditi Shantanu Jahagirdar

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Aditi Jahagirdar is a committed academician with over 31 years of teaching experience. She earned her Ph.D. in the field of Computer vision. She has more than 35 research papers to her credit. She holds two granted patents and two published patents. Renowned for her strong pedagogical skills, she actively adopts innovative teaching methodologies. Her excellence in teaching was recognized with the Best Teacher Award 2013 by MAEER’s Group of Institutions. She was conferred the “Distinguished Woman Researcher in Computer Vision – 2025” award by Venus International Foundation. Her research interests include computer vision, healthcare, Image and Video processing. She believes in lifelong learning and translating research into meaningful societal impact.
