Prof. Sandip Tukaram Chavan

Professor

About

Prof. Sandip T. Chavan is a highly accomplished Mechanical Engineering Professor at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, with an impressive 34 years of teaching and research experience. He has served as a member of the academic council of two universities and has been recognized for his outstanding contributions to the field of engineering with several awards, including the GYTI (Gandhian Young Technology Innovation 2016) award and the "Ideal Teacher Award" from MAEER's Pune in 2019. He is also the recipient of the "Outstanding Engineering Services to Society" award from "The Institute of Engineers" (India) Rajasthan State Centre Jaipur and Special Recognition for worthy work in the "Academic Excellence (Head of the Department)" category from Institution of Engineers and Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues Institute of Technology, Navi-Mumbai.
