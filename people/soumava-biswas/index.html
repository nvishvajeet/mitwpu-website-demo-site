Dr. Soumava Biswas

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Soumava Biswas obtained his PhD in chemistry from IISER Bhopal, India under the supervision of Professor Sanjit Konar in 2017. Then he worked with the research group of Prof. Masahiro Yamashita, at Tohoku University, Japan as a JSPS postdoctoral fellow for two years

Research interests

Metal-organic frameworks for gas storage and carbon capture, molecular nanomagnets, spin-crossover switches, and fluorescent sensors.
