Prof. Sachin Bhimrao Bhoite

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Sachin B. Bhoite is an accomplished academician and researcher with over 25 years of experience in teaching and research. He serves as an Associate Professor and Ph.D. Coordinator at MIT-WPU University, Pune, and holds a SET qualification and Ph.D. in Computer Science, specializing in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Edge Computing. He has published over 70 papers in Scopus/WoS-indexed journals and a Springer book chapter, and holds patents in IoT-based systems. As a recognized Ph.D. guide, he mentors scholars and reviews for leading journals, contributing actively as a keynote speaker and conference chair.
