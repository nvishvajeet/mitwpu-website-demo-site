Dr. Ranjana Anup Agrawal

Assistant Professor

About

My teaching philosophy is centered on experiential, research-driven learning that bridges theory with real-world application. I believe in nurturing curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical responsibility among students. By integrating emerging technologies such as AI and data-driven methods into the curriculum, I strive to create an engaging and future-ready learning environment. My motivation lies in mentoring students to become innovative problem-solvers and socially responsible professionals. Through interdisciplinary research, industry collaboration, and student-centric pedagogy, I actively contribute to MIT-WPU’s vision of academic excellence, innovation, and societal impact.
