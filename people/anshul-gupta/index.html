Prof. Anshul Gupta

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. Master’s in Design – LPU, Jalandhar

About

Mr. Anshul Gupta is a designer with a strong understanding of human behaviour and user- centric design principles. His approach focuses on creating solutions that not only meet functional requirements but also establish meaningful emotional connections with users.

He integrates experience design and gamification to develop innovative, practical, and engaging products. His work aims to address contemporary challenges while ensuring accessibility, inclusivity, and personalization. By blending usability with thoughtful interaction design, he strives to make every day experiences intuitive, impactful, and rewarding.

Research interests

Product design, Furniture design, Experience Design, Lighting Design, Additive Manufacturing, Gamification (Octalysis), Instructional Design, Business Canvas Model, Generative AI.
