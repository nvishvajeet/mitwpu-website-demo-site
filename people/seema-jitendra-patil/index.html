Prof. Seema Jitendra Patil

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Mrs. Seema J. Patil is a highly accomplished computer engineer, who graduated with distinction from SSBT's COET, Jalgaon in 2006. She completed her Master's degree from MIT Pune and went on to work as a lecturer in SMIT College, Jalgaon for a year. With a successful software testing training program from STC Technologies, Pune and an IBM certification in DB2 fundamental, she joined MIT-WPU as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering, where she has been teaching for the past 15 years. Prof. Patil has a wide range of teaching experience and has guided many undergraduate level projects. She has also coordinated various subjects and organized industrial visits for students. Her research work has been published in several international journals. Prof. Patil's dedication to her students and passion for teaching has made her an inspiring mentor and guide.
