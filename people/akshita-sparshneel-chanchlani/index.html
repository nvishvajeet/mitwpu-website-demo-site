Dr. Akshita Sparshneel Chanchlani

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Akshita Sparshneel Chanchlani has a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering. She is involved in the education team and in the technical industry as an Associate Head technical trainer and assistant professor with over 14 years of teaching with industrial and technical corporate training experience. She is known as a good communicator with excellent presentation skills. Moreover, she can guide and develop technical training materials by translating complex concepts into systematic and understandable terms using creative training and teaching methods. Dr. Akshita has a proven track record of preparing students to produce quality work across a variety of technical training like C and C++ programming, Core Java, Python Programming, Big Data Technologies, Machine Learning and Deep learning. Her research topic was in the domain of Machine Learning and Deep Learning and a healthcare application. Moreover, she has been working in the healthcare domain for five years using machine learning and deep learning technology. Dr. Akshita has two patents published in the healthcare domain in ML and DL and has a total of 15+ research paper publications in reputed journals. Her Areas of Domain are Healthcare, AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Python, Data Science, and Big Data Technologies.
