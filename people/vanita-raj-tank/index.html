Vanita Raj Tank

Assistant Professor

About

Vanita Raj Tank is an Assistant Professor at the School of Electronics and Communication. She holds an M.Tech degree in Digital Communication from MANIT Bhopal. Her research work includes a project on "Noise Reduction for Ultrasound wave" conducted at CWPRS Khadakwasla, Pune. She has published research papers in various international conferences and two publications in Quartile journals. Her areas of expertise include Signal Processing, Embedded Systems, and Digital Communication.

Research interests

Signal Processing, Embedded Systems, Digital Communication
