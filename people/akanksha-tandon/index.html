Dr. Akanksha Tandon

Assistant Professor

About

Ms. Akanksha Tandon is currently pursuing her Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at the National Institute of Technology, Rourkela. Her research focuses on energy-efficient Virtual Machine (VM) placement and optimization in cloud computing environments. She holds an M.Tech in Information System Security from NIT Jamshedpur and a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Punjabi University, Patiala. Her technical expertise includes CloudSim, iFogSim, Java, and data analytics. With a strong foundation in research and problem-solving, she contributes to advancing knowledge in cloud computing and IoT-based systems.
