Dr. Alok Kumar Yadav

Assistant Professor

Research

Research interests

Dr. Alok Kumar Yadav is an Assistant Professor in the School of Business at MIT-WPU, Pune. He has completed Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in the area of Economics from the Indian Institute of Management Mumbai (IIM Mumbai), India. He completed his Master of Philosophy (M. Phil.) and master’s degree in the Economics Area. Fundamentally, he believes in a pedagogy where the knowledge cultivated at institutions has to be nurtured in real-world settings. Teaching provides him the opportunity to practice what he has researched, yet at the same time, it helps the students stay updated with the recent advancements in the field of Economics. Thus, it emerges as a win-win situation for all the institute's stakeholders. Importantly, Dr. Yadav would like to develop an innovative teaching approach which may promote progressive critical thinking and create inclusive learning experiences that align with the institution’s academic values. He has published various research papers in highly reputed journals.
