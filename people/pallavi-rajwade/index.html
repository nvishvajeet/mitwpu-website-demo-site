Pallavi Rajwade

Assistant Professor, Program Co-ordinator BFA & MFA (Applied Arts)

About

Ms. Pallavi Rajwade is passionate about communication design and believes that curiosity and creativity make life fun and enriched. She has received her formal education in Applied Arts along with a Post Graduate Diploma in Public Relations & Advertising. She has worked as a Visualiser and Graphic Designer for over 15 years. in India as well as abroad. She has almost 5+ years of enriching experience in Academics as BFA and MFA Program Coordinator for AY 2023-2024. Also, she is currently pursuing PhD from Symbiosis International University. Currently, she is teaching courses such as Advertising, Branding, and Visualisation and has mentored final-year students for Visual Communication Design projects for the last 2 years at MIT WPU Department of Visual Arts, School of Design.

Research interests

Brand Perception and Packaging Visuals
