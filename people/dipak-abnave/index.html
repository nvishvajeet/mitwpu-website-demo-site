Dr. Dipak Abnave

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Dipak Abnave, PhD in Social Medicine and Community Health from JNU, New Delhi, is deeply committed to advancing the discourse on Health System Strengthening, health governance, diversity, equity, inclusion, and sustainability. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at MIT-WPU Pune, Dr. Abnave brings a wealth of experience from previous roles as an Assistant Professor at Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Tuljapur Campus, and as a Scientist-B for ICMR-NIRRCH, Mumbai. Dr. Abnave's professional journey has equipped them with extensive project implementation experience in health and development across various organizations, positioning them as a dedicated advocate for the right to health and health security.

Research interests

Health System Research, Health Governance, Human Rights and Health
