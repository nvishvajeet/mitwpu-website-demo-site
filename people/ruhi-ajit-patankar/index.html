Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Ruhi Patankar is an Assistant Professor and researcher specializing in computer science, engineering, and deep learning applications. She contributes to academic instruction and research at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University in Pune, with interests in event detection from social media and advanced computational methods.
