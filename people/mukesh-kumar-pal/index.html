Dr. Mukesh Kumar Pal

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Mukesh Kumar Pal is an Assistant Professor of Mathematics, specializing in Applied Mathematics. He is currently affiliated with the Department of Mathematics & Statistics at MIT-World Peace University, Kothrud, Pune, Maharashtra-411038, India

Research interests

Mathematical modelling of smart materials, elastodynamics, and wave propagation.
