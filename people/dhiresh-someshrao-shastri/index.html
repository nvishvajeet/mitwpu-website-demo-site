Prof. Dhiresh Someshrao Shastri

Lecturer

About

As a lecturer at DVKMIT School Of Polytechnic and Skill Development in Pune, Maharashtra, India, Prof. Dhiresh Someshrao Shastri has 18 years of teaching experience and 5 years of industrial experience. He prioritizes vibrant and lively student-teacher interactions and is passionate about teaching his subjects. Prof. Shastri's ultimate objective is to equip students with the necessary skills to overcome any challenge they may face in the world.
