Dr. Gajanan Malleshi Birajdar

Assistant Professor

About

Gajanan Birajdar is an Assistant Professor of Mathematics and Statistics with expertise in Geometric Function Theory. He obtained his Master's degree in Mathematics from Shivaji University, Kolhapur, and is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in Complex Analysis from the same university

Research interests

Geometric function theory and univalent functions.

Publications

  1. N.D.Sangle, G.M. Birajdar, Certain subclass of analytic function with negative coefficients defined by Catas operator, IJM, 62(3) (2020), 335-353.
  2. G.M.Birajdar, N.D.Sangle, Certain subclass of harmonic univalent functions defined by q-differential operator, Serdica Mathematical Journal, 48(1) 2022, 120-128.
  3. N.D.Sangle, G.M. Birajdar, New subclass of Biunivalent function associated to Horadam polynomials , IJGDC, 13?(2) (2020), 1-11
