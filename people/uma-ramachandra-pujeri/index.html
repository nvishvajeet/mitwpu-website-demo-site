Prof. Uma Ramachandra Pujeri

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Uma R Pujeri was born in Sangli, India in 1981. She earned her M.Tech degree from PSG Tech College of Engineering, Coimbatore in 2008 and received her doctorate degree from Anna University, Chennai in May 2017. Her research area focuses on computer network congestion control algorithms. With fourteen years of teaching experience, she has worked as an Assistant Professor at Adithya College of Engineering, Coimbatore for six years and is currently serving as an Associate Professor at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, Maharashtra. Dr. Pujeri is a Life Member of the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) and has conducted several workshops, FDPs, and conferences. Her post-doctoral fellowship from Srinivas University, Mangalore, under the guidance of Dr. P.S. Aitthal, is registered under the ID 20SUPDROO9. Dr. Pujeri has published 40 research papers in international journals, and her PhD thesis is available on the UGC website https://shodhganga.inflibnet.ac.in/handle/10603/196148. Contact her at uma.pujeri@mitwpu.edu.in.

Research interests

Computer Networks, Cryptography, loud computing, encryption algorithm, Post Quantum Lattice algorithm
