Dr. Rajkumar Damodar Komati

Assistant Professor

About

Rajkumar Damodar Komati is an Assistant Professor at the School of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), with a teaching experience of over 22 years and 2 years of industry experience. He completed his ME(Electronics) in 2005 and is currently pursuing his PhD from SPPU Pune. His subjects of interest include Basic Electronics, Digital Electronics, Digital VLSI design, Biomedical Electronics, and Image and Video processing, with a particular focus on the latter.
