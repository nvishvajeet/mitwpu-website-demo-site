About

Dr. Nitin N. Pise is a highly accomplished academician and researcher, with over 29 years of experience in teaching and 2 years of industrial experience. He received his Ph. D. Dr. He has published more than 100 research papers in National and International Conferences and Journals, showcasing his research and contribution to the field.
