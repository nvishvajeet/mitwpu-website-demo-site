Dr. Uday Vilas Jagadale

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D. in Mathematics – Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune M.Sc. in Mathematics – Department of Mathematics, SPPU, Pune B.Sc. in Mathematics – Fergusson College Pune

About

Dr. Uday Vilas Jagadale is an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at MIT World Peace University, Pune. His research interests lie in graph theory, discrete mathematics, and matroid theory.

Research interests

Graph theory, discrete mathematics, matroid theory, and connectivity of interconnection networks.

Academic work

  • 10 years of teaching experience in higher education teaching undergraduate and post graduate mathematics courses.
  • Research contributions primarily in graph theory and matroid theory, including studies on component connectivity, h-extra r-component connectivity, spanning-edge cyclability and disjoint path cyclability of interconnection networks, and splitting operations in matroids.
  • 1. Secured AIR 30 in CSIR-UGC NET Junior Research Fellowship, June 2016. 2. Qualified GATE-2015 with AIR 246 in Mathematics.

Teaching
