Prof. Rajashri Amit Kadam

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Rajashri Kadam is dedicated Associate Professor in the domain of Marketing and currently handling additional responsibility as the Associate Dean – Academics at the School of Business since January 2022. With over 22 years of combined industry and academic experience, she has been a passionate educator for the past 18 years, known for her interactive and learner- centric pedagogy. Dr. Kadam holds a Ph.D. in Marketing Management and an Executive Diploma in Digital Marketing from IIM Jammu. Her teaching interests include Advertising, Services Marketing, Integrated Marketing Communication, and Strategic Marketing. She has contributed significantly to academic research with publications in reputed national and international journals, Scopus-indexed book chapters, Case studies and numerous conference papers. She has also guided student-led case studies and research publications. She is also a Ph.D. supervisor and certified trainer, with prior experience in consulting and content development. Known for her strong rapport with students and alumni, Dr. Kadam continues to inspire learners with her dynamic teaching, academic leadership, and commitment to educational excellence. In her current leadership role, Dr. Kadam oversees academic operations for multiple undergraduate and postgraduate programs across the School of Business, including curriculum design, academic planning, evaluation systems, and industry integration. She is actively involved in promoting outcome-based education, NEP-aligned curriculum reforms, and faculty resource planning.
