Dr. Arti Girish Swami

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. M. Pharm, PhD

About

Dr. Arti Swami is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry at the School of Health Sciences and Technology, Dr. Vishwanath Karad, MIT World Peace University, Kothrud, Pune. She has over 15 years of academic experience with a strong emphasis on pharmaceutical research and advanced analytical sciences. D. Dr. Swami has published several research articles in reputed national and international journals, contributing significantly to the field of pharmaceutical analysis and quality-by-design approaches. Her research interests primarily focus on impurity profiling, Analytical Quality by Design (AQbD), Design of Experiments (DoE), in vitro–in vivo correlation (IVIVC), in vitro permeation testing (IVPT), and stability studies, with an aim to enhance product quality, robustness, and regulatory compliance.
