Dr. Pradnya Siddhivinayak Kulkarni

Associate Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D: Ph. D. In Information Technology - Federation University, Australia
  2. B.Tech: B. E. In Electronics Engineering - TKIET, Shivaji University, Kolhapur

About

Dr. Pradnya S. Kulkarni is an Associate Professor at MIT-WPU, whose teaching philosophy focuses on bridging the gap between theoretical computer engineering and industrial application. Guided by the university’s vision of holistic, research-driven education, she brings valuable international academic and industry experience from Australia, Canada, and India to create a collaborative learning environment. Her contributions include leading high-impact research in medical image processing. Through guiding industry-sponsored projects and coordinating academic curricula, she strives to empower students with the technical expertise and ethical grounding required for global leadership.
