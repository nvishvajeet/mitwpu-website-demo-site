Prof. Sarang Gore

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. M.DES. - Interdisciplinary School of Science.

About

Mr. Sarang Gore is passionate about crafting compelling visual narratives and nurturing creative talent. With a strong foundation in design and a deep commitment to teaching, he effectively bridges industry practices with academic learning.

He currently teaches UI/UX, Graphic Design, and Portfolio Development, guiding students to develop both conceptual clarity and practical skills. Actively engaged with the evolving landscape of visual communication, he encourages innovation, critical thinking, and industry readiness among aspiring designers.
