Dr. Harshali Digambar Zodpe

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Harshali Zodpe is a highly experienced academic, currently serving as an Associate Professor in the School of ECE. With an impressive 16 years of teaching experience, she is widely regarded as a leading authority in the field of electronics and communication engineering. Dr. Zodpe's academic background is characterized by a deep understanding of the fundamental principles of electronics and communication engineering. She completed her B.E. in Electronics Engineering from VNIT Nagpur in 2004 and went on to earn an M.Tech in VLSI Design from Nagpur University in 2006. She later obtained her Doctor of Philosophy (Electronics & Telecommunication) degree from Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2019, further solidifying her expertise in the field. Dr. Zodpe's research interests are focused on VLSI, Cryptography, and Embedded Systems. Her work in these areas has been instrumental in advancing the field of electronics and communication engineering, with her research being published in numerous top-tier scientific journals. Additionally, she has presented her work at conferences and symposia around the world.
