Associate Professor

About

Dr. Kiran Kisanrao Kokate is an Associate Professor in Chemistry & acted as head for two years in the academic year 2021-22 & 2022-23. He has a teaching experience of 24 years. He has completed his PhD from Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravti and his area of interest includes conducting…

Research interests

Conducting polymers and metal-oxide nanocomposites, including PEDOT/ZnO materials.
