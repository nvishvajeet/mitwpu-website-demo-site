Niraj Subhashrao Topare

Assistant Professor

About

Niraj S. Topare is a Chemical Engineering professional with a Master's degree and is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in the same field. He is an Assistant Professor in the School of Chemical Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune (MIT-WPU, Pune), where he has been teaching for more than 10 years.

Research interests

Separation Processes, Bio-energy (Biofuels, Biodiesel Production, Biochar), Wastewater Treatment, Photocatalysis and Ultrasound

Education

  1. PhD in Chemical Engineering from Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University Pune (Pursuing)
