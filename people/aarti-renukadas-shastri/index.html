Dr. Aarti Renukadas Shastri

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Aarti Shastri is an Assistant Professor in the field of Pharmacognosy and Phytochemistry, specializing in the extraction, isolation and separation of chemical constituents from herbal drugs. She has a PhD in the same field and has extensive experience in pharmacological and phytochemical evaluation of herbal drugs/formulations for new therapeutic indications. Her research work is focused on natural products and their therapeutic uses.
