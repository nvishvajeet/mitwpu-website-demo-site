Mrs. Gauri Rahul Dhongade

Teaching Associate

About

Gauri Dhongade has completed her M.Sc. in Computer Science and has qualified the State Eligibility Test (SET). She is currently pursuing her Ph.D. in the field of Cybersecurity and Machine Learning. She has a total of 14 years of teaching and industry experience. At present, she is working as a Teaching Associate in the School of Computer Science at MIT World Peace University, Pune. Her research areas include Cybersecurity, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Data Analysis, and Ransomware Detection. She has published research papers in National and International Conferences and Journals and has also contributed to patents in her domain. She has prior industry experience as a C#.NET Developer, which adds practical exposure to her teaching. She has actively participated in various FDP, Training Programs, workshops, seminars, and conferences at both National and International levels. She is actively involved in academic and administrative responsibilities, contributing to student development, research activities, and departmental coordination, thereby supporting a research-oriented and innovative learning environment.
