Dr. Omkar Kamalakant Kulkarni

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Omkar Kulkarni is an Assistant Professor in the School of Mechanical Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University in Pune, India. With 9 years of experience in education, teaching, and research, he has been actively involved in undergraduate and postgraduate teaching, as well as departmental and university-level activities. He recently earned his PhD in "Investigations on Micro-Forming of Bio-Materials" from MIT World Peace University and has published over 25 papers in optimization, algorithms, and micro-forming. He has also organized and participated in various workshops, conferences, and training programs.

Research interests
