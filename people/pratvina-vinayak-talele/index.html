Dr. Pratvina Vinayak Talele

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Pratvina Talele is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, School of Computer Science and Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, with 18+ years of experience in higher education. She previously served at MIT College of Engineering since 2007. Her research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Requirement Engineering with an emphasis on learner-centric and inclusive education. She has completed consultancy projects for Trident Services Pvt Ltd, Knowledge Waves Skill Development Centre Pvt. Ltd. and Pragmatic Techsoft Pvt. Ltd., translating academic research into industry-oriented solutions.
