Dr. Anu Nair

Programme Director & Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Anu Nair is a dedicated academician with over 13 years of experience in media and communication education, academic administration, and curriculum development. She joined as an Assistant Professor and is currently serving as Program Director at the Department of Media and Communication at MIT World Peace University, Pune. Her areas of specialization include Advertising, Branding, Consumer Psychology, Consumer Behaviour, Media Psychology and Strategic Communication.

Education

  1. Certificate Program in ‘Sustainable Business Strategy’ from Harvard Business School in 2021.
  2. M.B.A. (Media Management) from EMRC, DAVV, Indore in 2010.
  3. B.A. (Hons.) Journalism & Mass Communication from SJMC, DAVV, Indore in 2008.
