Devashish Shedge

Assistant Professor

About

Mr. Devashish Shedge is a Film Director and Senior Creative Director with over 35 years of experience across India and the UAE. With expertise in storytelling, advertising, and audio-visual production, he brings extensive industry knowledge into the classroom through a practice-driven approach to learning. By integrating the disciplines of cinema, advertising, and media education, he mentors students to develop creative thinking, technical excellence, and industry-ready skills.
