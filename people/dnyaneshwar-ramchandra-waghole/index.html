Dr. Dnyaneshwar Ramchandra Waghole

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Dnyaneshwar R Waghole holds a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering, along with an M.E. in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Heat Power and a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering. He has a total of 21.5 years of experience, including 20 years in teaching and 1.5 years in the industry. His research areas include NanoFluids, Nanomaterials & Allied Nano Materials, Biomedical Healthcare Technology, Heat Transfer Equipment, and Energy Harvesting Technology. Dr. Waghole has published 10 research papers in national and international journals and has filed a patent for "Heat Transfer Enhancement using Flow divider type Inserts." He is a member of the Solar Energy Society of India (SESI) and the Society of Automotive Engineering (SAE).

Research interests

heat transfer equipments, nanofluidics/nanomaterial, EV (BTMS/Fuel cell),Healthcare ,energy ,Operations research
