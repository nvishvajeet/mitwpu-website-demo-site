Dr. Trushita Sandeep Chaware

Assistant Professor

About

Trushita Chaware is a highly qualified academician, having recently completed her Ph.D. in the field of Communication. With years of experience in teaching both undergraduate and postgraduate courses, she has earned a reputation for being a dynamic and engaging educator, consistently receiving positive feedback from her students. Throughout her career, Trushita has also demonstrated a commitment to excellence in administrative responsibilities, successfully handling various roles assigned by her school. Additionally, she has contributed significantly to her field through the publication of 15 papers in prestigious conferences and journals.

Research interests

Wireless Communication, Network Security

Publications

  1. Review of joint encoding and encryption for image transmission using chaotic map, ldpc and AES encryption
  2. Performance of Turbo Product Code in WiMAX
  3. Secure Communication using TPC and Chaotic Encryption
  4. Design of a Microstrip Patch Antenna as a Moisture Sensor
  5. Turbo product code and AES Encryption for Wireless Communication
  6. Proposed Algorithm for Smart Traffic Control using Ultrasonic Sensors
