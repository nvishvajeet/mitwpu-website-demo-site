Dr. Ashish Shivkumar Utage

Assistant Professor

About

Ashish Shivkumar Utage is an Assistant Professor of Thermal Engineering at MIT World Peace University (MITWPU). With extensive teaching experience, he is committed to developing innovative pedagogical approaches that enhance student learning outcomes. He fosters a stimulating learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. Ashish's research focuses on areas such as Alternative refrigerants for air conditioners, and he has published several research papers in top-tier international journals and presented his work at various conferences. He actively collaborates with industry partners on research projects aimed at solving real-world problems.
