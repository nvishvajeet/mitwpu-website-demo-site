Dr. Sarika Sanjay Bobde

Assistant Professor

About

Dedicated teacher and researcher specializing in Data Structures, Software Engineering, Machine Learning with a Ph.D. in Code Refactoring. Clarity, inquiry, and character development are at the core of my teaching philosophy. In my opinion, education should foster ethical responsibility in addition to imparting knowledge. My goal is to establish an engaging and welcoming classroom where students are inspired to ask questions, investigate, and come up with new ideas. Specialisation: Computer Science and Engineering
