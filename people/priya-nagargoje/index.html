Prof. Priya Nagargoje

Assistant Professor

About

My teaching approach focuses on practical learning, curiosity, and industry relevance. I believe education should go beyond textbooks and help students develop the ability to think critically, solve real-world problems, and adapt to emerging technologies. I am motivated to create an engaging learning environment where students gain both strong conceptual understanding and hands-on experience, especially in areas like cloud computing, DevOps, and AI. Through mentorship, research, and industry collaboration, I aim to contribute to the vision of MIT World Peace University by nurturing skilled, ethical, and innovative professionals who can create meaningful impact in society.
