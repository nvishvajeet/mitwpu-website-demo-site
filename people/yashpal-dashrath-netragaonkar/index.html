Dr. Yashpal Dashrath Netragaonkar

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Yashpal Dashrath Netragaonkar is an Associate Professor in Education, who has served the field for over 21 years. His experience as a teacher spans across the levels of Secondary School and Higher Education.

Research interests

Teacher Education, Research in Education & Statistics
