Dr. Rashmi Suhas Phalnikar

Professor

About

I am guided by strong work ethics rooted in integrity, accountability, and methodological rigor, maintaining high ethical standards throughout research and development. With a proactive and collaborative approach, I emphasize attention to detail, continuous learning, and timely delivery of high-quality outcomes. This integrated perspective allows me to bridge technical excellence with responsible and impactful practice. My work encompasses software engineering practices, insightful data analysis, and user-centered interface design, with a focus on developing scalable, reliable, and efficient systems. I employ data-driven methodologies to extract meaningful insights while ensuring that software solutions remain intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs.
