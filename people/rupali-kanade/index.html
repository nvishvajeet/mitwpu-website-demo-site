Dr. Rupali Kanade

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Rupali A. Kanade is an enthusiastic academician with a strong background in teaching. She possesses over 12 years of experience in teaching. She completed her Ph.D. from department of statistics, Savitribai Phule Pune University.

Research interests

Optimal repair and replacement policies, Reliability theory.

Publications

  1. In Ph. D. thesis we have designed replacement policies based on number of down times under various sets of assumptions to avoid large financial loss. During the down time of the system, there will be extra expenditure on redundant component, operational cost, salaries to the employees etc. This causes a huge financial loss to the production process in industry. To avoid such big loss below policies may be useful.
  2. Optimal Replacement Policy based on the Number of Down Times. (2010). Published in Economic Quality Control, 25(1), 3-12. Coauthor: D. D. Hanagal.
  3. An Optimal Replacement Policy based on Number of Down Times with Priority in Use. (2010). Published in Economic Quality Control, 25(2), 243-251. Coauthor: D. D. Hanagal.
  4. Optimal Replacement Policy Based On Number of Down Times of the First Component. (2012). Published in Journal of Indian Society for Probability and Statistics, 13, 54-64.. Coauthor: D. D. Hanagal.
  5. Optimal Replacement Policy based on Number of Down Times for Cold Standby System when the Lifetime and the Repair Time are Dependent. (2011). Published in Journal of Reliability and Statistical Studies, 4(1), 41-52. Coauthor: D.D. Hanagal. (ISSN-0974-8024).
  6. Optimal Geometric Process Replacement Policies Based On Number Of Down Times. (2011). Published in International Journal of Reliability, Quality and Safety Engineering, 18(6), 495-513. Coauthor: D.D. Hanagal. (ISSN- 0218-5393).
