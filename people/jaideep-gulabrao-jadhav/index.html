Dr. Jaideep Gulabrao Jadhav

Professor

About

"Dr. Jaideep Jadhav is a seasoned academician with over 20 years of experience in the areas of Finance, Banking, Financial Services, Insurance and Analytics. He is currently working as a Professor in the School of Business (SoB) and has been associated with the institute for several years. Dr. Jadhav holds an MBA degree, a Master's degree in Commerce, and a Post Graduate Program (PGP) in Data Science. He also holds a second MBA degree in Finance. His research interests are in the areas of MSME Financing, Banking, Financial Analytics, Insurance Analytics, and International Finance. He has published several research papers in reputed national and international journals and has contributed chapters in books in his area of expertise.
