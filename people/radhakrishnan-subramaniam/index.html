Prof. Radhakrishnan Subramaniam

Professor Emeritus and Director Research

About

Prof. Radhakrishnan Subramaniam is dedicated to cultivating strong foundational knowledge and fostering a deep interest in the subject among students. He strives to create an academic environment that encourages innovative thinking and promotes the application of knowledge through research and development activities. His teaching and mentoring approach emphasises project-based learning, guiding both faculty members and students toward practical and research-oriented education. He actively promotes and leads R&D initiatives while mentoring postgraduate and Ph.D. projects. Prof. Dr. Radhakrishnan Subramaniam also believes in strengthening academic and research collaborations with other institutes to encourage collaborative research and the development of advanced materials for specialised applications.
