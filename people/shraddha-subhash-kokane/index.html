Dr. Shraddha Subhash Kokane

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Shraddha Kokane is a reader and writer in domain of banking and finance for over 15 years. She was a Program Head for MBA Banking in MIT WPU from 2018 to 2022 and was instrumental in designing the curriculum. In 2022 she was granted the RBI Faculty Scholarship in 2022 and in 2024 she was awarded an ICSSR-funded research. Her case studies are published with the Case Centre. Shraddha’s papers are indexed by Scopus and Web of Science. She can be reached out for collaboration on banking, finance, governance, and markets.
