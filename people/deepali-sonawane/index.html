Deepali Sonawane

Assistant Professor

About

Deepali Sonawane is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Application, committed to fostering a learner-centric environment aligned with MIT-WPU’s vision of “Powering the Future.” Her teaching philosophy emphasizes innovation, inclusivity, and practical knowledge, encouraging students to think critically and apply technology for societal impact. She leads with integrity and a commitment to continuous learning, while nurturing empathy and student growth. She actively promotes interdisciplinary collaboration and aims to empower students with the skills, values, and a progressive mindset needed to contribute meaningfully to education, innovation, and society.
