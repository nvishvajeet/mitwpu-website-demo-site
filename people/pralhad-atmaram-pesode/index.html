Dr. Pralhad Atmaram Pesode

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Pralhad Pesode is an Assistant Professor in the School of Mechanical Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University in Pune, Maharashtra, India. He is currently pursuing a PhD from the same university. He completed his postgraduation in Manufacturing Technology from NIT Trichy in Tamilnadu, India and his graduation in Mechanical Engineering from Government College of Engineering Aurangabad in Maharashtra, India. Prof. Pesode specializes in Material Science, Engineering Metallurgy, Advanced Manufacturing Processes, Biomaterials, Surface modification of biomaterials, Biomaterial coating, Composites and Nanocomposites for biomedical applications. He is a life member of several professional bodies, including the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) and the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE). Prof. Pesode has published more than 19 research papers in various international journals and conferences and has worked for numerous conferences, seminars, and educational workshops.

Research interests

Biomaterial, Surface modification of biomaterial, Material science, Materials Characterization, Advanced Manufacturing Processes, Nanocomposites for biomedical applications.
