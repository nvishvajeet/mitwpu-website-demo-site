Dr. Umesh Shrivastava

Associate Professor

About

A detail-oriented faculty and a seasoned practitioner of entrepreneurship, I am skilled at bringing cutting-edge solutions through interactive sessions on complex entrepreneurial cases and papers by bridging theory and practice. I am experienced in executing diverse research projects using various methodologies that have significant policy implications. I have published papers in Scopus-indexed journals of high repute and presented my research at national and international conferences. I have over a decade of experience leading a manufacturing unit involved in hydropower projects.

Education

  1. Ph.D. in Management (Tagore University)
  2. FPM in Entrepreneurship (EDII, Ahmedabad)
  3. Master’s in Business Administration (ITM, Navi Mumbai)
  4. Masters in Entrepreneurship and innovation (University of Sheffield, United Kingdom)
  5. Bachelor’s Degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (RGPV, Bhopal)

Academic work

  • Over 6 years of teaching experience at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels.
