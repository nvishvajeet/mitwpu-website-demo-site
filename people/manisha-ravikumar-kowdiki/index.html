Dr. Manisha Ravikumar Kowdiki

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Manisha Ravikumar Kowdiki is an Assistant Professor in the School of Electronics and Communication Engineering at MIT-WPU, Pune. She graduated in E & TC Engineering in 2001 and completed her M.E. in Communication System from Dr. BAMU, Aurangabad in 2013. She pursued her Ph.D. in Digital Image Processing using AI-ML from Savitribai Phule, Pune University and completed her research on "Time Series analysis of Hand Joint Trajectories for Indian Sign Language Recognition" in 2023. Dr. Kowdiki has a keen interest in the areas of Image Processing, Computer Vision, AI-ML, and Signal Processing, and has published several research papers in international journals and conferences. She has also guided UG and PG students in their projects and actively participates in curriculum development, effective delivery of lectures, and achieving course outcomes.
