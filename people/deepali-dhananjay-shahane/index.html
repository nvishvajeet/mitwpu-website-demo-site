Dr. Deepali Dhananjay Shahane

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Deepali Shahane is an academic professional with over 25 years of experience in teaching, research, and academic administration. She is currently serving as an Associate Professor at MIT World Peace University, Pune. Her academic interests include agile methodology, software engineering, information systems, digital transformation, and computer management. She has guided undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. students, and has also conducted pre-Ph.D. coursework sessions. She has been actively involved in curriculum design, aligning programs such as BBA, MBA, and MCA with NEP 2020 guidelines. Dr. Shahane has contributed to quality assurance as IQAC Convener and Secretary of the Board of Studies at MITWPU, and has participated in NBA and NAAC accreditation processes. Dr. Shahane has a publication record that reflects her research interests in agile practices, educational informatics, healthcare IT, digital transformation, and management education.

Research interests

Agile Methodology, Software Engineering, Information Systems, Computer Management, Digital Transformation, Analytical Study
