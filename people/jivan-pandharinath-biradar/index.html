Dr. Jivan Pandharinath Biradar

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Jivan Biradar is an experienced assistant professor of economics in the School of Economics and Commerce at MIT-WPU. He holds a Ph.D. and M.Phil. in economics from Pune University, and has over 10 years of teaching experience. Prior to joining MIT, he worked at the Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics, where he conducted research on various projects sponsored by NABARD and the Ministry of Agriculture, Government of India. His areas of research include banking, health insurance, rural development, agriculture finance, and public policy. He teaches microeconomics, macroeconomics, and managerial economics to undergraduate and postgraduate students.

Research interests

General area of research interest is in Development Economics. Specific area interest in Agriculture and Rural Development, Banking and Finance, Insurance
