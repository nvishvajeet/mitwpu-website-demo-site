Dr. Abdul Nasir Khan

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Abdul Nasir Khan is an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at MIT World Peace University. He completed his M.Sc. in Statistics in 2015 from Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh, and subsequently pursued his PhD at the same university, which he completed in 2021

Research interests

Ordered random variables, Bayesian inference, parameter estimation, and biostatistics.
