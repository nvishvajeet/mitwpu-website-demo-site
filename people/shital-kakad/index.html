About

Dr. Shital Kakad is an Assistant Professor at MIT World Peace University, Pune, with over 18 years of teaching experience in Computer Engineering and Information Technology. D. from Sardar Patel Institute of Technology, Mumbai University (2023).

Publications

  2. Kakad, Shital, and Sudhir Dhage. "Knowledge graph and semantic web model for cross domain." Journal of Theoretical and Applied Information Technology 100.16 (2022): 5202-5218.
  3. S. Kakad and S. Dhage, "Ontology Construction from Cross Domain Customer Reviews using Expectation Maximization and Semantic Similarity," 2021 International Conference on Emerging Smart Computing and Informatics (ESCI), Pune, India, 2021, pp. 19-23, doi: 10.1109/ESCI50559.2021.9396780.
