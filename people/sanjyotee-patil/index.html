Dr. Sanjyotee Patil

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D. Computer Science| DES Pune University – Current Savitribai Phule Pune University | MSC in Data Science and AI| CGPA: 9.30 Savitribai Phule Pune University | PGD in Data Science and AI | CGPA: 9.82 Fergusson College, Pune | BSC in Electronic Science | CGPA: 9.30

About

Ms. Sanjyotee Patil is a dedicated faculty member with an interdisciplinary background in Electronic Science and Data Science & Artificial Intelligence. Currently pursuing a Ph. D. in Computer Science, she combines academic rigor with teaching and industry experience. Her expertise includes Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Agentic AI, Generative AI, and AI-IoT systems. Her research focuses on the integration of Artificial Intelligence with IoT to develop intelligent, real-world applications. She is committed to fostering conceptual clarity, analytical thinking, and research-oriented learning, contributing to MIT-WPU’s vision of academic excellence and innovation.

Publications

  1. Ph.D. Research (Ongoing): Focused on advanced applications of Artificial Intelligence, including AI-IoT integration and intelligent systems design. Developed a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)-based PDF Chatbot using LLM frameworks for document querying and summarization. Designed and implemented a Deep Learning-based Skin Tone Detection and Cosmetic Recommendation System using MobileNetV2 and NLP techniques. Contributed to large-scale data processing and validation systems for Guinness World Record projects, handling over one million entries using Python-based automation.

Academic work

  • Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at MIT World Peace University, School of Computer Science and Engineering, she brings valuable academic and teaching experience to the role. She has over two years of teaching experience as a Junior Professor at the New Institute of Technology (NIT), where she delivered courses in Computer Science and Electronic Science. She has also served as a Visiting Faculty at DES, further strengthening her academic exposure. She possesses extensive experience in mentoring student projects, including software development, coding practices, project design specifications, and hardware-based implementations. Additionally, she is proficient in conducting laboratory sessions, guiding academic projects, and implementing outcome-based education with a strong focus on practical and experiential learning.
  • Associate Consultant at FMI (6 months) Experience in data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, forecasting, and dashboard development using Power BI and SQL Worked on deriving actionable insights for business decision-making Active involvement in AI-based research projects and large-scale data processing initiatives
  • Contributor to Guinness World Record for the Largest Online Photo Album of Book Covers (1,111,238 validated entries), in collaboration with Savitribai Phule Pune University and Sakal Media Group Contributor to Guinness World Record for the Largest Online Self-Portrait Album (1,042,538 validated entries) under the “Meri Maati Mera Desh” national initiative Recognized for contribution to large-scale data processing, validation, and automation using AI and data science techniques
