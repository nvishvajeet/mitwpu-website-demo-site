Dr. Tejaswini Pachpor

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Tejaswini A Pachpor is working as an Assistant Professor in the School of Biosciences and Technology, MIT-WPU, Pune. She received Ph.D. from SPPU for her doctoral work at the MACS Agharkar Research Institute, Pune. Her doctoral work focused on yeast metal interactions with special referrence to phytochelatin. Her doctoral research work resulted in technology transfer and indian patent. Dr Tejaswini has more than 9+ years of experience in teaching undergraduate and postgraduate students. She has 2 years of international postdoctoral experience and has published papers in international and national research journals. She worked as CO-PI for Elsevier green chemistry grant and was selected as YI for DBT Indiabioscience YIM 2024.

Research interests

Bioprocess technology, experimental design and optimisation, yeast biotechnology, bioethanol, and value-added biomolecules.
