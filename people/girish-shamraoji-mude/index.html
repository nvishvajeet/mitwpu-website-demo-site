Prof. Girish Shamraoji Mude

Associate Professor and Program Director

About

Dr. Girish Mude is Associate Professor and Program Director of BBA (Digital Marketing) and MBA (Marketing) at School of Business, MIT World Peace University, Pune, India. Skilled in Sales & Marketing, Training, Consultancy, Market Research. Has 18 years of work experience in Teaching and Industry. He holds a Ph.D. in Management, an MBA, and a PGDFT from the University of Pune, and is UGC-NET (twice) and SET qualified. He has completed a PGP in Data Science from KPMG and a Design Thinking course from IIT Madras. His research has been presented at esteemed institutions like IIMs and IITs. Dr. Mude has facilitated the International Winter School Program at the University of Hull, UK, and has visited London, Italy, Switzerland, France, and the UK for academic assignments and institutional collaborations. Recipient “Research Scholarship” from University of Vermont, USA; “Research Fellowship from EURAC, Italy” and “Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam MIT- WPU Research and Innovation Promising Prize 2019”. Dr Girish is also an experienced Consultant with a demonstrated history of working in the marketing and advertising industry.

Research interests

Consumer Behavior, Customer Centricity and Agritourism
