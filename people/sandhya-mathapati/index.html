Dr. Sandhya Mathapati

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D. Civil Structural Engineering, M.E. Structural Engineering, B.E. Civil Engineering

About

Dr. Sandhya Mathapati is working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at MIT-WPU, Pune. She has over 19 + years’ academic & industrial experience. D. in the Civil Structural Engineering in 2020. E. in Structural Engineering in 2007 and B. E. in Civil Engineering in 2004, from Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University Aurangabad Her key research interests include Structural Engineering, analysis of plates and shells, sustainable construction materials, and non-destructive testing methods. She has served as Subject Chairman for UG and PG courses in Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University Aurangabad, and is a Life Member of ISTE, IGS and IEI. She published 16+ research papers in SCI/Scopus/WoS/ Google scholar -indexed journals and conferences.
