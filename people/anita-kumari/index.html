Dr. Anita Kumari

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Anita Kumari is a dedicated academician and finance professional with a strong background in both research and teaching. She holds a PhD in Finance, an MBA in Finance, with extensive academic experience in the field of financial management and analysis. With deep expertise in statistical analysis tools such as SPSS, SEM, PLS, SEM, and AMOS. Her academic interests span financial markets, investment analysis, and corporate finance. She is actively engaged in research and has contributed to various national and international journals. Dr. Kumari is committed to developing industry- relevant financial acumen among her students and promoting ethical financial practices.

Research interests
