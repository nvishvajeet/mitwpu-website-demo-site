Dr. Vibha Utpal Patel

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Vibha Patel received her Ph.D. in Electronics & Communication from Gujarat Technological University in 2023. With over 12 years of teaching experience, she has made notable contributions to her field. Her research interests span wireless communication, digital communication, neural networks, machine learning, data science, electromagnetics and antennas, and digital signal processing. Dr. Patel's work has resulted in the grant of one Australian patent and one South African patent in her name. She has worked on various noteworthy projects, such as the TDMA-based data logger for AWS, moisture sensor using patch antenna, clustering analysis on world health data, and job recommender system, among others.

Research interests

wireless communication, Digital communication, Neural Networks, Machine Learning, Data Science, Electromagnetics and Antennas, and Digital signal processing.
