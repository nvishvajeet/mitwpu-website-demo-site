Dr. Kiran Nagare

Assistant Professor

About

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's also about empowering minds, igniting passions, and shaping futures." As an experienced Assistant Professor specializing in Commerce and Management, she has a proven track record of success and expertise in her field. She holds a PhD in Business Management, as well as a Master's in Business Administration (MBA) and a Master of Commerce (M.Com) with a specialization in Finance and Marketing from RTM Nagpur University. She qualified her MH- SET and UGC- NET in Commerce & Management. Her areas of expertise include digital marketing, research proficiency, teaching excellence, a student-centred approach, effective communication, technological proficiency, time management, and administrative skills. She has few remarkable research publications in her credit. With a demonstrated track record of success in academia, she is passionate about fostering student success and driving innovative learning experiences.

Additional information

  • Key Areas: Digital Marketing, Consumer Behaviour, Brand Collaborations, Google Analytics
