Dr. Chaitanya Girish Burande

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Chaitanya Girish Burande is a mechanical engineer with a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering (pursuing) and a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering - Design. He has 6 years of experience, with 5 years in teaching and 1 year in the industry. His research area mainly focuses on Biomechanical Applications. Prof. Burande's expertise lies in subjects such as Design of Machine Elements, Theory of Machine, Mechanical System Design, and Dynamics of Machinery. His aim is to impart his knowledge and skills to students and contribute to the field of mechanical engineering through research.

Research interests

Bio-Mechanical Engineering Applications.
