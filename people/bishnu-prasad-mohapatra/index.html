Dr. Bishnu Prasad Mohapatra

Assistant Professor

Research

Research interests

Dr. Bishnu Prasad Mohapatra is presently working as Assistant Professor in School of Liberal Arts, Dr. Viswanath Karad MIT-World Peace University, Pune and involved in Teaching and Research in Political Science. Dr. Mohapatra has about 25 years of experience in Teaching and Research in Political Science and peace studies. Specialised in governance, public policy, decentralisation, local democracy and economic development. He has obtained M.Phil in Political Science from Berhampur University, Odisha, India and completed his PhD from Centre for Economic and Social Studies (CESS) Hyderabad in Political Science in 2018. Dr. Mohapatra has been extensively worked in Research Studies in various parts in India. He has published 28 numbers of Journal Articles in various reputed journals apart from 2 Books and 9 Chapters in Edited Books. He has also published 3 Research Monographs and associated with many global professional bodies, policy think-tanks and Journals as Mentor, Editorial Board Member, Advisor and Expert Members.
