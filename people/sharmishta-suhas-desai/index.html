Dr. Sharmishta Suhas Desai

Professor

About

My teaching philosophy emphasizes experiential learning, research-driven education, and strong industry collaboration to prepare students for real-world challenges. I am motivated to cultivate innovation, critical thinking, and entrepreneurship among students through project-based learning and mentorship. My contributions include securing funded research grants from ICMR,AICTE etc, leading innovative projects such as drone-based plant identification and portable agribots, and mentoring a student-led startup operating nationally and internationally. Through research, industry partnerships, and innovation initiatives, I strive to support MIT World Peace University’s vision of value-based education, technological advancement, and creating socially responsible innovators.
