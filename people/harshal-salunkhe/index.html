Prof. Harshal Salunkhe

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Harshal Salunkhe completed a Ph.D. from North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon. Dr. Harshal has completed his master’s in business administration from North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon, specializing in Financial Management. Being an avid reader and a contributor to the field of research, He has also written 32 publications in Journals and attended 35 conferences. Dr. Harshal has been appointed a distinguished review member. Many researchers have also acknowledged Dr. Harshal's work on agricultural subsidies to date; his Google citation index is 165 & h-index is 7. He has filed four patents. With more than 17th experience in education and academia, his expertise in Commodity Market, Indirect Tax Financial Management, Income Tax, International Financial Management, Financial derivatives, Bank Management, Investment Management, Family Business Management, and Corporate Social Responsibility. He handled the UGC visit work at the school level. Apart from that, he has faced four times NAAC accreditation & one time NBA accreditation during his service. Dr. Harshal's Key Responsibility Areas (KRA) included IQAC, teaching, mentoring, research, publication, and being a member of various administrative committees, etc., which he was able to do in a most professional and efficient manner.
