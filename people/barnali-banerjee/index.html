Dr. Barnali Banerjee

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Barnali Banerjee is an accomplished academician and researcher with over 14 years of teaching and research experience. Her expertise lies in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science, with a strong interdisciplinary research background in satellite imagery analysis and predictive modeling. She is committed to fostering analytical thinking and innovation among students while contributing to impactful research aligned with global challenges. She has published in reputed journals including The Lancet and actively contributes as a reviewer and Ph.D. supervisor.
