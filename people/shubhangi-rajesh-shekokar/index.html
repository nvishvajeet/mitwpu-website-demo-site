Dr. Shubhangi Rajesh Shekokar

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. B.E. Civil, M. Tech -Geotechnical Engineering, Ph. D. (Civil Engineering)

About

Prof. Shubhangi Shekokar is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune. She received her B. E. in Civil Engineering from Government College of Engineering, Amravati in 2004 and her M. Tech in Geotechnical Engineering from College of Engineering Pune (COEP) in 2010. D. in Environmental Engineering from SGBAU, Amravati University under the guidance of Dr. Y. R. M. Rao. She has been teaching at the institute since August 2008 and has experience teaching various subjects like BCE, Survey, Soil Mechanics, Foundation Engineering, Building Design and Drawing, Estimation and Costing, Valuation and Slope Stabilization Techniques. Dr. Shekokar has published 5 research papers in Scopus Indexed Journals, 2 in National Journals, and 6 in International Conferences.
