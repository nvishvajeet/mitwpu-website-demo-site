Dr. Shubhangi Rajesh Shekokar

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Shubhangi Shekokar is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune. She received her B.E. in Civil Engineering from Government College of Engineering, Amravati in 2004 and her M. Tech in Geotechnical Engineering from College of Engineering Pune (COEP) in 2010. Presently, she has completed Ph.D. in Environmental Engineering from SGBAU, Amravati University under the guidance of Dr. Y.R.M. Rao. She is a life member of the Indian geotechnical Society (IGS), Institute of Public Health Engineers (IPHE) and the Indian Society of Technical Education (ISTE). She has been teaching at the institute since August 2008 and has experience teaching various subjects like BCE, Survey, Soil Mechanics, Foundation Engineering, Building Design and Drawing, Estimation and Costing, Valuation and Slope Stabilization Techniques. Dr. Shekokar has published 5 research papers in Scopus Indexed Journals, 2 in National Journals, and 6 in International Conferences.
