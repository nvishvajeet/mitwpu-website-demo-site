Dr. Poonam Inamdar

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Poonam R. Inamdar works in the field of synthetic inorganic chemistry. Her research area interests are the synthesis of the metal complexes and evaluation of their biological studies and biomolecular interactions. She is also working on projects which include protein interaction studies of the organic ligands for the design of potent pharmacophores using spectrometric techniques. Her research focus is mainly an exploration of biomolecular interaction studies of the compounds using UV, FTIR, Conductometry, Cyclic voltammetry, Fluorescence spectroscopy, Viscosity and Circular dichroism studies as well as gel electrophoresis studies of the metal complexes for the assessment of DNA cleavage studies.

Research interests

Bioinorganic Chemistry, Medicinal Chemistry, Molecular Modelling
