Prof. Parul Ghole

Assistant Professor

About

Parul Ghole is a distinguished Visual Artist with extensive experience in academia and design training. She has served as an Assistant Professor at JNAFAU(4 years), Hyderabad, and Bharati Vidyapeeth, Pune(1 year), and is currently (since jan 2023 )a faculty member at MIT-WPU,Pune, where her research interests include Illustration, Visualization, and Fundamentals of Design. Additionally, since 2019, she has contributed as a Senior Design Trainer at MAD School in Hyderabad. Her diverse roles highlight her commitment to fostering artistic talent and design principles across various educational institutions.
