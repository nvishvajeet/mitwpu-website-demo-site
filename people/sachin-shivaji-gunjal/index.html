Dr. Sachin Shivaji Gunjal

Assistant Professor

About

Mr. Sachin Gunjal holds a Master's degree in Mathematics and has qualified the State Eligibility Test (SET). With seven years of teaching experience, he has secured the 8th rank across all colleges in T.Y.B.Sc. under SPPU and the 2nd rank at M.Sc.(Maths) in SPPU

Research interests

Matroid theory, algebraic coding theory, and discrete mathematics.

Education

  1. Ph.D. in Mathematics – Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune M.Sc. in Mathematics – Department of Mathematics, Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune B.Sc. in Mathematics – Modern College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Shivajinagar, Pune

Academic work

  • 12 years of teaching experience in higher education, teaching undergraduate and postgraduate Mathematics courses.
  • Research contributions are primarily in Matroid theory and its applications in Algebraic Coding Theory. It includes the study of splitting operations on matroid representable over GF(p) and their applications.
  • Qualified Maharashtra SET exam in Mathematics held in December 2013 3 rd Rank in M.Sc. under all the Science colleges affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. 8 th Rank in B.Sc. under all the Science colleges affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University.

Academic service

  • Life member of the Academy of Discrete Mathematics and Applications (ADMA), an academic society dedicated to fostering high-impact research in Discrete Mathematics.

Teaching

  • Group Theory, Ring Theory, Field Theory, Topology, Computational Statistics, Linear Algebra and Differential Calculus, Integral Calculus, Differential Equations & Transform Techniques, Probability and Statistics, Distribution Theory, R Programming, Linear Algebra, Probability Distributions
