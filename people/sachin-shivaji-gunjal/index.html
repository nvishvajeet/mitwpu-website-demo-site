Dr. Sachin Shivaji Gunjal

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D. in Mathematics – Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune M.Sc. in Mathematics – Department of Mathematics, Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune B.Sc. in Mathematics – Modern College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Shivajinagar, Pune

About

Dr. Sachin Gunjal is a proficient mathematics educator and researcher with over a decade of teaching experience in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at MIT World Peace University, Pune. His commitment to professional development is evident through his participation in numerous faculty development programmes, workshops, and international conferences. His research interests lie in Matroid Theory, Algebraic Coding Theory and Discrete mathematics.

Research interests

Matroid theory, algebraic coding theory, and discrete mathematics.
