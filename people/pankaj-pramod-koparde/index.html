Dr. Pankaj Pramod Koparde

Program Coordinator & Assistant Professor

Education

  1. MSc Biotechnology (2010), IBB, SPPU Pune
  2. PhD in Ecology & Evolution (2019), Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) in collaboration with Salim Ali Center for Ornithology & Natural History (SACON) and National Center for Biological Sciences (NCBS), TIFR Bangalore
  3. PGD in Data Science (2022), RISE WPU & KPMG Netherlands

About

Evolutionary ecologist working in urban ecology, biogeography, conservation science, biodiversity informatics, citizen science, and science communication.

Research interests

Urban and evolutionary ecology; biogeography; biodiversity informatics; citizen science; science communication; odonates and owls.
