Rasika Ramendra Beohar

Assistant Professor

About

Meet Prof Rasika Beohar, an accomplished Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering at MIT World Peace University, Pune. With a Master's degree in E&TC Engineering (VLSI and Embedded systems), she brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise from over 15 years of teaching and one year of industrial experience. Prof Beohar has published 9 research papers in reputed international journals and conferences and has attended over 15 conferences and workshops. Her outstanding contributions have been recognized by the Bharatratna Maulana Azad Social, Education and Sports Association, Pune, who awarded her the coveted "Ideal Teacher Award" on 5th Sept 2022. In addition to her academic pursuits, Prof Beohar has also been selected as a "Volunteer" for the UNESCO Body & Mind Wellness Club in Maryland, USA, where she will assist with social media, organize events, and deliver meditation sessions."
