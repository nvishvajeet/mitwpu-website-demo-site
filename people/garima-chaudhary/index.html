Garima Chaudhary

Assistant Professor

About

Garima Chaudhary is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Visual Arts at the School of Design, MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU). She holds Bachelor’s, Master’s, and PhD degrees in Fine Arts from Aligarh Muslim University, reflecting a strong academic foundation. With over two years of experience in Visual Arts education, Garima combines academic rigor with practical expertise. Her professional skills encompass research, drawing, painting, branding, print and digital media, semiotics, and photography, with a particular research interest in photography, art, and design. In her teaching, she integrates creative vision with structured project management, enabling her to mentor students effectively. This approach helps students develop critical thinking and robust art skills, preparing them for diverse creative challenges. Garima’s commitment to both academic excellence and hands-on learning fosters a dynamic and supportive environment for aspiring artists and practitioners.
