Dr. Parshant Kumar

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Parshant Kumar is currently working as Assistant Professor in the School of Mechanical Engineering. His research interests are mainly focused on the development of composites for mechanical and tribological applications. Please refer to his personal webpage for more information. (https://sites.google.com/view/parshantkumar"Dr. Parshant Kumar is an accomplished Assistant Professor in the School of Mechanical Engineering, where he brings extensive expertise and knowledge to his work. His research interests are centered on the design and development of high-performance composites for mechanical and tribological applications. As an expert in his field, Dr. Kumar is highly skilled in the study of materials science and engineering, with a focus on the mechanical and tribological properties of composites. His work is characterized by a deep understanding of the fundamental principles governing the behavior of these materials, as well as a practical approach to their design and development. Dr. Kumar's contributions to the field of mechanical engineering have been widely recognized, both nationally and internationally. His research has been published in numerous top-tier scientific journals, and he has presented his work at conferences and symposia around the world. To learn more about Dr. Kumar's research and professional background, please visit his personal webpage at https://sites.google.com/view/parshantkumar. On this site, you can find additional information about his publications, research projects, and academic accomplishments." )

Composite Materials, Tribology, Experimental Mechanics, Mechanical Metallurgy
