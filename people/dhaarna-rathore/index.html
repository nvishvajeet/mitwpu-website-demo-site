CA Dhaarna Rathore

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. CA. Dhaarna Singh Rathore is an accomplished academic and finance professional with over 18 years of diverse experience in educational administration and financial Consulting. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at MIT WPU University , she has a distinguished track record in teaching, curriculum development, and research, particularly in the areas of ERP implementation and commerce. Dr. Rathore holds a Ph.D. in Commerce, an MBA in Finance, M. Com and is a Chartered Accountant, bringing a rich blend of academic knowledge and practical expertise to her role. She has made significant contributions, authoring two books, numerous scholarly papers, securing two patents, and obtaining one copyright for innovative teaching methodologies. Her career is marked by innovative leadership, successful program coordination, and a commitment to fostering student growth and success.

Research interests

Accounting, E Commerce, E Business, ERP, Finance, Management Accounting, IOT.
