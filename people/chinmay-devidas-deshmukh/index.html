Dr. Chinmay Devidas Deshmukh

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Chinmay D. Deshmukh is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Pharmacology in the School of Health Sciences and Technology at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Kothrud, Pune, India. He holds a B.Pharm degree from the Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research, Borgaon (Meghe), Wardha, Maharashtra, which he completed in 2005 with first division. Later, in 2007, he completed the Executive Master’s Program in Pharmacy Management (MBA) from Mitcon Institute of Management, Balewadi, Pune, Maharashtra. He also holds a Master of Pharmacy degree in Pharmacology, which he completed in 2009 with first division from Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, Belgaum, Karnataka. He has been teaching for the last 14 years and completed his Ph.D. in Pharmacology in 2017 from Pacific Academy of Higher Education and Research (PAHER), Udaipur, Rajasthan.

Research interests

Preclinical screening of diabetes and Inflammatory Disease models
