Dr. Aquil Ahmad Khan

Program Coordinator & Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Aquil Ahmad Khan believes that education in media and communication should effectively bridge theory, practice, and critical inquiry to foster students' intellectual and professional growth. He strives to create an interactive, research-driven learning environment that connects academic concepts with real-world media practices. His teaching approach combines conceptual clarity with experiential learning, enabling students to engage in newsroom simulations, fieldwork, and collaborative projects through courses such as Television Journalism, Digital Journalism, and Media Production. Drawing on his industry experience, he enriches classroom discussions with practical insights and contemporary perspectives. Aligned with MIT World Peace University's philosophy of life transformation, Dr. Khan emphasizes ethical responsibility, social awareness, and value-based education, nurturing skilled, reflective, and socially responsible media professionals.
