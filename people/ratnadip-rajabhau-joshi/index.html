Dr. Ratnadip Rajabhau Joshi

Associate Professor

Department of Materials Science & Engineering

Education

  1. PhD in Chemcial Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University

About

"Dr. Ratnadip R. Joshi is a highly accomplished academic administrator with a wealth of experience in various key roles. As Associate Dean for Quality Assurance and Chief Coordinator for the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC), Dr. Joshi has led the successful NBA accreditation of nine engineering programs. He has also played a pivotal role in devising and implementing mechanisms for Quality Management Systems (QMS) and Learning Management Systems (LMS) for professional courses. Dr. Joshi's impressive track record of academic administration includes the establishment of a private university, where he has framed rules, regulations, and ordinances, designed university frameworks and policies, and upgraded academic and research standards. He has also spearheaded innovation initiatives that address societal needs and increase the visibility of the organization. Dr. Joshi is an esteemed international advisor, having served as a consultant to Noe21-cBalance Geneva and CCE Finland. His extensive experience and expertise in academic administration make him a highly respected figure in the field. "
