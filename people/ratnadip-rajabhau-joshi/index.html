Prof. Ratnadip Rajabhau Joshi

Associate Professor

About

"Dr. Ratnadip R. Joshi is a highly accomplished academic administrator with a wealth of experience in various key roles. As Associate Dean for Quality Assurance and Chief Coordinator for the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC), Dr. Joshi has led the successful NBA accreditation of nine engineering programs. He has also played a pivotal role in devising and implementing mechanisms for Quality Management Systems (QMS) and Learning Management Systems (LMS) for professional courses.

Research interests

1. Affordable production of Green Hydrogen through thermochemical as well as bio-digetion routes 2. BioCNG from mixed agro-waste, particularly from millet crop residues 3. Delayed release Smart Bio-fertilizers 4. Health devices for Green Hydrogen and Oxygen concentrator 5. Biodegradation of pharmaceutical process effluents

Education

  1. PhD in Chemcial Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University

Publications

  2. Modeling and Simulation of Wax Deposition in Crude Oil Pipeline RR Joshi, International Journal of Engineering Research and Technology 10 (1), 297-300
  3. Simulation of biomass gasification reactor for fuel in gas turbine RR Joshi, B Kulkarni, International Journal of Chemical Sciences and Applications 3 (1), 232-240
