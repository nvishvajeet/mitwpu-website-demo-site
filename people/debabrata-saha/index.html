Dr. Debabrata Saha

Assistant Professor

Quantum Science & Technology Group

Education

  1. Ph.D., RRCAT Indore

About

With a research interest in experimental semiconductor physics, he has contributed to the field with his expertise. He has a passion for exploring the properties and characteristics of semiconductors, and has published several research papers in renowned international journals. His research findings have been appreciated in the scientific community, and he continues to strive towards new discoveries and advancements in this field.
