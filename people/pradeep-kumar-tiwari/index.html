Prof. Pradeep Kumar Tiwari

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Pradeep Kumar Tiwari is an Associate Professor at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University. He has previously worked at Birla Global University and Manipal University Jaipur as an Associate Professor and Assistant Professor. Dr. Tiwari holds a Ph.D. in the Faculty of Engineering, along with M.Tech in CSE and M.Phil in Computer Science. Currently he is pursuing a Postdoctoral. His research areas cloud computing, distributed systems, AI, and IoT. With over fifteen years of experience in teaching and research, Dr. Tiwari has authored more than fifty publications in Web of Science and Scopus indexed journals, conferences, and book chapters in the field of computer science. Additionally, he has received three patent grants and published three patents. One scholar has completed and five scholars are currently pursuing their Ph.D. under the supervision of Dr. Tiwari. Dr. Tiwari serves as the editor of two books indexed in Scopus and has actively contributed as the Organizing Chair and TPC member for numerous international conferences. Recognizing his outstanding contributions, Dr. Tiwari has been honored with the "Best Researcher" award by a reputed organization. He is also serving as a reviewer for several SCI and Scopus journals.
