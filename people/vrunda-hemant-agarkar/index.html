Vrunda Hemant Agarkar

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Vrunda Agarkar is working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Civil Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune. She is presently pursuing her PhD with SVNIT, Surat. She received her BE (Civil) degree in the year 1997 and M E (Civil) degree in the year 1999 from SVNIT, Surat. She joined MIT-WPU in the year 2007. Since then, she has taught various subjects like Basic Civil and Environmental Engineering, Applied Mechanics, Fluid Mechanics, Momentum Transfer, Surveying, Fluid Mechanics 2, Hydrology and Water Resource Engineering, Strength of Material, Civil Engineering and Survey Methods, Introduction to Civil Engineering (Elective F.Y.B. Tech).
