Dr. Rohini Revansiddappa Pujari

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. M. Pharm, PhD

About

Dr. Rohini R. Pharm. ) from Bharati Vidyapeeth’s Poona college of Pharmacy, Pune in 2007. Additionally, she received a D. Pharm. from the Institute of Pharmacy, Bharati Vidyapeeth, Pune affiliated with MSBTE and secured the third rank in Maharashtra. With over 16 years of teaching and 10 years of research experience, she has cracked GATE 2007 with an All India Ranking of 209. Dr. Pujari has been a principal investigator for a research project funded by BCUD, Savitribai Phule Pune University and has received a research grant of 2. 2 Lakhs. She has contributed to various research areas such as Neuropharmacology, Alzheimer’s disease, Cerebral ischemia Cancer chemotherapy, Toxicology, Immunopharmacology, Metabolic syndrome and more. She has over 70 review and research publications in several standard national and international journals, including 46 indexed in Scopus and 18 indexed in Web of Science. Furthermore, she has authored 15 book chapters and 04 textbooks. Dr. Pujari has presented her research work at various national and international conferences (40 presentations) and is actively involved in the research activities of her institution. Dr. Pujari has guided 12 M. Pharm. Her current Google Scholar H-index is 14 (I10 index- 16) with 807 citations.

Research interests

Neuropharmacology, Alzheimer’s disease, Cerebral ischemia Cancer chemotherapy, Toxicology, Immunopharmacology, Metabolic syndrome
