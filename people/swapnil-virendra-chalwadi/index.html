Prof. Swapnil Virendra Chalwadi

Assistant Professor

About

Swapnil Chalwadi is an accomplished Assistant Professor and Program Head at the School of Liberal Arts in MIT WPU, Pune, India. With over 8 years of experience in teaching, he specializes in the macroeconomic and monetary aspects of economic development. He has earned an M.Phil degree in Economics from the University of Hyderabad and a Master's in Economics from Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics, Pune. Swapnil's deep interest and expertise in macroeconomics have helped him publish research papers in reputed journals, making him a valuable asset to the academic community.

Research interests

behavioral economics, monetary economics, gender, and development.

Publications

  1. Chalwadi, Swapnil Virendra, et al. "Gender Differences in Inflation Expectations: Recent Evidence from India." Administrative Sciences 13.2 (2023): 1-14.
