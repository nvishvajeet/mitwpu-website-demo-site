Dr. Bhakti Amogh Paranjape

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Bhakti Paranjape holds a Bachelor's degree in Electronics from Shivaji University and a Masters in Digital Systems from Pune University. With over 14 years of teaching experience and 2 years of industry experience, she is currently working as an Assistant Professor at MIT-WPU. She is also pursuing a PhD in Machine Learning from Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University. Her expertise lies in the areas of digital systems, machine learning, and electronics.
