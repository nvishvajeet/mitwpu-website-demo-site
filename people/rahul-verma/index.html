Dr. Rahul Verma

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Rahul Verma is a trained and qualified sociologist with a passion for Pedagogy and Social Research. He possesses an in-depth understanding and interpretation of empirical reality, which has been acquired through the collection and analysis of primary data from various themes such as the use of Social Media, Social Change and Modernity, Religious Pluralism, and Inter-Religious Interaction. Driven by a natural curiosity towards investigating social phenomena, he employs both Qualitative and Quantitative research methods.

Research interests

Classical Sociological Theories, Sociology of Religion, Social Networks, Sociology of Industry Publication Published a research paper titled “The Prevalence of Religious Pluralism and Inter-Faith Dialogue in India: An Empirical Comparison of Hindus and Muslims of Bhopal” in Panjab University Research Journal (Arts), January-June Issue 2019, ISSN: 0970-5260, pp – 46-67.
