Dr. Prajakta Vishal Adsule

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Prajakta Adsule is an accomplished academician and researcher in the field of Pharmaceutical Chemistry. She graduated with a First Class degree in Pharmacy from Shivaji University, Kolhapur in 2003, and subsequently completed her M.Pharm and Ph.D. degrees with First Class from Shivaji University Kolhapur and Savitribai Phule Pune University respectively. Dr. Adsule has 16 years of experience in teaching and 6 years of research experience. She is currently serving as an Assistant Professor at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, School of Pharmacy, Pune. Dr. Adsule has published numerous research papers in national and international journals and is a life member of APTI and IPA. She has also received research grants and awards for her contributions to the field.

Research interests

Synthetic Chemistry,Pharmaceutical Organic Chemistry,Computer Aided Drug Design,Green Chemistry,Microwave Synthesis

Publications
