Sonal Manohar Muluk

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Sonal Muluk is an accomplished academician with over 16 years of experience in higher education. She currently serves as an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), Pune. Specializing in Marketing Management, she is widely recognized for her student-centric and application-oriented teaching methodology.

Research interests

Research Interests: Marketing Management, Sustainability, Circular Economy
