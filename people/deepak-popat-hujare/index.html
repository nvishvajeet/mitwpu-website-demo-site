Dr. Deepak Popat Hujare

Professor & Program Director

About

Dr. Deepak Hujare is currently working as Professor in School of Mechanical Engineering, Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune.He is in the field of teaching since 1998 and having 22 years of teaching experience and 3 years industrial experience. He is dealing with subjects Advanced Mechanical Vibrations, Dynamics of Machinery, NVH and Design Engineering at undergraduated and post graduate level.He has guided more than 25 under graduate academic Projects and almost 8 post graduate academic projects.

Research interests

NVH, Condition Monitoring, FEA, Acoustics and Vibration, System Modelling, Bio Mechanics

Education

  1. He has completed his school education in 1989 from a village Nagardeola and Diploma in Mechanical Engineering from Government Polytechnic Jalgaon (Maharashtra) in 1992. Afterwards he has completed his graduation B.E. Mechanical in 1995 with distinction, post graduation M.Tech. Mechanical Enginnering (Machine Design) from V.J.T.I. Mumbai from University of Mumbai in 2006 with distinction and Ph.D. Mechanical Engineering in the field of Mechanical vibrations from College of Engineering Pune, Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2018.

Additional information

  • Employment: He has started his career as a Graduate Trainee Engineer in 1995 at Kalyani Brakes Limited, Jalgaon. He has also worked there, as a Senior Design/Process Engineer in Design Engineering department. Then he has shifted to teaching profession in 1998 and joined as a Lecturer at SSVPS College of Engineering Dhule. In 2007, he has joined in MIT Pune as a Sr. Lecturer (Selection Grade). In 2008, he has been approved as Assistant Professor and from 2011-2019 he has worked as Associate Professor in the same college. Currently he is working as a Professor in Dr. Vishwanath karad MIT World Peace University.
