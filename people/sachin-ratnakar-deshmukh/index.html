Sachin Ratnakar Deshmukh

Assistant Professor

About

Meet Sachin Ratnakar Deshmukh, an Assistant Professor in Mechanical Engineering (Workshop) with more than 15 years of experience, including 5 years in reputed industries. Sachin is currently pursuing his Ph.D. and holds an MTech degree. His expertise lies in the fields of Additive Manufacturing and Hybrid Manufacturing. He is passionate about sharing his knowledge with his students and helping them excel in their careers.

Research interests

Additive Manufacturing, Hybrid Manufacturing

Publications
