Prof. Siddharth Shriram Shimpi

Associate Professor

About

Siddharth Shimpi is an academician having over 16 years of experience in education, training, research, and corporate. His core strengths are curriculum development, capacity building, team management, and relationship management. He has a Ph. D. in Management from the University of Pune preceded by an MBA (Marketing Management). He has also completed Post Graduate Certificate Programme in Market Research and Data Analytics from MICA (formerly known as Mudra Institute of Communications), Ahmedabad. He is a researcher and MS Excel trainer. He has 10 Scopus/ABDC/Referred research publications. His areas of expertise include consumer behaviour, e-commerce management, digital marketing, customer relationship management, business analytics, and retail management.
