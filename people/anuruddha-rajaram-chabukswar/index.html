Dr. Anuruddha Rajaram Chabukswar

Professor

About

Dr. A. R. Chabukswar is an esteemed Professor in Pharmaceutical & Medicinal Chemistry at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, with a rich academic and research experience of 25 years. He has made significant contributions to the field, having published over 100 research papers in various National & International Journals. His remarkable achievements include securing 2 Major Research Grants (DST RS. 31.60 Lacs & AICTE - Rs. 11.33 Lacs) and being a Co-PI for CSIR Funded Project (Amount Rs 21.31 Lacs). Additionally, he has completed 2 Minor Research projects worth Rs. 4.9 Lacs and has 2 granted patents to his credit. Dr. Chabukswar has successfully guided 05 Ph.D. students, and 07 Ph.D. students are currently working under his guidance. He has also mentored more than 65 M. Pharm students, helping them achieve their academic and professional goals. Furthermore, he has authored 5 books and 10 book chapters, showcasing his expertise in the field of Pharmaceutical & Medicinal Chemistry. Dr. Chabukswar has been an active participant in various National & International Conferences, where he has presented his research papers. He is a well-respected member of the academic community, and his significant contributions have been acknowledged through various awards and accolades.
