Prof. Pooja Pravin Gundewar

Associate Professor

About

Pooja Gundewar is a highly qualified engineer with extensive experience in the field of Instrumentation Engineering. She graduated from SGGS College of Engineering, Marathwada University in 1992, and went on to complete her master's degree in M.E. (Instrumentation) from Govt. College of Engineering, Pune, Pune University in 2005. She has since pursued her Ph.D. from VIT Research Centre, SPPU, Pune, which she successfully completed in 2021.

Research interests

Computer Vision, Digital Image Processing, Transducer and Signal Conditioning, Robotics and Automation, Mechatronics
