About

I have been working with MIT for more than 3 decades. I believe in getting connected to students with deep knowledge of the course and motivating them to reach their best level. A teacher should always be a good student as technology keeps changing and teachers keep learning. I worked as HOD, Computer Engineering under SPPU, HoS, FYBTech under WPU and currently working as Professor at DCET and ICC Chairperson at University level.

Research interests

Computer Science and Engineering Computer Vision, Embedded Systems, Microprocessors & Microcontrollers
