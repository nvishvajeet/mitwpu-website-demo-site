About

I am a dedicated academician with a strong interest in teaching, and student development in the field of Computer Science. My teaching philosophy focuses on creating an interactive learning environment where students are encouraged to think critically, explore new ideas, and apply knowledge to real-world problems. I am motivated to continuously improve my teaching methods through research and innovation, particularly in deep learning and video processing. I aim to contribute to MIT-WPU’s vision by promoting academic excellence, ethical values, and research-driven learning that prepares students for future technological challenges. Specialisation: Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
