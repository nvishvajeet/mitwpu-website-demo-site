Dr. Rohit Raghunath Ghadge

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Rohit Ghadge is a distinguished professional who has made a significant impact in the field of Mechanical Engineering. Dr. Ghadge is a faculty member at the School of Mechanical Engineering, where he brings his rich experience and knowledge to educate and inspire the next generation of mechanical engineers. Dr. Ghadge is a seasoned professional with a well-rounded career that spans over two decades. He has honed his skills and expertise in Mechanical Engineering through a combination of industrial and academic experience. With 3.5 years of industry experience and around 20 years of teaching experience, Dr. Ghadge is a well-respected figure in the field. As an educator, Dr. Ghadge has dedicated himself to imparting knowledge and skills to students, nurturing their potential and helping them grow as engineers. He is passionate about creating a stimulating learning environment that encourages students to explore and innovate, equipping them with the tools they need to excel in their careers. In addition to his extensive teaching experience, Dr. Ghadge also has hands-on experience in the industry. This practical experience has given him a unique perspective on the field of Mechanical Engineering, allowing him to bring real-world examples and insights to his classroom teaching. Overall, Dr. Ghadge's wealth of knowledge, expertise, and experience make him a valuable asset to the School of Mechanical Engineering and an inspiring teacher and mentor to his students.
