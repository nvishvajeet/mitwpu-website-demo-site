Dr. Ganesh Pralhadrao Borikar

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Ganesh P. Borikar is an expert in the field of manufacturing engineering and leads the Central Workshop at MIT-WPU. His role involves upgrading and setting up advanced machines and equipment in line with industry practices to train students in modern technology. Dr. Borikar's expertise lies in the domains of additive manufacturing and micro manufacturing. He supports and contributes to innovation and projects related to manufacturing engineering and smart work. With his extensive knowledge and experience, Dr. Borikar plays a vital role in shaping the future of manufacturing technology at MIT-WPU.

Research interests

Additive manufacturing, Vacuum and Investment Casting
