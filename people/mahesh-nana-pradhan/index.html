Prof. Mahesh Nana Pradhan

Associate Professor and Program Coordinator (R&A)

About

With over 25 years of teaching experience in the prestigious MIT-WPU, Prof. Mahesh Pradhan is a renowned figure in the field of mechanical engineering. His two-year industrial experience at Bajaj Tempo Ltd has contributed to his vast knowledge in the industry. Prof. Pradhan completed his M.E in Mechanical from COEP, Pune and has authored several research papers in the field of design and composite materials. As the Dept. Coordinator of the Centre for Industry Academia Partnership (CIAP) and Internship Coordinator, he has played a key role in shaping the academic curriculum of MIT-WPU. He is also the coordinator of the Rickshaw and Taxi Fare Meter Testing Centre and has served as a committee member for the implementation of electronic Rickshaw and Taxi fare meters in Maharashtra State. Prof. Pradhan is also credited with setting up the Mechatronics and Robotics laboratory in MIT-WPU and is currently serving as the laboratory incharge.

Research interests

Design, Composite Material, Robotics, Mechatronics
