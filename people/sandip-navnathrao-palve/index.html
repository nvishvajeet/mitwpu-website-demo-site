Dr. Sandip Navnathrao Palve

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Sandip Navnathrao Palve joined MIT-WPU in 2003 and is currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering. He is ompleted his PhD in 2020 from Pune University and has published more than ten Research papers included Internatinal Journals and conferences. He is a life member of Indian Society of Remote Sensing and various other world Reputed Universities and organisations.
