Ashish Singh Tomar

Assistant Professor

About

Ashish believes that design education should be hands-on, empathetic, and rooted in real human needs. His goal is not just to teach tools and techniques, but to help students observe deeply, think critically, and build solutions that truly matter. What drives him is the joy of seeing young minds grow curious and confident. At MIT-WPU, he hopes to create a warm, collaborative learning space where students feel encouraged to experiment, fail, and learn. With his background in product development and UX, he would love to contribute to projects that blend technology with social impact, staying true to the institution's vision of nurturing responsible, innovative leaders.

Education

  1. M.Des - Product Design and Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru
  2. B.Tech - Mechanical Engineering, Jagannath University, Jaipur

Academic work

  • 5+ Years of Teaching Experience at VIT Vellore, WeSchool Mumbai
  • Year Long Project with SID, IISc and Oorja Energy Engineering Services Pvt Ltd
  • Mentored a team of 6 students for the Grand Final of SIH 2023
  • Second Place in NDIN Online Design Hackathon 23rd-24th May 2020

Teaching

  • Product Design Studio Project, Product Design Management, Strategy and Innovation, Design for Industrial Experiences, Design for EdTech, and Design for E- Commerce.

Additional information

  • Areas of Expertise: Product Design, UI UX Design, Design Management, Service Design, Prototyping
