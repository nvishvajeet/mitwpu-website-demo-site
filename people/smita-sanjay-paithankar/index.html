Smita Sanjay Paithankar

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Smita S Paithankar is a highly qualified and experienced educator in the field of Electrical Engineering. She completed her B.E. in Electrical Engineering from Nagpur University in 2000, followed by an M.Tech in Integrated Power System from G.H.Raisoni College of Engineering in 2008. With over 9 years of teaching experience and 8 months of industry experience, Prof. Paithankar has a strong command over subjects such as Basic Electrical Engineering, Electrical Technology, Basic Electronics Engineering, and Power devices and Machines. She is an active member of the Institution of Engineers (IE) and the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE). Prof. Paithankar has also been involved in various academic and administrative responsibilities throughout her career.
