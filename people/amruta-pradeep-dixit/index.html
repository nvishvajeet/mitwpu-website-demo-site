Prof. Amruta Pradeep Dixit

Assistant Professor

About

Ms. Amruta Dixit brings over 16 years of rich experience in teaching and academic mentoring in the field of finance and accounting. She has taught diverse subjects including Financial Management, Cost & Management Accounting, Financial Accounting, and Business Simulation. Currently pursuing her Ph.D. in Financial Management under SPPU, she is also a certified Business Simulation trainer from Marketplace Simulations (USA). Apart from her teaching responsibilities, Ms. Dixit contributes actively to curriculum restructuring, student mentoring, project guidance, academic audits, and SOP design. She has two patents to her credit. Ms. Dixit is committed to supporting students not just in academics but also in their holistic professional and personal development.
