Dr. Anusha Raghavendra Pai

Associate Professor

About

A highly experienced and academically accomplished faculty member in Computer Science and Engineering with over 25 years of dedicated service in higher education, committed to academic excellence, innovation, leadership, and value-based learning, while contributing significantly to institutional development through various key academic and administrative responsibilities.
