Dr. Ujjwala Shitalkumar Rawandale

Assistant Professor

About

Mrs. Ujjwala Rawandale holds a Bachelor's degree in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering from North Maharashtra University and a Master's degree in Digital Electronics from Amravati University. She is currently pursuing a Ph.D. from SCOE Research Centre, Savitribai Phule Pune University. With 19 years of teaching experience, she is currently working as an Assistant Professor in the School of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University. She has presented papers at various prestigious conferences and published research papers in reputed journals. She is an IEEE member and a Life Member of ISTE, and has taken up several administrative responsibilities.
