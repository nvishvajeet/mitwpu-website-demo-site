Ashok Shripati Deokar

Assistant Professor

About

Mr. Ashok S. Deokar has successfully completed his MCA and MBA from Savitribai Phule Pune University. He is presently pursuing his Ph.D. at the same university, specializing in Big Data and Digital Marketing. With over 22 years of teaching experience & 1.5 as Industry Experience, he has imparted knowledge across a wide range of subjects including Database Management Systems, Advanced DBMS, Web Technologies, Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, Mobile Application Development, Operating Systems, Network Technologies, Data Structures, C, and C++. He has contributed extensively to academia through the publication of numerous research papers in reputed journals and conferences and holds two patents to his credit. In addition, he has actively participated in various STTPs and FDPs organized by AICTE, IITs, and leading universities. In 2025, he joined MIT World Peace University as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Applications, where he also serves as the CAP Coordinator. Previously, he worked as a College Examination Officer for Savitribai Phule Pune University. Beyond his academic responsibilities, he has successfully organized and conducted several co-curricular activities for students and professional organizations. He is also a certified ISO auditor, with hands-on experience in managing ISO audits.]
