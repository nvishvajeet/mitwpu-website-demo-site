Dr. Akanksha Jaiswal

Assistant Professor

About

She is a faculty member specializing in Operations and Supply Chain Management. She holds a Ph.D. from the University of Allahabad and an MBA from IIIT Allahabad, bringing a strong academic foundation to her work. With 2 years of corporate experience and 6 years of teaching in the field, she combines industry insights with academic rigor. Her areas of interest include process optimization, logistics management, and strategic supply chain design, with a focus on connecting theoretical knowledge to practical business challenges.
