About

Dr. Deepali Pankaj Javale is Assistant Professor in Computer Engineering & Technology at MIT World Peace University, Pune, with 24+ years of teaching experience. Her research spans Healthcare Analytics, Machine Learning, IoT, and Data Science. She holds 15+ Indian copyrights and 3 patents and has received Best Paper awards. She has mentored winning teams at Smart India Hackathon and SNDT Innovation Mahakumbh. A reviewer for Informatics in Medicine Unlocked (Elsevier, Q2), she has also delivered invited talks for FDPs and AICTE-ISTE sponsored programmes on Data Science and IoT.
