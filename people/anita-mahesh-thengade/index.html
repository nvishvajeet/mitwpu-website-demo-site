Anita Mahesh Thengade

Assistant Professor

About

Dr. Anita Thengade is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, India. With over 24 years of teaching experience, She earned her Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded. Her research interests include Machine Learning, Medical Image Processing, and Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality. She has published more than 30 research papers in reputed international journals and conferences and holds two granted patents. She was also selected through the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) for the position of Assistant Professor early in her career.
