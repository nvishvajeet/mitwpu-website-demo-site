Prof. Sukhada Prasad Bhingarkar

Associate Dean - External Relations

About

Dr. Sukhada Bhingarkar is an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, MIT World Peace University in Pune. With over 20 years of teaching experience, she earned her PhD in Information Technology from Mumbai University. Dr. Bhingarkar's research interests span across Cloud Computing, Information Security, and Machine Learning, which have been published in more than 40 research papers in renowned international journals and conferences.

Research interests

Cloud Computing, Information Security, Machine Learning
