Pallavi Utkarsh Nehete

Lecturer in Computer Science & Engineering

About

Mrs.Pallavi U. Nehete is working as an Lecturer at School of Polytechnic, Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, India. She has 14 years of teaching experience.She has completed his post-graduation, Master of Engineering in Information Technology from Pune University. She is pursuing PhD from Symbiosis International University, Pune. She has presented various conference papers in National and International Conference and also published papers in international journals such as IEEE, UGC and Other.

Research interests

Machine Learning,Computer Vision
