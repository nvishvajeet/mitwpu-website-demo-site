Dr. Bharat Sambhu Chaudhari

Dean - Research and Development

Education

  1. B.E. in Industrial Electronics, Amravati University, 1989
  2. M.E.Tel.E., Jadavpur University, 1993
  3. Ph.D. in Engineering, Jadavpur University, 2000

About

Dr. Bharat S. Chaudhari is Senior Professor (Higher Administrative Group) and Dean of Research and Development at MIT-WPU. His work spans AIoT, TinyML, low-power wide-area networks, silicon photonics, optical networks, switching, and routing.

Research interests

AIoT, TinyML, LPWANs, Si-Photonics, Optical Networks, Switching & Routing Technologies
