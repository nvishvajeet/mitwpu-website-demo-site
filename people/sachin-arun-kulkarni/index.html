Prof. Sachin Arun Kulkarni

Associate Professor & Head of Department

About

Dr. Sachin Kulkarni is a distinguished academician with a Ph.D. in Physics, specializing in nanomaterials. With an impressive career spanning over 24 years in teaching and 5 years in industry, he brings a rare blend of academic excellence and practical insight to his profession. Currently serving as an Associate Professor and Head of the Department of Physics, he has played a pivotal role in shaping both curriculum and research culture.

Research interests

Nanomaterials and nanocomposites: synthesis, characterisation, and applications.
