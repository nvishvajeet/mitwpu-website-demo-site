Dr. Mrunal Ninad Annadate

Associate Professor

About

Dr. Mrunal Ninad Annadate holds a Ph.D. in Electronics Engineering from SPPU and has over 24 years of experience in the Technical Educational Field. With extensive teaching experience, she possesses skills in SQL, Python, Java, C, C++, VHDL, ALP, MATLAB, and DBMS. She successfully manages multiple portfolios such as being a member of the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC), NAAC coordinator at the school level, Auditor, and a member of PMQIC, to name a few. Dr. Annadate has published several research papers in reputed journals and holds two patents, one granted and one filed under her name. She has also contributed a book chapter to the field of Electronics Engineering. Her expertise in various technical fields and active involvement in multiple committees and projects demonstrates her dedication to excellence in education."

Research interests

Image/Video Processing, Biomedical Engineering, Programming/Coding
