Prof. Rajeev Sengupta

Associate Professor & Program Director

About

Dr. Rajeev Sengupta brings over two decades of industry experience in the BFSI sector across leadership roles in insurance, banking, and financial services. Currently serving as Associate Professor and Program Director for Banking & Finance at MIT-WPU, he leads academic delivery for BBA-BFI, MBA Finance, and MBA-BFS programs. A PhD from KIIT University, Dr. Sengupta holds qualifications in Finance, Insurance (FIII), Law, and Education Management. He has published extensively in Scopus, Springer, Routledge, and ABDC-indexed journals, with research interests spanning financial literacy, bancassurance, behavioural finance, and sustainable finance. His teaching portfolio includes Managerial Economics, Financial Management, Corporate Finance, Financial Markets, Financial Planning & Wealth Management and Legal Aspects of Business.
