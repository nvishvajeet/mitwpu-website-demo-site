Dr. Prashant Pralhad Malavadkar

Professor & Program Director

About

Dr. Prashant P. Malavadkar is Professor and Head of the mathematics and statistics department of Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT-World Peace University in Pune, India. He has a Ph.D. in mathematics, as well as M.Sc., SET, and M.Phil. degree

Research interests

Matroid theory, graph theory, combinatorics, and discrete mathematics.
