Jagruti Divakar Patil

Assistant Professor

Research

Research interests

Dr. Jagruti Patil is a distinguished Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune. She has earned a Ph.D. in Structural Engineering with a specialization in Earthquake Engineering from MIT-ATD University, Pune. Additionally, she holds an M.E. in Structural Engineering and a B.E. in Civil Engineering from Pune University. Apart from teaching responsibilities, Dr. Patil is also responsible for coordinating social media and admissions activities at MIT-WPU.
