About

My teaching philosophy focuses on fostering critical thinking, innovation, and practical problem-solving among students. I aim to integrate theoretical knowledge with real-world applications, particularly in areas such as artificial intelligence, data science, and optimization. I am motivated to create an engaging learning environment that encourages research, collaboration, and ethical values. Through effective teaching, mentoring, and research contributions, I strive to support MIT-WPU’s vision of developing socially responsible professionals and advancing knowledge that contributes to technological progress and societal well-being.
