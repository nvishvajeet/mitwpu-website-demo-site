Dr. Arunabh Pandey

Assistant Professor

Education

  1. Ph.D. MTech (Transportation Engineering), BE (Civil Engineering)

Research interests

My research focuses on eliminating the challenges of waste utilization and improving their performance in concrete pavements. My core research interest is to probe the microstructure of low carbon concrete by focusing on composition-microstructure-performance relationships in cement concrete and using the resultant changes in microstructure and phase composition to understand the long-term performance of the concrete pavement.
