Aparna Ashok Kamble

Assistant Professor

About

Prof. Aparna Kamble holds a BE degree from Shivaji University Kolhapur and an M.E degree from Savitribai Phule Pune University, Maharashtra, India in 2004 and 2012 respectively. Completed PhD in 2019 in the domain of WSN. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Engineering & Technology at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune, Maharashtra, India, with over 19 years of teaching experience. Her research interests revolve around wireless sensor networks and distributed systems.
