Prof. Vijaya Vijaykumar Pangave

Lecturer (Selection Grade)

About

Mrs. Vijaya Pangave is a highly experienced and knowledgeable Senior Lecturer in the E/Tc department, with a total teaching experience of 25.8 years. She holds an M.Tech in Communication System and a B.E. in E&TC. Mrs. Pangave specializes in subjects such as MIC, EEC, ECN, BEM, and LIC, and currently works as an Assistant Professor in MITWPU, School of Polytechnic & Skill Development. With her expertise in the field of electronics and communication, she has proven to be an asset to her students and colleagues alike.
