Priyamwada Sachindra Mahajani

Assistant Professor

About

Priyamwada Mahajani is an Assistant Professor in the School of Engineering and Technology at MIT World Peace University. She received her Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Telecommunication and Master of Engineering in Electronics and Telecommunication from Govt. College of Engineering, Pune, India. Ms. Mahajani's research interests lie in the areas of signal processing and error control coding for communication systems. She is passionate about developing innovative techniques to improve the performance of communication systems, and her research has focused on developing novel signal processing algorithms for communication applications. As an Assistant Professor, Ms. Mahajani is committed to teaching and mentoring students, sharing her knowledge and expertise with the next generation of engineers. Her dedication to her students and her contributions to the field of signal processing make her a valuable member of the academic community.
