Dr. Anushri Kulkarni

Assistant Professor

About

I am Dr. Anushri Nishant Kulkarni, an experienced academician with over 15 years of teaching in Electronics and Computer Engineering. I hold a Ph.D. in Wireless Sensor Networks and have published research in reputed SCOPUS-indexed journals and conferences. My teaching philosophy centers on outcome-based education, conceptual clarity, and research-driven learning. I am committed to fostering innovation, critical thinking, and ethical engineering practices among students. Actively involved in academic coordination, accreditation processes, and project mentoring, she integrates modern ICT tools and interdisciplinary approaches to prepare students for industry, research, and lifelong learning.
