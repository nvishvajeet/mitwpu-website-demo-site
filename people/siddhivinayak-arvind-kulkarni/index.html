  1. Ph.D: PhD: Griffith University, Australia
  2. M.Tech: Masters in IT: Griffith University, Australia
  3. B.Tech: BE: SPPU, Pune India

About

He has been awarded the esteemed title of Honorary Research Fellow (HRF) by the Faculty of Science and Technology at Federation University Australia in recognition of his outstanding research contributions.

Dr. Kulkarni has also received the Griffith Sciences International Alumnus Award from Griffith University, Australia for his noteworthy achievements in teaching, research, and community service on an international level. His research interests include Pattern Recognition, Machine Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Health Informatics.
