Prof. Siddhivinayak Arvind Kulkarni

Professor

Research

Research interests

Dr. Siddhivinayak Kulkarni holds a Master's degree and a Ph.D. from Griffith University, Australia, and has worked at prestigious universities such as Nipissing University in Canada and Federation University in Australia. He has been awarded the esteemed title of Honorary Research Fellow (HRF) by the Faculty of Science and Technology at Federation University Australia in recognition of his outstanding research contributions.
