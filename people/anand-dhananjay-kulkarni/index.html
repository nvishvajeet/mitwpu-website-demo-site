Dr. Anand Dhananjay Kulkarni

Program Coordinator (Chemical) & Assistant Professor

Department of Materials Science & Engineering

About

Anand D. Kulkarni is an expert in biodiesel production and optimization, with extensive experience in comparing different methods for controlling wax deposition in crude oil transportation, including magnetic field and pour point depressants. His current research is centered on the valorization of biomass through chemical and biological pathways. Specifically, he is exploring ways to upgrade waste glycerol - a byproduct of biodiesel production - into high-value products such as polyglycerol and polyhydroxy alkynoates (PHA).

Research interests

Flow Assurance, Biofuels, Biopolymers, Education
