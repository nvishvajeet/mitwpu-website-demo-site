Life Transformation Centre (LTC)

Explore the Life Transformation Centre at MIT-WPU, fostering personal growth, emotional well-being, leadership skills, and holistic development.

  • Life Transformation Centre (LTC)

The Life Transformation Centre (LTC) is dedicated to nurturing purpose-driven, resilient, and socially conscious individuals. Inspired by the philosophy of Vijigīṣū Jīvanavādī Yuvān (विजिगीषु जीवनवादी युवा), LTC promotes holistic development through five core pillars: Agriculture and Nature, Physical Fitness and Sports, Patriotism and Nation Building, Teamwork and Leadership, and Spirituality.

Guided by the principle of "Connecting the Soul to the Soil," LTC blends experiential learning with Bharatiya values, encouraging students to develop self-awareness, leadership, discipline, and a deeper sense of purpose. Through immersive experiences, students are empowered to become compassionate leaders and responsible contributors to society.
