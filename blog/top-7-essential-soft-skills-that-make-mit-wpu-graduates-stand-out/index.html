Top 7 Essential Soft Skills That Make MIT-WPU Graduates Stand Out

MIT-WPU recognizes the importance of these skills and provides its students with extensive soft skills training across all its domains.

Reconstructed from the retained MIT-WPU public capture. View official source

While college education mainly revolves around learning technical skills, the importance of soft skills has steadily increased with time. Gone are the days when these skills were considered secondary. Students need both technical and soft skills in their personal and professional journeys.

MIT-WPU recognizes the importance of these skills and provides its students with extensive soft skills training across all its domains. Along with the technical aspects of every course and its subjects, the students acquire soft skills that help them succeed in life. Every soft skills program at MIT-WPU is designed to provide each student with a perfect educational package. The faculty believes that holistic development is the best way to move forward in life. All courses at MIT-WPU involve soft skill training, irrespective of the discipline our students choose.

Breaking Down MIT-WPU's Power Pack of Soft Skills

Let us explore a few prominent student-oriented soft skills we teach :

No soft skills program is complete without teaching students effective communication. Whether a student is studying to be a lawyer, data scientist, clinical pharmacist, or other professionals, they will be trained in effective communication. Every profession and field of study requires you to communicate with people. Excellent communication skills help you engage with others and put your point across in the best way possible. The faculty at MIT-WPU trains students in verbal and non-verbal communication. While verbal communication involves the use of language, non-verbal communication is all about body language and gestures.

The institute also emphasizes being a good listener. Our students are taught how to listen with intent. They learn the difference between “hearing” and “listening.” Great communicators are always great listeners.

There is hardly any profession or business that involves working in complete isolation. Even if you work remotely, you most likely have your team. Teamwork is an important part of the soft skills program MIT-WPU has for its students across all disciplines. Our students are taught how to collaborate with their team members while moving toward common goals. Teamwork is about valuing a team’s unity while respecting every member individually.

At MIT-WPU, these students are given numerous projects throughout their academic journeys to develop their team-building skills. Students pursuing business administration and related fields need to master teamwork to become successful leaders. These soft skills initiatives at MIT-WPU are designed to boost every student’s confidence, resolve conflicts, and encourage collaboration toward a shared goal.

Humans have come so far in the world mainly because of their ability to reason. Critical thinking is the key to solving most problems in your personal and professional lives. At MIT-WPU we encourage critical thinking by giving students the freedom to make their own decisions and choose their own paths. Our faculty organizes debates and discussions across the board to give students a platform to voice their opinions. Such sessions enhance people’s ability to think critically and clearly.

Soft skills training in this area also involves exploring multiple ways to solve a problem after completely understanding it. The soft skill training at MIT-WPU helps students with their analytical skills, which are especially useful in computer science, data analytics, and related fields.

Everyone operates out of a fixed allotment of hours daily. In this regard, time management is all about how you utilize these hours. Our students also learn the value of time by allocating it effectively to multiple activities. But sometimes they get too carried away by hard study or complete leisure. So, we help them maintain a healthy work-life balance. Even our faculty encourages them to strike the right balance between the two extremes.

This soft skills training of time management comes in handy during examinations, projects, research work, and almost every phase of a student’s life. Making time management your priority definitely takes you far.

Rigidity is the enemy of progress. Students who cannot adapt to different circumstances will likely experience difficulties in their professional journey. MIT-WPU's soft skill training also teaches students the art of adaptability, a skill that is often acquired through difficult experiences. We encourage our students to keep an open mind and adjust to circumstances that seem uncomfortable or strange at first. Change is always difficult but adapting to it is often the only way forward.

Mastering adaptability helps our students adjust well in corporate environments once they leave us. Whether they work as entrepreneurs, managers, or employees, adapting to different situations will always make their journeys fulfilling.

All our MBA and related courses focus on developing leadership skills. Being a good leader is way more complicated than it looks. Managing a team of different minds is no less than a mammoth challenge! Our soft skills training prepares the students to lead by example and make the most of their positions once acquired. Leadership is all about taking initiative, working for the common good, and always thinking about what’s best for the team.

When our students occupy positions of authority, this skill helps them build healthy relationships with their subordinates at all levels. Effective leadership skills also help students in their personal lives. They teach them how to take charge, stand up for themselves, and navigate matters when the going gets tough.

People often dive too deep into the notion that the world outside is full of cunning people. While many people can be difficult to deal with, the solution is never to suppress your emotions. At the same time, being a slave to your emotions will never give you the results you need.

At MIT-WPU, we teach our students the importance of emotional intelligence and help them develop the same during their courses. EQ as a skill lets our students know themselves better while managing their emotions. Being mindful and understanding of others’ emotions forms the core of an emotionally intelligent person.

The MIT-WPU Edge : Where Theory Meets Practice

MIT-WPU commits to every student’s holistic development. Our faculty teaches our students some of the most valuable soft skills influencing their professional and personal lives. From effective communication and leadership to teamwork and adaptability, the students passing out from MIT-WPU are prepared to take on the world. Irrespective of their chosen domain, they will receive comprehensive training in these skills (and many more) along with technical knowledge.
