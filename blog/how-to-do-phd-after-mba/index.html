Detailed Guide about Pursuing PhD after MBA

Pursuing a PhD after an MBA represents a transformative academic journey that bridges practical business knowledge with cutting-edge research and scholarly exploration. This advanced academic path is designed for professionals who aspire to contribute meaningfully to management scholarship, drive innovation, and develop deep expertise in specialised areas of business and management.

The PhD programme provides an opportunity to transition from being a business practitioner to becoming a thought leader and research expert. It offers a unique platform to investigate complex business challenges, develop innovative solutions, and contribute original knowledge to the field of management. Whether you're passionate about organisational behaviour, strategic management, marketing insights, or emerging business technologies, a PhD allows you to dive deep into specialised research domains.

Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for a PhD programme after an MBA, candidates typically need to meet the following requirements :

  • A master's degree in Management, Business Administration, or an equivalent postgraduate qualification
  • Minimum aggregate marks of 55% or equivalent CGPA in the master's degree
  • Candidates with NET/SLET/GATE qualification receive additional preference
  • Successful completion of the university's entrance examination
  • Qualifying scores in national-level tests like UGC-NET, CSIR-NET, or GATE
  • Personal interview
  • Preferred but not mandatory
  • Candidates with relevant work experience in academia or industry are often given additional weightage
  • Demonstrated research potential or published work can strengthen the application

Program Duration

The PhD programme in Management and Business Administration typically follows a structured timeline

  • Minimum Duration: 3 years
  • Maximum Duration: 5-6 years
  • Part-time and full-time options available
  • Comprehensive coursework in the first year
  • Research proposal development and approval
  • Extensive research and thesis preparation
  • Final thesis submission and public defense

Stepwise Application Process

The steps to apply for your application to the PhD programme are as follows

  • Research potential research areas
  • Identify potential research guides
  • Prepare a preliminary research concept
  • Complete the online application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Academic transcripts
  • Entrance exam scores
  • Research proposal draft
  • Initial document verification
  • Entrance exam evaluation
  • Research aptitude assessment
  • Receipt of admission offer
  • Payment of initial fees
  • Formal registration

Research, Thesis, and Key Points

When preparing to pursue a PhD after MBA, you need to be clear about your area of research, thesis and some other key considerations, as mentioned below

  • It is important to pay emphasis on original and innovative research
  • An interdisciplinary approach is mostly encouraged
  • Also, alignment with current business challenges and global trends is highly appreciated
  • The thesis must be developed under the supervision of experienced faculty members
  • With regular progress reviews and opportunities for industry collaborations
  • Also, a thesis with potential for interdisciplinary research projects is more rewarding
  • Choose a research area aligned with personal interests and industry relevance
  • Develop strong relationships with potential research supervisors
  • Continuously update research methodology skills
  • Participate in academic conferences and workshops
  • And consider potential publication opportunities
