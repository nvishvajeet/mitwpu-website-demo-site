MIT-WPU Scholarship Advantage for Your Career after JEE

MIT-WPU offers a range of scholarships to support deserving students financially. Here's a breakdown:

  • Dr. Vishwanath Karad Scholarship: This prestigious scholarship grants a complete 100% waiver of your tuition fees for the entire programme duration. However, competition is fierce as it's only awarded to the top 5% of scorers across all programmes.
  • MIT-WPU Merit Scholarship-I: This scholarship offers 50% financial aid, covering half of your total academic fees throughout the programme. It's a great option for high-performing students, with awards available to up to 10% of admitted students.
  • MIT-WPU Merit Scholarship-II: This scholarship waives 25% of your total academic fees for the programme. It's a fantastic opportunity to ease the financial burden, and scholarships are offered to up to 12% of admitted students.
  • Scholarships to Elite Sportspersons: These scholarships are awarded to elite sportspersons based on their performance at nationally or internationally recognised sporting events. Eligibility extends to those participating in or winning medals at events such as the West zone inter-university, all India inter-university, national, or international levels while representing MIT-WPU or a government sports organisation.

  • Hon'ble Shri. T. N. Seshan Scholarship: This special scholarship is exclusively available to School of Government students who are dependents of in-service public servants with a minimum of 5 years of government service. This scholarship amounts to 15% of the programme fee.
  • Criteria for continued Scholarship Year on Year: To retain the scholarship, students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 8 throughout the programme with no backlogs, maintain 80% attendance per semester, and adhere to the university's Code of Conduct. Violations result in warnings or forfeiture of the scholarship.

At MIT-WPU, the specific JEE percentile required for scholarships varies depending on the chosen engineering programme. However, a minimum of 85% and above in JEE is typically needed to pursue studies at MIT-WPU. Higher percentiles generally increase the likelihood of receiving scholarships. So, aiming for a higher percentile not only enhances your chances of admission but also opens doors to various scholarship opportunities.
