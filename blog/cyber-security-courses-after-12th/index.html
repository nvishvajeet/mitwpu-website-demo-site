The number of cybercrimes are increasing rapidly, and so is the demand for security experts. Now, let us discuss the importance and future of Cyber Security.

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security is one of the most important aspects of safeguarding critical data from increasing digital threats. It primarily focuses on practices to secure computer systems and networks from cyber threats like malware (viruses, ransomware, spyware), phishing attacks, social engineering, denial-of-service attacks, and insider threats.

Cyber security professionals employ a multi-layered approach to reduce risks and respond to incidents, combining technical, physical, and procedural security measures.

Importance of Cyber Security

Cyber security is also crucial in protecting sensitive information and critical infrastructure. It leads to fostering innovation and maintaining trust. In short, it is the fundamental aspect of the modern digital world. It also includes accuracy of data, availability and accessibility of Data, when needed along with Data encryption, Data back up and recovery and regular software updates.

Cyber security programmes in Pune after 12th

Cyber security programmes such as B. Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security and Forensics) and M. Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) are available after completing the 12th Grade. These programmes empower students to identify, mitigate, and respond to cyber security threats effectively, by putting a strong emphasis on proactive monitoring and robust defence strategies.

Course Curriculum

While pursuing the knowledge, you will get an opportunity to learn about several aspects of the subject. The curriculum for B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security and Forensics) covers topics such as

  • Networking and Security

The curriculum for M.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) includes

  • Data Privacy and Compliance
  • Advanced Security Management and Cyber Laws
  • Identity Access and Management
  • Mobile and Wireless Security
  • Distributed Networks and Blockchain Technology

Salary Prospects

Cyber security jobs offer lucrative salary prospects to freshers as well as expert professionals. As a fresher, you can earn up to Rs. 4 to 6 LPA, whereas high-level profiles such as CISOs can receive salaries in the range of Rs. 25 to 50 LPA or more.

**Note: The salary package may vary from company to company and level of experience.
