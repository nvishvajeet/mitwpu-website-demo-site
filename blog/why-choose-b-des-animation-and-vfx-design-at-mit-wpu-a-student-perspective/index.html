Choosing B. Des. Animation and VFX Design at MIT-WPU presents a dynamic journey into the world of visual storytelling, offering a unique blend of creativity and technical prowess from a student's viewpoint. This Bachelor’s in Design programme equips aspiring animators and VFX artists with comprehensive skills to thrive in the competitive industry landscape. Commitment to holistic education ensures a robust foundation in animation principles, coupled with cutting-edge VFX techniques at MIT-WPU. From mastering character animation to crafting immersive visual effects, students delve into every aspect of animation and VFX. Through hands-on projects and industry exposure, this program prepares students for a fulfilling career in animation and VFX.

Inside the Programme: Curriculum Overview and Specialisations

Inside the B. Des. in Animation and VFX Design at MIT-WPU Pune lies a rich curriculum tailored to nurture budding animators and VFX specialists. Students build a solid base through animation courses that explore character design, storyboarding, and 2D animation techniques. VFX courses delve into 3D modelling, compositing, and green screen technology, equipping students with industry-standard software proficiency. Students also explore specialised subjects such as character animation, motion graphics, compositing, and visual effects techniques.

With access to state-of-the-art animation suites and 3D modelling tools, learners engage in hands-on projects that emulate industry scenarios. Animation and VFX Programme not only focus on technical skills but also emphasise artistic expression and storytelling. Through elective courses and industry internships, students can further tailor their expertise, paving the way for a successful career in the dynamic field of animation and visual effects.

Advantages of Pursuing B. Des. Animation and VFX Design at MIT-WPU

Here are 7 Advantages of pursuing B. Des. Animation and VFX Design at MIT-WPU:

  • Industry-Focused Curriculum: The programme goes beyond theory, offering a curriculum designed by industry professionals. This ensures you learn the most relevant skills and software in today's Animation and VFX studios.
  • Advanced Resources and Facilities: MIT-WPU provides access to cutting-edge resources and facilities. This includes animation suites equipped with industry-standard software and powerful computers to bring your creative visions to life.
  • Learn from the Best: The faculty at MIT-WPU includes a blend of experienced educators and industry experts. This unique combination offers theoretical grounding alongside practical insights from seasoned professionals.
  • Specialisation Options: The B. Des. in Animation and VFX Design allows you to delve deeper into your passion. Choose a specialisation in animation or VFX based on your artistic interests and career goals.
  • Industry Placements and Career Opportunities: The programme boasts strong industry connections, opening doors to internship and placement opportunities at leading animation and VFX studios. These opportunities provide valuable real-world experience and a head start in your career.
  • Network with Industry Professionals: Through guest lectures, workshops, and industry events, you'll gain the opportunity to connect and network with industry professionals. Building relationships can be invaluable as you launch your career in Animation and VFX.
  • Holistic Programme Benefits: The B. Des. programme goes beyond technical skills development. You'll also gain experience in storytelling, teamwork, and critical thinking, preparing you for a well-rounded career in the creative industries.
