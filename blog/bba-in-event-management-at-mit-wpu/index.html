BBA in Global Marketing & Event Management at MIT-WPU is an exceptional undergraduate programme uniquely tailored to equip students with global business and event management expertise. This specially designed degree integrates key components like brand management, public relations, business law, event planning, marketing, financial accounting, and more. The curriculum, curated by industry leaders, ensures comprehensive learning in event coordination, media, and law.

The programme emphasises developing skills essential for global sustainability and growth in a competitive landscape. Students gain proficiency in marketing, branding, and event management nationally and internationally, opening doors to lucrative internships and rewarding careers. MIT-WPU stands out as the best college for BBA in Global Marketing & Event Management, offering a solid foundation for success in these dynamic fields.

What Sets MIT WPU Apart?

What sets MIT-WPU apart is its commitment to providing a holistic and enriching educational experience:

Case-based Learning Approach: MIT-WPU adopts a dynamic case-based learning approach, ensuring students develop practical problem-solving skills relevant to real-world scenarios.

Corporate Exposure: Students benefit from a robust interface with the corporate world through industry visits, summer internships, and live projects, fostering a hands-on understanding of business dynamics.

International Collaboration: Including a business simulation course in collaboration with Capstone or Marketplace, USA, adds a global perspective, enhancing students' adaptability to international business environments.

Industry Insights from Leaders: Regular guest lectures, seminars, and workshops by eminent corporate leaders provide valuable industry insights, bridging the gap between academia and the professional world.

Mentor-Mentee System: MIT-WPU employs a strong mentor-mentee system, ensuring personalised guidance and support for students throughout their academic journey.

Centre for Industry-Academia Partnerships: The dedicated Centre for Industry-Academia Partnerships facilitates structured assessments, training, and grooming activities, enhancing students' preparedness for internships and job placements.

Technology Business Incubator (TBI): The MIT-WPU Technology Business Incubator supports early-stage entrepreneurs and students with funding, mentoring, and network connections, fostering innovation and entrepreneurial spirit.

Immersive Programmes: MIT-WPU stands out with its diverse rural, national, and international immersion programmes, providing students with a global perspective and a well-rounded education beyond the classroom.

Unveiling the Dynamic Curriculum at MIT-WPU

The curriculum at MIT-WPU is a comprehensive blend of theory and practical application designed to empower students with the skills needed for success in the dynamic fields of global marketing and event management. With a focus on real-world scenarios, the case-based learning approach ensures practical problem-solving abilities. This forward-thinking approach equips students with the knowledge and expertise that today's competitive business landscape demands.
