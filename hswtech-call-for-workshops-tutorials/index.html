Submit your proposal for workshops or tutorials at HSWTech 2025. Collaborate with MIT-WPU to share knowledge and inspire innovation.

Proposals are invited from groups or individuals interested to host workshops and tutorials on conference themes and relevant fields during the pre/conference period. The purpose of workshops and tutorials is to provide an opportunity for participants from academia, industry, government and other related parties to present and discuss novel ideas on current and emerging topics relevant to the conference theme. We encourage workshops that bring together people working on new and developing research topics that favour highly interactive discussions. Tutorial proposals are encouraged that go in-depth into topics of interest to the fields of conference.

Please submit a proposal in MS Word format (2 to 4 pages) in the following format at hswtech@mitwpu.edu.in

  • Title and Acronym of Activity
  • Requested Duration (in Hrs)
  • Abstract (Up to 200 words, to be used for the website)
  • Description of the Workshop/Tutorial Content
  • Name, Affiliation, Homepage, Short Biography and Contact Information of Experts
  • Proposed Plan of the Workshop/Tutorial (Approx. timeline and type of activities)
  • Intended Audience and Expected Number of Participants
  • How the funding would be acquired from external sources for conducting workshop/tutorial
  • Any Other Information
