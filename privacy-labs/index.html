A Memorandum of Understanding was signed between Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University and Ziroh Labs, in 2022 to create a Center of Excellence in the field of Data Privacy and Security, the first of its kind in India. The Center of Excellence (Privacy Lab) under MoU was inaugurated on 13 th September, 2022.

The Privacy Lab is intended as a shared educational and research space that will offer tremendous opportunities to advance scientific discovery in the area of Data Privacy and validation while also serving as a valuable technology resource to academia and the industry. Ziroh Labs has provided 50+ licences of its product named as “Ziroh Store” which are installed in Privacy Lab as a testament to the vision.
