Member of Parliament, Lok Sabha (Inc, Hingoli, Maharashtra) & Former National President, Indian Youth Congress

Shri. Rajiv Satav Congress crusader, elected to the 16th Lok Sabha in 2014 from Hingoli constituency in Maharashtra. Pune University alumnus, was President of Maharashtra Pradesh Youth Congress from 2008 - 2010 and then promoted to President of the Indian Youth Congress from 2010 to 2014. He is Member of Internal Parliamentary Committee of Congress Party.
