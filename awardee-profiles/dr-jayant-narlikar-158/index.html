Padma Vibhushan, Emeritus Professor, Inter -university Centre for Astronomy and Astrophysics (Iucaa)

Dr. Jayant Vishnu Narlikar needs no introduction. He is one of those very few scientists in India who have contributed to the field of Astrophysics throughout their life. Prof. Narlikar was the founder director of IUCAA -Inter University Center for Astronomy and Astrophysics at Pune, and is now an Emeritus Professor at IUCAA.
