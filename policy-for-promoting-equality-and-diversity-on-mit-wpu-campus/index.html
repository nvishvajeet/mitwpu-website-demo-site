At MIT-WPU, we are committed to fostering an inclusive environment that promotes equality and diversity across our campus. Our policy aims to create a learning atmosphere where all individuals feel valued and respected, regardless of their background. MIT World Peace University upholds the Principles of Non-Discrimination, Equality, Diversity, Accessibility and Inclusion as cornerstone values of our institution. The university also provides support services, resources and mechanisms for addressing concerns related to equal opportunities. We believe that diversity enriches the educational experience and enhances innovation and creativity. By actively promoting these values, we strive to ensure that every member of our community can thrive academically and socially, contributing to a vibrant campus life.

Our university provides comprehensive support through dedicated centers, mentorship programmes and professional development opportunities. We maintain an open-door policy for students seeking guidance and support in their academic journey.

Clubs/Committees

Various clubs and committees at MIT-WPU play a pivotal role in promoting diversity and inclusion. These groups organise events, workshops and discussions that encourage students to engage with diverse perspectives. They serve as platforms for students to express their identities, share experiences and collaborate on initiatives that foster understanding and respect among all community members.

Anti-Racism Resources

MIT-WPU provides extensive anti-racism resources aimed at educating the campus community about the importance of equity and inclusion. These resources include workshops, literature and support networks designed to empower individuals in their journey towards becoming allies and advocates for social justice.

Student Exchange Programmes

Our student exchange programmes offer unique opportunities for cultural exchange and global learning. By partnering with international institutions, we enable students to immerse themselves in different cultures, enhancing their understanding of global diversity and fostering cross-cultural collaboration.

International Immersion

MIT-WPU promotes international immersion experiences that allow students to engage with diverse communities around the world. These programmes not only broaden academic horizons but also cultivate empathy and global citizenship among participants.

Prevention of Harassment & Discrimination

We prioritise the prevention of harassment and discrimination through comprehensive policies and training programmes. Our commitment is reflected in our proactive approach to creating a safe environment where all individuals can learn without fear of bias or prejudice.

POSH Committee

The Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) Committee at MIT-WPU is dedicated to addressing issues related to harassment on campus. This committee works tirelessly to ensure that all members of our community are aware of their rights and responsibilities while providing support for those affected by harassment or discrimination.

Assistance & Guidance to International Students

MIT-WPU offers dedicated assistance to international students, helping them navigate academic and cultural challenges. Our support services include orientation programmes, counseling and resources tailored to ensure a smooth transition into campus life.
