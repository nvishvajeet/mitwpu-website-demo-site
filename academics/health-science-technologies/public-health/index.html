Department of Public Health

Overview

The Department of Public Health at MIT-WPU is dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in public health with a clear mission: ‘Health for all.

’ The department focuses on educating students about the socio-economic disparities and their impact on the well-being of populations, as well as addressing public health sector needs in a sustainable manner.
