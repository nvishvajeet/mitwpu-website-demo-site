MIT School of Government

Overview

The MIT School of Government (MIT-SOG) was created by Shri. Rahul V. Karad, Executive President of Dr.

Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, with the aim of producing ethical, passionate, and dedicated political leaders for the country from diverse backgrounds. Good political leadership from Panchayat to Parliament can only ensure good governance and vibrant democracy in a country with this thought.
