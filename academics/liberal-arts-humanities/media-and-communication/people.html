Dr. Mithila Arvind Biniwale

Program Coordinator & Assistant Professor

Dr. Mithila Biniwale believes in integrating critical inquiry with practice-based learning, encouraging students to engage with media as both a field of scholarly investigation and creative practice. She is committed to fostering research orientation, critical thinking, and reflective engagement while equipping students with the analytical and production skills required to navigate the contemporary media landscape. Through experiential pedagogies, including documentary filmmaking, field-based learning, podcast production, workshops, and industry interactions, she bridges the gap between theory and practice. By nurturing informed, socially conscious, and innovative media professionals, Dr. Biniwale contributes to MIT World Peace University's vision of progressive transformation through education, innovation, and culture.

Dr. Aquil Ahmad Khan

Program Coordinator & Assistant Professor

Dr. Aquil Ahmad Khan believes that education in media and communication should effectively bridge theory, practice, and critical inquiry to foster students' intellectual and professional growth. He strives to create an interactive, research-driven learning environment that connects academic concepts with real-world media practices. His teaching approach combines conceptual clarity with experiential learning, enabling students to engage in newsroom simulations, fieldwork, and collaborative projects through courses such as Television Journalism, Digital Journalism, and Media Production. Drawing on his industry experience, he enriches classroom discussions with practical insights and contemporary perspectives. Aligned with MIT World Peace University's philosophy of life transformation, Dr. Khan emphasizes ethical responsibility, social awareness, and value-based education, nurturing skilled, reflective, and socially responsible media professionals.

Dr. Anu Nair

Programme Director & Assistant Professor

Dr. Anu Nair is a dedicated academician with over 13 years of experience in media and communication education, academic administration, and curriculum development. She joined as an Assistant Professor and is currently serving as Program Director at the Department of Media and Communication at MIT World Peace University, Pune. Her areas of specialization include Advertising, Branding, Consumer Psychology, Consumer Behaviour, Media Psychology and Strategic Communication.

Ravi Nikam

Assistant Professor

Mr. Ravi Nikam is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Media & Communication at MIT-WPU, bringing extensive experience from both the media industry and academia. He believes that meaningful learning is achieved through a balance of theoretical understanding and hands-on practice. His teaching philosophy emphasizes experiential learning, critical thinking, ethical storytelling, and industry relevance, empowering students to become creative, socially responsible media professionals. Passionate about documentary filmmaking and visual storytelling, he is committed to fostering innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and research-oriented learning, contributing to MIT-WPU's vision of developing globally competent, socially conscious, and future-ready students.

Devashish Shedge

Assistant Professor

Mr. Devashish Shedge is a Film Director and Senior Creative Director with over 35 years of experience across India and the UAE. By integrating the disciplines of cinema, advertising, and media education, he mentors students to develop creative thinking, technical excellence, and industry-ready skills.
