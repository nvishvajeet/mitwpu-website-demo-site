Dadasaheb Phalke International Film Institute

Overview

Dadasaheb Phalke International Film Institute is established with the aim of training young India into building promising careers in the highest-grossing industries - Cinema, OTT, Digital Media, among others.

The School focuses on teaching the art of filmmaking that challenges traditional stereotypes and provides a comprehensive education with an emphasis on the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the industry.
