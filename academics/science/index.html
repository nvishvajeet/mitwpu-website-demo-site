MIT-WPU School of Science & Environmental Studies

Overview

The School of Science and Environmental Studies at MIT-WPU offers a diverse range of programmes that combine the study of scientific principles with a focus on environmental sustainability. The School offers numerous specialisations in wildlife, sustainability and regulations.

The School collaborates with industry and corporate organisations, research centres and NGOs to provide students with exposure to CSR and Environmental Civic Liability to understand the market trends and demands.
