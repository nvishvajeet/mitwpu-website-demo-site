Prof. Prakash Mohan Mainkar

Associate Professor and Director Admissions

Dr. Prakash Mohan Mainkar obtained his Bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering from Shivaji University in 2001, followed by a Master's degree in Electronics (VLSI Design) in 2007. He later completed his Doctor of Philosophy (Engineering) from RTM Nagpur University, Nagpur, India, in 2019. Currently, he serves as the Director of Admissions and an Associate Professor in Electronics & Communication Engineering at MIT-WPU. Along with his teaching responsibilities, he actively participates in various institute committees and contributes to the development of laboratory infrastructure and curriculum. Dr. Mainkar regularly engages with industry professionals to explore employment opportunities for students and facilitate international university collaborations for MIT-WPU. He has undertaken several international study tours to universities in China, Singapore, Hong Kong, and Nepal, in his capacity as an International Study Tour Coordinator. Over the past 8 years, he has also served as a National Study Tour Coordinator and organized visits to numerous industries throughout India to provide students with exposure to real-world applications of their coursework.

Dr. Savitri Nathrao Jadhav

Assistant Professor

Dr. Savitri N. Jadhav is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering with over 18+ years of academic and research experience. She completed her Ph.D. from Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2022. Handwritten MODI character recognition was her topic of research. Her research interests span Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML), Embedded AI, Image Processing, Pattern Recognition, and Edge AI. She has contributed extensively to interdisciplinary domains including UAV-based flood detection, anomaly detection using embedded AI, and intelligent agricultural systems. She has published numerous research papers in reputed journals and conferences and holds multiple Indian patents in areas such as crop damage assessment, non-invasive glucose monitoring, and UV-C disinfection systems. She is actively involved in consultancy projects with industry partners like KPIT Technologies and Bharat Flow Analytics, focusing on real-world AI and embedded system applications. A professional member of IEEE and associated with the Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society, she is committed to advancing research, innovation, and student mentorship in emerging technologies.
