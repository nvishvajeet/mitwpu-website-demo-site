Hafeez Contractor School of Architecture

Learn about MIT-WPU's Hafeez Contractor School of Architecture in Pune - its vision, faculty, and focus on sustainable, innovative, and socially responsible design.

About

The Hafeez Contractor School of Architecture is committed to nurturing socially responsible, ethical, and globally competent architects who can shape the built environment through creative design, technological innovation, and sustainable practices.

The School offers a rigorous, studio-based learning experience that integrates advanced digital technologies, research, and interdisciplinary collaboration to prepare students for the evolving demands of the architectural profession. Emphasizing ethical practice and adherence to the standards of the Council of Architecture (CoA), the School fosters strong industry and community partnerships to create meaningful societal impact.

With a focus on design innovation and research, the School encourages students to address contemporary challenges in urbanism, climate change, digital fabrication, and sustainable development, empowering them to become future-ready professionals and leaders in architecture and the built environment.

Vision

  • To develop globally recognized ethical, socially conscious, and creative architectural professionals contributing to the art and science of nation-building.

Mission

To produce high-quality Architecture Graduates from students of diversified backgrounds by providing:

  • Academic Excellence : Achieving a balance between artistic expression and technical proficiency to solve complex spatial and urban challenges.
  • Innovation & Research : Promoting higher education, design-led research, and entrepreneurship to advance the future of sustainable habitats and heritage conservation.
  • Professional Ethics : Inculcating social responsibility, ethical practice, and global professional standards through industry-academia partnerships.
  • Interdisciplinary Synergy : Serving as a center of excellence that collaborates across disciplines of art, engineering, and social sciences.
