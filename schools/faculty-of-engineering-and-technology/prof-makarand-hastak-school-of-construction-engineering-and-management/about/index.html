About

The Prof. Makarand Hastak School of Construction Engineering & Management at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University is envisioned as a nationally and globally eminent academic unit dedicated to advancing the science, engineering, and management of the built environment. At a time when India is undergoing an unprecedented infrastructure revolution—expressways, metros, tunnels, airports, smart cities, green buildings, and industrial corridors—the construction industry demands highly skilled, techno-managerially proficient, and ethically grounded professionals.

The School addresses this challenge by integrating engineering precision, management intelligence, technological advancement, and value-based education into a unified academic ecosystem. The School functions under the strategic guidance of global leader Prof. Makarand (Mark) Hastak, Chairman of the Academic and Industry Advisory Board. What differentiates the School is its commitment to bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world construction practice—creating professionals capable of addressing India's USD 1.4 trillion infrastructure mission and contributing meaningfully to the global built environment.
