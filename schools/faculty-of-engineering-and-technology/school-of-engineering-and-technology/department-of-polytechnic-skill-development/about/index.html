About

The MIT-WPU Department of Polytechnic is dedicated to delivering value-based education and fostering entrepreneurial skills, ensuring that students are well prepared for both employment and entrepreneur-ship. Since its inception in 1996, the department (formerly Sri Savitribai Phule Polytechnic) has been a prominent institution in Maharashtra, featuring state-of-the-art infrastructure and a distinguished faculty. The department promotes a problem-solving mindset and emphasises social innovation, aiming to develop well-rounded individuals with the confidence and skills necessary for real-world challenges. Through its comprehensive academic programmes, the department focuses on holistic development, preparing students to excel in their professional and entrepreneurial endeavours.

Our Objectives

  • To impart industry-oriented technical and skill-based education in emerging engineering domains.
  • To develop innovative and entrepreneurial professionals capable of solving real-world problems.
  • To promote sustainable and socially responsible engineering practices for societal development.
  • To foster ethical values, teamwork, communication, and collaborative learning skills.
  • To nurture lifelong learners adaptable to evolving technologies and global needs.

Vision

  • To be a leading Polytechnic nurturing skill-based education that empowers society through innovation, sustainability, collaborative learning, lifelong learning, and global harmony.

Mission

  • To impart quality, skill-based technical education aligned with industry needs, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship.
  • To promote sustainable engineering practices and collaborative learning through industry interaction and experiential methodologies.
  • To develop competent, ethical, and lifelong learners who contribute to societal development and global harmony.
