About

The Department of Bioengineering at MIT-WPU, Pune offers an interdisciplinary programme that seamlessly integrates biology, engineering, and technology to address some of the most pressing global challenges. Situated within the vibrant ecosystem of the School of Engineering and Technology and the School of Computer Science and Engineering - both with a rich legacy of over 43 years - the department benefits from a strong foundation of academic excellence and innovation.

Students are mentored by dedicated faculty with international research and industry experience and gain insights from experts across allied disciplines. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, offering access to 20+ advanced laboratories, including the Central Instrument Facility, to nurture innovation and research capabilities.

With a GATE-aligned curriculum and a diverse range of professional elective tracks such as Biomedical Engineering, Biomechanics, Bioinformatics, Bioprocess Engineering, and Genetic Engineering, the department equips students with both technical expertise and industry readiness. Integrating Artificial Intelligence and sustainable technologies, it prepares graduates for impactful careers in biotechnology, healthcare, and environmental innovation, empowering them to lead the global Bio-Revolution.

Beyond academics, the programme promotes holistic development through transformative experiences like Rural, National, and International Immersions, as well as participation in initiatives such as RIDE and SLDP. RIDE (Research, Innovation, Design and Entrepreneurship) focuses on fostering an entrepreneurial mindset, while SLDP (Social Leadership Development Program) builds social leadership and civic responsibility. This ensures that every student evolves as a compassionate, capable, and future-ready professional.
