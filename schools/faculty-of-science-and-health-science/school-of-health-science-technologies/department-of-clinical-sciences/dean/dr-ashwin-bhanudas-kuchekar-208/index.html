Dean

Message

It is a privilege to welcome you to the School of Health Sciences and Technology at MIT World Peace University. Our School is committed to preparing professionals who combine scientific knowledge, practical competence, and ethical responsibility in the service of health and wellbeing.

To our students, use this time to build discipline, curiosity, and a strong work ethic. Focus on understanding the science behind healthcare, developing your practical skills, and learning how to make responsible decisions. Ask questions, stay engaged, and take every opportunity to learn from real health challenges around you. Knowledge and technical skills will help you grow in your career, but honesty, integrity, and compassion will define the kind of professional you become. To our parents, safety, wellbeing, and overall development of our students remain central to our responsibilities. Through structured academic mentoring, counselling support, and career guidance, we strive to ensure that every student is prepared for meaningful opportunities across the research, clinical sciences, pharmaceutical and healthcare industries, public health, and wellness.

Our faculty members bring valuable experience from research and industry. Their role goes beyond classroom teaching. They guide students through hands on training, and industry engagements so that learning remains closely connected with healthcare practice. Our programmes integrate classroom instruction with practical work and industry collaborations, allowing students to apply their knowledge in real life.

Join us as we pursue excellence in health sciences and healthcare innovation, working to improve health, wellbeing, and quality of life for individuals and communities, and contributing to a healthier future for generations to come.
