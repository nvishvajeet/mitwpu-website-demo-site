About

The Department of Yogic Sciences at the School of Health Science and Technologies, MIT-WPU offers a rewarding learning experience through its M.Sc Yoga and Meditation programme.

The School has comprehensive academic and research tie-ups with several universities to provide students with industry exposure. The Department of Yogic Sciences is established to spread the knowledge of Yoga and meditation among the young generation. The postgraduate programme offers an amalgamation of the practice, art, science and philosophy of Yoga and its benefits on the physical, emotional, psychological and spiritual levels. The programme also offers short-term courses and industry exposure through internships, training, seminars and guest lectures for enhancing the learning experience of the students.

Our Objectives

  • To integrate various disciplines for the holistic development of learners.
  • To advance cognitive ability, critical thinking, and personality development.
  • To develop the information base of learners through different Yoga practices.

Major Career Tracks

  • Yoga Teachers

  • International Instructor/ Researcher/Instructor at Medical Institutes
  • Yoga Trainer
