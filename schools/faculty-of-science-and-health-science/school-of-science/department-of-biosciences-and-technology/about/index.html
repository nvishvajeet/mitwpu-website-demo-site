About

The Department of Biosciences & Technology offers industry-oriented undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes that focus on contemporary and futuristic technical and scientific knowledge in the fields of biological sciences, and biotechnology. The curriculum promotes self-directed learning, independent learning, industry-focused education with critical thinking, project-based learning, problem solving, experiential training, and research. The department takes immense pride in the remarkable accomplishments and scholarly contributions of its distinguished and seasoned faculty members. Their profound expertise directly enriches the student's; learning experience, ensuring a seamless translation of faculty knowledge into valuable student insights.

The Department of Biosciences and Technology is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, including a Microbial Culture laboratory, an Animal Tissue Culture laboratory, a Bioanalytical laboratory, Microbiology lab and a Molecular biology Laboratory. These facilities provide students with the opportunity to gain hands-on experience and develop technical skills.

In order to develop industry ready skills in the students, the department offers internships, industry visits, and national and international immersion programmes apart from the regular interactions with practicing professionals and researchers. The internships and training programs have been designed to create industry-ready professionals which are highly sought-after by the industry.

Our Objectives

  • To develop minds inquisitive about the biosciences and the world around
  • To train students to think in a scientific manner and carry out scientific investigations to draw verifiable conclusions
  • To develop the skill of effective communication of scientific ideas, and arguments in multiple ways
  • To innovate ways of application of scientific ideas, technological and otherwise for the betterment of human beings.
