About

The Department of Education at WPU School of Education, MIT-WPU, is dedicated to moulding students into conscientious, mindful, and empathetic educators for the future. The forward-looking curriculum of the B.Ed. and M.Ed. degree programmes, curated by esteemed academicians integrates the most effective pedagogical techniques and theories. A blend of regular faculty and guest lectures, along with focused classroom management sessions, is instrumental in training candidates to comprehend student psychology effectively & overcome the challenges of the academic environment. These programmes are designed to empower future educators with the ability to amalgamate the best practices from Western pedagogy with the ideals of India's Gurukul system, fostering a more student-centric learning environment. The aim is to prepare teachers who can skillfully navigate the ever-evolving landscape of education, making classrooms more engaging and tailored to the needs of each student.

Our Objectives

  • To mold students into dedicated, mindful, and sensitive teachers of tomorrow
  • To prepare educational leaders for learning environments such as classrooms, schools, and other learning organisations.
  • To train the students to prepare and deliver learner-centric lectures and manage classrooms effectively.

Major Career Tracks
