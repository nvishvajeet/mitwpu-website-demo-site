About

The Department of Liberal Arts at MIT-WPU stands proudly among the top 100 institutes for Liberal Arts in India. Offering a diverse array of undergraduate and postgraduate degree programmes encompassing varied disciplines like English, Psychology, Political Science, and Liberal Arts, the department champions interdisciplinary knowledge and experiential learning. Each programme is thoughtfully curated and designed to cultivate analytical prowess, and interpersonal and intrapersonal skills, alongside fostering creative and critical thinking abilities, and honing research skills. The teaching methodology incorporates workshops, fieldwork, audio-visual aids, and continual internal assessment, serving as catalysts for creative and critical thinking, empowering students to make astute decisions. Led by industry experts, these programmes strive to kindle students’ fervour, creativity, independent thinking, and research acumen.

Our Objectives

  • To develop the ability to look at the world from various perspectives
  • To encourage intellectual grounding in humanistic inquiry
  • To imbibe a sense of independent thinking and sound analysis for conclusion making
