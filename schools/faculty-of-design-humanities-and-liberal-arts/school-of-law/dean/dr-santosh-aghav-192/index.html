Message

Dear Students and Esteemed Parents,

Warm greetings from School of Law, Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune It gives immense pleasure and pride to welcome you to our School of Law, which is well equipped with distinctive knowledge dissemination endeavours and also comparative enrichment of required skills and values, in the light of professional’s engagements to sharpen your professional skills. Legal education is more than a career path — it is a commitment to justice, equity, and societal progress, larger services to various concerns and expectations by satisfactory participation. At our school, we are dedicated to nurturing not just legal professionals, diverse legal expertise and focussed specialisations but also responsible citizens and thought leaders who can shape the future. Here, you will find a multi-faceted rich curriculum, a diverse and inclusive environment, and a faculty composed of experienced scholars and practitioners with dynamic approach. Our programmes blend academic excellence with real-world relevance, empowering students with critical thinking, ethical reasoning, and practical skills necessary to excel in today’s dynamic legal and upcoming landscape. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of legal education, guided by values of integrity, innovation, and justice. Whether your goal is to become a lawyer, judge, a policymaker, or a change-maker in any field, we invite you to begin that journey with us. Join us — and become part of a community that stands for law, liberty, and leadership.
