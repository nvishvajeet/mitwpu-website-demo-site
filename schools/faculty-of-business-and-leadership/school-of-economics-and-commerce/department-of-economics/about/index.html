About

The Department of Economics at MIT World Peace University is a distinguished department committed to nurturing the next generation of policymakers, leaders, and economists. With a strong focus on developing true leaders and collaborative team players, the department equips students with essential skills required for a seamless transition into the corporate world and public service. Offering a comprehensive range of academic programs—including undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. degrees in Economics —the department caters to diverse interests and career aspirations.

The department provides students with a unique opportunity to gain exposure to both national and international academic advancements and industrial trends. Through internships, conferences, research opportunities, and workshops, it creates an environment that emphasizes academic excellence along with practical relevance, preparing students to make a meaningful real-world impact.

Our Objectives

  • To inculcate the passion for economics among students
  • To imbibe in them a sense of curiosity and scientific outlook while analysing the concepts in economics
  • To train students to view institutions like government and markets from a social and economic point of view
  • To make students independent researchers and sound professionals in the various diverse fields of economics
