Schools

Ram Charan School of Leadership

The School of Leadership at MIT-WPU focuses on developing ethical, innovative leaders equipped with the skills needed to thrive in diverse sectors. It offers programs that combine leadership principles with practical applications, helping students build strong decision-making abilities and strategic thinking. The school emphasizes creating leaders who can address global challenges.

About

The Ram Charan School of Leadership at MIT-WPU stands as a distinguished institution offering a comprehensive spectrum of business leadership programmes tailored to both students and seasoned professionals. At its core, the school is dedicated to shaping the future generation of corporate leaders, providing them with exceptional educational pathways that span a wide array of critical topics. These encompass various facets of leadership, including styles and effective communication, conflict resolution, strategic planning, and team building. The curriculum, thoughtfully crafted by top-tier industry experts, equips students with invaluable leadership proficiencies, fostering both personal and professional growth. This educational journey is underpinned by a commitment to contributing to the betterment of society.

The Ram Charan School of Leadership offers state-of-the-art flagship management programmes designed to provide exceptional academic rigor, contemporary industry relevance, and a strong foundation in leadership to high-potential students. Rooted in the philosophy of accessible excellence, these programmes aim to develop future-ready professionals, ethical leaders, and entrepreneurial thinkers through a transformative learning experience. School offers three premium flagship programmes:

  • Post-Graduate Diploma in Management (PGD)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Business Administration - Entrepreneurship and Family Business Management (MBA-EFBM)

Our Objectives

  • Building the pipeline of future leaders
  • Transform the leadership training landscape in India through innovative, relevant, and meaningful curriculum and pedagogy
  • Bring together national and global thought leaders to deliver a well-designed curriculum
  • Shape young minds capable and willing to take the company and nation to the next level.

Strengthening Global Commitment to Ethical and Sustainable Leadership - Ram Charan School of Leadership, MIT World Peace University, Pune is proud to be recognized as a Signatory Member of the Principles for Responsible Management Education (PRME), an initiative supported by the United Nations Global Compact.

By joining this prestigious global network, the institution reaffirms its commitment to responsible leadership, ethical management education, and sustainable development. PRME brings together leading business and management institutions worldwide to prepare future leaders who can address social, environmental, and economic challenges with responsibility and vision.

As a PRME Signatory, RSoL is committed to integrating the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and PRME’s Seven Principles into its teaching, research, and institutional collaborations, further strengthening its mission of creating globally conscious and socially responsible leaders.
