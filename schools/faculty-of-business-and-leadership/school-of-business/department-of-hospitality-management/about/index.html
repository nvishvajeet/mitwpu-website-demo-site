Department of Hospitality Management

MIT-WPU's Department of Hospitality Management seeks to produce experts in customer service, operations, and hotel management. The program gives students professional exposure and practical experience to equip them for prosperous futures in the exciting hotel sector.

About

The Department of Hospitality Management at MIT-WPU offers industry-curated BBA degree and certificate programmes to equip students for success in the hospitality industry. These practical courses, featuring project-based experiential learning, are led by industry experts and esteemed academicians. The department boasts modern laboratories featuring cutting-edge equipment and facilities, ensuring every student gains a comprehensive understanding of the hospitality industry through hands-on learning and real-world exposure.

It provides the prospect of a structured learning process, including both the theoretical and practical aspects of the industry incorporating a clear pattern of professional development.

Our Objectives

  • To develop globally competent hospitality professionals equipped with industry-relevant knowledge, skills, and ethical values.
  • To deliver experiential and practice-oriented learning that integrates theoretical concepts with real-world hospitality operations.
  • To foster innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership within the dynamic hospitality and tourism industry.
  • To inculcate professional ethics, service excellence, and cultural sensitivity essential for global hospitality environments.
  • To build strong industry collaborations and global exposure opportunities that enhance employability and career readiness.
