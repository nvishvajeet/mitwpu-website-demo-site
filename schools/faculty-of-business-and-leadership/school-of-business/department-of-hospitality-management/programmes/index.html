Department of Hospitality Management

Various initiatives meant to equip students for leadership roles in the hotel sector are provided by MIT-WPU's Department of Hospitality Management. Emphasizing critical skills such customer service, management, and operations, these courses provide students with both academic understanding and real-world experience.
