Department of Business

Combining academic knowledge with practical experience, the MIT-WPU Department of Business emphasizes offering a strong education in business management.

About

The Department of Business at MIT-WPU provides a comprehensive curriculum encompassing core, interdisciplinary, and elective courses, bolstered by skill-based certifications. The pedagogical approach combines innovative teaching methods, immersive hands-on training, live projects, industry site visits, capstone projects, structured summer internships, enlightening seminars, hands-on workshops, and access to international credit programmes. Esteemed academicians and industry stalwarts collaborate to deliver industry-aligned training, equipping students with the prowess to tackle the dynamic corporate landscape.

Our Objectives

  • To enhance a national and global perspective amongst potential leaders and entrepreneurs
  • To infuse strategic approach, analytics, and technology into various disciplines of BBA and MBA studies
  • To create a solid managerial foundation among students to overcome challenges in businesses
  • To build socially and ethically sensitive managers
