Join the MIT-World Peace University (MIT-WPU) family and be a part of a thriving academic ecosystem that champions innovation, excellence, and holistic education. At MIT-WPU, we don't just educate; we inspire, collaborate, and create a meaningful impact. Whether you're a seasoned academic, an industry expert, or an aspiring professional, this is your opportunity to grow, contribute, and shape the future of education.

Why Choose MIT-WPU?

  • Be Part of a Legacy: Work with one of India's premier private universities, renowned for its cutting-edge research, transformative learning experiences, and global partnerships.
  • Innovate and Inspire: Engage with world-class faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and pioneering research that pushes the boundaries of knowledge.
  • Grow Beyond Limits: Thrive in a workplace that fosters continuous learning, leadership development, and holistic personal and professional growth.
  • Industry-Driven Exposure: Collaborate with top industry leaders, take part in groundbreaking research, and contribute to real-world problem-solving.
  • A Vision for Peace and Sustainability: Be a changemaker in an institution that integrates peace, sustainability, and ethical values into education and research.

Employee Welfare Highlights

Employee Outbound & Management Training : We prioritize leadership and teamwork through structured outbound training programmes that enhance problem-solving, communication, and strategic thinking, empowering our employees to drive innovation and success.

HR Activities & Learning & Development Cell : Our commitment to continuous professional growth is reflected in our Faculty Development Programmes (FDP), Management Development Programmes (MDP), and specialised training modules, ensuring our faculty and staff stay ahead in their fields.

Research & Academic Incentives : We support our employees with travel grants for national and international conferences, along with incentives for funded projects, research, and patents, fostering a culture of innovation.

Global Faculty Exchange : With collaborations across 40+ international universities, we facilitate faculty exchange programmes that enhance knowledge sharing and academic growth.

Technology-Driven HRMS : Our advanced HR Management System (HRMS) streamlines payroll, attendance tracking, performance evaluation, and digital record-keeping, ensuring efficiency, transparency, and seamless operations.

Equal Opportunity Workplace : We are committed to fostering an inclusive and diverse work environment with merit-based hiring, gender equality, and fair career growth opportunities.

Employee Safety & Well-being : The well-being of our employees is our top priority. We implement comprehensive safety measures, mental health support, ergonomic workplace practices, wellness initiatives, and yoga sessions. We also proudly celebrate International Yoga Day to promote holistic health.

Disability & Diversity Inclusion : Our campus is equipped with barrier-free infrastructure, assistive technologies, and tailored support services to ensure an accessible and inclusive workplace for individuals with disabilities.
