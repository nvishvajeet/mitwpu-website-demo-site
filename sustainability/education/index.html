Overview

The institution integrates sustainability and environmental awareness into its academic and research framework. Several courses related to environment and sustainability are offered across disciplines to promote green learning. Faculty and students contribute scholarly publications addressing sustainability challenges. Events such as RIDE and Co-Creation foster innovation and dialogue on sustainable development. The institution supports Swayam/NPTEL(National Program on Technology Enhanced Learning) MOOCs local chapter and remote center for sustainable learning. The institution also conducts international collaborative programs and community service projects focused on environmental impact. Sustainability-driven startups are encouraged and supported through incubation initiatives. Education is further strengthened through the effective use of ICT tools, and dedicated research funds are allocated to advance sustainability-focused research and innovation.

Our programmes blend rigorous academics with hands-on experience:

  • Application-oriented learning through fieldwork and live projects
  • Industry partnerships with government agencies and NGOs for internships
  • Global perspective via national and international study tours
  • Research excellence with opportunities for innovative sustainability projects
  • Career readiness through skill-enhancement modules and industry exposure
  • Interdisciplinary Approach to collaborate sustainability with other fields as well
  • Guest lectures from industry pioneers
  • Workshops on emerging environmental technologies
  • Seminars addressing current sustainability challenges
  • Collaborative projects with environmental organizations

MIT-WPU Career Development Centre (CDC)

The Career Development Centre (CDC) at MIT-WPU conducted a series of structured programmes across all Schools and Divisions to strengthen student employability, industry readiness, and global exposure.

  • AMCAT Employability Assessments: Conducted for all divisions, AMCAT evaluates aptitude, reasoning, communication, and domain knowledge, providing recruiter-recognised employability scores. Specialised modules for Law and Liberal Arts include Legal Aptitude and domain-specific tests.
  • Predictive Index & Cognitive Tests: Implemented for select Schools to assess behavioural and cognitive traits, supporting better teamwork, self-awareness, and hiring alignment.
  • Aptitude & Life Skills Training: Structured programmes focus on problem-solving, communication, teamwork, and professional etiquette. Tailored sessions prepare students across disciplines for placement challenges.
  • Company-Specific Training (CST): Industry-driven sessions that familiarise students with recruitment processes, technical requirements, and company culture, leading to higher placement success.
  • Placement Preparatory Bootcamps: Intensive short-term programmes on aptitude, communication, and domain skills simulate real placement processes and ensure industry readiness.
  • Master Classes & Higher Studies Initiatives: Expert-led sessions on UGC NET, higher education pathways, and global education fairs such as Achievers’ Dialogue, Eduxplore, and Global Education Fair connect students with international opportunities.
  • Infosys Springboard Pragati Programme: Enables students to gain digital, technical, and communication skills through certified self-paced learning.
  • AI-Based Mock Interviews (WorqHat): AI-driven interviews for Health Sciences and Science students offer instant feedback on communication, technical skills, and confidence.

Overall, these integrated initiatives bridge the gap between academia and industry, empowering students with the skills, confidence, and global outlook essential for career success.

R.I.D.E.

R.I.D.E. (Research, Innovation, Design, and Entrepreneurship) is MIT-WPU's flagship event where innovators, researchers, entrepreneurs, and students come together to exchange cutting-edge ideas and drive sustainable solutions. This transformative five-day summit empowers over 7,500 students to develop eco-conscious ventures and breakthrough technologies that address real-world environmental challenges. Through structured learning journeys and mentorship, RIDE aims to create more than 1,500 student-led ventures, fostering a new generation of sustainability-minded entrepreneurs. The event serves as a catalyst for green innovation, connecting young minds with industry experts to accelerate the development of solutions that benefit both business and the planet. At RIDE, sustainability isn't just a theme—it's the foundation for building tomorrow's responsible enterprises.

National Immersion

The National Immersion Programme at MIT-WPU provides students with hands-on learning experiences through visits to leading national institutions and industries.
