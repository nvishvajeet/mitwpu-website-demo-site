  • MIT-WPU Alumni Network: A Lifelong Community

The alumni community of MIT World Peace University is a powerful and diverse network that spans the globe. From passionate leaders and innovators to community advocates and change-makers, our alumni are a living testament to the rich legacy of their alma mater and the values we instill in our graduates. With a strong foundation in academic excellence and personal growth, our alumni continue to excel in their professional and personal lives, creating meaningful impact wherever they go.

Since the first graduating class, our alumni have made their mark in every corner of the world—shaping industries, influencing public policy, advancing research, and enriching communities. Today, more than 1,00,000 graduates stand as a testament to the enduring power of the education that we have imparted.

Our alumni are a source of pride, inspiration, and strength. Whether you graduated a few years ago or several decades ago, you are part of a dynamic, ever-growing community that remains connected through shared experiences, values, and aspirations.

Why Stay Connected?

Staying connected with MIT World Peace University offers opportunities for personal and professional growth, access to a global network of peers, and the chance to give back to future generations of students. Your journey doesn’t end with graduation—it’s just the beginning.

Join us in celebrating and continuing the legacy of excellence by staying engaged with your alma mater. Your achievements inspire us, your stories connect us, and your continued involvement strengthens the foundation for tomorrow's leaders.

Director's Message

Director - Alumni Relations

Dear MIT-WPU Alumni,

Welcome to the MIT-WPU Alumni Portal!

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the Alumni Relations portal of MIT World Peace University. Our alumni are our true ambassadors, carrying forward the values, knowledge, and spirit of MIT-WPU across the globe. This department is designed to strengthen the lifelong bond between you and your alma mater. Through networking, knowledge sharing, and collaborative opportunities, we aim to create a vibrant alumni community that continues to contribute to the growth of our university, society, and the nation at large.

Your achievements are a source of pride and inspiration for our students and faculty. We look forward to your active engagement in mentoring, career guidance, placements, research collaborations, and in nurturing the next generation of leaders. Let us come together to build bridges between the past, present, and future, making the MIT-WPU alumni network a global force of positive change.

Alumni Association Board

The primary responsibility of the Alumni Association Board of MIT-WPU is to strengthen the alumni community by cultivating enthusiasm, commitment, and motivation through initiatives such as membership drives, events, and lifelong learning opportunities. Their role extends to inspiring alumni to maintain a lifelong connection with the university’s global network, developing an external profile for alumni activities and MIT-WPU, both in India and abroad, while also forging an effective partnership with MIT-WPU and its Alumni Association.
