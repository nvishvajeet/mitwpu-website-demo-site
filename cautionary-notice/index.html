Cautionary Notice

Keep updated with MIT-WPU's Cautionary Notice. To guarantee clarity and steer clear of false information, grasp key policies, disclaimers, and official information.

  • Cautionary Notice

MIT-WPU, Pune cautions all stakeholders and the general public against fraudulent activities and misrepresentation of our university’s name. We have received reports of unauthorised individuals and entities:

  • Falsely claiming association with MIT-WPU
  • Making fraudulent job offers using our name
  • Issuing fake admission offers and collecting fees
  • Misusing our logo and brand identity
  • Operating deceptive websites and social media accounts

Please note that MIT-WPU:

  • Conducts all admissions strictly through our official website (www.mitwpu.edu.in)
  • Never makes payment requests through personal accounts
  • Issues job offers only through our official HR department
  • Uses only verified social media handles and official email domains

For your security, verify all communication through our official channels. Report suspicious activities to our administrative office immediately. MIT-WPU will take strict legal action against any unauthorised use of our name or fraudulent representations.

Stay vigilant. Stay secure.
