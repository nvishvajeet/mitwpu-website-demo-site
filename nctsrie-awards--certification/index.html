To encourage quality research and academic excellence, the following awards will be presented during the conference:

  • Best Paper Award
  • Best Research Paper (Doctoral Scholar) Award

Awards will be decided by an expert review committee based on originality, relevance, methodology, and policy implications.

  • Participation Certificate will be provided to all registered participants.
  • Paper Presentation Certificate will be awarded to authors who present their papers during the conference.
  • Best Paper Certificates will be issued to award winners.
