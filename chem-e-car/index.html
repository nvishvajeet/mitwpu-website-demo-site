Established in 2018 under the School of Chemical Engineering, Team Chem-E-Car is a testament to the dedication and passion of chemical engineering students. We recognised the need for a platform that goes beyond the classroom to explore and apply our engineering skills, and we've been on an exciting journey ever since.

Governed by the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE), our primary mission is to participate in the prestigious Chem-E-Car competition during the Student Regional Conference for the Southeast Asian Region. This competition serves as a challenging arena for showcasing our engineering prowess and innovation. Join us at Team Chem-E-Car as we strive for excellence, innovation, and global recognition. We are the driving force behind the future of chemical engineering, and we invite you to be part of our journey. Let's engineer solutions and race towards success together.
