MIT World Peace University has established the Advanced Welding and Simulator laboratory as per the Industry 4.0 perspective with future vision. Advanced Welding and Simulator Lab is a very unique facility housing the virtual simulator physical environment application of the welding process. Virtual Reality and Augmented reality flexible training solution which provides the tools to easily manage different courses with students, regardless of their level of knowledge and learning pace. This unique facility is beneficial in developing practical skills and practical experience in welding without any hazard and also interactively learning independently from a location.
