About MIT-WPU

Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, the flagship institution of the MAEER Group, has a distinguished legacy of over four decades in higher education. MIT-WPU has a distinguished 43-year legacy of fostering excellence in academics. With more than 150 contemporary academic programs and a global alumni network exceeding 100,000 professionals, the University is committed to advancing excellence in education anchored in peace, ethics, innovation, and national development. Under the visionary leadership of Dr. Rahul V. Karad, Executive President, MIT-WPU continues to contribute meaningfully to the evolution of Indian higher education for the twenty-first century.
