Team Vegapod Hyperloop is a dynamic, student-led initiative incubated at MIT-WPU, comprising of 40 talented students from various disciplines, dedicated towards advancing hyperloop technology. We craft sub-scale hyperloop prototypes, incorporating cutting-edge technologies such as levitation, vacuum systems, contactless propulsion, regenerative braking, power electronics, and cooling systems.

We have proudly represented India and MIT-WPU on the global stage, showcasing our innovations at prestigious forums like the SpaceX Hyperloop Competition, European Hyperloop Week and the Canadian Hyperloop Conference. Our mission is to develop a sustainable mode of transportation for the near future.

Our Achievements

SpaceX 2019 : Ranked amongst top 3 teams in Asia and top 30 out of 1,600 teams worldwide.

EHW 2022 (Delft, the Netherlands) : Represented India with a fully operational and cost-efficient pod.

EHW 2023 (Scotland, the UK) : Ranked in the top 6 globally, the only Asian team to qualify for the final demonstration and successfully clearing the Testing and Safety Documentation (TSD) process.

EHW 2024 (Zurich, Switzerland) : Achieved a top 2 position globally in the mechanical, electrical, and guidance subsystems, and proudly won the 2024 Outreach Award for our efforts in promoting Hyperloop technology on the International stage.

Additionally : We are proud to share that we were invited to the official residence of Union Minister of Road Transport and Highways of India, Shri Nitin Gadkari, in New Delhi, where he reviewed our work and provided valuable feedback.
