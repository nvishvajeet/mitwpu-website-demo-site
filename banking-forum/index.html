Banking Forum Club, a dynamic student-led initiative that emerged from the School of Management at MIT World Peace University. Established in April 2021, the Banking Forum Club is driven by the mission to cultivate a keen interest in banking and financial services within our campus community. The club achieves this goal through a diverse array of activities, competitions, and innovative approaches that extend to social media, marketing, and the publication of "BankBiz" magazine. As a vital bridge between the industry and our student body, we strive to provide students with updated information and a comprehensive understanding of the banking sector, equipping them with the expertise essential for a successful career in this field. This club fosters an environment for networking and collaboration, connecting students with industry professionals and fellow enthusiasts. We are committed to promoting the significance of the banking sector in the economy and illustrating the management skills crucial for organisational success. Moreover, we delve into ethical considerations in the business of banking, encouraging personal ethics and ethical theory application.
