About the Club

Nummerates is a premier student-driven club at MIT WPU dedicated to nurturing innovative ideas and fostering an environment of academic excellence. Our mission is to support students from their first year by providing a dynamic platform to explore and implement their innovative ideas. The club offers a collaborative environment where students can engage with experienced seniors, seek guidance on academic subjects, internships, and placements. Additionally, Nummerates serves as a platform to showcase one's talents and connect with some of the brightest minds in the field.

Achievements

Innovative Projects : Successfully mentored numerous student-led projects, many of which have received accolades in inter-collegiate competitions.

Workshops and Seminars : Conducted several workshops on advanced mathematical concepts and innovative techniques, significantly enhancing the skill set of our members.

Collaborations : Partnered with industry leaders for various events, offering students valuable networking opportunities and insights into real-world applications.

Working Environment

The working environment at Nummerates is both stimulating and supportive. We encourage collaborative learning and innovative thinking through various activities

Collaborative Projects : Students work together on cutting-edge projects, leveraging diverse skills and perspectives.

Mentorship : Senior members provide mentorship and guidance, ensuring that juniors receive valuable insights and support.

Interactive Sessions : Regular interactive sessions and workshops are conducted to keep members abreast of the latest developments and trends in mathematics and innovation.

Post / Upcoming Events

Hosting KPIT Sparkle : A prestigious event showcasing innovative solutions and ideas, offering students the chance to present their work to a broader audience.

Treasure Hunt (Pi Day) : A tradition that combines problem-solving with fun, encouraging students to engage in a creative and competitive environment.

Seminars and Speaker Sessions : Regular seminars and sessions featuring industry experts, providing members with direct exposure to industry trends and practices.

FinXpo

Technical Interview Speaker Session : A session dedicated to preparing students for technical interviews, featuring experienced professionals who share their insights and tips

Recruitment Dates

First Round - August 20th, 2024 : An online aptitude test assessing mathematical and analytical skills.

Second Round : Personal interviews for those who qualify the first round; specific dates will be communicated post-qualification.

Third Round : Offline interviews with the president and core members of the club; dates will be finalized after the second round.
