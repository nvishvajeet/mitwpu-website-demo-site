Ninox Nature Club's mission is to connect students with nature and promote sustainable practices. Our objectives include organising field trips, hosting expert talks, conducting research using citizen science data, raising awareness through outreach, and exploring technology for wildlife research and conservation. Our club is dedicated to nurturing a deep appreciation for the complexity of life and its connection to nature. Through our diverse activities, we aim to inspire active participation in conservation efforts.
