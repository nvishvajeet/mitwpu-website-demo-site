B.Tech Petroleum Engineering

Study B.Tech Petroleum Engineering at MIT WPU Pune. Home to Asia's first subsea engineering lab, NIRF ranked with a strong placement record. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The B.Tech Petroleum Engineering programme at MIT-WPU offers a comprehensive curriculum covering both upstream and downstream aspects of petroleum engineering. The syllabus includes a wide range of topics, from exploration and drilling to oil production, refining, and processing. Additionally, it incorporates data analytics and emerging technologies, ensuring students are well-prepared for industry advancements.

This industry-centric programme combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications to equip students for the dynamic challenges of the oil, gas, and energy sectors. Recognised by the Society of Petroleum Engineers, MIT-WPU becomes the leading university in Maharashtra for petroleum engineering, providing excellent career prospects and unique industry exposure to the students.

Eligibility

A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC, with English as a compulsory subject and A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in any one of the following subject groups:

  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics),
  • Physics & Mathematics with any Technical Vocational Subject
  • PCB* (Physics, Chemistry, Biology*),

[*Biology/Biotechnology is applicable only for the Bioengineering Programme.] (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). OR A minimum 60% aggregate marks in a Diploma in Engineering & Technology in a relevant branch from a government-approved technical board.

Selection Process

Admissions are based on the scores of JEE 2026/ MHT-CET 2026/ PERA-CET 2026, or MHT-CET-B* 2026/ NEET 2026* (*applicable only for the Bioengineering programme), along with a 500-word Statement of Purpose (SOP) written by the candidate

Programme Highlights

  • Experiential Learning : The programme offers hands-on experience in the petroleum field through a context-oriented curriculum that bridges theory with practical applications.
  • Cutting Edge Laboratories : Access advanced labs for Petroleum Reservoir Characterisation, Drilling Engineering, and Petroleum Exploration, enabling students to learn through tools and industry-relevant software.
  • Partnerships with Industries : Collaborations with leading organisations provide opportunities for research, training, and development, ensuring industry readiness.
  • Industry Exposure : Includes six-month internships and national/international immersion programmes, giving students the chance to work with top petroleum firms.
  • Startup Booster : The MIT-WPU Technology Business Incubator (TBI) supports budding entrepreneurs with funding, mentorship, and a robust network of innovative business professionals.
  • Holistic Learning : Offers programmes on human development, technology ethics, and the societal implications of energy, fostering a well-rounded education.

Programme Outcomes

  • Design efficient processes for exploring, extracting, and producing petroleum resources, applying principles of geology, chemistry, and engineering.
  • Design petroleum systems and elements to meet defined objectives and to assure the safety, security, sustainability and compliance of all applicable codes and standards.
  • Design and perform simulations, or use data analysis tools to optimise the Petroleum production process.
  • Work with multidisciplinary teams, demonstrate and adapt to various settings.
  • Critically assess ethical standards and societal contributions of petroleum engineering solutions in the context of sustainability.
