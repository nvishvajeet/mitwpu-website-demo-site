B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Cloud Computing)

Build a cloud career with B.Tech CSE Cloud Computing at MIT WPU Pune. AICTE approved, NIRF ranked programme with a strong campus placement record. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Cloud Computing) at MIT-WPU is designed to prepare students for the dynamic and rapidly growing field of cloud technologies. By 2028, cloud computing will shift from being a technology disruptor to becoming a necessary component for maintaining business competitiveness, according to Gartner, Inc.

IT spending on public cloud services continues to rise unabated. In 2024, worldwide end-user spending on public cloud services is forecast to total $679 billion and is projected to exceed $1 trillion in 2027.

Through a rigorous curriculum, hands-on projects, internships and research projects students will gain practical experience in building scalable, secure and robust cloud solutions and will become adept at leveraging leading cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure.

The programme emphasises the critical management of exponential data with Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability security triads. Along with the efficient resource management solutions in the cloud, reflecting the growing reliance on data across various industries. By integrating AI-driven cloud technologies and modern application practices, the programme ensures that graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the industry.

Eligibility

A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC, with English as a compulsory subject and A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in any one of the following subject groups:

  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics),
  • Physics & Mathematics with any Technical Vocational Subject
  • PCB* (Physics, Chemistry, Biology*),

[*Biology/Biotechnology is applicable only for the Bioengineering Programme.] (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). OR A minimum 60% aggregate marks in a Diploma in Engineering & Technology in a relevant branch from a government-approved technical board.

Selection Process

Admissions are based on the scores of JEE 2026/ MHT-CET 2026/ PERA-CET 2026, or MHT-CET-B* 2026/ NEET 2026* (*applicable only for the Bioengineering programme), along with a 500-word Statement of Purpose (SOP) written by the candidate

FAQs

While sharing a fundamental computer science curriculum with the regular B.Tech CSE programme, this specialisation incorporates a dedicated curriculum of cloud architecture, virtualisation, cloud security, containerisation, serverless computing, and multi-cloud environments. Students get hands-on experience with major cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) and industry-relevant certifications.

The programme covers various programming languages including Python, Java, JavaScript, and others relevant to cloud development and infrastructure management.

Students can participate in tech clubs, coding competitions, hackathons, cultural festivals, sports events, and other activities organised by the university.

Key courses include:

  • Core CS courses: Data Structures, Algorithms, Database Management, Computer Networks
  • Programming languages: Python, Java, JavaScript
  • Cloud-specific: Cloud Architecture, Virtualisation Technologies, Cloud Security, DevOps, Containerization Infrastructure as Code, Platform Services, Serverless Computing, Multi-cloud Strategies
