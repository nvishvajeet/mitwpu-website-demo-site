Pursue a BBA in International Business at MIT-WPU Pune. Industry-integrated curriculum, global exposure, 100% placement support. Admissions open 2026

Programme Overview

The BBA in International Business programmeat MIT-WPU is tailored for ambitious global leaders who wish to succeed in today’s interconnected economy. This programme provides an extensive curriculum that integrates fundamental business concepts with global trade, cross-cultural management, and international marketing strategies.

Students will acquire practical knowledge by engaging in hands-on projects, collaborating with industries, and experiencing actual business situations. Emphasising the cultivation of critical thinking and leadership abilities, the BBA International Business programme equips graduates to manage the intricacies of global markets and promote organisational achievement.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Or Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • The BBA in International Business prepares students with a global market perspective to handle and adapt to shifts in the worldwide market.
  • Graduates are equipped for varied career roles in different fields, such as export/import, supply chain management, global marketing, and finance.
  • The curriculum incorporates a case-based learning methodology, guaranteeing practical comprehension via real projects and site visits.
  • Students undertake a 4-6 week compulsory summer internship, acquiring essential experience in practical business environments.
  • Industry involvement through guest talks, seminars, and workshops is conducted by notable business executives.
  • An effective mentor-mentee relationship supports students during their educational path.
  • Engage in business simulations alongside Capstone or Marketplace, USA, improving strategic thinking abilities.
  • Opportunities to utilise a Centre for Industry-Academia Collaborations for organised evaluations and employment assistance.
  • Utilise the MIT-WPU Technology Business Incubator for entrepreneurial ventures seeking financial support, guidance, and networking options for budding entrepreneurs.
  • Engaging learning opportunities including rural, national, and international immersion initiatives to expand global viewpoints and cultural awareness.
