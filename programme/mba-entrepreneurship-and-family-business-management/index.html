Programme

MBA Entrepreneurship and Family Business Management

MBA Entrepreneurship & Family Business is specialized two-year programme designed to create entrepreneurial leaders and professionals who are prepared to take on the unique challenges and opportunities of family-run businesses.

Programme Overview

The MBA in Entrepreneurship and Family Business Management is a rigorous two-year postgraduate programme designed to develop next-generation entrepreneurs, startup founders, and successors of family-owned enterprises. The programme integrates entrepreneurial strategy, innovation, governance, and leadership development with deep experiential learning through incubators, live ventures, and global immersion. It is ideally suited for individuals aspiring to launch new ventures, scale startups, or professionalize and lead family businesses across generations in a rapidly evolving, innovation-driven economy.

Unique Programme Differentiators

Program-Embedded Global Immersion (2-3 Weeks)

The Ram Charan School of Leadership (RSOL), MIT-WPU presents the Global Immersion Programme (GIP) 2026 - a mandatory, credit-bearing (3 credits) international experience designed to build global business insights and cross-cultural leadership skills.

Delivered in collaboration with reputed international partner universities, the programme offers a 2–3 week global immersion with no additional fees. Students also earn two international certificates in:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Business Analytics

Duration: 2–3 Weeks International Exposure

Schedule: September 2026 to November 2026

Global Immersion Programme

A 2–3 week immersion delivered in collaboration with leading global partner universities, offering global exposure, cross-cultural learning, and international certifications. This programme is an integral part of the curriculum and is included in the fee, with no additional cost to students.

Leadership Lab Across Both Semesters

A dedicated Leadership Lab runs across both semesters to ensure continuous leadership development alongside academic learning.

Industry-Integrated Capstone Research Project

The programme concludes with an industry-integrated Capstone Research Project, enabling applied problem-solving and industry-linked research.

Global Collaborative Modular Teaching

A modular teaching approach with international faculty and senior corporate experts, with one course each semester, ensuring strong contemporary industry relevance.

AI-based Leadership Curriculum + Applied Tools Exposure

Artificial intelligence is integrated into learning, decision-making, and career planning, supported by exposure to tools such as:

Python | R | Tableau | Power BI | SAP | ERP Systems

Rural Immersion and Life Transformation Programme

A distinctive immersion experience designed to strengthen values-based leadership, reflection, and holistic development.

Experiential Learning & Case-Based Pedagogy

Grounded in real-world business contexts and a practice-oriented learning approach through real business cases, industry analysis, mini projects, simulations, and role-plays.

Minor Specialization Flexibility

Students choose one Major Specialization from Marketing, Human Resource Management, Finance, Business Analytics, or Operations and Supply Chain Management. Business Analytics is offered as a default Minor for all Major specializations, strengthening data-driven decision-making capabilities.

Students opting for Business Analytics as a Major may choose one Minor from HR, Finance, Marketing, or Operations and SCM, enabling highly customized career pathways.

Student Clubs and Experiential Learning Ecosystem

At the Ram Charan School of Leadership, student learning extends beyond the classroom through a dynamic ecosystem of clubs that foster research excellence, cultural awareness, and social responsibility, contributing to the holistic development of future leaders.

The Research Nexus Forum promotes a strong research culture through student-faculty collaboration, mentoring, and interdisciplinary inquiry, enabling high-quality research, publications, and industry-linked studies.

Niramay Club, the socio-cultural platform of the School, nurtures culturally rooted and socially conscious leaders by encouraging cultural expression, community engagement, mental well-being, and leadership grounded in empathy, purpose, and integrity.

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 3/ 4- year graduation degree in any stream from a UGC-approved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). AND A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) examinations, with English as a compulsory subject. AND A valid non-zero score in the relevant entrance examination conducted by the competent authority.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of a valid score in CAT 2025, XAT 2026, NMAT 2025, GMAT, CMAT 2026, MAH-CET 2026, PERA 2026 or MAT (September 2025 onwards). AND Candidates will also be evaluated through 50 marks online CBT, Group Discussion (GD), Extempore, Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule, along with consideration of their Past Academic Record and Work Experience. AND The valid (non-zero) national level entrance score is mandatory for confirmation of admission.
