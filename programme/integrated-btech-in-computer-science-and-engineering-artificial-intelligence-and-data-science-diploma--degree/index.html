B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Computer Science and Engineering - Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Study B.Tech CSE in AI and Data Science after 10th at MIT WPU Pune. Integrated diploma to degree path with a NIRF ranked curriculum. Apply now for 2026.

Programme Overview

The MIT-WPU B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Computer Science and Engineering - Artificial Intelligence & Data Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is a unique six-year programme that prepares you from school through to a professional computer science engineer. The programme not only provides a strong foundation in computer science but also covers cutting-edge tools such as AI and Data Science.

Starting with fundamental subjects like programming, mathematics, and science, the programme progresses to advanced topics including Business Intelligence, Edge AI, Quantum Computing, and Medical Image Processing. By the end of the course, you will be well-versed in the latest trends in AI and Data Science, equipping you for a wide range of career opportunities in these ever-expanding fields.

Major Tracks

  • Business Intelligence, Edge AI
  • Bio-Inspired Computing, Medical Image Processing
  • Human Computer Interface, Augmented Reality & Virtual Reality
  • Time Series Analysis & forecasting, Quantum Computing

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in the Class 10/ SSC examination from a government-approved board or its equivalent, with Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule.

FAQs

MIT-WPU offers an B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Computer Science and Engineering - Artificial Intelligence & Data Science that begins as a diploma programme for the first 3 years, which then transitions into a full-fledged B.Tech course for the next 3 years. This continuous learning model combines foundational knowledge with specialised skills in AI, data science, and engineering.

In the initial years, students focus on fundamental subjects like mathematics, science, and programming. As the course progresses, students delve into advanced topics such as AI, data science, machine learning, and software engineering. The curriculum offers a balanced combination of theory, real-life projects, and practical exposure through internships, ensuring that students are industry-ready by the time they complete the programme.

The Diploma B.Tech programme is distinct from other programmes because it allows students to begin right after class 10 and provides a seamless journey through both diploma-level and degree-level education over six years. This approach integrates foundational knowledge with advanced industry trends and technologies, offering a smooth transition into an engineering career without the need for a separate diploma or degree.

After completing the Diploma B.Tech, you can pursue careers as an AI/ML engineer, data scientist, or software developer. You will be eligible for roles across sectors such as IT, finance, healthcare, and telecommunications, including positions like AI/ML engineer, data scientist, business intelligence developer, and software architect. The comprehensive education equips you to work in a wide range of tech-driven industries.
