Programme Overview

The BBA in Banking, Finance, and Insurance at MIT World Peace University is an all-encompassing programme aimed at students' enthusiasm about succeeding in the vibrant financial industry.

This interdisciplinary programme delivers a triple specialisation, equipping students with comprehensive insights into banking operations, financial analysis, and principles of insurance. Students gain from compulsory internships at esteemed organisations, providing practical exposure that deepens their grasp of practical finance.

The curriculum focuses on experiential learning via case studies and projects, preparing graduates with vital skills for prosperous careers in banking and finance. Emphasising industry-specific training and job placement support, the BBA in Banking Finance and Insurance equips students to address the changing requirements of the financial sector.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Or Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • The BBA programme in Banking, Finance, and Insurance provides a distinctive triple specialisation, preparing students with an extensive understanding of each of the three areas.
  • Acquire essential practical experience via compulsory internships at prominent organisations such as RBI, HDFC, and ICICI, improving comprehension of the financial industry.
  • The programme prioritises experiential learning with case studies, simulations, and project-based tasks, enabling students to utilise theoretical concepts in real-world situations.
  • Cultivate essential skills for financial analysis, risk management, and investment strategies that are in high demand within the banking and finance sector.
  • Networking with industry experts via guest talks, workshops, and collaborations, offering useful insights and possible career connections.

  • The programme corresponds with well-known industry certifications like CFA and CFP, improving job prospects and career opportunities for graduates.
  • Take advantage of personalised career assistance at the Centre for Industry-Academia Partnerships (CIAP), facilitating smooth transitions into the workforce.
  • Acquire a strong foundation in vital business fields like management, accounting, and economics to enhance your specialised financial expertise.
  • Highlight the importance of professionalism and ethical conduct in financial choices, equipping students to responsibly manage the intricacies of the banking and finance industries.
