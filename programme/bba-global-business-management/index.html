Study BBA Global Business Management at MIT WPU Pune. UGC approved with an international business curriculum and 3500+ industry hiring partners. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The BBA in Global Business Management at MIT-WPU aims to equip students with the intricacies of the global business environment. This programme provides an extensive curriculum that includes key topics like marketing, finance, human resource management, and business analytics, guaranteeing a thorough education. Students participate in experiential learning by visiting industries, working on live projects, and completing a required internship with top firms such as Tata Motors and Volkswagen.

The programme focuses on strategic analysis and business investigation, preparing graduates with the ability to recognise and address international business issues efficiently. Furthermore, students gain from a strong mentor-mentee framework and perspectives from industry professionals via guest lectures and workshops. By emphasising practical applications and an engaging business simulation course partnered with Capstone or Marketplace, USA, students are ready to thrive in various career opportunities. Graduates are equipped to make significant contributions to businesses in India and around the world.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate score in 10+2/Class 12th or in equivalent examination with English subject in any stream from Govt. Approved Institution or equivalent (at least 45% marks, in case of Reserved Class category candidate belonging to Maharashtra State only) Or Minimum 60% aggregate score in 3-year Diploma from any Govt approved Institution or equivalent with English as a subject. Or Minimum 60% aggregate score in Competency and Vocational Courses (MCVC) from any Govt approved Institution or equivalent with English as a subject. Note: All International Baccalaureate (IB) students are required to score a minimum of 24 points for six subjects

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • The BBA Global Business Management programme offers an extensive curriculum including key topics such as marketing, finance, human resources, and business analytics, providing a thorough education.
  • Students participate in experiential learning through industry tours, hands-on projects, and a required 4-6 week internship with top firms like Tata Motors and Volkswagen, utilising theoretical concepts in practical scenarios.
  • In partnership with Capstone or Marketplace, USA, the programme features a dynamic business simulation that introduces real-world challenges to improve practical skills.
  • Renowned business executives lead guest lectures and workshops, offering students important perspectives on industry developments and methods.
  • A strong mentor-mentee framework enhances personal and professional growth via mentorship from seasoned faculty.
  • Preparation for competitive tests like CAT/GMAT/ATMA/XAT/CET improve students' preparedness for higher education and competitive assessments.
  • Centre for Industry-Academia Partnership promotes internships and employment opportunities, guaranteeing that students acquire pertinent work experience.
  • The programme ensures complete placement assistance, assisting students in obtaining fulfilling career options across various industries.
