Programme Overview

The M.Sc. Chemistry (Organic Chemistry) programme equips you with advanced knowledge in organic chemistry, focusing on the synthesis, properties, and reactions of organic molecules, heterocycles, and natural products. The uniqueness of this programme lies in a specialised curriculum on organic chemistry which focuses on practical application of organic synthesis. This programme enables you to develop and design synthetic routes that are safe, cost effective, environmentally sustainable and efficient. The curriculum emphasises both theoretical learning and hands-on laboratory work, enabling you to gain skills in the synthesis of organic molecules, experimental design and spectroscopic techniques for structural determination. Through research projects, you are encouraged to think critically and contribute innovative ideas in the field of organic chemistry. This programme prepares you for careers in academia, research institutions, government agencies, and various industries, including pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and materials science.

Programme Highlights

  • Gain in-depth knowledge of organic reactions, synthesis strategies, and modern techniques in creating complex organic molecules for pharmaceuticals, polymers, and materials science
  • Special focus on process chemistry which will gain knowledge in scale up reactions, troubleshooting existing processes, process monitoring using modeling and simulation software
  • Master cutting-edge spectroscopic methods such as NMR, IR, and mass spectrometry to analyse and interpret molecular structures
  • Learn eco-friendly approaches in organic synthesis, focusing on minimising waste and using sustainable resources in line with the principles of green chemistry.
  • Engage in hands-on research projects in collaboration with leading chemical industries and research institutes, solving real-world problems in organic chemistry and innovation.
  • Benefit from extensive career opportunities in pharmaceuticals, contract research organisation agrochemicals, flavors and fragrances, biotechnology, and academic research, with access to internships and placements in renowned chemical companies.
