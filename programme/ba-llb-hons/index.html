Programme

BA LL.B. (Hons)

Study BA LLB Hons at MIT WPU Pune. BCI approved integrated law programme with moot courts and a strong all India placement record. Apply now for 2026.

Programme Overview

MIT-WPU’s BA LLB Hons. is an extensive five-year degree programme that combines legal studies with humanities disciplines like sociology, political science, and psychology. This distinct blend enables students to grasp the wider social context in which legal systems function, equipping them with diverse career opportunities in law and associated areas.

The curriculum aims to align with current industry demands and features comprehensive hands-on training via moot court activities, internships, and engagement in Model United Nations (MUN) and parliamentary discussions. Curriculum is delivered by seasoned faculty, which includes practising attorneys and international specialists, guaranteeing that students obtain a top-notch education endorsed by the Bar Council of India.

Furthermore, the programme includes a specialised Employability, Placement, and Career Advancement Cell that assists students in obtaining internships and job placements. Through international and rural immersion programmes, graduates are prepared to address real-world challenges effectively. The BA LLB Hons. programme at MIT-WPU not only nurtures legal knowledge but also cultivates crucial abilities for thriving in today’s ever-changing landscape.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score of CLAT or MH CET LAW will be considered for the selection process; however, the candidate must also appear for the MIT-WPU CET Personal Interaction (PI) as per the schedule

Programme Highlights

  • The BA LLB Hons. programme merges legal studies with humanities disciplines like sociology, political science, and psychology, offering a comprehensive education.
  • Students engage in practical training through moot court activities, Model United Nations (MUN), and parliamentary discussions, acquiring real-world experience in legal procedures.
  • Classes are delivered by seasoned legal experts, comprising active attorneys and global educators, guaranteeing top-notch education accredited by the Bar Council of India.
  • The Employability, Placement, and Career Growth Cell assists students in obtaining internships and job opportunities, improving their career outlook.
  • The university provides international, national, and rural immersion programmes designed to help students grasp real-world challenges and enhance their problem-solving abilities.
  • The coursework also promotes public speaking and oratory abilities through different clubs, activities, equipping students for successful communication.
  • Regular guest lectures like the Vidhi Saamvad Series include talks by industry professionals on pertinent legal issues, enhancing students' educational experiences.
  • Students benefit from extensive library resources in addition to the main university library, offering a wide range of research materials to enhance their learning.

Programme Outcomes

  • Graduates will have an extensive grasp of legal concepts combined with knowledge from humanities disciplines, allowing them to examine the societal framework within law.
  • Through involvement in moot court sessions, internships, and real-world projects, students will have acquired crucial practical skills, equipping them for successful practice in the legal profession.
  • The programme improves students' communication abilities via activities like debates and presentations, enabling them to express legal arguments effectively and convincingly.

FAQs

Throughout the BA LLB Hons programme at MIT-WPU, students develop crucial abilities like Critical Thinking, Analytical Skills, Research, Writing, Communication, Advocacy, and Problem-Solving Skills. These abilities are essential for achievement in the legal profession and more.

The BA LLB Hons programme provides several specialisations, enabling students to concentrate on fields like Corporate Law, Criminal Law, Intellectual Property Rights, and Environmental Law. This adaptability allows students to customise their education based on their professional goals.

Indeed, MIT-WPU offers specialised assistance via its Placement, and Career Advancement Cell. This office helps students with job placements, internships, and career advice, making sure they are ready for the job market after graduation.
