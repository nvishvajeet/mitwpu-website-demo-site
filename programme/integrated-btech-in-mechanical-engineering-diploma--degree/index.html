Programme Overview

The B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Mechanical Engineering course integrates school education with a professional career in engineering, making it a balanced, directed programme suited for students after class 10. This allows them to smoothly transition into a complete mechanical engineering career. This six-year programme encompasses fundamental subjects such as mathematics and science before progressing to advanced aspects of mechanical engineering, including machinery design, manufacturing, and the Internet of Things, AI, and automation.

The programme aims to combine theoretical knowledge with practical application. It equips you for adaptable engineering positions in sectors such as automotive, construction, and robotics. MIT-WPU's degree programme, with a strong focus on applied projects, internships, and research, is one of the top mechanical engineering courses in India.

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in the Class 10/ SSC examination from a government-approved board or its equivalent, with Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule.

The programme covers topics related to mechanical engineering and robotics, including mechanical design, control engineering, robotics, AI, IoT, and automation. Students also get the opportunity to work on live projects across AI, IoT, and robotics, gaining hands-on experience that prepares them for their future careers.

Through the Center of Industry-Academia Partnerships, the university offers 100% placement assistance, connecting students with top-tier firms. Career guidance, resume building workshops, mock interviews ensure that students are industry-ready.
