Programme

MBA Construction Management

Explore the 2-year MBA in Construction Management at MIT WPU. Industry-integrated curriculum with BIM, digital construction, site immersion, and strong placement support. Admissions open!

Programme Overview

The MBA Construction Management is a specialized postgraduate programme focused on developing managerial and leadership skills for the construction and infrastructure sector with a strong emphasis on digital construction. The programme integrates management principles with modern digital technologies such as Building Information Modelling (BIM) and project planning tools to enhance project planning, execution, and control. Through an industry- oriented and practice-driven approach, graduates are prepared for leadership roles in construction, infrastructure, and project management organizations.

Scholarship

Based on student merit, MIT-WPU offers multiple scholarships to promote inclusion and academic excellence.

These are stated as below,

i. Dr. Vishwanath Karad Scholarship This scholarship provides a 100% fee waiver for the entire duration of the Programme and is applicable for 5% of the total students’ intake. (Subject to the fulfilment of conditions mentioned under the respective policy).

Eligibility

Minimum 55% aggregate marks in a graduation degree in Civil Engineering/ Construction Engineering/ Infrastructure Engineering/ Environmental Engineering/ Architecture or Allied Branches* (Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Production Engineering) from a UGC-approved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 50% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). AND A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC)/ 3-year Diploma examinations (post SSC), with English as a compulsory subject.

Selection Process

Candidates will also be evaluated through a 50-mark Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule, along with consideration of their past academic record and work experience. AND Admission will be based on the merit of a valid score in CAT 2025, XAT 2026, NMAT 2025, GMAT, CMAT 2026, MAH-CET 2026, PERA 2026 or MAT (September 2025 onwards) with valid (non-zero) national-level entrance score.

Note : Working professionals will get higher weightage in the selection process.

Curriculum Philosophy

The curriculum is industry-oriented, outcome-based and research-enriched. It includes:

  • Digital Construction & BIM
  • Project Controls: Cost, Schedule, Risk
  • Contracts, Claims & Legal Frameworks
  • Construction Methods, Equipment & Safety
  • Sustainability and Smart Infrastructure
  • Real Estate Economics & Valuation
  • Emerging Tech: AI, Robotics, Automation
  • Research Methodology & Consultancy Practice
