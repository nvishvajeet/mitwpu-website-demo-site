M.Sc. Yoga and Meditation

Immerse yourself in the transformative power of yoga and meditation with the M.Sc. (Yoga and Meditation) program at MIT-WPU. Deepen your understanding of yogic practices, mindfulness techniques, and holistic wellness. Our comprehensive curriculum, experienced faculty, and serene environment provide an immersive learning experience. Prepare for fulfilling careers as yoga instructors, wellness coaches, and holistic health practitioners. Join MIT-WPU's M.Sc. (Yoga and Meditation) program and embark on a journey of self-discovery and professional growth. Apply now to nurture your mind, body, and spirit.

Programme Overview

The M.Sc in Yoga and Meditation is a unique, interdisciplinary programme that integrates the knowledge of Yoga, Ayurveda, Naturopathy, Psychology, Spirituality, Indian traditions and heritage and more. The students learn in depth about Sanskrit grammar, Yoga sutra, Bhagavad Gita, Vedanta, diet, nutrition and more. The programme focuses to inculcate the skills of team building, peaceful communication, human dynamics and conflict resolution in the students for their overall development. The students inculcate research skills through the research project in the last semester. The curriculum incorporates experiential learning through activities such as field visits, study tours and rural immersion programmes.

FAQs

It is an interdisciplinary programme integrating Yoga, Ayurveda, Naturopathy, Psychology, Spirituality, Indian traditions, Sanskrit grammar, Yoga Sutra, Bhagwat Gita, Vedanta, diet, and nutrition. It also focuses on team building, peaceful communication, human dynamics, and conflict resolution.

It is a full-time programme and spans 2 years.

The programme includes field visits, study tours, rural immersion programmes, and a research project in the final semester.

Graduates can pursue careers in wellness centres, yoga therapy, research, teaching, holistic health consultancy, and related fields. The programme also prepares students for leadership roles in yoga and meditation sectors.
