Ph.D. in Management

Certify your expertise in areas like Finance, Marketing, Human Resource, and Computer Application with a Ph. D. in Management. MIT-WPU enables you to conduct meaningful research in multiple domains leading to results that matter.

Programme Overview

The Ph.D. programme in Management at the WPU School of Business is a prestigious and rigorous programme designed to produce high-quality academic management thinkers with advanced knowledge and expertise in Financial Management, Marketing Management, Human Resource Management, and Computer Application. The doctoral programme strongly emphasises research, encouraging students to undertake original and impactful research projects. Doctoral students work closely with faculty supervisors to identify research questions, develop research methodologies, collect and analyse data, and contribute novel insights to academia and industry. The programme's culmination is completing a doctoral dissertation, which represents a significant contribution to knowledge in the chosen area of specialisation. The WPU School of Business has a team of renowned faculty members who are experts in their respective fields of study. These faculty members actively engage with doctoral students, providing mentorship, guidance, and support throughout the research process. Their expertise and research contributions enrich the learning experience of doctoral candidates and contribute to the overall academic excellence of the programme. Recognising the interdisciplinary nature of management research, the programme encourages students to explore connections with other disciplines such as economics, psychology, sociology, and engineering. This interdisciplinary approach fosters innovation and allows students to gain insights from diverse perspectives, enhancing the depth and breadth of their research contributions. WPU School of Business emphasises a global perspective in its curriculum and research agenda. Students are encouraged to explore international issues, cross-cultural management challenges, and global business trends in their research endeavors. In addition to academic coursework and research, the programme provides professional development and networking opportunities. Candidates have access to workshops, seminars, conferences, and industry partnerships that enhance their skills in academic writing, presentation, networking, and job market preparation. These activities help students develop the necessary competencies for successful careers in academia, research, consulting, or industry leadership roles.
