BCA Science (Hons)

Study BCA at MIT WPU Pune. UGC approved, application focused IT programme with 240+ labs and 3500+ all India recruiting partners. Apply now for 2026.

Programme Overview

MIT-WPU's BCA Science (Hons) programme equips students with comprehensive IT training, blending core programming languages (C, Java, Python) with emerging technologies like AI, ML, Cloud Computing, and IoT. This balanced curriculum develops both theoretical knowledge and practical application through hands-on projects using cutting-edge technologies.

Students gain essential professional skills in Business Communication and Research Methodology while developing critical problem-solving abilities for today's data-driven industries. The programme offers internships and global collaborations, providing real-world experience through strong industry partnerships.

Under NEP 2020, students can get an Honours option in their fourth year, creating pathways to both immediate employment and advanced education. Graduates emerge versatile and job-ready for diverse sectors including IT, healthcare, finance, and marketing, possessing the multifaceted skill set required in today's dynamic, tech-forward professional environment.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in science stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). OR Minimum 55% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule
