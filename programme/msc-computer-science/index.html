Programme Overview

The M.Sc. in Computer Science at MIT-WPU is an intensive programme with a dynamic curriculum designed to deepen students' understanding of computer science along with enhancing their practical skills in terms of software design and development.

The programme spans over two years, and the curriculum covers essential topics such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Web Development, alongside advanced subjects like Network Security and Operating Systems.

Students during the programme engage in practical learning through mini-projects, practical assignments, and a six-month full-time internship with leading IT companies, for real-world exposure. The programme offers electives in cutting-edge areas including Next Generation Databases, IoT, and DevOps, helping the students to tailor their education to their interests.

The curriculum ensures the overall development of the students, with the addition of Yoga and Peace studies graduates are made to enhance their analytical skills that are necessary to formulate computing solutions for impactful careers in various sectors. With strong industry connections and 100% placement assistance, this programme at MIT-WPU positions students for success in the evolving tech landscape.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in 3/4-year graduation degree in B.Sc. (Computer Science), BCS, B.Sc. (IT) from a UGC-approved institution/university or its equivalent (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule

Programme Highlights

  • The programme offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers essential topics such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Network Security, and Advanced Operating Systems, for an all-round education.
  • Students engage in practical learning experiences with mini-projects, practical assignments, and a six-month full-time internship with leading IT companies.
  • Students can choose from a variety of electives, including Next Generation Databases, IoT, Digital Image Processing, and DevOps, allowing expansive educational exposure.
  • Graduates can benefit from international collaborations and partnerships with renowned institutions like the University of Wisconsin-Parkside, facilitating collaborative research opportunities and gaining global perspectives.
  • The curriculum offers holistic development by integrating Yoga and Peace studies as a part of the programme and to promote physical and mental well-being alongside academic growth.
  • Graduates are offered 100% placement assistance and connections to top recruiters, for successful careers in the tech industry.

FAQs

Graduates of the M.Sc Computer Science programme can pursue various career paths, including roles such as Software Developer, Data Scientist, Cybersecurity Analyst, AI/Machine Learning Engineer, DevOps Engineer, and IT Consultant. The diverse skill set acquired during the programme prepares them for impactful positions in the technology sector.

A solid undergraduate background in computer science is crucial for admission to an M.Sc. programme as it provides foundational knowledge in programming, algorithms, and systems design. This background ensures that students can effectively engage with advanced concepts and coursework, facilitating a smoother transition into graduate-level studies.

The curriculum of M.Sc CS programme in India is designed to align with international standards by incorporating contemporary topics such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Cybersecurity. Many Indian universities also emphasise practical learning through internships and projects, ensuring that graduates are equipped with skills that meet global industry demands.

Master of Science Graduates in Computer Science in India enjoy numerous career prospects in different sectors. Leading firms recruiting these graduates include:

  • Information Technology (IT) Companies
  • Global Companies firms like Tech Mahindra, KPIT Cummins, etc.
  • Startups
  • Consulting Firms
  • Research Institutions
