Programme Overview

The B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Civil Engineering - Smart Infrastructure & Construction from MIT-WPU integrates school-level education with advanced, top-tier civil engineering topics. Beginning with a solid grounding in mathematics, science, and language skills, the programme progresses naturally into specialist engineering studies, including structural and environmental engineering, surveying, and smart infrastructure technologies such as artificial intelligence, drones, and data science.

The programme emphasises lifelong learning by integrating advanced technologies like AI, IoT, and robotics into the curriculum. Students gain practical experience in applying these innovations to construction and urban systems, equipping them to tackle the challenges of modern civil engineering. This programme prepares students to be leaders in the field, contributing to global civil engineering initiatives with a focus on sustainability and cultural heritage.

Major Tracks

  • Sustainable Construction Materials & Management
  • Intelligent Irrigation Technologies

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in the Class 10/ SSC examination from a government-approved board or its equivalent, with Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • State-of-the-art Facilities : New, modern laboratories, updated instruments, and tools for research and practice, exposing students to the latest civil engineering practices.
  • Industry Internships : You get the opportunity to work on mass manufacturing with leading companies such as Tata Motors, JCB, Volkswagen, Mercedes-Benz, Kirloskar Pneumatic Co. Ltd., and others.
  • MOOCs & Interdisciplinary Courses : Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) are included in the syllabus to help you cover more ground and perfect your skills.
  • Immersion Programmes : Broaden your educational horizon through national study exchange programmes, offering insights into engineering practices across different regions of India.
  • Collaborations With Top Industries : MIT-WPU has excellent tie-ups with organisations like Tata Technologies, Mercedes-Benz, Blue Star, etc., providing you with ample opportunities for live projects and industry insights.
  • Skill-Based Courses : Well-structured courses introduce practical and industrial skills in robotics, IoT, and AI-related applications, laying a foundation for future advancements in civil engineering.
  • Guest Lectures & Seminars : Attend lectures and seminars conducted by industry experts at various points during the course.
