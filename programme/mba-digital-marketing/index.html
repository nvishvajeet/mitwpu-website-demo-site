Programme Overview

In today’s dynamic era, having a compelling online presence can make or break a business. The MBA programme in digital marketing offered by MIT-World Peace University is well regarded and equips students to grasp the strategic insights of digital marketing.

This extensive two-year degree programme enables graduates with technical know-how and strategic insights to easily navigate through the digital marketing complexities. The programme works around areas like Social Media Advertising, Market Research & Analytics, Search & Display Marketing, Content Marketing, Email Marketing, and Digital Branding.

In addition to the theoretical knowledge, the programme focuses on experiential learning which enables students to thrive in real-world scenarios. This hands-on experience provides opportunity for students to manage online marketing campaigns and help them build brand across various social media platforms through social media marketing and social media optimisation.

At MIT-WPU we follow dynamic teaching methodology to enhance practical learning experience through real-world projects and insights from industry experts. Additionally, the curriculum also includes mandatory internship for crucial exposure to the digital marketing landscape, ensuring that students gain complete understanding of the principles of digital marketing but also know how to implement them effectively.

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 3/ 4- year graduation degree in any stream from a UGC-approved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). AND A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) examinations, with English as a compulsory subject. AND A valid non-zero score in the relevant entrance examination conducted by the competent authority.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of a valid score in CAT 2025, XAT 2026, NMAT 2025, GMAT, CMAT 2026, MAH-CET 2026, PERA 2026 or MAT (September 2025 onwards). AND Candidates will also be evaluated through 50 marks online CBT, Group Discussion (GD), Extempore, Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule, along with consideration of their Past Academic Record and Work Experience. AND The valid (non-zero) national level entrance score is mandatory for confirmation of admission.
