Programme Overview

The B.Sc. Computer Science course at MIT-WPU is a full-time study with an emphasis on programming, databases, web development, and emerging technologies such as AI, machine learning, cybersecurity, etc. The curriculum focuses heavily on practical application and prepares students to tackle real-world issues and drive innovation in the tech sector. The programme starts with the principles of computer science and advances into specialisations such as cloud computing, data science, network security, and blockchain technology.

The programme also offers practical experience through real-world projects and industry partnerships and prepares students for careers in software development, cybersecurity, and data analysis. The programme also offers international exposure with partners such as the University of Wisconsin, providing opportunities for exchange programmes and joint research.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in science stream with Mathematics, & Statistics subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). OR Minimum 55% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule
