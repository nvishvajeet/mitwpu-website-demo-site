Programme

M.Sc. Microbiology

Enhance your expertise and garner more skills in the domain with MIT-WPU’s M.Sc. Microbiology programme. Dive deeper into modules like biotechnology, bioinformatics, and more with industry internship opportunities.

Programme Overview

The M.Sc Microbiology programme is designed to introduce students to the principles and contemporary techniques in the field of microbiology. The programme includes modules on microbial biotechnology, bioinformatics and data analytics, food and dairy, agriculture, breweries, and food technology. In addition to core subjects in microbiology, there are interdisciplinary tracks in communications skills, peace and yoga, interview preparation, research writing, and other areas to help students develop a well-rounded skill set. The programme also includes a research project and an industrial internship in the second year, which provide students with practical experience and help them develop their skills and knowledge.

Eligibility

Agriculture, or B.Tech. (Biotechnology and related streams) from a UGC-approved institution/university or its equivalent (minimum 45% for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Programme Outcomes

  • Gain in-depth knowledge of core and applied microbiology, including microbial biotechnology, bioinformatics, food and dairy microbiology, agriculture, breweries, and food technology.
  • Develop advanced laboratory skills in microbial isolation, characterisation, identification, and the use of modern analytical and biochemical techniques.
  • Acquire research competence through a mandatory research project and industrial internship, enabling practical experience and industry readiness.
  • Apply molecular biology, biostatistics, and bioinformatics tools to solve real-world problems in health, industry, and the environment.
  • Demonstrate scientific communication, data analysis, and research writing skills for effective dissemination of scientific knowledge.
  • Cultivate professional and ethical responsibility, including laboratory safety, quality assurance, and adherence to international standards.
  • Prepare for diverse careers in research, industry, quality control, academia, and further studies such as Ph.D. or M.Phil in microbiology.
