Programme Overview

MA in Political Leadership and Government is a unique master's programme developed for future leaders in the domains of politics and governance. It was established with the goal of developing moral, passionate, and dedicated political leaders for the nation from all walks of life and at the grassroots level. The programme provides a thorough understanding of a number of facets, including international and public relations, media, NGO, political strategy, analysis, the election process, and functional politics. Two rigorous internships are offered as well for practical knowledge application. The programme also boasts a national and international study tour, as well as guest speakers who are fully engaged in the politics and administration of the nation.

Programme Highlights

  • Political communication : Includes public relations, branding, social media management, advertising, and written and spoken communication.
  • Constituency Management : Focuses on the development and effective management of constituencies for MPs, MLAs, corporators, Zilla Parishad, and Gram Panchayat members
  • Campaign & Elections Management : Encompasses planning for various elections. Involves strategic campaign planning utilising media, research, data analysis, and psephology
  • Hands-on Learning : Minimum 6-month internship with offices of political parties or political leaders
  • Immersive Learning : National study tour to Delhi is organised to give first-hand exposure to meet policymakers, legislators, bureaucrats, institutions & media houses

Programme Outcomes

  • Demonstrate a comprehensive understanding of political systems, governance structures, and the functioning of government at local, national, and international levels, with a special focus on Indian politics and comparative political systems.
  • Apply advanced concepts and practical skills in political communication, including public relations, branding, social media management, and effective written and spoken communication for political contexts.
  • Strategically plan, manage, and analyse political campaigns and elections using research, data analysis, psephology, and media tools to ensure effective electoral outcomes.
  • Effectively manage constituencies and understand the nuances of constituency development for various elected positions, including MPs, MLAs, corporators, and local government representatives.
  • Critically analyse public policy, engage in advocacy, and contribute to policy-making processes at multiple governance levels, drawing on theoretical and practical insights.
  • Exhibit leadership qualities rooted in ethical, passionate, and dedicated service, with a commitment to inclusive development, social justice, and peacebuilding.
  • Integrate interdisciplinary research methods to analyse contemporary political issues and undertake independent projects or research in the field of political leadership and government.
  • Communicate complex political ideas and strategies effectively to diverse audiences, utilising both traditional and digital platforms.
  • Gain practical exposure and professional experience through compulsory internships with political leaders, parties, NGOs, and government institutions, as well as through national and international study tours.
  • Engage with and learn from experienced practitioners in politics, governance, administration, and media, fostering a network for future leadership roles.

FAQs

The MA in Political Leadership and Government (MPG) is a two-year, full-time postgraduate programme designed to develop future leaders in politics and governance. It combines academic learning with practical exposure to political processes, public administration, and leadership skills.

The curriculum covers a wide range of subjects such as :

  • Political communication
  • Constituency management
  • Campaign and election management
  • International relations and public policy
  • Psephology and data analytics
  • Legislative procedures
  • Budget making and analysis
  • It also includes two rigorous internships, a national study tour, and immersive learning experiences.

Students participate in:

  • National and international study tours (including a visit to Delhi to meet policymakers and legislators)
  • Field visits to government offices, NGOs, and political party offices
  • Hands-on campaign and election management activities.

Graduates can pursue roles such as:

  • Political Analyst
  • Policy Research Associate
  • Political Strategist
  • Constituency Manager
  • Election Campaign Manager
  • Social Media Strategist
  • Legislative Assistant

Many alumni have secured positions in political parties, government agencies, NGOs, and policy think tanks.
