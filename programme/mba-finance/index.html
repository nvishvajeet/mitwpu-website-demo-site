MBA Finance

Excel in the world of finance with an MBA in Finance from MIT-WPU. Acquire expertise in financial analysis, investment management, corporate finance, and risk assessment. Join MIT-WPU, a prestigious institution renowned for its excellence in business education. Develop critical financial decision-making skills and gain a deep understanding of financial markets. Prepare for rewarding career opportunities in investment banking, financial consulting, corporate finance, and more. Apply now to elevate your finance career and lead in the ever-evolving financial landscape with MIT-WPU's MBA in Finance program.

Programme Overview

The success of any organisation depends on the smooth and efficient management of its financial data. In order to accomplish particular goals and objectives, the process entails organising, planning, regulating, and monitoring financial data. The knowledge of financial experts becomes quite helpful in this situation.

MIT-WPU offers an extensive MBA Finance two-year degree programme that helps students to get hold of essential skills required to excel in the finance industry. The programme is meticulously crafted to help students gain knowledge about the following:

MBA Finance programme includes interactive learning opportunities, real-world case studies, and projects with an industrial focus. Graduates have an understanding of the complex financial business principles through guest lectures from industry and immersions in related financial organisations.

Programme plays an important role in developing analytical and critical thinking abilities while laying a strong foundation for financial concepts. Graduates from this programme will be well-equipped to handle the financial obstacles faced throughout the journey.

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 3/ 4- year graduation degree in any stream from a UGC-approved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). AND A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) examinations, with English as a compulsory subject. AND A valid non-zero score in the relevant entrance examination conducted by the competent authority.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of a valid score in CAT 2025, XAT 2026, NMAT 2025, GMAT, CMAT 2026, MAH-CET 2026, PERA 2026 or MAT (September 2025 onwards). AND Candidates will also be evaluated through 50 marks online CBT, Group Discussion (GD), Extempore, Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule, along with consideration of their Past Academic Record and Work Experience. AND The valid (non-zero) national level entrance score is mandatory for confirmation of admission.

Programme Outcomes

  • Financial Management Expertise : Students gain a deeper understanding of financial management principles, encompassing financial analysis, budgeting techniques and strategic decision-making processes.
  • Investment Analysis and Portfolio Management : Gain the ability to evaluate investment opportunities, manage diverse portfolios and make informed strategic investment choices.
  • Risk Management : Through this extensive programme students will learn about risk management concepts, enabling them to identify risks and assess and mitigate financial risks
  • Corporate Finance : Our students also acquire critical knowledge related to corporate financial planning, capital structure optimisation and development of effective financial strategies.
  • International Finance : The robust curriculum covers the complexities of international financial markets and global economic trends, foreign exchange dynamics, international trade considerations and multinational financial management strategies.
  • Ethics and Corporate Governance: The Programme lays the stress on importance of ethical considerations in financial decision-making while providing insights into corporate governance principles.

FAQs

There are numerous career options available after getting the postgraduate degree in the Finance sector, especially in this data-driven, dynamic corporate world. Following are the few options available to choose from:

  • Corporate Finance Manager
  • Capital Markets Associate.
  • Investment Banker.
  • Private Equity Manager.
  • Venture Capitalist.
  • Portfolio Manager.
  • Portfolio Analyst.

Preparation strategies include:

  • Reviewing fundamental financial concepts and theories.
  • Practicing common interview questions related to finance.
  • Conducting mock interviews with peers or mentors.
  • Researching the company and its financial standing to tailor your responses accordingly

Students will develop following skills essential for success in finance roles:

  • Analytical skills
  • Strategic planning abilities
  • Effective communication techniques
  • Leadership qualities.

Key subjects often included in an MBA in Finance curriculum are :

  • Corporate Finance
  • Investment Analysis
  • Risk Management
  • Financial Markets
  • Portfolio Management

The curriculum incorporates international finance topics that equip students with insights into global markets and economic trends necessary for navigating complex financial environments effectively.
