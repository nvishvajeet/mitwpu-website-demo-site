Programme

M.Tech Civil Engineering (Structural Engineering)

Learn advanced skills and dive deeper into structural engineering with MIT-WPU’s M.Tech Civil Engineering programme . Master areas like environmental engineering, earthquake engineering, and sustainable construction.

Programme Overview

The M.Tech in Civil Engineering (Structural Engineering) programme at MIT-WPU, recognised among the best M.Tech. colleges in India, cultivates critical thinking, analytical, technical, professional, management, and problem-solving skills essential for structural engineering. This M.Tech programme seamlessly integrates research for a comprehensive education. The pedagogy emphasises value-based learning, providing hands-on training with modern tools and computer simulation methods that meet international standards. Research areas include sustainable construction, environmental engineering, and earthquake engineering, all supported by advanced laboratories.

The programme offers industry-based projects and internships with top organisations such as L&T, ACC, and the Indian Railways, ensuring students gain valuable industry exposure. Additionally, it fosters a multidisciplinary approach, enhances practical perspectives, and develops entrepreneurial abilities and ethical practices for lifelong learning. Graduates are well-prepared for diverse careers in civil engineering and related sectors.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in a 4- year graduation degree in the relevant Engineering branch* from a UGCapproved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination, along with a Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule.

A valid score in GATE score (2026/ 2025/ 2024), PERA-CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases, the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) Score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule.

For admission under sponsored category, candidate must have a minimum of two years of full-time work experience in a registered firm, company, industry, educational/ research institute, Government Department, or Government Autonomous Organization in the relevant field in which admission is sought. A Sponsorship Certificate (in the prescribed format) is mandatory for admission under this category.

* For more information regarding programme-wise branch eligibility, please refer to the M.Tech. rules published on the official website.

FAQs

Graduates of this programme can pursue roles as Structural Design Engineers, Bridge Specialists, Seismic Consultants, Research Scientists, or BIM Specialists.

The programme combines advanced theoretical curriculum with hands-on laboratory sessions, industry projects, and computational training. Students apply concepts through structural testing and real-world design challenges.

Students can research earthquake-resistant structures, sustainable materials, computational optimisation, and structural health monitoring. The department provides laboratory facilities and faculty mentorship for thesis work.

The curriculum includes comprehensive software training (ETABS, STAAD Pro) and exposure to BIM, parametric design, and digital twin technologies. Students learn modern digital workflows relevant to contemporary practice.
