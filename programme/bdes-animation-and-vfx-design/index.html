B.Des. Animation and VFX Design

Study B.Des Animation and VFX at MIT WPU Pune. Hands on digital production labs, film and media industry exposure and strong creative placements. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The B.Des. (Animation and VFX Design) at MIT-WPU is a progressive, multidisciplinary programme that combines creativity with cutting-edge technology, leading the way in the highly sought-after fields of Animation and Visual Effects (VFX).

Focused on storytelling, narrative form, and character development, this programme ensures that you develop the skills needed to create engaging, emotionally impactful content. The curriculum spans a wide range of topics, building a solid foundation in the history of animation and VFX, alongside modern practices and future trends.

Students will learn to create content for diverse audiences with a user-centred design approach. Moreover, a strong emphasis on ethical storytelling will guide you to approach your craft with responsibility and integrity.

As a forward-thinking programme, it integrates innovative technologies such as virtual reality (VR) and artificial intelligence (AI) into the learning experience. These emerging technologies are causing continuous disruption in the creative industries, and the programme is designed to prepare you to work in this ever-evolving field of digital media. It is not just aimed at technical experts, but at creative minds who can transcend the boundaries of possibility to tell the compelling visual stories of tomorrow.

Our curriculum ensures you're proficient with the tools demanded by top animation and VFX studios.

MIT-WPU’s B.Des. Animation & VFX programme blends creative and technical skills, and it prepares you for the exciting world of animation and the visual effects industry. The B.Des. (Animation and VFX Design) curriculum at MIT-WPU is meticulously designed to provide you with a comprehensive and future-forward education, covering the fundamentals, essentials, and advanced aspects of both animation and VFX. You will gain in-depth knowledge and practical skills in:

  • 2D Animation: Master the foundational principles of classic and digital 2D animation, including character movement, storytelling through 2D visuals, and industry-standard techniques.
  • 3D Animation: Develop expertise in creating realistic 3D characters and environments, encompassing modeling, rigging, animation principles in a 3D space, and advanced rendering techniques.
  • Visual Effects: Learn cutting-edge VFX techniques for seamlessly integrating computer-generated imagery with live-action footage, creating stunning simulations, and bringing imaginative concepts to life.
  • Storytelling & Scriptwriting: Cultivate your ability to develop strong narratives specifically for animation and VFX, mastering scriptwriting principles, visual storytelling techniques, and narrative design for various media.
