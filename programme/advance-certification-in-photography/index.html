Programme Overview

The Advance Certification in Photography at MIT-WPU is a one-year part-time programme that provides a thorough grounding in the technical, creative, scientific, and aesthetic aspects of photography. The programme covers a variety of photography genres, including advertising, fashion, wildlife, medical photography, and more. Students gain practical experience through workshops and guest lectures from ace photographers in each genre, as well as demonstrations in studios and in the field. The programme aims to prepare students to effectively practice photography and face real-life challenges in the field.

FAQs

The Advanced Certification in Photography at MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) is a comprehensive professional programme designed to elevate photography skills from basic to advanced levels. The programme combines technical expertise, creative development, and industry-relevant practices to prepare students for careers in various photography specialisations.

The curriculum covers a wide range of subjects including :

  • Advanced camera operations and equipment handling
  • Lighting techniques (studio and natural lighting)
  • Composition and visual storytelling
  • Post-production and digital editing
  • Specialised photography (portrait, landscape, product, fashion)
  • Photography business essentials
  • Portfolio development

Yes, the programme emphasises practical application. Students participate in multiple on-location shoots, studio sessions, and real-world photography assignments. Field trips to culturally and visually significant locations are organised throughout the programme.

Graduates of this programme pursue various career paths including:

  • Commercial photography
  • Fashion and portrait photography
  • Photojournalism
  • Product and advertising photography
  • Event photography
