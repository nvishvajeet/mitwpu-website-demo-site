Programme Overview

Unlock your potential and the future of work administration in future with MIT-WPU’s two years comprehensive undergraduate programme BBA in Human Resource Management. The curriculum is designed by professionals to give students a strong foundation in business administration and specialised understanding of human resources.

The programme helps students in understanding key areas including organisational change management, talent acquisition, and conflict resolution by combining academic learning with real-world projects.

Internships and industrial projects play a pivotal role in the academic journey, as they provide practical exposure to students and equip them to become well-known leaders, adept problem solvers, and change agents in the complicated workforce of today.

Graduates of the BBA in Human Resource Management programme are prepared significantly to create impact in their organisations by developing leadership abilities and addressing HR issues in a variety of industries.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Or Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • The impactful case-based learning approach is used where practising students achieve practical problem-solving skills.
  • Students gain valuable corporate exposure by participating in a variety of interactive interventions, such as industry visits and live projects.
  • Curriculum with integrated ability enhancement subjects to gear students for success in the professional world of corporations.
  • In collaboration with Capstone or Marketplace, USA, the programme features a dynamic business simulation subject providing students with hands-on, real-world business challenges.
  • A mandatory 4-6 week summer internship with companies like Tata Motors, JCB, Volkswagen, and many more assures that the students apply their learning in the classroom to the practical world, thereby supporting experiential learning.
  • Guest lectures, seminars, and workshops by the country's most distinguished corporate leaders give students industry insight as a part of this learning process. It has a healthy mentor-mentee system that supports the student institutionally and fosters both personal and professional development.
  • Specialised classes for CAT/GMAT/ATMA/XAT/CET make students ready for competitive examinations and broaden their career prospects.

Programme Outcomes

  • Apply strategic HR management knowledge to address complex workforce challenges.
  • Analyse organisational needs and develop effective talent acquisition and retention strategies.
  • Implement HR policies and practices in compliance with legal and ethical standards.
  • Utilise research-based methodologies to investigate and resolve human resource issues.
  • Demonstrate effective communication skills in conveying HR concepts to diverse stakeholders.
  • Work collaboratively in multidisciplinary settings to address HR challenges.
  • Apply contemporary HR practices to foster a positive organisational culture.
  • Assess societal, legal, and ethical implications in HR decision-making.
  • Uphold ethical principles and professional responsibilities in HR practice.
  • Leverage technology and modern tools for efficient HR activities.
  • Apply HR principles to project management, contributing to organisational success.
  • Recognise the significance of continuous professional development in the field of Human Resource Management.
