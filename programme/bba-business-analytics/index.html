Programme

BBA Business Analytics (Hons)

BBA in Business analytics designed for students interested in exploring the conceptual side of business & gaining expertise in data-driven Business decision. Admission open for 2025, enroll now!

Programme Overview

Today's competitive workplace demands data analysis and visualisation for building business intelligence; as it equips a firm to build a competitive edge, therefore helping them in making smart decisions.

MIT-WPU’s BBA in Business Analytics is meticulously designed to give students abilities and skills that are needed in the application of multiple tools designed to analyse and visualise data. The student's analytical ability develops over the period of the time, and they are ready to make a meaningful input into strategic as well as day-to-day operations.

Students learn to apply analytical techniques to deal with challenging business problems at the very outset. One of the highlights of the programme is the business simulation programme, in partnership with Capstone, which immerses students in real-world situations and offers hands-on exposure in a safe setting.

The BBA in Business Analytics also focuses on practical application by having numerous projects and pre-employment training programmes. This integrated approach ensures that graduates are well-prepared to navigate the ever-evolving industry landscape and confidently address real-world challenges. By graduation, students are equipped with the skills necessary to have a meaningful impact on the business sector.

Major Tracks

  • Data Science and Machine Learning
  • Business Intelligence and Data Visualisation
  • Marketing Analytics
  • Operations and Supply Chain Analytics

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Or Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • Utilises a case-based learning approach to enhance practical problem-solving skills.
  • Provides exposure to the corporate world through guest lectures, workshops on data analytics tools, industry visits, and live projects.
  • Includes embedded ability enhancement programmes designed for success in professional environments.
  • Features a dynamic business simulation programme in collaboration with Capstone or Marketplace, USA.
  • Requires a mandatory 4-6 week summer internship with leading companies such as Assetcues, TSL Consultancy, Ethx Softcon Pvt Ltd, and more.
  • Offers insights from eminent corporate leaders through guest lectures and seminars.
  • Implements a robust mentor-mentee system for personal and professional development.
  • Provides specialised classes for competitive exam preparation (CAT/GMAT/ATMA/XAT/CET).
  • The Centre for Industry-Academia Partnerships (CIAP) facilitates internships and job placements through structured assessments and training.
  • The MIT-WPU Technology Business Incubator (TBI) supports budding entrepreneurs with funding and mentorship.
  • Engages students in rural, national, and international immersion programmes to address complex societal issues.
