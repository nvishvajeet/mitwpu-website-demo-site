B.Com (Hons.)

Study B.Com Hons at MIT WPU Pune. UGC approved commerce programme with finance, analytics and 3500+ industry recruiting partners across India. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The MIT-WPU B.Com (Hons.) programme has an extensive and application-oriented curriculum that equips you with qualifying abilities for various business and finance careers. Cost Accounting, Banking and Finance, and Marketing Management specialisations provide a strong foundation in core business disciplines.

In addition to the industry-centric content, the curriculum is enriched with case studies, expert sessions, and live projects, making it a highly application-oriented programme in sync with actual business practices.

Apart from theoretical curriculum, practical skills are honed through electives and a choice-based credit system that helps students to customise the studies as per career options. Industry experts, business leaders, and international academic immersion programmes all ensure you get a taste of global business trends.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Or Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.
