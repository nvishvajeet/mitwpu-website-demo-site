Programme Overview

The BBA in Entrepreneurship and Family Business Management at MIT-WPU is a thorough programme aimed at budding entrepreneurs and individuals seeking to improve their skills in managing family businesses. This engaging programme merges entrepreneurial concepts with real-world applications, equipping students to manage companies internationally. The programme includes key topics like marketing, finance, and organisational behaviour, promoting critical thinking and strategic decision-making skills. .

Students gain valuable experience from hands-on learning via industry tours, practical projects, and a compulsory internship with esteemed firms such as Tata Motors and Volkswagen. The programme includes a strong mentor-mentee framework, guest talks by industry experts, and focused curriculum for competitive exam readiness. Emphasising innovation and flexibility, graduates are prepared to manoeuvre through the intricacies of the business environment, rendering them essential assets in conventional companies or startup initiatives. The programme focuses on practical applications, preparing students to address modern business issues effectively.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate score in 10+2/Class 12th or in equivalent examination with English subject in any stream from Govt. Approved Institution or equivalent (at least 45% marks, in case of Reserved Class category candidate belonging to Maharashtra State only) Or Minimum 60% aggregate score in 3-year Diploma from any Govt approved Institution or equivalent with English as a subject. Or Minimum 60% aggregate score in Competency and Vocational Courses (MCVC) from any Govt approved Institution or equivalent with English as a subject. Note: All International Baccalaureate (IB) students are required to score a minimum of 24 points for six subjects

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • The programme provides an extensive curriculum that combines entrepreneurial abilities and family business management, addressing key topics such as marketing, finance, and organisational behaviour.
  • Students participate in industry tours, real-life projects, and a required 4-6 week internship with top firms like Tata Motors and Volkswagen, applying theory in a practical setting.
  • In partnership with Capstone or Marketplace, USA, this programme features a Dynamic Business Simulation course that introduces real-world challenges to improve practical skills.
  • Prominent industry figures deliver guest lectures and workshops, offering important perspectives on contemporary market trends and practices.
  • A strong mentor-mentee framework promotes personal and professional growth through guidance from seasoned faculty members.
  • Preparation for competitive exams like CAT/GMAT/ATMA/XAT/CET improves students' preparedness for advanced studies and competitive tests.
  • The specialised Centre for Industry-Academia Partnership aids in internships and job placements, enabling students to acquire valuable work experience.
  • The programme promises 100% job assistance, aiding students in finding fulfilling career options across different industries.
