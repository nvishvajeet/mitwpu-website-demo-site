Programme Overview

The Bachelor of Commerce Financial Analysis (Hons.) programme is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge required to excel in the modern finance world. In collaboration with the prestigious CFA Institute, a globally recognised institution from the USA known for its Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) qualification, this programme offers a specialised curriculum that aligns with key areas of finance.

Key focus areas include Financial Analysis, Corporate Finance, Portfolio Management, and Wealth Management, providing a solid foundation for those aspiring to build careers in finance. In addition to core subjects from the Commerce and Management domains, the programme incorporates elements of the CFA curriculum, offering students an edge in professional financial qualifications.

With a robust emphasis on Accounting & Finance, the Bachelor of Commerce (Financial Analysis) ensures students are well-prepared to meet the demands of the evolving financial landscape.

Major Tracks

  • Corporate Finance & Financial Reporting
  • Wealth Management & Investment Banking
  • Global Financial Systems & Compliance
  • Core CFA Curriculum & Exam Preparation

Programme Highlights

  • Globally Recognised Certification : Earn the Investment Foundations Certificate by the CFA Institute, USA, along with on-campus training, strengthening students’ understanding of finance and investment concepts.
  • Strong Financial Foundation : B.Com provides comprehensive insights into accounting, economics, and business management, ensuring students understand key financial concepts.
  • Advanced Financial Expertise: CFA focuses on specialised areas such as investment analysis, portfolio management, and ethical practices, offering a deeper, global perspective on finance.
  • Enhanced Employability: The CFA designation is highly respected in the finance industry, giving graduates a competitive edge in the job market.
  • Career Advancement: The integrated programme equips students with both academic and professional skillsets, opening doors to higher-paying roles and faster career growth.
  • Preparation for CFA Qualification: Students are encouraged to pursue the full CFA qualification alongside or after their undergraduate degree, enhancing their career prospects.
  • Global and Local Financial Insight: The programme offers a balance of global investment strategies and an understanding of the Indian financial landscape, making students well-rounded professionals.
  • In-Demand Financial Skills: Graduates are equipped with critical financial analysis, portfolio management, and corporate finance skills, preparing them for various roles in the finance sector.

Programme Outcomes

  • Comprehensive Financial Knowledge: Graduates will possess a strong foundation in accounting, economics, and business management, complemented by advanced expertise in financial analysis and portfolio management.
  • Professional Competency: Students will develop the technical skills and ethical understanding required to excel in various finance roles, such as financial analyst, portfolio manager, or wealth management advisor.
  • Global and Local Market Expertise: Graduates will gain insights into both global investment strategies and the unique challenges of the Indian financial landscape, enabling them to make informed decisions in diverse financial contexts.
  • CFA Readiness: The programme prepares students to pursue the prestigious Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) certification, enhancing their credentials and professional standing in the finance industry.
  • Enhanced Employability: Students will emerge as highly sought-after professionals, equipped with a combination of academic knowledge and practical skills that provide a competitive edge in the job market.
  • Leadership in Finance: Graduates will be prepared to take on leadership roles within financial institutions, corporations, and investment firms, contributing to strategic decision-making and business growth.
