MFA (Applied Arts)

MFA Applied Arts at MIT WPU Pune is a 2 year creative postgraduate for artists who want to push their practice further. UGC approved. Apply now for 2026.

Programme Overview

The MFA in Applied Arts at MIT-WPU offers an immersive and intensive learning experience tailored for those seeking advanced study in visual arts. This Master of Fine Arts degree enables students to master their chosen area of specialisation, with a curriculum designed to explore the complexities of applied arts in depth.

The programme places significant emphasis on research and critical analysis, aiming to advance artistic skills through rigorous research methodologies. Students are encouraged to channel their creativity in a structured, research-focused manner.

Key to the programme is the development of skills to create contextual solutions to challenges within the diverse spectrum of visual arts and its subfields. The curriculum fosters a collaborative approach, teaching students to address the needs of various stakeholders in their creative processes. A hallmark of this postgraduate programme is the cultivation of investigative skills and the integration of various artistic disciplines and knowledge areas within visual arts. Under faculty mentorship, students adopt a self-directed approach to their studies, emphasising critical thinking and problem-solving. For those seeking a Master of Fine Arts degree, this postgraduate programme at MIT-WPU is among the most comprehensive and rewarding options available. As a notable offering among MFA colleges, the programme is ideal for those looking to elevate their expertise in visual arts through a structured and research-intensive curriculum. The Master of Fine Arts degree from MIT-WPU is a significant step for those aspiring to advance their careers in various creative industries. Graduates are well-prepared for a wide range of career paths, including Advertising, Branding, Packaging Design, Publication Design, Graphic Design and E-Learning, Event & Art Direction, social media, and many more. The MFA in Applied Arts at MIT-WPU is more than just a degree Programme; it's an immersive experience that blends artistic skill development with rigorous research training, paving their path to becoming future innovators in the world of visual art.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in 4-year graduation degree in Fine Arts or allied fields from a UGC-approved institution/university or its equivalent (minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the Drawing Test, Personal Interaction (PI) score, Portfolio Assessment conducted by the University according to the prescribed schedule
