Programme

M.Sc. Economics

2-year M.Sc. Economics at MIT-WPU Pune — PI-based admission, no entrance exam. Covers Advanced Econometrics, Derivatives & Risk Analysis, Health Economics, and Green Economics. CIAP internships. Apply 2026.

Programme Overview

The M.Sc. Economics programme at MIT-WPU offers a rigorous and contemporary curriculum designed to enhance analytical and cognitive abilities, preparing students for success in a rapidly evolving economic landscape.

Students gain a solid foundation in areas such as Microeconomics, Macroeconomics, and Econometrics, while also exploring emerging fields like Derivatives, Risk Analysis, and Green Economics-key to understanding the future of finance and sustainability.

The programme emphasises practical learning, integrating real-world projects, internships, and direct engagement with industry professionals. Students develop expertise in advanced data analysis using industry-standard tools like R, Python, and Stata, equipping them to tackle complex economic challenges.

Guided by distinguished faculty and industry experts, students are encouraged to think critically and innovate. Whether aspiring to careers in finance, consulting, public policy, or academia, graduates of this programme are empowered with the knowledge and skills necessary for impactful and versatile careers.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in 3/4-year graduation degree with Economics as a subject (either as minor or major or elective) from a UGC-approved institution/university (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University according to the prescribed schedule

Programme Outcomes
