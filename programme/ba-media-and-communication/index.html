Study BA Media and Communication Hons at MIT WPU Pune. Journalism, PR and digital media with real labs and strong all India placement support. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The BA in Media and Communication at MIT-WPU is an engaging programme aimed at providing students with crucial competencies in media, journalism, and communication. This practical oriented programme integrates the newest digital communication methods and emerging technologies, making certain that students stay up-to-date in a fast-changing media environment.

Through hands-on learning experiences, such as practical projects and internships with prominent organisations, students can customise their education to match their career goals. The programme additionally provides two specialisation options in the last year, enabling concentrated study.

Moreover, incorporating yoga and peace studies fosters comprehensive growth. Graduates of the BA in Mass Media and Communication are thoroughly equipped for thriving careers in numerous fields within the media industry.

Programme Highlights

  • The BA Media and Communication programme encompasses an extensive curriculum including various subjects, such as journalism, digital communication methods, and emerging media technologies, equipping students for the changing industry.
  • Students participate in experiential learning through practical projects and tasks, enabling them to utilise theoretical concepts in real-world scenarios and customise their education according to their professional objectives.
  • In the final year, students get the opportunity to select from two specialisation options, allowing them to concentrate on fields that resonate with their interests and goals in the media sector.
  • The programme involves an internship that is mandatory to be done with top organisations, offering significant practical experience and networking possibilities in the media sector.
  • The curriculum includes sections on yoga and peace studies, fostering overall health and wellness leading to an overall development alongside academic education of an individual.
  • Graduates receive 100% support for securing internships and job placements with top media agencies, ensuring they are well-equipped to launch successful careers in the field of media and communication.

Programme Outcomes

  • Critical Media Analysis : Demonstrate ability to critically analyse media content and understand its social, cultural, and political implications.
  • Professional Content Production : Produce professional-quality content across multiple media platforms (print, broadcast, digital) using industry-standard tools.
  • Compelling Storytelling : Craft engaging narratives that effectively communicate messages to diverse audiences across various media formats.
  • Research Proficiency : Conduct systematic research using appropriate methodologies to inform content creation and media strategies.
  • Effective Communication : Communicate clearly and persuasively in written, oral, and visual formats to diverse audiences.
  • Digital Adaptation : Navigate emerging digital platforms and technologies while adapting to the changing technological landscape.
  • Ethical Media Practice : Apply ethical principles in media production and demonstrate awareness of legal frameworks governing media operations.
  • Collaborative Leadership : Work effectively in teams, demonstrate leadership abilities, and manage resources in media production environments.
  • Industry Readiness : Develop professional portfolios showcasing media competencies and understanding of media industry structures.
  • Global and Cultural Awareness : Create media content with sensitivity to global contexts, diverse audiences, and intercultural considerations.
