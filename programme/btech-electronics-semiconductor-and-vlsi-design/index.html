Programme Overview

The B.Tech in Electronics (Semiconductor and VLSI Design) at MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) is designed to create industry-ready semiconductor professionals who will drive India’s journey toward technological self-reliance. The programme integrates core electronics engineering, semiconductor device physics, and VLSI design methodologies, aligned with the India Semiconductor Mission and global advancements in chip design and fabrication.

Students gain hands-on experience in chip design, verification, and embedded system development using industry-standard tools such as Synopsys, Cadence, Mentor Graphics, and FPGA platforms. The curriculum emphasizes experiential learning, industry collaboration, and research-driven projects, enabling students to solve real-world challenges in fabless design, semiconductor manufacturing, and AI-integrated electronics.

With India’s semiconductor market projected to grow from USD 52 billion in 2024–25 to USD 103.4 billion by 2030 (13% CAGR), MIT-WPU ensures its graduates are equipped with enhanced technical, analytical, and professional skills demanded by this rapidly expanding sector. Through industry internships, global collaborations, and advanced laboratory training, students emerge as competent, innovation-driven, and future-ready engineers ready to lead in the semiconductor and VLSI domain.

Major Tracks

  • Semiconductor Device Design and Fabrication – CMOS, MEMS, and nanomaterial- based device design; cleanroom process simulation.
  • VLSI Design and Verification – Front-end and back-end design flows using Verilog/SystemVerilog and industry EDA tools.
  • Embedded Systems and SoC Design – Hardware–software co-design, FPGA prototyping, and IoT-based SoC applications.
  • AI/ML for Chip Design and Semiconductor Manufacturing – Machine learning for yield prediction, defect detection, and design optimization.
  • Photonics and Quantum Electronics – Emerging domains in optoelectronic and quantum device design.

Eligibility

A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC, with English as a compulsory subject and A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in any one of the following subject groups:

  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics),
  • Physics & Mathematics with any Technical Vocational Subject
  • PCB* (Physics, Chemistry, Biology*),

[*Biology/Biotechnology is applicable only for the Bioengineering Programme.] (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). OR A minimum 60% aggregate marks in a Diploma in Engineering & Technology in a relevant branch from a government-approved technical board.

Selection Process

Admissions are based on the scores of JEE 2026/ MHT-CET 2026/ PERA-CET 2026, or MHT-CET-B* 2026/ NEET 2026* (*applicable only for the Bioengineering programme), along with a 500-word Statement of Purpose (SOP) written by the candidate
