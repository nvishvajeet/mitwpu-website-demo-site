Programme

M.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science And Analytics)

2-year GATE-based M.Tech in Data Science and Analytics at MIT-WPU Pune — covering machine learning, big data, cloud computing, and NLP with industry internships via CIAP. Apply 2026.

Programme Overview

The M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science and Analytics) at MIT-WPU is an advanced and futuristic postgraduate programme. This programme is designed to prepare students for the latest skills in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science.

The curriculum of this programme mainly focuses on the design, development, and analysis of computational systems, emphasising high-performance, and scalable software and hardware solutions. Students during this programme will gain practical experience through project-based learning and essential technologies such as Cloud Computing, Big Data Analytics, and Data Visualisation.

The programme prioritises practical exposure with internships facilitated by the Centre for Industry-Academia Partnerships (CIAP), collaborating with leading tech giants such as Tata Motors and Volkswagen. Additionally, students can choose electives for specialisation in areas like Natural Language Processing and Data Management for Machine Learning. With access to state-of-the-art facilities, high-quality labs and industry connections, graduates are ready to drive innovation and leadership in the rapidly growing field of Data Science and Analytics.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in a 4- year graduation degree in the relevant Engineering branch* from a UGCapproved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination, along with a Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule.

A valid score in GATE score (2026/ 2025/ 2024), PERA-CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases, the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) Score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule.

For admission under sponsored category, candidate must have a minimum of two years of full-time work experience in a registered firm, company, industry, educational/ research institute, Government Department, or Government Autonomous Organization in the relevant field in which admission is sought. A Sponsorship Certificate (in the prescribed format) is mandatory for admission under this category.

* For more information regarding programme-wise branch eligibility, please refer to the M.Tech. rules published on the official website.

FAQs

To pursue an M.Tech programme in Computer Science and Engineering, especially in Data Science and Analytics, the student must possess proficiency in web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and familiarity with programming languages like C and Java, as well as data structures and SQL. Also, strong analytical skills, with an aptitude for problem-solving will serve as an added advantage.

MIT-WPU offers several scholarships to support M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering [Data Science And Analytics] students based on merit, to support high achievers. For more details on eligibility and application procedures, students must refer to the university's official website or contact the admissions office

M.Tech students specialising in Data Science can collaborate with academic institutions, industry partners, and research organisations for research purposes. They can apply to funded projects, internships, and conferences that promote knowledge sharing. Additionally, they can publish their research findings in national and international journals, contributing to advancements in the field.

The Indian government has initiated various programmes to advance data science and analytics, acknowledging its significance for economic development and innovation. Main initiatives consist of Digital India Initiative, National Data Sharing and Accessibility Policy (NDSAP), Skill India Mission and Atal Innovation Mission.
