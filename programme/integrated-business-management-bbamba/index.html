Programme Overview

The Integrated Business Management [BBA+MBA] programme by MIT World Peace University is the integration of undergraduate and postgraduate business management studies. The five-year degree programme is specifically designed to equip students with social, managerial, and entrepreneurial capabilities to effectively handle the requirements of today's global business environment.

The curriculum incorporates ten semesters, offering student exchange opportunities, national and international immersion experiences, and internships. Careers in a variety of industries, including sales, marketing, finance, human resources, operations, supply chain management, business analytics, and information systems management, are made possible by Integrated Business Management (BBA+MBA). Students can also focus on particular fields like corporate social responsibility, sports management, hospitality, or healthcare.

The programme is open to 12th standard graduates and culminates in an MBA degree upon successful completion. Alternatively, students have the option to exit after four years with a BBA (Hons) degree.

Programme Highlights

  • Self-Paced Learning: Students have exit options at the end of the 3rd year and 4th year with an undergraduate degree.
  • Streamlined Path: Complete both bachelor’s and master’s programmes without needing to appear for another entrance examination after the bachelor’s degree.
  • Problem-Solving Focus: Emphasis on critical thinking and decision-making skills through real-world case studies.
  • Multiple Internships: Gain experience with small and medium enterprises, large corporations, and NGOs to understand business functions and decision-making contexts.
  • Experiential Learning: Engage in simulations and outbound activities designed to develop future-ready managers.
  • One-on-One Mentoring: Receive guidance from industry leaders and alumni throughout the programme.
  • International Opportunities: Participate in immersion programmes, semester exchange programmes, and dual degree options.
  • National and International Context: Understand the broader context in which individuals, businesses, and societies operate.

Programme Outcomes

  • Demonstrate a comprehensive understanding of core business management principles and practices across various functional areas such as marketing, finance, human resources, and operations.
  • Analyse the impact of economic, social, political, legal, and technological environments on business operations at both domestic and global levels, with a particular emphasis on ethical and sustainable considerations.
  • Formulate and implement effective business strategies and policies based on sound analysis and evaluation of internal and external factors.
  • Demonstrate effective communication, interpersonal, and teamwork skills to collaborate effectively with diverse stakeholders and lead teams towards achieving organisational goals.
  • Apply advanced strategic thinking and decision-making skills acquired through the integrated MBA curriculum to lead and manage organisations effectively.
