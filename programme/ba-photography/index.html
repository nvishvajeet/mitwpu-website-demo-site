BA Photography (Hons)

Study BA Photography Hons at MIT WPU Pune. Work in professional studios with industry mentors and a strong creative placement record across India. Apply now

Programme Overview

The BA in Photography at MIT-WPU is an engaging, skill-focused programme that equips students with thriving careers in professional photography. This all-inclusive programme highlights practical, experiential education, providing students with crucial skills in lighting, digital camera technology, and image editing programmes like Lightroom and Photoshop.

Students investigate different specialisations, such as Fashion and Portrait Photography, Advertising and Commercial Photography, and Photojournalism. The curriculum additionally features components on communication abilities and environmental studies, fostering comprehensive growth. Under the mentorship of skilled faculties and industry experts, students develop their distinct creative styles and visual problem-solving skills. The programme provides complete placement support, guaranteeing that graduates are equipped to succeed in the competitive photography field.

Major Tracks

  • Fashion & Portrait Photography
  • Nature & Wildlife Photography
  • Digital Imaging & Design Techniques
  • Brand & Commercial Imaging
  • Visual Storytelling & Documentary Photography
  • Fine Art Photography
