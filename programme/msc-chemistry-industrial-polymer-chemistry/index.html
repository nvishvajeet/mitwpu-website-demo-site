M.Sc. Chemistry (Industrial Polymer Chemistry)

Unveil the secrets of matter with an MSC in Chemistry from MIT-WPU. Dive into the fascinating world of atoms, molecules, and chemical reactions. Gain expertise in organic, inorganic, and analytical chemistry, as well as spectroscopy and material science. Experience hands-on experiments in state-of-the-art laboratories and enhance your problem-solving skills. Join our esteemed MSC program and unlock exciting career opportunities in research, industry, and academia. Apply now to become a leader in the field of chemistry with MIT-WPU.

Programme Overview

The M.Sc. Chemistry (Industrial Polymer Chemistry) degree programme is meticulously structured to prepare students for the dynamic polymer industries and research sectors. It offers an interdisciplinary curriculum that spans essential chemistry, polymer chemistry, and polymer engineering. By engaging in lectures, laboratory work, research projects, and industrial internships, students develop a profound comprehension of both the fundamental principles and practical applications of polymers. They collaborate with esteemed faculty members, and experts in their respective domains, and actively contribute to cutting-edge research, addressing real-world challenges.

The PG programme in polymer chemistry provides access to state-of-the-art laboratory facilities and a wealth of scientific resources, enriching student’s knowledge and facilitating valuable networking opportunities with industry professionals. Specialising in Industrial Polymer Chemistry, students acquire a holistic understanding of polymer synthesis, structure-property relationships, polymer processing techniques, testing and characterisation, and diverse applications, thereby preparing them for rewarding careers in industry, academia, research, and advanced studies.

Programme Highlights

  • Taught by Faculty with Diverse Expertise : Benefit from committed faculty with extensive academic, research, and industry experience, ensuring a comprehensive and practical approach to broaden their horizons to connect the theoretical concepts with real applications. Emphasis on research projects aim to well-prepare students for the challenges and opportunities of the polymer world.
  • Interdisciplinary Science and Engineering Blend : Enrol in an interdisciplinary, applied, and job-oriented programme that offers a unique 70:30 blend of Science and Engineering, ensuring students are prepared with a versatile skill set sought after by both scientific and engineering fields ranging from aerospace to healthcare and beyond. Exposure to the student enrichment programmes through the experts from industry, academia and alumni to impart the current trends and future prospects of the polymer field.
  • Extensive Industry Exposure and Internship : Engage in specific industry sponsored live projects and on-job training beyond your curriculum. Immerse yourself in a 6-month full-time industry internship with reputable companies and research institutions as per your inclination and skills, gaining practical, hands-on training on various equipment/ instruments.
  • Placement Support : To explore the countless opportunities in the world of polymers, our unique programme is the real gateway to turn your dreams into reality. The placement cell and the department support students through aptitude tests, mock interviews, group discussions and communication skills to equip students with the confidence for the recruitment process.
  • Merit-Based Scholarship and Paid Internship Opportunities : Avail yourself of scholarship opportunities based on merit, recognising and rewarding academic excellence, making quality education accessible to meritorious students. Deserving students also have an opportunity to get paid during their internship.
  • Personalised Mentorship Support : Experience a robust mentor-mentee interaction system that forms a strong support network, grooming students throughout their academic journey and helping them navigate career choices.
  • Global, National, and Rural Immersion : Engage in unique immersion programmes/ exhibitions at international, national, and rural levels, providing diverse experiences that broaden perspectives and enhance cultural and contextual understanding.

Programme Outcomes

  • Foundational Mastery: Impart sound knowledge of fundamental theories and concepts of polymer science and technology and other branches of chemistry to make them ready for advanced studies or careers.
  • Critical Thinking and Practical Proficiency: Students will develop critical thinking skills and a practical mindset through an exploration of core principles, mechanisms, problem-solving techniques, analytical skills, and hands-on training. This will empower them to arrive at logical conclusions in complex scenarios, fostering adaptability in diverse professional settings.
  • Contemporary Problem Solvers: Students will be equipped to address contemporary challenges in polymer science by applying their knowledge to develop innovative materials and methodologies.
  • Multidimensional Contribution: Students will cultivate a multidisciplinary approach through active engagement in industries, research and development, and entrepreneurial endeavours.
