Programme

BBA Hospitality and Hotel Administration (Hons)

Enroll now in the 3-year BBA programme in Hospitality and Hotel Administration, designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of the hotel industry. Admission open for 2025!

Programme Overview

The hospitality industry is experiencing robust growth, leading to an increasing demand for skilled professionals. The BBA in Hospitality and Hotel Administration at MIT-WPU equips students with a comprehensive understanding of the sector, covering all essential aspects of hospitality management.

This meticulously designed programme, developed by industry experts, focuses on critical areas such as food and beverage management, front office operations, accommodation management, personnel management, and financial accounting, aligning with industry standards.

The curriculum encompasses vital subjects including consumer behavior, sales strategies, marketing techniques, and management processes relevant to the hospitality sector. Students will acquire a well-rounded education that includes insights into entertainment, catering, and culinary arts.

The programme emphasises practical training through national and international internships while also fostering academic knowledge. It encourages entrepreneurial initiatives and nurtures students' inherent talents. Regular engagement with industry professionals ensures that students stay informed about current trends, enhancing their employability upon graduation.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). OR Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. During the Personal Interaction assessment is based on Communication skills, Analytical skills, General Awareness and Personality Traits.

The BBA in Hospitality and Hotel Administration programme at MIT-WPU emphasises practical training through a structured internship component. Students engage in a 4 to 6-week internship during their studies, which provides them with hands-on experience in real-world hospitality settings. This practical exposure is crucial for understanding industry operations and enhancing employability

The curriculum covers several essential areas within hospitality and hotel administration, including:

  • Food and Beverage Management
  • Accommodation Management
  • Personnel Management
  • Financial and Hotel Accounting
  • Consumer Behavior and Marketing Strategies
  • Students also develop skills in leadership, communication, problem-solving, and sustainability practices relevant to the hospitality industry
