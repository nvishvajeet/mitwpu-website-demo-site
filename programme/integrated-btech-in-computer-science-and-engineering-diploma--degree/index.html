Programme Overview

MIT-WPU's B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Computer Science and Engineering offers a seamless educational pathway for students who have completed their 10th standard education. This six-year comprehensive programme bridges secondary education with advanced engineering studies, creating a structured transition from school to university environment.

Students receive thorough training in core computer science domains including software development, data science, and cybersecurity, along with proficiency in essential programming languages such as C, C++, Java, and Python. This contemporary educational approach develops not only technical expertise but also cultivates crucial critical thinking, problem-solving, and research capabilities essential for thriving in today's dynamic technological landscape.

The programme provides students with early exposure to cutting-edge technologies including artificial intelligence, machine learning, and network security. The curriculum balances conceptual understanding with practical application, enhanced through industry collaborations, live internships, research opportunities, and real-world challenges

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in the Class 10/ SSC examination from a government-approved board or its equivalent, with Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule.
