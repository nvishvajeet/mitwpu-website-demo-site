B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Electronics and Communication Engineering - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Study B.Tech ECE in AI and ML after 10th at MIT WPU Pune. Integrated 3+3 year AICTE approved programme with modern labs. Apply now for 2026 admissions.

Programme Overview

The B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Electronics and Communication Engineering - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at MIT-WPU is a six-year programme that starts with the basics before advancing into specialised fields such as computer vision, deep learning, augmented reality (AR), and virtual reality (VR). It combines fundamental electronics with AI and machine learning and prepares you to enter cutting-edge fields like robotics, biomedical engineering, and automotive electronics.

Early exposure to practical learning equips you to design and develop intelligent systems for healthcare, business automation, and other sectors. Learn AI and ML with mentoring from expert instructors while acquiring both knowledge and practical skills. Such early exposure gives you a competitive advantage to forge a successful career in contemporary tech domains.

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in the Class 10/ SSC examination from a government-approved board or its equivalent, with Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • Advanced Facilities : The programme provides modern laboratories with advanced tools and technologies available in the areas of AI, ML, IoT, and Robotics.
  • Industry Partnerships : MIT-WPU has strong collaborations with top-notch industries like Tata Motors, Chevrolet Cars, Volkswagen, Mercedes-Benz, Kirloskar Pneumatic Co. Ltd., Cognizant, Infosys, etc., which assist in providing students with on-the-job training and live projects.
  • Industry Internships : Work with various companies, such as Tata Motors, Sandvik Asia, John Deere, and CUMMINS India Ltd. These internships are great for practising your skills in real-world situations.
  • MOOCs & Interdisciplinary Courses : The curriculum includes Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) and interdisciplinary courses that enhance study related to business management, communication, and entrepreneurship.
  • Learn From Industry Experts : These courses are delivered by industry experts or leaders, keeping you updated on industry trends and future innovations.
  • Immersion Programmes : These opportunities expose you to a broader aspect of the industry from other parts of Bharat.
