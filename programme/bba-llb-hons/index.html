Programme

BBA LL.B. (Hons)

Study BBA LLB Hons at MIT WPU Pune. BCI approved 5 year dual business and law degree with moot courts and a strong all India placement record. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The BBA LLB Hons programme at MIT-WPU is an extensive five-year curriculum that combines management education with legal studies. This cutting-edge programme provides students with a solid grounding in law and business, addressing key fields like finance, marketing, and human resources.

During the programme, students participate in hands-on training, such as moot court simulations and internships, that improve their comprehension of actual legal practices. The curriculum is created to be modern and focuses on practical experience, guaranteeing that graduates are fully equipped for careers in business and legal disputes.

Through the Vidhi Saamvad Series and various other programmes, students gain advantages from engaging with industry professionals, such as high court judges and senior advocates. Furthermore, the programme includes a specialised Employability, Placement, and Career Advancement Cell that assists students in shaping their career trajectories. Focusing on skill enhancement and hands-on expertise, the BBA LLB Hons programme at MIT-WPU equips students with thriving careers in law and other areas.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • The BBA LLB Hons programme offers an integrated curriculum that merges management and legal studies, allowing students to gain an in-depth comprehension of both disciplines over five years.
  • Students participate in mock court activities, apprenticeships, and actual case analyses, guaranteeing they acquire practical experience in legal procedures.
  • Classes are led by expert instructors including seasoned legal professionals, active attorneys, and global faculty, enriching the educational experience with varied viewpoints.
  • Frequent engagement with high court judges, senior lawyers, and corporate in-house counsels offers important perspectives on the legal and business fields.
  • The Employability, Placement, and Career Progression Cell aids students in shaping their career trajectories and enhancing their employability ratings.
  • The programme includes a modern curriculum with up-to-date legal practices and management techniques, equipping students with the changing needs of the sector.
  • Being part of the Literary and Debate Committee enables students to improve their speaking and rhetorical abilities through diverse engagements such as MUNs and parliamentary debates.
  • The university provides international, national, and rural immersion experiences to raise students' awareness of real-world challenges and develop advanced problem-solving abilities.
