B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security and Forensics)

Study B.Tech CSE Cybersecurity at MIT WPU Pune. AICTE approved programme. 3500+ companies hire from campus with a strong all India placement record. Apply for 2026.

Programme Overview

MIT-WPU's B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering programme with specialisation in Cyber Security and Forensics provides comprehensive education in the critical domains of cyber security, ethical hacking, network security, and cryptography.

This specialised programme develops graduates with advanced capabilities to identify, analyse, and protect information systems using cutting-edge technological tools. Students bridge theory and practice through hands-on applications in real-world scenarios, industry internships, and practical projects.

Through strategic international industry collaborations, MIT-WPU prepares qualified Computer Science graduates who are equipped to address complex global cybersecurity challenges.

Eligibility

A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC, with English as a compulsory subject and A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in any one of the following subject groups:

  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics),
  • Physics & Mathematics with any Technical Vocational Subject
  • PCB* (Physics, Chemistry, Biology*),

[*Biology/Biotechnology is applicable only for the Bioengineering Programme.] (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). OR A minimum 60% aggregate marks in a Diploma in Engineering & Technology in a relevant branch from a government-approved technical board.

Selection Process

Admissions are based on the scores of JEE 2026/ MHT-CET 2026/ PERA-CET 2026, or MHT-CET-B* 2026/ NEET 2026* (*applicable only for the Bioengineering programme), along with a 500-word Statement of Purpose (SOP) written by the candidate

FAQs

The curriculum covers areas such as network security, ethical hacking, cryptography, digital forensics, cyber laws, and information security management. These subjects provide the necessary foundation to protect the integrity of computer systems and investigate crimes carried out using the internet.

The curriculum strongly emphasises practical application through immersive learning experiences. Students participate in industry internships and specialised workshops, while gaining expertise in state-of-the-art laboratories. These facilities are equipped with professional-grade tools and digital forensics softwares. This comprehensive approach ensures graduates develop the practical skills necessary to address complex cybersecurity challenges in real-world environments.

MIT-WPU maintains an ideal student-to-faculty ratio, ensuring personalised attention and fostering increased interaction between students and industry-experienced professional faculty.

Specialisations ensure that students are equipped with the right skill sets for careers such as Cyber Security Analyst, Penetration Tester, Digital Forensics Specialist, and Cloud Computing Security Architect in sectors like IT, banking, or government.

MIT-WPU offers a comprehensive and robust curriculum that provides hands-on training, practical exposure, and strong connections with industries, helping you defend digital systems effectively. Information security is a dynamic and exciting field that attracts individuals interested in working with technology, solving problems, and defending critical, sensitive information against cyber attacks. It is especially suitable for those who want to work with computers and security systems.

Candidates must have passed Class 10+2/ 12th with a minimum 50% aggregate (45% for reserved category from Maharashtra) in PCM or PCB or Physics & Mathematics with any Technical Vocational Subject. Physics, Mathematics/ Biology* & English are Compulsory subjects with Chemistry/Biotechnology* or Technical Vocational Courses in Class 10+2/ 12th or equivalent examination. OR Passed Diploma in Engineering & Technology with minimum 60% marks in a relevant branch from a government-approved board.
