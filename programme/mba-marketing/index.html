MBA Marketing

Master the art of marketing with an MBA in Marketing from MIT-WPU. Gain expertise in consumer behaviour, brand management, digital marketing, and market research. Join MIT-WPU, a prestigious institution renowned for its excellence in business education. Develop strategic thinking, analytical skills, and innovative marketing techniques. Prepare for dynamic career opportunities in advertising, sales, product management, and more. Apply now to accelerate your marketing career and stand out in the competitive business world with MIT-WPU's MBA in Marketing program.

Programme Overview

In the current fast moving marketplace, strategic marketing approach is the key to the effectiveness of any product or service. This approach has geared up the need of skilled and professionally trained marketing professionals, making it one of the top choices among other MBA programmes.

The MBA in Marketing programme at MIT-WPU recognises the pivotal role of marketing across various industries. The programme is skillfully designed with a commitment to its practical implementation incorporating dynamic learning experiences that promote critical thinking and innovation.

The programme provides hands-on experience preparing students for the upcoming industrial challenges and equips them with the skill set required to thrive in the career. Students here engage in experiential case studies, industry immersions and projects that helps them in bridging the gap between theory and its practical application.

MIT-WPU students emerge out as pro marketers and influential leaders, prepared to navigate through the complexity of today’s industrial landscape.

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 3/ 4- year graduation degree in any stream from a UGC-approved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). AND A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) examinations, with English as a compulsory subject. AND A valid non-zero score in the relevant entrance examination conducted by the competent authority.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of a valid score in CAT 2025, XAT 2026, NMAT 2025, GMAT, CMAT 2026, MAH-CET 2026, PERA 2026 or MAT (September 2025 onwards). AND Candidates will also be evaluated through 50 marks online CBT, Group Discussion (GD), Extempore, Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule, along with consideration of their Past Academic Record and Work Experience. AND The valid (non-zero) national level entrance score is mandatory for confirmation of admission.

Programme Highlights

  • Case-Based Learning : The programme incorporates a dynamic case-based learning approach that allows students to apply their theoretical concepts practically and deepen their understanding of real-world scenarios.
  • Internal Competence for Outer Demand (ICODs) : ICOD helps in strengthening core competencies enabling students to navigate through the upcoming industrial challenges
  • Skills Enhancement tracks : Integrated within the curriculum, these tracks are designed to develop the essential skills required for success in the competitive corporate landscape.
  • Global Exposure : With MIT-WPU students get global exposure through rural, national, and international immersion programmes where they learn about diverse cultural, economic, and business environments for a well-rounded educational experience.
  • Advisory Board Insights : Leverage the expertise of a distinguished Advisory Board composed of national and international experts who provide valuable guidance and insights into industry trends.
  • Guest Lectures : Gain knowledge from guest lecturers, management experts, and international faculty members who enhance the overall educational experience with their real-world insights and expertise.

Programme Outcomes

  • Strategic marketing expertise to produce successful solutions that cater to a company's unique marketing requirements.
  • Analyse and assess difficult marketing problems with sound research techniques and critical thinking.
  • Generate marketing plans that are effective and at the same time creative.
  • Leadership experience that will be helpful in a variety of marketing positions.
  • In-depth knowledge through case studies, real-world projects, and group industry immersions.
  • Deliver marketing concepts to your target audiences, both technical and non-technical stakeholder marked clearly and effectively.
  • Uphold social responsibility by taking into account cultural, legal, and societal aspects when making any marketing decisions.
  • Perform well in interdisciplinary teams and demonstrate flexibility in the dynamic field of marketing.
  • Foster a mindset of lifelong learning and professional development to ensure ongoing success in marketing careers.
