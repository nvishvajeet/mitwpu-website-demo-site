Programme

BBA Sales and Marketing (Hons)

Sales and marketing roles are among the highest hiring areas in India. BBA Sales and Marketing at MIT WPU Pune gives you the skills to get there. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The BBA in Sales and Marketing programme at MIT-WPU provides a comprehensive foundation in business principles, with a focused emphasis on key areas such as sales strategies, consumer behaviour, digital marketing, and brand management. Through a dynamic blend of theoretical insights and hands-on experiences, students gain the skills needed to thrive in today’s competitive business environment. This programme equips students to understand market dynamics, drive business growth, and excel in leadership roles, fostering strong communication abilities essential for high-impact positions in diverse industries.

Major Tracks

  • Sales Strategies and Operations
  • Marketing Management
  • Digital Marketing
  • Brand Management and Advertising
  • Entrepreneurship and Start-up Marketing
  • Market Research and Analytics

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Or Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.

Programme Highlights

  • The programme offers a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical insights into sales, marketing strategies, consumer behaviour, digital marketing, and market research.
  • Focuses on building critical skills such as communication, negotiation, customer relationship management, and brand development.
  • Includes modules on emerging trends such as digital marketing, social media, SEO, SEM, and data-driven marketing strategies.
  • Collaboration with industry leaders for hands-on projects and internships, providing real-world exposure to the sales and marketing domain.
  • Regularly organised sessions with industry experts and guest lecturers to understand contemporary marketing challenges and innovations.
  • Training and mentorship for students aspiring to venture into entrepreneurship, with specific focus on marketing and sales-driven startups.
  • Opportunities for international exchange programmes and collaborations with universities abroad to understand global marketing trends and sales techniques.
  • Exposure to CRM software, marketing analytics tools, and AI-powered marketing techniques to stay ahead in the digital world.
