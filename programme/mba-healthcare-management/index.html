Programme Overview

The MBA in Healthcare Management is a dynamic two-year degree programme designed to equip students with the specialised skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving healthcare sector. As the field of healthcare continues to expand at an unprecedented pace, this programme prepares candidates to navigate and excel in various domains beyond traditional hospital management. These include healthcare insurance, healthcare IT, pharmaceutical management, clinical research, home healthcare, consultancies, public health, medical devices and equipment, diagnostic services, and more. With a curriculum that blends management principles with healthcare-specific expertise, this programme offers a comprehensive education that opens doors to diverse career opportunities in one of the most critical and fast-growing industries globally.

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 3/ 4- year graduation degree in any stream from a UGC-approved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). AND A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) examinations, with English as a compulsory subject. AND A valid non-zero score in the relevant entrance examination conducted by the competent authority.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of a valid score in CAT 2025, XAT 2026, NMAT 2025, GMAT, CMAT 2026, MAH-CET 2026, PERA 2026 or MAT (September 2025 onwards). AND Candidates will also be evaluated through 50 marks online CBT, Group Discussion (GD), Extempore, Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule, along with consideration of their Past Academic Record and Work Experience. AND The valid (non-zero) national level entrance score is mandatory for confirmation of admission.

Programme Highlights

  • The programme is meticulously designed to offer both theoretical knowledge and practical exposure, adhering to Bloom’s Taxonomy and the guidelines of the National Education Policy.
  • Students will develop key competencies in data analysis and interpretation, problem-solving, logical business decision-making, computation, communication, management, and leadership.
  • Graduates are equipped to excel in roles within health authority organisations, primary care trusts, hospital administrations, and various other healthcare sectors.
  • The programme emphasises hands-on learning through exposure to advanced management practices, analytical and healthcare insights, and research-driven innovations in resource allocation.
  • Students will engage in a deep exploration of political, cultural, and ethical issues in healthcare, preparing them to navigate complex challenges with a well-rounded perspective.

Programme Outcomes

  • Equip students with a deep understanding of the healthcare landscape at both national and global levels, enabling them to navigate and influence the industry's dynamics effectively.
  • Foster the development of essential skill sets, including conceptual, interpersonal, and technical skills, necessary for thriving in the healthcare industry.

  • Cultivate socially responsible global citizens capable of managing and leading diverse healthcare organisations with a focus on ethical and sustainable practices.

FAQs

An MBA in Healthcare Management is a postgraduate-level programme that combines business administration principles with specialised knowledge in healthcare systems. It prepares students for leadership roles in hospitals, clinics, pharmaceutical companies, and other healthcare organisations.

Graduates can pursue various roles, including:

  • Hospital Administrator
  • Healthcare Consultant
  • Health Informatics Manager
  • Healthcare Finance Manager
  • Medical Practice Manager
  • Pharmaceutical Project Manager
  • Medical Director

Salaries can vary based on roles and experience, but here are some average figures:

  • Hospital Administrator: ₹5.4 LPA
  • Healthcare Consultant: ₹8 LPA
  • Health Informatics Manager: ₹10 LPA
  • Pharmaceutical Project Manager: ₹11.4 LPA
  • Medical Director: ₹15 LPA

Key skills include:

  • Leadership and management abilities
  • Strategic decision-making
  • Knowledge of healthcare policies and regulations
  • Financial management skills
  • Effective communication and interpersonal skills
  • Drug Development Product Management Specialisation
  • The Business of Healthcare Specialisation
  • Integrative Nursing Specialisation
  • Medical Billing and Coding Certificate
  • Medical Office Administration Certificate

Students interested in pursuing a career in healthcare, can gain a number of major advantages by supplementing their studies with a Master of Business Administration in Healthcare Management. Hospitals, clinics, and a variety of other facilities operate similarly to regular business, thus skills and expertise are easily transferred.
