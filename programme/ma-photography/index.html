Programme Overview

Master the art of commercial photography with the M.A. programme at MIT-WPU. Programme equips you with the theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience to excel. You willl delve into visual storytelling, technical finesse, and market adaptation. Our holistic curriculum explores the evolution of photography, studio work, visual literacy, and branding strategies. Embracing innovation and adaptability, we foster a community of versatile professional photographers prepared to thrive in the ever-changing industry. Elevate your skills and launch your career in commercial photography.

Major Tracks

  • Advertising & Commercial Photography
  • Fashion & Editorial Photography
  • Photojournalism & Documentary Photography
  • Contemporary Portrait Photography

Programme Highlights

  • Engage in a Comprehensive Curriculum: Dive deep into a curriculum that seamlessly blends theoretical knowledge with practical application. Programme is specifically designed to meet the demands of the thriving commercial photography industry.
  • Practical Experience: Gain hands-on experience through real-world projects, industry collaborations, and internships. Apply your skills in practical settings and build a strong professional portfolio to showcase your talent.
  • Expert Faculty: Learn from a team of esteemed industry professionals and renowned experts. Our faculty members bring a wealth of experience and invaluable insights to guide you on your photographic journey.
  • Advanced Facilities: Access cutting-edge facilities and resources, including studios, advanced equipment, and industry-standard software, to elevate your learning experience and unleash your creative potential.
  • Career Support: Receive dedicated career support services tailored to launch your successful career in commercial photography. This includes resume building, portfolio development, valuable networking opportunities, and job placement assistance.
