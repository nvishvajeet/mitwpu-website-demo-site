Programme Overview

The BBA in Digital Marketing at MIT-WPU is an interactive four-year undergraduate programme designed to prepare students for the rapidly evolving digital landscape. This programme combines fundamental business training with focused guidance in digital marketing strategies, tools, and analysis. Learners will explore key subjects such as search engine optimisation (SEO), social media promotion, content marketing, and data analysis. The programme emphasises hands-on learning through practical projects, internships, and collaborations with industry, ensuring that students gain real-world experience.

With a team of industry experts, the programme imparts insights into current trends and efficient strategies in digital marketing. Students also participate in workshops and seminars that enhance their abilities and comprehension.

After finishing the programme, graduates will have the analytical and strategic skills necessary to excel in various roles within the digital marketing sector. The BBA in Digital Marketing at MIT-WPU prepares students to become innovative professionals ready to meet the challenges of today's digital economy.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Or Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule. A valid score in MAH BBA CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases,the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MITWPU as per the prescribed schedule.
