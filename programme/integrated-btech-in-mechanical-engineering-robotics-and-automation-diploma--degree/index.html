Programme

B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Mechanical Engineering - Robotics and Automation

The Integrated B.Tech Mechanical Engineering at MIT-WPU with a specialisation in Robotics and Automation is an innovative interdisciplinary programme tailored for students who want to immediately initiate their career in the said field right after class 10. This programme surpasses traditional education by seamlessly integrating mechanical, electronics, and computer science domains.

Programme Overview

B.Tech. After 10th (3 years Diploma + 3 years Degree) in Mechanical Engineering with specialisation in Robotics and Automation combines higher secondary education with a specialised mechanical engineering curriculum, focused on robotics and automation.

Starting with basic concepts, it progresses into Mechanical Design, Control Engineering, Robot System Building, and other areas, requiring a blend of theoretical education and practical experience to solve today’s challenges in manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, healthcare, and other fields. This programme develops the technical expertise, innovation, and problem-solving skills you need to lead and make a difference in the rapidly growing robotics and automation industry.

Major Tracks

  • Mechanical Design & Simulation
  • Control Engineering
  • Materials, Manufacturing & Automation
  • IOT and Artificial Intelligence
  • Robot System Building

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in the Class 10/ SSC examination from a government-approved board or its equivalent, with Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule.

The programme covers topics related to mechanical engineering and robotics, including mechanical design, control engineering, robotics, AI, IoT, and automation. Through a combination of theory and practical applications, students learn to build systems incorporating robotics, AI, automation models, and data-driven decision-making.

MIT-WPU offers industry internships, immersion programmes and hands-on projects in sectors such as automotive, manufacturing, and robotics. Students also get the opportunity to work on live projects across AI, IoT, and robotics, gaining hands-on experience that prepares them for their future careers.

Through the Industry-Academia Partnerships Centre, the university offers 100% placement assistance, connecting students with top-tier firms like Tata Technologies, Infosys, and Cognizant. Career guidance, resume building, mock interviews, and networking opportunities ensure that students are industry-ready.
