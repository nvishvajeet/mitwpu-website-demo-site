B.Tech (2nd Year Lateral Entry) After 3 Years of Diploma in Engineering

Already completed your diploma? Join B.Tech 2nd year directly at MIT WPU Pune. AICTE approved lateral entry with 3500+ industry partners. Apply now for 2026.

Programme Overview

MIT-WPU's B.Tech (2nd Year Lateral Entry) programme provides diploma holders a streamlined programme to complete their engineering degree in just three years. This specialised pathway builds upon your existing practical foundation while introducing advanced theoretical concepts across engineering disciplines. By directly entering the second year of B.Tech studies, you save valuable time while gaining comprehensive technical expertise. The programme seamlessly bridges the gap between diploma and degree-level engineering education, positioning you for earlier entry into competitive job markets.

The curriculum blends advanced theoretical knowledge with your practical diploma experience, creating well-rounded engineering professionals. You'll benefit from MIT-WPU's cutting-edge facilities, expert faculty mentorship, and extensive industry partnerships that enhance your career prospects through integrated job placement support.

Eligibility

A minimum of 60% marks in a Diploma in Engineering and Technology in the relevant branch, obtained in the academic years 2025-26, 2024-25, or 2023-24, from an AICTE-approved or Central/State Government approved Institution/Board or its equivalent. (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State)

Selection Process

Admission is based on the candidate’s Diploma scores and a 500-word Statement of Purpose (SOP).
