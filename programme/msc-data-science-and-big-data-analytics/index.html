Programme Overview

The M.Sc. in Data Science and Big Data Analytics at MIT-WPU is a cutting-edge programme that merges statistics, mathematics, and software programming to offer a thorough understanding of data management and analysis.

Over the span of two years, this programme equips students with the skills needed to collect, store, and analyse large datasets, empowering them to use analytics as a vital tool for decision-making.

With a strong emphasis on practical learning, students engage in mini-projects, hands-on assignments, and internships that cover essential topics like Data Warehousing, Machine Learning, Big Data Architecture, Python programming, and Cloud Computing. The curriculum also highlights data visualisation techniques using tools such as Microsoft Power BI and Tableau.

Crafted by industry experts, the programme includes professional electives like Data Analytics Pipeline and Big Data Security to align with current market demands. Students gain real-world experience through internships with top companies like Deloitte and Infosys. Emphasising holistic development through Yoga and Peace studies, graduates are well-prepared for leadership roles in the IT sector, supported by 100% placement assistance.

Programme Highlights

  • The programme involves a comprehensive curriculum including statistics, mathematics, and software programming for an in-depth understanding of data collection, organisation, storage, and analysis.
  • Students gain practical understanding through hands-on projects, practical assignments, internships, and immersion programmes, involving domains such as Data Warehousing, Machine Learning, Big Data Architecture, Python programming, and Cloud Computing.
  • The programme offers proficiency in industry-relevant tools like Microsoft Power BI and Tableau, along with exposure to No-SQL databases and professional electives including Data Analytics Pipeline, Business Analytics, and Big Data Security.
  • The curriculum is developed by and delivered by expert faculty with extensive experience in academia, industry, and research.
  • Students gain practical exposure from internship opportunities with leading multinational companies such as Deloitte Consulting, Cognizant, Infosys, and L&T Infotech, providing valuable insights into industry demands.
  • The programme emphasises the integration of Yoga and Peace studies for holistic development while offering access to a strong global alumni network for enhanced networking and job opportunities.
  • The programme curriculum helps develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills among students, preparing them for research-oriented leadership roles in the IT sector.
  • The programme also guarantees 100% placement support to help students secure promising careers in the IT sector.
