Programme

MBA Banking and Financial Services

Build a comprehensive business foundation and advance your skills with MIT-WPU’s MBA in Banking and Financial Services program. The course trains you in domains like banking operations, financial markets, strategic decision-making, risk management, and much more.

Programme Overview

The MBA in Banking and Financial Services at MIT-WPU is a specialised postgraduate programme tailored to meet the growing demand for skilled professionals in the financial sector. With the global banking and financial services market projected to grow significantly, there has never been a better time to pursue a career in this field.

According to the Indian Brand Equity Foundation (IBEF), the rapid growth of the Indian financial services industry and the need for qualified professionals in the sector highlight the importance of an MBA in this field.

This programme provides a deep understanding of banks' operating principles, the financial markets, banking risk management, and strategic financial decision-making. You are also introduced to various segments of the banking industry, such as corporate finance, international banking, and financial analysis, which gear you toward becoming a managerial professional in the financial services industry.

Apart from core financial knowledge, MIT-WPU also includes leadership and management skills. We shape you with core knowledge in a wide spectrum of finance and pure economics, strategic thinking, decision-making, and team management. The programme features a strong practical element, with scope for you to work with case studies through internships and consulting projects.

The MBA programme integrates essential ethical considerations and regulatory compliance knowledge to ensure you are prepared to navigate the financial services industry responsibly and sustainably. By the end of the programme, you will be equipped with the skills to manage financial risks, make informed financial decisions, and lead in a dynamic, technology-driven environment.

Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 3/ 4- year graduation degree in any stream from a UGC-approved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 55% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). AND A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) examinations, with English as a compulsory subject. AND A valid non-zero score in the relevant entrance examination conducted by the competent authority.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of a valid score in CAT 2025, XAT 2026, NMAT 2025, GMAT, CMAT 2026, MAH-CET 2026, PERA 2026 or MAT (September 2025 onwards). AND Candidates will also be evaluated through 50 marks online CBT, Group Discussion (GD), Extempore, Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule, along with consideration of their Past Academic Record and Work Experience. AND The valid (non-zero) national level entrance score is mandatory for confirmation of admission.

Programme Highlights

  • Specialised Financial Expertise : Banking operations, financial markets, risk management, and financial decision-making
  • Leadership Development : Curriculum is aimed at increasing leadership, strategy, and team management skills.
  • FinTech Integration : Curates technological integration in finance and introduces students to the future of banking and FinTech evolution.
  • Practical Learning and Case Studies : Real financial problems learned via case-based learning, internships and consultancy projects.
  • Ethical and Regulatory Focus : All greater emphasis in making financial decisions on ethics, corporate governance and regulatory compliance.
  • International Focus : International finance and cross-border training are core components of the curriculum.
  • Industry Certifications : Tie-ups with CFA, CFP, NiSM, and NCFM certifications for greater employability.
  • Career Development : Strong focus on career advancement and entrepreneurial opportunities in financial consulting, risk management, and corporate finance.

Programme Outcomes

  • Effectively apply the concepts of banking operations, financial markets, and financial services in real-world banking environments, including managing products such as loans and investments.
  • Design strategies that recognise, react, and oversee monetary danger in banking establishments.
  • Leverage sophisticated financial analysis methodologies to assist in smart and tactical financial decision-making, enhancing effectiveness in banking and overall improvements in financial services delivery.
  • Apply knowledge of the regulatory environment to ensure compliance with banking regulations and manage legal and ethical challenges in financial transactions.
  • Showcase knowledge and skills to handle cross-border banking activities and navigate the maze of global capital markets.
  • Leveraging your finance background, become an entrepreneur. Offer consultancy services to businesses that require keen financial strategies, investments, and risk management.
