Programme Overview

The B.Des. M.Sc. (Visual Communication Design) at MIT-WPU is a modern and innovative course designed to shape your career in visual communication, covering both traditional and emerging design domains. This programme focuses on visual storytelling through key design principles such as graphic design, typography, branding, film design, and immersive technologies like AR, VR, and Motion Graphics. The dynamic curriculum enables you to understand how visual communication is essential for reporting across today's diverse media platforms.

Specialising in areas like branding, digital media, interactive design, photography, animation, and film production, this programme offers practical learning opportunities. You will engage in real-world projects, such as designing websites, creating brand identities, and working on motion design, which will prepare you to excel in the industry upon graduation. The curriculum also emphasises user-centred design and cultural awareness, ensuring that the artefacts you create are impactful and socially responsible.

In addition, the programme encourages entrepreneurship, fostering both technical and creative skills with a focus on developing your own projects and businesses. Internships, live projects, study tours, and alumni networks provide valuable industry exposure and networking opportunities. Upon completing this programme, you will be equipped for a variety of roles in advertising, digital marketing, film production, and interactive media, and will be well-prepared to thrive in the design industry of the technological age.

Programme Highlights

  • Cutting Edge Knowledge & Skills : Keeps you current with the latest tools around graphic design, branding, web design and motion graphics, including emerging technologies like AR and VR.
  • Theory to Practice : Understanding how to implement theory into making more functional and impactful visual design
  • Internships: Internships and live projects with the leading industry experts enable professional networking and hands-on exposure.
  • Integrative Curriculum : The integration of film production, branding, design theory and interactive media within the programme facilitates richer holistic perspectives of visual communication design among students.
  • Global Challenges to Innovations : Identify real-world problems and develop innovative design solutions that demonstrate social responsibility and sustainability while having a global perspective.
  • Entrepreneurship Support : Promotes entrepreneurship by creating an environment of support via networking, funding and mentorship with the MIT-WPU Pune Technology Business Incubator (TBI).
  • Alumni & Mentor Network : A professional network of alumni and mentors that assists you in your career development.

Programme Outcomes

  • Incorporate graphic design, motion graphics, and branding into visually appealing and effective visual designs.
  • Become proficient in visual communication tools such as cinematography, 3D modelling, digital editing and interactive media.
  • Create memorable brand identities, website designs, and multimedia content across a range of channels.
  • Integrate design principles to develop user-centred designs which are appealing to a large audience, balancing diversity & inclusion.
  • Collaborate with industry partners on emerging interdisciplinary fields in design, addressing challenges beyond the products we build and pinpointing socio-cultural issues in the present.
  • Critically evaluate visual designs and their decisions.
  • Start your own design startup or freelancing business and foster entrepreneurial thinking.
