Programme Overview

The M.Tech in E-Mobility at MIT-WPU is a futuristic programme consciously developed to prepare students for impactful careers in the rapidly evolving field of electric vehicles or EVs. This programme offers a dedicated curriculum that covers both the basics and advanced concepts regarding sustainable transportation like electric vehicle design, battery technology, and power electronics, ensuring students are equipped with the latest industry knowledge and applications.

MIT-WPU with its state-of-the-art facilities, including the Electric Vehicles Siemens Lab and the Rubiscape Center of Excellence, ensures that students benefit from hands-on training and research opportunities. The programme also focuses on strong industry connections through collaborations with leading organisations like Siemens and Tata Power, enhancing networking and career prospects.

Additionally, the programme also allows a flexible structure for working professionals to conduct research within their current roles, facilitating career advancement and growth. On completion of this programme, the graduates emerge as skilled engineers ready to contribute to innovative solutions in e-mobility, addressing global challenges in transportation sustainability.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in a 4- year graduation degree in the relevant Engineering branch* from a UGCapproved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination, along with a Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule.

A valid score in GATE score (2026/ 2025/ 2024), PERA-CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases, the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) Score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule.

For admission under sponsored category, candidate must have a minimum of two years of full-time work experience in a registered firm, company, industry, educational/ research institute, Government Department, or Government Autonomous Organization in the relevant field in which admission is sought. A Sponsorship Certificate (in the prescribed format) is mandatory for admission under this category.

* For more information regarding programme-wise branch eligibility, please refer to the M.Tech. rules published on the official website.

FAQs

E-mobility works in two stages:

  • Converting electrical energy stored in batteries into mechanical energy to power vehicles
  • Utilising components like inverters and electric motors for efficient propulsion.

For an M.Tech e-Mobility, one must possess cross-domain engineering knowledge. Also, proficiency in disciplines like problem-solving, integrated systems, vehicle systems, data analytics, telecommunication, CAD, battery systems, control systems, and more is an advantage.

The career scope for M.Tech e-Mobility is extensive, offering opportunities in electric vehicle design, battery technology, power electronics, and sustainable transportation systems across various industries.

The programme focuses on advanced topics in electric vehicles, smart electric mobility, robotics, automation, and related technologies. It emphasises hands-on training, industry exposure, and practical projects to prepare students for careers in the evolving mobility sector.

The curriculum typically includes subjects like Electric Vehicle Technology, Battery Management Systems, Power Electronics, Electric Drives, Embedded Systems, Smart Mobility Solutions, Charging Infrastructure, Vehicle Dynamics, and Advanced Control Systems, along with electives and project work.
