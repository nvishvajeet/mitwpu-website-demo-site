M.Pharm Pharmaceutics

PCI-approved M.Pharm Pharmaceutics at MIT-WPU, Pune. Expert training in drug formulation, novel drug delivery & GMP. GPAT accepted. Stipend available. Top pharma placements. Apply 2026.

Programme Overview

The M.Pharm in Pharmaceutics programme at MIT-WPU is designed to prepare students with advanced knowledge and skills in areas like drug formulation and delivery systems. This programme includes a cumulative learning module that contains practical training and research along with a theoretical understanding of advanced concepts. This approach enables students to explore various aspects of pharmaceutical sciences, including dosage form design, quality control, and regulatory affairs. With a curriculum that blends theoretical concepts with hands-on experience, the programme commits to preparing graduates for diverse roles in the pharmaceutical industry, academia, and research organisations.

The programme also promotes innovation and critical thinking, essential for addressing contemporary challenges in drug development in current times. Join the M.Pharm Pharmaceutics programme at MIT-WPU to advance your career in this dynamic and evolving field, and contribute to improving healthcare outcomes for betterment of the society.

Eligibility

Minimum 55% aggregate marks in a B. Pharm. degree from an institution recognized by the Pharmacy Council of India or an equivalent examination from a government-approved university/ institution (Minimum 50% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Additionally, Candidates must be registered with the State Pharmacy Council. Those who are not registered at the time of admission must submit the registration certificate within one month of securing admission.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the GPAT 2026, 2025, or 2024 score and Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University according to the prescribed schedule. For admission under sponsored category, candidate must have a minimum of two years of full-time work experience in a registered firm, company, industry, educational/research institute, Government Department, or Government Autonomous Organization in the relevant field in which admission is sought. A Sponsorship Certificate (in the prescribed format) is mandatory for admission under this category
