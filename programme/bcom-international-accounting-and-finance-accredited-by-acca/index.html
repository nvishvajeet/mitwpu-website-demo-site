B.Com International Accounting and Finance (Hons.)

Study B.Com in International Accounting and Finance at MIT WPU Pune. ACCA accredited with a global finance curriculum and strong placement record. Apply now.

Programme Overview

MIT-WPU B.Com offers a top-notch programme in International Accounting and Finance accredited by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) This specialised curriculum offers a comprehensive study of international accounting standards and financial practices. These topics provide an overview of global financial practices, allowing you to have a holistic view of the financial world.

By incorporating ACCA's quality standards into the academic framework, MIT-WPU offers students the unique opportunity to earn exemptions of up to the Skill Level under the ACCA Qualification. Industry engagement is a strong pillar of the programme, ensuring you immerse yourself in the practical workings of the real world through practical learning, industry case studies, and masterclasses with industry veterans. The programme prepares you for long-standing careers at multinationals and financial institutions around the world.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).   • Integrated Curriculum : B.com International Accounting and Finance curriculum is carefully curated to meet ACCA standards. You can get exemptions for 9** subjects. This allows you to fast-track your professional certification.
  • Career Opportunities : You are well-equipped for a wide array of roles in multinationals, finance, and accounting sectors. The programme also provides networking opportunities with industry leaders and their committees, which can increase your employability.
  • Global Recognition: This programme is accredited by the Association Chartered Certified Accountants Association (ACCA), making sure graduates are given a qualification recognised across more than 170 countries.
