Programme

B.Com Fintech (Hons.)

Finance meets technology in B.Com Fintech at MIT WPU Pune. UGC approved with a digital finance curriculum and 3500+ industry hiring partners. Apply now.

Programme Overview

The B.Com Fintech (Hons.) programme at MIT-WPU delivers an innovative finance education that combines advancements in technology with the core principles of finance. The curriculum is regularly updated to include the latest developments in blockchain technology, data analytics, digital currency, and cybersecurity. These are fundamental skills required to navigate a fast-paced, evolving financial world.

Collaborative projects with fintech companies and industry-specific workshops help you not only learn but also apply your knowledge in real-time. This programme is designed to equip you with the skills needed to thrive in fast-paced environments, whether within startups or traditional financial institutions undergoing digital transformation. The focus is on fostering an entrepreneurial mindset and a creative approach to problem-solving.

Major Tracks

  • Principles of Economics
  • Data Analytics
  • Corporate and Cyber Law
  • Forensic Accounting

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Class 10+2/ HSC or its equivalent examination in any stream with English subject (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State). Or Minimum 60% aggregate marks in any 3 years Engineering Diploma from State Government approved Institution/ board.

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination and the Personal Interaction (PI) score, conducted by the University as per the prescribed schedule

Programme Highlights

  • Innovative Curriculum : This programme combines finance with technology, exploring new trends like data analytics, digital currency, cybersecurity, and blockchain technology. You learn how technology is revolutionising traditional financial services.
  • Networking Opportunities : Partnerships with the tech industry create opportunities for you with access to industry leaders and potential employers. Workshops and regular hackathons encourage you to innovate and use your creativity.
  • Skill Development : Students gain technical skills essential for careers in the rapidly evolving fintech landscape. Programme cover programming basics for financial applications, data analysis techniques, and the regulatory environment surrounding fintech innovations
  • Capstone Projects : Get involved in practical capstone projects to create fintech solutions that focus on real-life financial issues. This practical learning is key to developing a strong portfolio as a fintech professional.

Programme Outcomes

  • Extract insights from data analysis and market dynamics to inform decision-making processes
  • Leverage financial technology innovations — such as blockchain and AI — for financial solutions.
  • Apply your knowledge to global financial systems, regulations and market structures (focused on fintech)

  • Lead in fintech-focused integration of technology with business strategy.

FAQs

Core subjects in the programme include areas like blockchain, data analytics, digital banking, financial regulations, and fintech innovations.

MIT-WPU offers 100% internships assistance through CIAP and industry collaborations with some of the leading fintech companies and industry leaders.

The programme consists of modules in business communication, leadership and team collaboration which help you develop soft skills and realise leadership potential.

The highest Cost to Company (CTC) for the programme is at INR 11.75 LPA, while the average salary ranges from 6 to 10 LPA. (data as per Glassdoor)

B.Com Fintech from MIT-WPU is your entry point into finance, a sector currently defined by rapid evolvement through the pragmatic accessibility that technology brings to the table. You will learn all aspects of practical knowledge including financial services, banking and risk management, as well as innovative topics like blockchain technology, cybersecurity and financial data analytics.

This programme will help you gain the financial and technology skills required to flourish in this fast-growing landscape of fintech. MIT-WPU boasts experienced faculty, an industry-based curriculum, & internship opportunities that are geared toward nurturing your career in fintech. You will have access to a world-class education, with expert faculty and resources at your fingertips, as well as the opportunity to collaborate with like-minded individuals from around the world who are making their mark in financial technology.

The B.Com Fintech curriculum at MIT-WPU consists of subjects that are significant to learning finance and technology. These subjects include major topics like Financial Analysis, Corporate Finance, Portfolio Management, Management Accounting, Wealth Management.
