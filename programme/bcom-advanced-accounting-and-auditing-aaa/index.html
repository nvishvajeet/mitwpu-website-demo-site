Programme Overview

MIT-WPU’s B.Com in Advanced Accounting and Auditing programme equips students with advanced accounting principles and auditing. This programme provides you with detailed aspects from the CA (Intermediate) level of the ICAI. It includes modern accounting issues, forensic accounting, and international auditing standards.

Mandatory internships allow you to gain practical experience that helps you apply what you learned in a concrete setting. Guided by industry leaders and academicians, you’ll develop a firm understanding of ethical practice and regulatory compliance.

Major Tracks

  • Business Economics
  • Business Statistics
  • Financial Accounting
  • Management Accounting
  • Corporate and Business Laws

Programme Highlights

  • Specialised Focus : The programme provides a comprehensive overview of the areas of advanced accounting and auditing. Students explore subjects including forensic accounting, risk assessment and financial reporting standards.
  • Expert Faculty : Learn from seasoned industry professionals and academics with real-world experience. You get to attend guest lectures and seminars that give you more exposure to current trends and practices.
  • Practical Exposure : Internships and real-world projects give you practical training, making you more attractive to employers. Partnerships with local businesses offer priceless knowledge of the world of accounting.
  • Technological Integration : Modern accounting software and technology are integrated into the curriculum so that students are trained in those technologies that will determine the future of accounting.

Programme Outcomes

  • Become familiar with international capital markets, international accounting standards and up-to-date finance regulations.
  • Employ your knowledge of accounting and auditing in real-time situations where every decision you make propels the business forward.
  • Get to know CA subjects and thorough knowledge of starting accounting and auditing.
  • Establish high standards of ethics in the areas of financial reporting, auditing and corporate governance.
  • Prepare for leadership positions at multinational corporations, accounting firms, and financial institutions.
