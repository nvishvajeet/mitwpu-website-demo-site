Specialise in construction management with M.Tech Civil at MIT WPU Pune. AICTE approved 2 year programme with industry projects and strong faculty. Apply now

Programme Overview

M.Tech in Civil Engineering programme with a specialisation in Construction Engineering and Management MIT-WPU is a programme dedicated to equip students with advanced knowledge and skill sets in the field of construction management and engineering practices.

This programme spans two years, and the curriculum includes theoretical concepts as well as practical applications. The programme offers a detailed syllabus that focuses on areas such as project management, construction technology, and sustainable practices. Students can also choose from specialised electives that include advanced structural engineering, construction materials, and quality management, complemented by hands-on training through industry internships.

The programme inculcates leadership and managerial skills among students, preparing them for roles in project planning, execution, and supervision within the construction sector. With a strong focus on innovation and sustainability, this M.Tech programme aims to produce professionals capable of addressing new-age challenges in civil engineering. The graduates who pursue this degree are well-prepared for successful careers in various domains, including infrastructure development, consultancy, and research.

Eligibility

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in a 4- year graduation degree in the relevant Engineering branch* from a UGCapproved institution/ university or its equivalent (Minimum 45% aggregate marks for candidates belonging to the Reserved Category from Maharashtra State).

Selection Process

Admission will be based on the merit of the MIT-WPU CET 2026 Entrance Examination, along with a Personal Interaction (PI) score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule.

A valid score in GATE score (2026/ 2025/ 2024), PERA-CET 2026 will also be considered for the selection process; in such cases, the candidate must additionally appear for the Personal Interaction (PI) Score conducted by MIT-WPU as per the prescribed schedule.

For admission under sponsored category, candidate must have a minimum of two years of full-time work experience in a registered firm, company, industry, educational/ research institute, Government Department, or Government Autonomous Organization in the relevant field in which admission is sought. A Sponsorship Certificate (in the prescribed format) is mandatory for admission under this category.

* For more information regarding programme-wise branch eligibility, please refer to the M.Tech. rules published on the official website.

FAQs

The M.Tech Civil Engineering (Construction Engineering and Management) is an advanced postgraduate programme. The student of this programme learns about the latest construction techniques, project management, and sustainable practices within the civil engineering domain.

Having relevant work experience for admission to the M.Tech programme adds to the applicant’s profile. But candidates without any work experience can also directly be admitted to the programme.

There are various scholarships and stipends available for the meritorious students. For further details visit https://mitwpu.edu.in/scholarships

Salary expectations for M.Tech Civil engineering graduates in Construction Engineering and Management (CEM) can differ according to experience, location, and industry needs. Graduates usually make between INR 6 LPA and INR 10 LPA.

Certainly, obtaining an M.Tech in Civil Engineering (CEM) creates numerous global career prospects. Graduates can pursue job opportunities in international construction companies, engineering firms, and global corporations.
