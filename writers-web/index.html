The School of Liberal Arts underlines the aesthetic sensibilities and artistic expression in education which helps its essence to thrive in its departments. Such is a pursuit of the exclusive literary club by the Department of English here at MIT World Peace University where Writers’ Web prevails in its motto to foster the art, craft, and advocacy of language and literature. We believe that it’s human desire to have something to express and words do find a way into our hearts and minds easily. Writers' Web was established in 2018, in the presence of Javed Akhtar and Shabana Azmi, in order to find hidden wordsmiths among the student community and a commune to celebrate the fascinating world of literature.
