ASME

ASME is a prestigious official student association of the School of Mechanical Engineering, MIT-WPU which undertakes Student Events, provides networking and social infrastructure to the Mechanical under-graduates and conducts the Mechanical Alumni Relation Chain.

American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) MIT-WPU Student Section, an initiative aimed at fostering technical excellence, innovation, and professional development among students in the domains of mechanical engineering, robotics, and allied fields. The section will serve as a platform for organizing workshops, expert lectures, competitions, and industry interactions, thereby bridging the gap between academia and industry.
