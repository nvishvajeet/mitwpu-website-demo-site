Director - Branding & Communications

Mr. Haranath Ghosh brings over 24 years of extensive experience in brand management, corporate communications, marketing, public relations, and event management across education, media, advertising, and financial sectors. Throughout his distinguished career, he has successfully operated in both national and international markets, driving impactful content creation, digital promotion, advertising campaigns, and strategic communication initiatives.

He holds an MBA in Marketing and a B.Sc. (Hons) in Physics from Delhi University. Before joining MIT-WPU, Mr. Ghosh served as Director of Branding & Communications at Chandigarh University. He also led marketing efforts for Infinity Media Networks, a Johannesburg-based media conglomerate, for over six years. Additionally, he was Regional Manager at ICICI Bank, overseeing the Premium and Travel Cards business for North India. Earlier in his career, he gained valuable experience with reputed advertising agencies in Delhi.

Under his visionary leadership and deep business acumen, organizations have experienced significant growth in brand visibility and business performance. Besides his corporate expertise, Mr. Ghosh is also an accomplished author of three published fiction novels, reflecting his passion for storytelling. He has spearheaded numerous successful national campaigns across TV, print, and outdoor media, strengthening brand equity for multiple organizations.
