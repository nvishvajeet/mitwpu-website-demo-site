Founder President’s Office

Prof. Dr. Vishwanath D. Karad

Founder President

Prof. Dr. Vishwanath D. Karad established MAEER in 1983 and founded MIT World Peace University. His institutional vision brings education, science, spirituality, and the pursuit of world peace into one public mission.

Founder President’s Office · Head of Staff: Yogesh Parkhi · yogesh.parkhi@mitpune.edu.in · vdk@mitpune.edu.in

Executive President’s Office

Dr. Rahul V. Karad

Executive President

Dr. Rahul V. Karad serves as Managing Trustee and Executive President of MAEER’s MIT Group of Institutions and Executive President of MIT-WPU. His work centres on value-based education, youth leadership, and public life.

Executive President’s Office · Head of Staff: Padmanabh Deshmukh
